7 An Act relating to the State Capitol Preservation
Commission; authorizing the placement of a monument
8 near the State Capitol Arch; directing the State
Capitol Commission to work with private entities to
9 erect the monument; providing for codification; and
declaring an emergency.
10
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
15 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 4110.2 of Title 74, unless there
16 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
17 The State Capitol Preservation Commission or designee is hereby
18 authorized to permit and arrange for the placement of the Gold Star
19 Family Monument. This monument shall be designed, constructed, and
20 placed on the Capitol Grounds by private entities at no expense to
21 the State of Oklahoma. The State Capitol Preservation Commission or
22 designee is authorized to assist private entities in placing the
23 monument in the northwest corner behind the State Capitol Arch and
24 arranging a suitable time for its placement.
ENGR. H. B. NO. 4486 Page 1
1 SECTION 2. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
2 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
3 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
4 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
5 Passed the House of Representatives the 11th day of March, 2026.
