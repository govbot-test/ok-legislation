9 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
10 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 12-201 of Title 43A, unless
11 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
12 A. The State of Oklahoma shall designate the Department of
13 Mental Health and Substance Abuse Services to have primary oversight
14 over the suicide prevention and crisis service activities and
15 essential coordination with designated 988 Lifeline Crisis Centers
16 to provide crisis intervention services and crisis care coordination
17 to individuals accessing the 988 Mental Health Lifeline program from
18 any jurisdiction within Oklahoma twenty-four (24) hours a day, seven
19 (7) days a week.
20 B. The Oklahoma Department of Mental Health and Substance Abuse
21 Services (ODMHSAS) shall have primary oversight of suicide
22 prevention and crisis service activities.
