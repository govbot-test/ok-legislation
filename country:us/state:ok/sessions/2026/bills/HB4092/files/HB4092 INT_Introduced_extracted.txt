6 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
7 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 12-201 of Title 43A, unless
8 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
9 A. The State of Oklahoma shall designate the Department of
10 Mental Health and Substance Abuse Services to have primary oversight
11 over the suicide prevention and crisis service activities and
12 essential coordination with designated 988 Lifeline Crisis Centers
13 to provide crisis intervention services and crisis care coordination
14 to individuals accessing the 988 Mental Health Lifeline program from
15 any jurisdiction within Oklahoma twenty-four (24) hours a day, seven
16 (7) days a week.
17 B. The Oklahoma Department of Mental Health and Substance Abuse
18 Services (ODMHSAS) shall have primary oversight of suicide
19 prevention and crisis service activities.
20 C. The designated 988 Lifeline Crisis Centers shall meet 988
21 Lifeline Program requirements and best practices guidelines for
22 operational, performance and clinical standards.
23 D. The ODMHSAS shall use its authority to promulgate rules and
24 regulations to allow appropriate information sharing and
Req. No. 15629 Page 5

1 comm unication between and across crisis and emergency response
2 systems for the purpose of real-time crisis care coordination
3 including, but not limited to, deployment of crisis and outgoing
4 services and linked, flexible services specific to crisis response.
5 E. The state or related public health authority shall assure
6 active collaborations and coordination of service linkages between
7 the designated centers and crisis receiving and stabilization
8 services for individuals accessing the 988 Lifeline through
9 appropriate information sharing regarding availability of services.
10 F. The state or related public health authority and designated
11 988 Lifeline Crisis Centers shall utilize technology to enhance
12 communication and coordination in the delivery of behavioral health
13 crisis services, and for data collection, analysis and sharing.
