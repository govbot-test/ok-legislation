13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
15 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3004 of Title 56, unless there
16 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
17 A. For the purposes of this section:
18 1. “Alzheimer’s diagnosis” means a clinical diagnosis of
19 Alzheimer’s disease as classified under International Classification
20 of Diseases (ICD) codes G30.0, G30.1, G30.8, and G30.9 of the most
21 recent edition of the ICD;
22 2. “Cognitive assessment and care planning services” shall have
23 the same meaning as the services described in accordance with
24
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - HB2268 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 Curr ent Procedural Terminology billing code 99483, as established by
2 the American Medical Association;
3 3. “Dementia diagnosis” means a clinical diagnosis of dementia
4 as classified under ICD code range F01 through F03 of the most
5 recent edition of the ICD; and
6 4. “Health care provider” means a licensed physician, physician
7 assistant, or Advanced Practice Registered Nurse recognized by the
8 Oklahoma Board of Nursing as a Certified Nurse Practitioner or
9 Clinical Nurse Specialist.
10 B. The state Medicaid program shall provide coverage of and
11 reimbursement for cognitive assessment and care planning services
12 delivered to a Medicaid member who has received an Alzheimer’s
13 diagnosis or dementia diagnosis from a health care provider.
14 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
