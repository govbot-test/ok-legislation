6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to revenue and taxation; amending 68
O.S. 2021, Section 1355, as amended by Section 1,
8 Chapter 370, O.S.L. 2023 (68 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section
1355), which relates to exemptions from sales tax;
9 providing for complete exemption for the sale of
motor vehicles; amending 68 O.S. 2021, Section 1361,
10 which relates to consumer to pay tax; removing
requirement for consumer to pay sales tax on sale of
11 motor vehicle in the same manner as motor vehicle
excise tax; amending 68 O.S. 2021, Section 1402,
12 which relates to excise tax on storage; removing
requirement for consumer to pay sales tax on sale of
13 motor vehicle in the same manner as motor vehicle
excise tax; amending 68 O.S. 2021, Section 1404, as
14 amended by Section 2, Chapter 370, O.S.L. 2023 (68
O.S. Supp. 2025, Section 1404), which relates to
15 exemptions from use tax; providing for complete
exemption for the sale of motor vehicles; amending 68
16 O.S. 2021, Section 2106, which relates to exemptions
to excise tax in lieu of other taxes; removing
17 exemption for sales tax collected on motor vehicles;
providing an effective date; and declaring an
18 emergency.
