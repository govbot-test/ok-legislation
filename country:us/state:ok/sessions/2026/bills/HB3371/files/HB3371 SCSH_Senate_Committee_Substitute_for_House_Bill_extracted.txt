13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 4 O.S. 2021, Section 30.7, is
15 amended to read as follows:
16 Section 30.7. The Oklahoma Department of Agriculture, Food, and
17 Forestry may deny a license, or renewal thereof, or revoke a license
18 of any applicant, animal shelter operator, or commercial pet breeder
19 who fails to meet the standards of animal care, fails to display a
20 commercial pet breeder license number as required by Section 30.10
21 of this title, or fails to follow the application process adopted by
22 the Department, or if the person:
23 1. Is convicted of a crime involving animal cruelty;
24
Req. No. 3878 Page 1
1 2. Is convicted of violating the Commercial Pet Breeders and
2 Animal Shelter Licensing Act more than three times;
3 3. Is convicted of a type of felony specified by subparagraphs
4 a through pp aaa of paragraph 2 of Section 571 of Title 57 of the
5 Oklahoma Statutes;
6 4. Is convicted of a felony punishable under the Oklahoma
7 Racketeer-Influenced and Corrupt Organizations Act; or
8 5. Has held or applied for a United States Department of
9 Agriculture license pursuant to the Animal Welfare Act and whose
10 license was suspended or revoked, or whose application was refused
11 due to the improper care of animals.
12 SECTION 2. AMENDATORY 4 O.S. 2021, Section 30.10, is
13 amended to read as follows:
14 Section 30.10. A. An animal shelter operator and commercial
15 pet breeder shall prominently display a copy of the animal shelter
16 license or commercial pet breeder license at the facility of the
17 animal shelter or commercial pet breeder. A commercial pet breeder
18 shall include the commercial pet breeder license number in each
19 advertisement for the sale or transfer of an animal by the
20 commercial pet breeder. A commercial pet breeder shall include in
21 each contract for the sale or transfer of an animal by the
22 commercial pet breeder the commercial pet breeder license number.
23 B. As used in this section, “advertisement” means any attempt
24 by a person or entity to induce the purchase, sale, trade, or
Req. No. 3878 Page 2
1 tran sfer of a pet by a commercial pet breeder through the use of a
2 notice or announcement. An advertisement shall include, but not be
3 limited to, any communication disseminated through the following:
4 1. Visual or audio communications such as radio, television,
5 film, streaming services, or any other broadcast or recorded format;
6 2. Printed materials such as newspapers, magazines, flyers,
7 brochures, billboards, business cards, or other physical
8 publications; and
9 3. Digital and electronic communications such as websites,
10 social media platforms, mobile applications, electronic mail, text
11 messages, digital display boards, and any other internet-based or
12 electronic communication systems.
13 SECTION 3. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
