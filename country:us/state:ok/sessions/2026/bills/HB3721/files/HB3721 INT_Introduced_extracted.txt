Re: RBH No. 15912
RBH No. 15912 would allow a surviving spouse or surviving children
of a member not participating in the system’s Back DROP plan. This
change is for Police Retirement and Pension System, Firefighter
Retirement System and Oklahoma Law Enforcement Retirement System.
This bill amends OPLAAA to define the above change as non fiscal.
RBH No. 15912 is a non fiscal bill as defined by OPLAAA as amended.
