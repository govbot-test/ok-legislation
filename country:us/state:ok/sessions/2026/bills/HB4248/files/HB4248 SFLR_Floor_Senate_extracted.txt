8 An Act relating to medical marijuana; prohibiting
certain persons from selling certain beverages to
9 persons under certain age; prohibiting certain
persons from furnishing certain beverages to persons
10 under certain age; prohibiting certain persons under
certain age from possessing certain beverages;
11 providing for codification; and providing an
effective date.

13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
16 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 7401 of Title 63, unless there
17 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
18 No person shall knowingly sell, deliver, or furnish any beverage
19 containing hemp to any person under twenty-one (21) years of age, or
20 possess any beverage containing hemp if under twenty-one (21) years
21 of age.
22 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
