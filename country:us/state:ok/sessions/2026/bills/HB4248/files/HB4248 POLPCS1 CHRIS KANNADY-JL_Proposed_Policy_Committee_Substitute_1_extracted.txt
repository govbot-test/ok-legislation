9 An Act relating to public health and safety;

13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
15 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 7401 of Title 63, unless there

17 No beverages containing hemp shall be sold to any person under
