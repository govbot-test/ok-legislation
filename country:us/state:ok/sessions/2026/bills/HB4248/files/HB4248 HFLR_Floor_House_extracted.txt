11 An Act relating to public health and safety;
prohibiting the sale of beverages containing hemp to
12 anyone of a specific age; providing for codification;
and providing an effective date.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 7401 of Title 63, unless there
18 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 No beverages containing hemp shall be sold to any person under
20 the age of twenty-one (21) years.
21 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
