1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 2nd Session of the 60th Legislature (2026)
3 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
FOR
5
6
7 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
8 An Act relating to public health and safety;
prohibiting the sale of beverages containing hemp to
9 anyone of a specific age; providing for codification;
and providing an effective date.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 7401 of Title 63, unless there
15 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 No beverages containing hemp shall be sold to any person under
17 the age of twenty-one (21) years.
18 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
