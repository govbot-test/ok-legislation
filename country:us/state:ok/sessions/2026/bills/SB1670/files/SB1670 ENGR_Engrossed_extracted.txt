6 An Act relating to higher education; amending 70 O.S.
2021, Section 3206.3, which relates to research and
7 development at institutions of higher education;
directing certain policy established by the Oklahoma
8 State Regents for Higher Education to include certain
tiered system of ownership and require certain
9 compensation; adding area of encouragement for
certain development; requiring certain policies to be
10 reviewed with certain frequency; updating statutory
language; providing an effective date; and declaring
11 an emergency.
