6 An Act relating to memorial highway and bridge
designations; designating the CPT David Ward Neely
7 Memorial Highway; providing for codification; and
providing an effective date.
8
9
10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
11 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
12 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1698.510 of Title 69, unless
13 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
14 The section of U.S. Highway 169 beginning at the Double Creek
15 Bridge extending south to the county line between Nowata County and
16 Rogers County shall be designated as the “CPT David Ward Neely
17 Memorial Highway”. The Department of Transportation shall cause
18 suitable markers bearing that name to be placed on the highway.
19 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
20 COMMITTEE REPORT BY: COMMITTEE ON AERONAUTICS AND TRANSPORTATION
February 16, 2026 - DO PASS
