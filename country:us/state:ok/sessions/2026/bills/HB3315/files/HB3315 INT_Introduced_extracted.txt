Section 1:
1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 2nd Session of the 60th Legislature (2026)
4
5
6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to higher education; requiring the
Oklahoma State Regents for Higher Education conduct
8 feasibility study for implementing three-year
bachelor's degree programs; requiring study
9 investigate and analyze certain issues; requiring
State Regents to submit study report; providing for
10 codification; providing an effective date; and
declaring an emergency.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
15 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3252 of Title 70, unless there
16 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
17 A. The Oklahoma State Regents for Higher Education shall
18 conduct a thorough study to determine the feasibility of
19 implementing bachelor's degree programs that require only three (3)
20 years, or ninety (90) credit hours, to complete in this state. The
21 study shall investigate a variety of fields of study and determine
22 the feasibility of reducing specific course requirements, quantity
23 of electives, and total credit hours required for graduation. The
24
Req. No. 13936 Page 1
1 stud y shall consider and evaluate potential issues related to
2 accreditation and degree marketability.
3 B. On or before July 1, 2027, the State Regents shall prepare
4 and submit to the Governor, the Speaker of the Oklahoma House of
5 Representatives, and the President Pro Tempore of the Oklahoma State
6 Senate a report concerning the findings of the study.
7 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
8 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
9 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
10 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
11 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
