15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 2 O.S. 2021, Section 7-414, is
17 amended to read as follows:
18 Section 7-414. A. The provisions of the Oklahoma Milk and Milk
19 Products Act shall not be construed to:
20 1. Include incidental sales of raw, unpasteurized cow milk,
21 goat milk, or sheep milk directly to consumers at:
22 a. at the farm where the milk is produced,

1 b. at feed stores; however, the container the milk is
2 being sold out of must be conspicuously labeled with
3 signage stating "Unpasteurized Milk",
4 c. at a farmer's market; however, the container the milk
5 is being sold out of must be conspicuously labeled
6 with signage stating "Unpasteurized Milk", or
7 d. via delivery from the farm where the milk is produced;
8 2. Preclude Prohibit the advertising of the incidental sale of
9 raw, unpasteurized cow milk, goat milk, or sheep milk; and or
10 3. Prohibit any farmer or producer from making cheese using
11 milk or cream produced on the farm of such farmer or producer.
12 B. For purposes of this section, incidental sales of goat milk
13 are those sales where the average monthly number of gallons sold
14 does not exceed one hundred (100).
15 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
