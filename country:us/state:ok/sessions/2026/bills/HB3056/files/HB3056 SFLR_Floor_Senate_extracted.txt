12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 2 O.S. 2021, Section 7-414, is
14 amended to read as follows:
15 Section 7-414. A. The provisions of the Oklahoma Milk and Milk
16 Products Act shall not be construed to:
17 1. Include incidental sales of raw, unpasteurized cow milk,
18 goat milk, or sheep milk directly to consumers at:
19 a. at the farm where the milk is produced,
20 b. at feed stores; however, the container the milk is
21 being sold out of must be conspicuously labeled with
22 signage stating "Unpasteurized Milk",
23
24
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - HB3056 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 c. at a farmer's market; however, the container the milk
2 is being sold out of must be conspicuously labeled
3 with signage stating "Unpasteurized Milk", or
4 d. via delivery from the farm where the milk is produced;
5 2. Preclude Prohibit the advertising of the incidental sale of
6 raw, unpasteurized cow milk, goat milk, or sheep milk; and or
7 3. Prohibit any farmer or producer from making cheese using
8 milk or cream produced on the farm of such farmer or producer.
9 B. For purposes of this section, incidental sales of goat milk
10 are those sales where the average monthly number of gallons sold
11 does not exceed one hundred (100).
12 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
