8 An Act relating to crimes and punishments; making
certain acts unlawful; providing penalties; defining
9 term; providing for codification; and providing an
effective date.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1525 of Title 21, unless there
15 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 A. 1. It shall be unlawful for any person, acting as a straw
17 person or party, to knowingly and willfully purchase, attempt,
18 endeavor and conspire, or endeavor or conspire to obtain and
19 purchase or obtain or purchase, any property within this state on
20 behalf of a foreign national.
21 2. It shall be unlawful for a foreign national to knowingly and
22 willfully purchase, attempt, endeavor and conspire, or endeavor or
23 conspire to obtain and purchase or obtain or purchase, any property
24 within this state.
ENGR. H. B. NO. 3495 Page 1
1 B. Any person who violates the provisions of this section
2 shall, upon conviction, be guilty of a Class D1 felony offense
3 punishable by imprisonment as provided for in subsections B through
4 F of Section 20N of Title 21 of the Oklahoma Statutes.
5 C. As used in this section:
6 1. "Foreign national" means a person who is neither a citizen
7 nor a national of the United States and who entered the United
8 States by fraud or misrepresentation; and
9 2. "Straw person or party" means a third party who is put up in
10 name only to take part in a transaction. This term includes, but is
11 not limited to, a nominal party to a transaction, one who acts as an
12 agent for a foreign national for the purpose of taking title to
13 property and executing whatever documents and instruments the
14 foreign national may direct respecting the property, or a person who
15 purchases property for a foreign national to conceal the identity of
16 the foreign national or to accomplish some purpose otherwise not
17 allowed.
18 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
