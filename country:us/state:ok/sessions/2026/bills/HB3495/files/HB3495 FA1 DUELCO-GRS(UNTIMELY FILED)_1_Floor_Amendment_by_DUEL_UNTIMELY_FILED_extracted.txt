FLOOR AMENDMENT
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
State of Oklahoma
SPEAKER:
CHAIR:
I move to amend HB3495
Page 1 Section 1 Lines 17
Of the Engrossed Bill
By inserting before the word "It" the number "1.";
Page 1, Section 1, Line 21.5: By inserting the following language: "2. It
shall be unlawful for a foreign national to knowingly and willfully
purchase, attempt, endeavor and conspire, or endeavor or conspire to
obtain and purchase or obtain or purchase, any property within this
state.";
Page 1, Lines 23 & 24 and Page 2, Lines 1 & 2: By deleting all language
beginning with the word "felony" on Line 23, Page 1 through the word
"imprisonment" on Line 2, Page 2 and inserting in lieu thereof, the
following language: "Class D1 felony offense punishable by imprisonment as
provided for in subsections B through F of Section 20N of Title 21 of the
Oklahoma Statutes"; and
Page 2, Section 1, Line 3: By deleting after the word "section" the comma
and inserting in lieu thereof, the following:
":

Section 2:
1. "Foreign national" means a person who is neither a citizen nor a
national of the United States and who entered the United States by fraud
or misrepresentation; and

Section 3:
2."
