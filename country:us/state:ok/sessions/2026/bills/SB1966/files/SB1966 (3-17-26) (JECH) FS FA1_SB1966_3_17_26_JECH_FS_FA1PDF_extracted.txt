8 An Act relating to memorial highway and bridge
designations; designating the Clyde and Grace Cook
9 Memorial Bridge; designating the Terry Walker
Memorial Interchange; designating the Sheriff Marty
10 Grisham Memorial Interchange; designating the MoMM2
Arlon Francis Wetsel Memorial Bridge; designating the
11 John Skelley Memorial Highway; designating the CPT
David Ward Neely Memorial Highway; designating the
12 Patrolman Mark Harris Memorial Interchange;
designating the PV2 Earl Maggart Memorial Bridge;
13 providing for codification; and providing an
effective date.
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
18 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1698.510 of Title 69, unless
19 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
20 The bridge on U.S. Highway 70 crossing over Winters Creek
21 approximately three (3) miles northeast of Bennington in Bryan
22 County shall be designated as the “Clyde and Grace Cook Memorial
23 Bridge”. The Department of Transportation shall cause suitable
24 permanent markers bearing that name to be placed on the bridge.
Req. No. 3809 Page 1
1 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
2 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1698.511 of Title 69, unless
3 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
4 The interchange of Interstate 35 and Blue Bell Road in Springer,
5 Carter County, shall be designated as the “Terry Walker Memorial
6 Interchange”. The Department of Transportation shall cause suitable
7 permanent markers bearing that name to be placed at the interchange.
8 SECTION 3. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
9 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1698.512 of Title 69, unless
10 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
11 The interchange of Interstate 35 and State Highway 32 in
12 Marietta, Love County, shall be designated as the “Sheriff Marty
13 Grisham Memorial Interchange”. The Department of Transportation
14 shall cause suitable permanent markers bearing that name to be
15 placed at the interchange.
16 SECTION 4. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1698.513 of Title 69, unless
18 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 The bridge on U.S. Highway 283 crossing over Elm Fork Red River
20 north of Mangum in Greer County shall be designated as the “MoMM2
21 Arlon Francis Wetsel Memorial Bridge”. The Department of
22 Transportation shall cause suitable permanent markers bearing that
23 name to be placed on the bridge.
