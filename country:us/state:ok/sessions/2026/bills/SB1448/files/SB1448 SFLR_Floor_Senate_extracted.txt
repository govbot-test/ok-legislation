6 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Consumer Protection
Act; amending 15 O.S. 2021, Section 754, which
7 relates to exemptions; modifying exemptions; and
providing an effective date.
8
9
10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
11 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 15 O.S. 2021, Section 754, is
12 amended to read as follows:
13 Section 754. Nothing in the Oklahoma Consumer Protection Act
14 shall apply to:
15 1. Publishers, broadcasters, printers or other persons insofar
16 as an unlawful practice as defined in Section 753 of this title
17 involves information that has been disseminated or reproduced on
18 behalf of others without knowledge that it is an unlawful practice;
19 2. Actions or transactions regulated under laws administered by
20 the Corporation Commission or;
21 3. Actions or transactions required or authorized under
22 specific laws or regulations administered by any other regulatory
23 body or officer acting under statutory authority of this state or
24 the United States, or to acts;
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - SB1448 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 4. Acts done by retailers or other persons acting in good faith
2 on the basis of information or matter supplied by others and without
3 knowledge of the deceptive character of such information or matter;
4 and or
5 3. 5. The collection of monies denominated as gross receipts
6 tax on mixed beverages, sales tax or use tax, or asserted injuries
7 or damages that are monies that have been collected as, or
8 denominated as, gross receipts tax on mixed beverages, sales tax or
9 use tax, and which have been remitted to the Oklahoma Tax Commission
10 or other governmental taxing authority.
11 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
12 COMMITTEE REPORT BY: COMMITTEE ON JUDICIARY
