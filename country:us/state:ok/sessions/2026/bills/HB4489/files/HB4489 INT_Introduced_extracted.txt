13 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY Section 1, Chapter 188, O.S.L.
14 2024 (17 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section 180.13), is amended to read as
15 follows:
16 Section 180.13. A. While the Oklahoma Corporation Commission
17 is subject to the Oklahoma Open Meeting Act, Corporation
18 Commissioners are exempt from and may discuss administrative,
19 operational, and procedural matters of the Corporation Commission
20 between or among Commissioners, even if a quorum is present,
21 provided the discussions do not address appropriation of Corporation
22 Commission funds and no official action is taken. Corporation
23 Commissioners may not discuss pending legislative matters before the
24 Corporation Commission without complying with the Oklahoma Open

1 11. Assessing performance reviews and duties of the Director of

3 B. Corporation Commissioners may attend and participate in
4 conferences, training, educational, press, and social events, where
5 the topic of these events is the Corporation Commission, even if a
6 quorum is present, provided that no official action is taken and any
7 discussion of the business of the Corporation Commission is
8 incidental to the event.
9 C. Corporation Commissioners may not discuss any pending
10 legislative proceedings of the Corporation Commission without
11 complying with the Oklahoma Open Meeting Act.
12 D. Corporation Commissioners may attend and participate in
13 meetings and proceedings of the Legislature, even if a quorum is
14 present, provided that no official action is taken.
15 E. As used in this subsection, the term "event" means any
16 matters authorized in subsections A through D of this section.
17 After participating in any events authorized by this section, the
18 Corporation Commission shall be required to provide timely
19 documentation, in the form and manner as determined by a public vote

22 of providing public notice of the subject matter received by or
23 discussed between or among Commissioners, even if a quorum is
24 present, outside of a public meeting. Such documentation shall be
