9 An Act relating to professions and occupations;
amending 59 O.S. 2021, Section 46.4, as last amended
10 by Section 4, Chapter 138, O.S.L. 2024 (59 O.S. Supp.
2025, Section 46.4), which relates to the Board of
11 Governors of the Architects, Landscape Architects and
Licensed Interior Designers of Oklahoma; extending
12 sunset date; updating statutory language; providing
an effective date; and declaring an emergency.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 59 O.S. 2021, Section 46.4, as
17 last amended by Section 4, Chapter 138, O.S.L. 2024 (59 O.S. Supp.
18 2025, Section 46.4), is amended to read as follows:
19 Section 46.4. There is hereby re-created, to continue until
20 July 1, 2026 July 1, 2031, in accordance with the provisions of the
21 Oklahoma Sunset Law, a board to be known as the “Board of Governors
22 of the Architects, Landscape Architects and Licensed Interior
23 Designers of Oklahoma”, hereinafter referred to as the Board. The
24 Board shall be composed of eleven (11) members including six persons

1 who are duly licensed to practice architecture and are in good
2 standing in this state, two persons who are duly licensed to
3 practice landscape architecture and are in good standing in this
4 state, two persons who are duly licensed interior designers and who
5 are active and in good standing, and one lay member. Each member of
6 the Board shall be a qualified elector of this state, and the
7 architect, landscape architect, and licensed interior designer
8 members shall have had five (5) years’ licensing experience as the
9 professional position requires in this state. Re-creation of the
10 Board shall not alter existing staggered terms. Board members,
11 other than the lay member, shall be appointed for a period of five
12 (5) years thereafter; provided, that nothing herein shall affect the
13 tenure of office of anyone who is a member of the Board on May 31,
14 1957. A member may be reappointed to succeed such membership. The
15 architect, landscape architect, or the licensed interior designer
16 members may be appointed by the Governor from a list of nominees
17 submitted by respective professional societies of this state.
18 Membership in a professional society shall not be a prerequisite to
19 appointment to the Board. The lay member of the Board shall be
20 appointed by the Governor to a term coterminous with that of the
21 Governor. The lay member shall serve at the pleasure of the
22 Governor. Provided, the lay member may continue to serve after the
23 expiration of the term of the member until such time as a successor
24 is appointed. Vacancies which may occur in the membership of the

1 Boar d shall be filled by appointment by the Governor. Each person
2 who has been appointed to fill a vacancy shall serve for the
3 remainder of the term for which the member the person shall whom he
4 or she will succeed was appointed and until a successor, in turn,
5 has been appointed and shall have qualified. Each member of the
6 Board, before entering upon the discharge of the duties of the
7 member, shall make and file with the Secretary of State a written
8 oath or affirmation for the faithful discharge of official duties.
9 Each member of the Board and staff shall be reimbursed for travel
10 expenses pursuant to the State Travel Reimbursement Act.
11 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
12 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation

15 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
