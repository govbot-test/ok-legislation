3/24/2026 2:11:09 pm
FLOOR AMENDMENT
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
State of Oklahoma
SPEAKER:
CHAIR:
I move to amend HB3796
Page 19 Section 4 Lines 8
Of the Engrossed Bill
By inserting after the words "Long-Term Care Insurance Act." the following
language:
"Life insurance policies, including any riders or accelerated benefit
provisions attached thereto, shall not be considered long-term care
insurance for purposes of the Long-Term Care Insurance Act unless such
policy or rider is expressly marketed as long-term care insurance."
AMEND TITLE TO CONFORM TO AMENDMENTS
