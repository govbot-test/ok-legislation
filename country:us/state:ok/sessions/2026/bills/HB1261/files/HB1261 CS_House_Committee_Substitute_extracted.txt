7 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
8 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3-111 of Title 47, unless there
9 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
10 A. The Oklahoma Wrecker Services Division, a division of the
11 Department of Labor, is hereby created, which shall consist of such
12 divisions, sections, committees, offices and positions as may be
13 established by the Department of Labor or by law. The Division
14 shall employ a Division Director and a team of three employees to
15 investigate consumer complaints related to overcharging for
16 nonconsensual towing, recovery, storage fees, and violations of the
17 rules promulgated by the Oklahoma Wrecker and Towing Services Board.
18 Division employees shall act as analysts, administrative assistants,
19 administrative technicians, and investigators.

21 exercised by the Transportation Division of the Oklahoma Corporation
22 Commission relating to wrecker and towing services shall be fully

8 SECTION 3. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
9 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1003 of Title 40, unless there
10 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
11 There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving fund
12 for the Department of Labor, to be designated the “Oklahoma Wrecker
13 and Towing Services Revolving Fund”. The fund shall be a continuing
14 fund not subject to fiscal year limitations. All monies accruing to
15 the credit of the fund are hereby appropriated and shall be budgeted
16 and expended by the Department of Labor for the purpose of
17 administering the Oklahoma Wrecker Services Division. Expenditures
18 from the fund shall be made upon warrants issued by the State
19 Treasurer against claims filed as prescribed by law with the
20 Director of the Office of Management and Enterprise Services for
21 approval and payment.
