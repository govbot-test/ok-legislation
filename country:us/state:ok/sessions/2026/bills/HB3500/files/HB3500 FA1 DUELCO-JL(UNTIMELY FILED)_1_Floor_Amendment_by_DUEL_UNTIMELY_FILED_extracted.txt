6 B. The signature, consent or agreement of or notice to a
7 grantee beneficiary or beneficiaries of a transfer-on-death deed
8 shall not be required for any purpose during the lifetime of the
9 record owner.
10 C. A designated grantee beneficiary may accept real estate
11 pursuant to a transfer-on-death deed only on behalf of himself,
12 herself, or a legal entity over which he or she has proper
13 authority. A beneficiary shall not accept such real estate on
14 behalf of another designated beneficiary.
15 D. Each designated grantee beneficiary wishing to accept real
16 estate pursuant to a transfer-on-death deed shall execute an
17 affidavit affirming:
18 1. Verification of the record owner’s death;
19 2. Whether the record owner and the designated beneficiary were
20 married at the time of the record owner’s death; and
21 3. A legal description of the real estate.
22 E. The grantee shall attach a copy of the record owner’s death
23 certificate to the beneficiary affidavit. For a record owner’s
24 death occurring on or after November 1, 2011, the beneficiary shall

5 record owner’s death occurring before November 1, 2011, such
