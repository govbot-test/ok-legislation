1 Section 13-101.2. A. There is hereby created the Lindsey
2 Nicole Henry Scholarships for Students with Disabilities Program.

4 Program is established to provide a scholarship to a private school

8 beginning with the 2010-2011 school year.

10 need may exercise his or her parental option and request to have a

15 (IEP), an individualized service plan (ISP), or meets

19 b. the student has had an individualized service plan
20 pursuant to Section 1-4-704 of Title 10A of the
21 Oklahoma Statutes any time prior to notifying the
22 State Department of Education of the intent to
23 participate in the program,
