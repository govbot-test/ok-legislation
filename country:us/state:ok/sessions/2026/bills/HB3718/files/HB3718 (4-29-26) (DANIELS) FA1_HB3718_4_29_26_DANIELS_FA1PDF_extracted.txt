I move to amend House Bill No. 3718 as follows:
1. On page 4, line 8, by deleting after the word “within” and before the word “days” the language
“five (5)” and by inserting the language “fifteen (15) school”;
2. On page 4, line 10, by deleting all new language from the word “provide” through the word
“form” on line 12 and by inserting the following language: “schedule a meeting to review existing
data with a parent or legal guardian”;
3. On page 4, line 13, by deleting all new language from the word “If” through the comma
following the word “paragraph” on line 15;
4. On page 4, line 18, by inserting after the word “signed” the language: “special education
evaluation”;
5. On page 4, line 19, by inserting after the word “form” and before the period the following
language: “, except when federal law provides an exception to the forty-five-school-day period”;
6. On page 4, line 20, by deleting subparagraph c in its entirety and by relettering the subsequent
subparagraph;
7. On page 5, line 8, by deleting all new language from the word “For” through the period on line
11;
8. And, by amending the title to conform.
