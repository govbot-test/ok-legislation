10 date; providing exceptions; amending 22 O.S. 2021,

exception; amending 22 O.S. 2021, Section 152, as

(22 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section 152), which relates to

3 SECTION 3. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
4 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3020 of Title 12, unless there
5 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
6 A. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, any provision of
7 a settlement agreement that has the purpose or effect of concealing
8 the details relating to a claim of child sexual abuse, as defined in
9 Section 843.5 of Title 21 of the Oklahoma Statutes, or child sexual
10 exploitation, as defined in Section 1-1-105 of Title 10A of the
11 Oklahoma Statutes, and including such offenses set forth in Title 21
12 of the Oklahoma Statutes, is void and unenforceable as contrary to
13 the public policy of this state. Provided, however, that
14 identifying information concerning a victim of child sexual abuse or
15 child sexual exploitation shall be deemed and maintained as
16 confidential.
17 B. Subject to subsection C of this section, subsection A of
18 this section shall apply to an agreement entered into before, on, or
19 after the effective date of this act.
20 C. An individual or entity may not enforce or attempt to
21 enforce an agreement or a provision of an agreement described by
22 subsection A of this section and entered into before the effective
23 date of this act unless:
