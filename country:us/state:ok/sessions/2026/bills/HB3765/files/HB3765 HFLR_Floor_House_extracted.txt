16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
18 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1796 of Title 21, unless there
19 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
20 Any alien or person who is not a citizen of the United States
21 who has unlawfully acquired title to or owns land in this state
22 either directly or indirectly through a business entity, trust, or
23 foreign government enterprise, and who uses such land to commit a
24 felony offense in violation of state law shall, upon conviction, be
HB3765 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 guil ty of a felony punishable by imprisonment in the custody of the
2 Department of Corrections for a term not to exceed five (5) years,
3 or by a fine not to exceed One Thousand Dollars ($1,000.00), or by
4 both such fine and imprisonment. In addition, all property seized
5 pursuant to this section shall be subject to the forfeiture
6 provisions provided for in Section 1738 of Title 21 of the Oklahoma
7 Statutes.
8 SECTION 2. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
9 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
10 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
11 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
