8 An Act relating to the Governmental Tort Claims Act;
amending 51 O.S. 2021, Section 153.1, which relates
9 to actions associated with the housing of inmates;
limiting actions to specific incarcerating entity;
10 and declaring an emergency.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 51 O.S. 2021, Section 153.1, is
15 amended to read as follows:
16 Section 153.1. A. Nothing in the Governmental Tort Claims Act
17 shall be construed as allowing an action or recovery against this
18 state, against any city, town or county that is the sole beneficiary
19 of a public trust, or against any employee of this state or any
20 city, town or county of this state due to the housing of federal
21 inmates or inmates from another state in facilities owned or
22 operated by private prison contractors. If a public trust that has
23 as its sole beneficiary a city, town or county has a facility that
24 houses federal inmates or inmates from another state, the immunity
ENGR. H. B. NO. 3974 Page 1
CORRECTED
1 prov ided for in the Governmental Tort Claims Act shall not apply to
2 that trust.
3 B. In an instance of a shared inmate housing facility by any
4 combination of the state, cities, counties, or tribes any otherwise
5 allowable action or recovery by or on behalf of an inmate, shall be
6 limited to the specific state, city, county or tribes responsible
7 for placing the inmate in such shared inmate housing facility.
8 SECTION 2. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
9 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
10 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
11 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
12 Passed the House of Representatives the 25th day of March, 2026.
