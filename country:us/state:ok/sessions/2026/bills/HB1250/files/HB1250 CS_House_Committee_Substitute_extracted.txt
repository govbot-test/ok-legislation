18 SECTION 2. There is hereby created in the State Treasury a
19 revolving fund for the Office of the Attorney General to be
20 designated the "Public Safety Technology Revolving Fund". The fund
21 shall be a continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations,
22 and shall consist of all monies received by the Office of the
23 Attorney General as designated by law. All monies accruing to the
24 credit of said fund are hereby appropriated and may be budgeted and
