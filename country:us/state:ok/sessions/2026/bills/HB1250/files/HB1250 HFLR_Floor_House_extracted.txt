18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
19 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
20 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 20m of Title 74, unless there is
21 created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
22 SECTION 2. There is hereby created in the State Treasury a
23 revolving fund for the Office of the Attorney General to be
24 designated the "Public Safety Technology Revolving Fund". The fund
HB1250 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 shal l be a continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations,
2 and shall consist of all monies received by the Office of the
3 Attorney General as designated by law. All monies accruing to the
4 credit of said fund are hereby appropriated and may be budgeted and
5 expended by the Office for the purpose of funding grants to local
6 law enforcement divisions to cover some of the costs associated with
7 using technology to better interface with the public. Expenditures
8 from said fund shall be made upon warrants issued by the State
9 Treasurer against claims filed as prescribed by law with the
10 Director of the Office of Management and Enterprise Services for
11 approval and payment.
12 SECTION 3. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
