6 An Act relating to schools; amending 70 O.S. 2021,
Section 5-129, which relates to the school activity
7 fund; removing requirement to deposit certain funds
by end of the next business day; eliminating
8 reference to deposits for less than certain amount;
requiring deposit when funds reach certain amount;
9 requiring certain funds to be deposited at least once
per business week; and providing an effective date.
