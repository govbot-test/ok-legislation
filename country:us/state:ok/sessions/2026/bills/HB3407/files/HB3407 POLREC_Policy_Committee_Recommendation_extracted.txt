9 An Act relating to property; creating right to sell,
salvage, or remove manufactured home and its contents
10 for certain real property owners; requiring written
notice to be sent via certified mail; establishing
11 requirements of the contents of the written notice;
providing for codification; and providing an
12 effective date.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 690 of Title 60, unless there is
18 created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 In the absence of any other agreement between the real property
20 owner and the owner of a manufactured home, any person, firm, or
21 corporation that allows an individual to use or occupy real property
22 in order to utilize a manufactured home shall have the right to
23 sell, salvage, or remove the manufactured home and its contents if
24 the owner of the manufactured home has failed to contact the real

1 prop erty owner or to provide payment for the use of the real
2 property for ninety (90) days; provided, however, that prior to
3 exercising such right, the real property owner shall:
4 1. Within thirty (30) days after the failure of the
5 manufactured homeowner to make payment or otherwise contact the real
6 property owner, send by certified mail, return receipt requested,
7 written notice to all recorded lienholders and to the last-known
8 address of the manufactured home owner stating the intent of the
9 real property owner to exercise the right to sell, salvage, or
10 remove the manufactured home and its contents if payment for use and
11 occupying the real property is not made. The written notice shall
12 include a description sufficient to identify the manufactured home
13 that is subject of the notice, including, if available, the
14 manufacturer's serial number, vehicle identification number (VIN),
15 title number, lot number, or other identifying characteristics, and
16 the physical location of the manufactured home;
17 2. If, thirty (30) days after the mailing of the first notice,
18 payment or contact has not been made, send a second written notice
19 by certified mail, return receipt requested, to all recorded
20 lienholders and to the last-known address of the manufactured home
21 owner restating the intent to exercise such right and including the
22 same identifying information required by paragraph 1 of this
23 section; and
