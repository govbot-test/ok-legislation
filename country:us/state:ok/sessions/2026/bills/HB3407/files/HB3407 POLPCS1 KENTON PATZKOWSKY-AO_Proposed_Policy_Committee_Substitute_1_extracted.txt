9 An Act relating to property; creating right to sell,

16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
18 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 690 of Title 60, unless there is
19 created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
20 A. In the absence of any other agreement between the real
21 property owner and the owner of a manufactured home, any person,
22 firm, or corporation who allows an individual to use or occupy real
23 property in order to utilize a manufactured home shall have the
24 right to sell, salvage, or remove the manufactured home and its

1 cont ents if the owner of the manufactured home has failed to contact
2 the real property owner or to provide payment for the use of the
3 real property for ninety (90) days; provided, however, that prior to
4 exercising such right, the real property owner shall:
5 1. Within thirty (30) days after the failure of the
6 manufactured homeowner to make payment or otherwise contact the real
7 property owner, send by certified mail, return receipt requested,
8 written notice to the last-known address of the manufactured home
9 owner stating the intent of the real property owner to exercise the
10 right to sell, salvage, or remove the manufactured home and its
11 contents if payment for use and occupying the real property is not
12 made. The written notice shall include a description sufficient to
13 identify the manufactured home that is subject of the notice,
14 including, if available, the manufacturer's serial number, vehicle
15 identification number (VIN), title number, lot number, or other
16 identifying characteristics, and the physical location of the
17 manufactured home;
18 2. If, thirty (30) days after the mailing of the first notice,
19 payment or contact has not been made, send a second written notice
20 by certified mail, return receipt requested, to the last-known
21 address of the manufactured home owner restating the intent to
22 exercise such right and including the same identifying information
23 required by paragraph 1 of this act; and
