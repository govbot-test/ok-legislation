13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 510.2 of Title 59, unless there
15 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 A. A physician shall provide an evidence-based, validated
17 mental health screening instrument as prescribed by the State Board
18 of Medical Licensure and Supervision to each patient during or
19 before a routine primary care visit at least once annually. This
20 requirement shall not apply to physicians who do not practice direct
21 client care.
22 B. The Board shall promulgate rules pertaining to the required
23 screening instrument and shall create such information and
24 educational materials necessary to implement the provisions of this
