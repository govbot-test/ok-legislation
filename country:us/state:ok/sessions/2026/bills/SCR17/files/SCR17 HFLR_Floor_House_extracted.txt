10 A Concurrent Resolution recognizing the long
friendship between Oklahoma and Ireland;
11 commemorating economic relations between Ireland and
Oklahoma; welcoming esteemed guests from Ireland;
12 recognizing ties between Ireland and the United
States and the Irish ancestry of many Oklahomans;
13 commemorating the celebration of St. Patrick’s Day
throughout the state; and directing distribution.
14
15
16 WHEREAS, Oklahoma and Ireland have enjoyed a long friendship
17 that predates Oklahoma’s statehood and Ireland’s independence; and
18 WHEREAS, in 1847, during the Irish famine, the Choctaw Nation
19 raised $170.00 for famine relief, over $5,000.00 in today’s money,
20 which was a fortune at the time, even though they had recently been
21 forcibly been moved to Oklahoma on the Trail of Tears; and
22 WHEREAS, in 2017, Ireland commemorated the gift of the Choctaw
23 Nation with the dedication of the Kindred Spirits sculpture,
24 featuring a circle of eagle feathers, in County Cork; and

18 Irish descent; and
