16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
18 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
19 There is hereby appropriated to the Office of Educational
20 Quality and Accountability from any monies not otherwise
21 appropriated from the General Revenue Fund of the State Treasury for
22 the fiscal year ending June 30, 2027, the sum of Seven Hundred
23 Eighty-five Thousand Dollars ($785,000.00) or so much thereof as may

1 be n ecessary to perform the duties imposed upon the Office of
2 Educational Quality and Accountability by law.
3 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
4 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
5 Funds appropriated by Section 1 of this act shall be utilized as
6 follows:
7 Seven Hundred Eighty-five Thousand Dollars ($785,000.00) to
8 implement the provisions of Enrolled House Bill No. 4268 of the 2nd
9 Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature.
10 SECTION 3. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
11 SECTION 4. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
12 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
