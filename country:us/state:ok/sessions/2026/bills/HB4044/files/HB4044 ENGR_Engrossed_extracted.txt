8 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Office of Educational
Quality and Accountability; appropriating certain
9 funds in certain amount for certain purpose;
directing utilization of certain amounts for certain
10 purposes; providing for noncodification; providing an
effective date; and declaring an emergency.
11
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
16 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
17 There is hereby appropriated to the Office of Educational
18 Quality and Accountability from any monies not otherwise
19 appropriated from the General Revenue Fund of the State Treasury for
20 the fiscal year ending June 30, 2027, the sum of Seven Hundred
21 Eighty-five Thousand Dollars ($785,000.00) or so much thereof as may
22 be necessary to perform the duties imposed upon the Office of
23 Educational Quality and Accountability by law.
24
ENGR. H. B. NO. 4044 Page 1
1 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
2 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
3 Funds appropriated by Section 1 of this act shall be utilized as
4 follows:
5 Seven Hundred Eighty-five Thousand Dollars ($785,000.00) to
6 implement the provisions of Enrolled House Bill No. 4268 of the 2nd
7 Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature.
8 SECTION 3. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
9 SECTION 4. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
10 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
11 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
12 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
13 Passed the House of Representatives the 14th day of April, 2026.
