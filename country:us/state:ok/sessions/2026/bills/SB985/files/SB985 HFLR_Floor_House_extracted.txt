11 An Act relating to agriculture; creating the Oklahoma
Local Food for Schools program; specifying purpose
12 for program; providing for promulgation of rules;
creating the Oklahoma Local Food for Schools
13 Revolving Fund; establishing fund procedure;
providing source of funds; authorizing the Oklahoma
14 Department of Agriculture, Food, and Forestry to use
certain funds; stating purpose of fund; providing for
15 codification; and providing an effective date.

18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
19 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
20 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 5-60.7 of Title 2, unless there
21 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
22 A. The Oklahoma Department of Agriculture, Food, and Forestry
23 shall operate an “Oklahoma Local Food for Schools” program for the
24

CORRECTED
1 purp ose of facilitating the purchase of local foods by school
2 districts.
3 B. The Department shall encourage school districts to partner
4 with local producers and meat processors by providing reimbursement
5 to the school district for local food costs.
6 C. The Department is authorized to promulgate rules necessary
7 for the implementation of this act.
8 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
9 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 5-60.8 of Title 2, unless there
10 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
11 There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving fund
12 for the Oklahoma Department of Agriculture, Food, and Forestry to be
13 designated the “Oklahoma Local Food for Schools Revolving Fund”.
14 The fund shall be a continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year
15 limitations, and shall consist of all monies received by the
16 Department from legislative appropriations for the purpose of
17 facilitating the purchase of local food by school districts pursuant
18 to the Oklahoma Local Food for Schools program created in Section 1
19 of this act. All monies accruing to the credit of the fund are
20 hereby appropriated and may be expended by the Department for the
21 purpose provided for in this section. Expenditures from the fund
22 shall be made upon warrants issued by the State Treasurer against
23 claims filed as prescribed by law with the Director of the Office of
24 Management and Enterprise Services for approval and payment.
