17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
18 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 70 O.S. 2021, Section 18-108, is
19 amended to read as follows:
20 Section 18-108. A. It is the intent of the Legislature to
21 provide a free public kindergarten for every five-year-old child in
22 this state.
23 B. Each day during which a child attends a kindergarten for two
24 and one-half (2 1/2) hours or more shall be counted as one hundred

1 perc ent (100%) of one (1) day of average daily attendance. Each day
2 a kindergarten student is on the membership roll in a school
3 district shall be counted as one hundred percent (100%) of one (1)
4 day of average daily membership.
5 C. It shall be the duty of every school district in this state
6 to provide and offer kindergarten free of tuition for every child
7 residing in the district who attains the age of five (5) years on or
8 before the first day of September during the school year such
9 kindergarten is offered. The duty to provide and offer kindergarten
10 may be satisfied by transferring kindergarten children to other
11 school districts which accept them and provide kindergarten for such

14 criteria established by the district.
15 D. A kindergarten program may be offered jointly by school
16 districts that have formed interlocal cooperative agreements
17 pursuant to Section 5-117b of this title.

19 the duty of every school district in this state to provide and offer
20 a full six-hour day of kindergarten free of tuition for every child
21 residing in the district who attains the age of five (5) years on or
22 before the first day of September during the school year
23 kindergarten is offered. The duty to provide and offer kindergarten
24 may be satisfied by intra-district transfer to a school offering

5 based upon selection criteria established by the district. A school

20 demonstrating that the child will turn the required age during the
