8 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Higher Learning
Access Program; amending 70 O.S. 2021, Section 2603,
9 as last amended by Section 1, Chapter 485, O.S.L.
2025 (70 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section 2603), which
10 relates to eligibility requirements; adding
technology center school education to eligibility;
11 updating references; expanding eligibility for
students in the court-ordered custody of a private
12 nonprofit child-placing agency or a federally
recognized Indian tribe during certain time; defining
13 who a parent is for a child of a certified teacher;
amending 70 O.S. 2021, Section 2605, as last amended
14 by Section 2, Chapter 485, O.S.L. 2025 (70 O.S. Supp.
2025, Section 2605), which relates to student
15 agreements; updating references; expanding
eligibility for students in the court-ordered custody
16 of a private nonprofit child-placing agency or a
federally recognized Indian tribe during certain
17 time; removing income requirement for student in the
custody of the Oklahoma Department of Human Services,
18 in the court-ordered custody of a private nonprofit
child-placing agency or a federally recognized Indian
19 tribe during certain time; requiring Oklahoma State
Regents for Higher Education verify standard or
20 alternative certificate is in a classroom subject;
expanding definition of certified teacher; clarifying
21 computation of employment for purposes of
eligibility; requiring standard or alternative
22 certification be in a classroom subject as determined
by the State Regents; clarifying effect of service as
23 an emergency or provisionally certified teacher; and
providing an effective date.
