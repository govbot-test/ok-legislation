1 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
2 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1025.1 of Title 59, unless there
3 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
4 A. The Construction Industries Board is authorized and directed
5 to administer the swimming pool contractor provisions of the
6 Swimming Pool Contractor Licensing Act and exercise all incidental
7 powers provided in the Construction Industries Board Act, Chapter
8 26A of Title 59 of the Oklahoma Statutes, that are necessary and
9 proper to implement and enforce the provisions of the Swimming Pool
10 Contractor Licensing Act and the rules promulgated pursuant thereto.
11 B. The Construction Industries Board shall have the authority
12 to administer and enforce the provisions of the Swimming Pool
13 Contractor Licensing Act in accordance with the powers set forth in
14 Chapter 26A of Title 59 of the Oklahoma Statutes, and specifically
15 Section 1000.4 of Title 59 of the Oklahoma Statutes including, but
16 not limited to, the authority to make, prescribe, enforce, amend,
17 and repeal rules governing the licensure of swimming pool
18 contractors, including but not limited to establishing minimum
19 qualifications, license categories and limitations, establishing
20 fines and penalties for violations, initiating disciplinary
21 proceedings, and enforcement of the act.

1 A. As used in the Swimming Pool Contractor Licensing Act:

4 2. "Swimming pool contractor" means an individual person who
