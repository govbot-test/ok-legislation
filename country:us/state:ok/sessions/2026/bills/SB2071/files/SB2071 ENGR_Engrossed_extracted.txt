6 An Act relating to agriculture; amending 2 O.S. 2021,

Section 2:
Sections 7-401, 7-403, 7-415, and 7-421, which relate
7 to the Oklahoma Milk and Milk Products Act; updating
statutory references; modifying definitions;
8 increasing fees for production and sale of certain
milk products; clarifying jurisdictional authority of
9 dairy inspection and grading programs; updating
statutory language; and providing an effective date.

15 Section 7-401. Sections 4 7-401 through 23 7-421 of this act
16 title shall be known and may be cited as the “Oklahoma Milk and Milk
17 Products Act”.

3 SECTION 3. AMENDATORY 2 O.S. 2021, Section 7-415, is
4 amended to read as follows:
5 Section 7-415. A. A fee of one cent ($0.01) two cents ($0.02)
6 shall be assessed on each one hundred (100) pounds of raw milk
7 produced in this state. Each co-op, marketing agent, or processing
8 plant where the raw milk is not collected by a co-op or marketing
9 agent, doing business in Oklahoma this state shall pay the fees each
10 calendar month to the Commissioner of Agriculture to be credited to
11 the Milk and Milk Products Inspection Revolving Fund of the State
12 Treasury.
13 B. There is hereby assessed a fee of one cent ($0.01) two cents
14 ($0.02) on each one hundred (100) pounds of Grade A milk or milk
15 products processed or offered for retail sale in Oklahoma this
16 state. Each milk plant doing business in Oklahoma this state shall
17 pay the fees each calendar month to the Commissioner to be credited
18 to the Milk and Milk Products Inspection Revolving Fund of the State
19 Treasury.
20 C. A fee of two cents ($0.02) shall be assessed on each one
21 hundred (100) pounds of milk or milk products produced or processed
22 in another state and imported into Oklahoma shall be the same amount
23 assessed on milk or milk products shipped to that state from
24 Oklahoma this state. The person shipping or causing the shipment of
ENGR. S. B. NO. 2071 Page 5
1 milk or milk products shall be responsible for paying the fees semi-
2 annually on January 1 and July 1 of each year to the Commissioner
3 and such fees shall be credited to the Milk and Milk Products
4 Inspection Revolving Fund of the State Treasury.
5 SECTION 4. AMENDATORY 2 O.S. 2021, Section 7-421, is
6 amended to read as follows:
7 Section 7-421. The Oklahoma State Department of Agriculture,
8 Food, and Forestry is hereby designated as the official state agency
9 of this state for purposes of cooperating with and implementing the
10 dairy inspection and grading program under the jurisdiction of the
11 United States Department of Agriculture, the United States
12 Department of Health and Human Services, the United States Public
13 Health Service, and the United States Food and Drug Administration.
14 SECTION 5. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
15 Passed the Senate the 10th day of March, 2026.
