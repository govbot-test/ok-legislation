I move to amend Senate Bill No. 2071, on Page 5, Line 4 ½, by inserting a new SECTION 3 to read
as follows, by renumbering subsequent sections, and by amending the title to conform:
“SECTION 3. AMENDATORY 2 O.S. 2021, Section 7-414, is amended to read as
follows:
Section 7-414. A. The provisions of the Oklahoma Milk and Milk Products Act shall not be
construed to:
1. Include incidental sales of ungraded raw milk or raw milk products directly to consumers at
the farm where the ungraded raw milk is or raw milk products are produced;
2. Preclude the advertising of the incidental sale of goat milk; and
3. Preclude the advertising of the sale of ungraded raw milk and raw milk products; and
4. Prohibit any farmer or producer from making cheese using milk or cream produced on the
farm of such farmer or producer.
B. For purposes of this section, incidental sales of goat milk are those sales where the average
monthly number of gallons sold does not exceed one hundred (100).”
