16 amended to read as follows:
17 Section 1-270. A. As funding permits, the State Department of
18 Health shall foster and coordinate implementation of a plan for a
19 statewide coordinated system of care for stroke, which shall include
20 special focus and attention on evidence-based treatment for stroke.
21 Such system shall include, but shall not be limited to:
22 1. Recommendations from the Oklahoma Hospital Advisory Council
23 and medical experts in stroke care;
