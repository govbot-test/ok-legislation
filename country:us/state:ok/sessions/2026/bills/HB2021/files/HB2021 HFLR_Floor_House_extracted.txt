10 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
11 An Act relating to schools; creating the Oklahoma
Kids After-School Grant Program (OKAGP); locating the
12 program at the Department of Human Services;
providing parameters on entities eligible to apply;
13 citing certain exemptions; authorizing the
promulgation of rules; creating the Oklahoma Kids
14 After-School Grant Program Revolving Fund;
establishing revolving fund characteristics;
15 declaring funds appropriated; providing for
codification; and providing an effective date.
16
17
18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
19 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
20 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 10001 of Title 70, unless there
21 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
22 This act shall be known and may be cited as the "Oklahoma Kids
23 After-School Grant Program" (OKAGP).
24
HB2021 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
2 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 10002 of Title 70, unless there
3 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
4 There is hereby created at the Department of Human Services the
5 Oklahoma Kids After-School Grant Program (OKAGP). This grant
6 program shall be for community-based organizations that run after-
7 school programs for children grades kindergarten through twelve.
8 These entities may qualify for an exemption from the Oklahoma Child
9 Care Facilities Licensing Act based on the listed exemptions found
10 in Section 403 of Title 10 of the Oklahoma Statutes. These entities
11 must also have a minimum of five locations in Oklahoma, in which
12 they operate themselves or through an alliance. The Department of
13 Human Services may promulgate rules to implement this grant program.
14 SECTION 3. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
15 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 10003 of Title 70, unless there
16 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
17 There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving fund
18 for the Department of Human Services to be designated the "Oklahoma
19 Kids After-School Grant Program Revolving Fund". The fund shall be
20 a continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations, and shall
21 consist of all monies received by the Department of Human Services
22 from appropriations or donations. All monies accruing to the credit
23 of said fund are hereby appropriated and may be budgeted and
24 expended by the Department for the purpose of awarding grants under
HB2021 HFLR Page 2
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 the Oklahoma Kids After-School Grant Program. Expenditures from
2 said fund shall be made upon warrants issued by the State Treasurer
3 against claims filed as prescribed by law with the Director of the
4 Office of Management and Enterprise Services for approval and
5 payment.
6 SECTION 4. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
7
8 COMMITTEE REPORT BY: COMMITTEE ON APPROPRIATIONS AND BUDGET, dated
02/25/2026 - DO PASS, As Amended and Coauthored.
