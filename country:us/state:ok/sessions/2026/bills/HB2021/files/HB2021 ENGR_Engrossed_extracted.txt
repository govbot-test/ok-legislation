7 An Act relating to schools; creating the Oklahoma
Kids After-School Grant Program (OKAGP); locating the
8 program at the Department of Human Services;
providing parameters on entities eligible to apply;
9 citing certain exemptions; authorizing the
promulgation of rules; creating the Oklahoma Kids
10 After-School Grant Program Revolving Fund;
establishing revolving fund characteristics;
11 declaring funds appropriated; providing for
codification; and providing an effective date.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
16 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 10001 of Title 70, unless there
17 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
18 This act shall be known and may be cited as the "Oklahoma Kids
19 After-School Grant Program (OKAGP)".
20 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
21 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 10002 of Title 70, unless there
22 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
23 There is hereby created at the Department of Human Services the
24 Oklahoma Kids After-School Grant Program (OKAGP). This grant
ENGR. H. B. NO. 2021 Page 1
1 prog ram shall be for community-based organizations that run after-
2 school programs for children grades kindergarten through twelve.
3 These entities may qualify for an exemption from the Oklahoma Child
4 Care Facilities Licensing Act based on the listed exemptions found
5 in Section 403 of Title 10 of the Oklahoma Statutes. These entities
6 must also have a minimum of five locations in Oklahoma, in which
7 they operate themselves or through an alliance. The Department of
8 Human Services may promulgate rules to implement this grant program.
9 SECTION 3. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
10 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 10003 of Title 70, unless there
11 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
12 There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving fund
13 for the Department of Human Services to be designated the "Oklahoma
14 Kids After-School Grant Program Revolving Fund". The fund shall be
15 a continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations, and shall
16 consist of all monies received by the Department of Human Services
17 from appropriations or donations. All monies accruing to the credit
18 of said fund are hereby appropriated and may be budgeted and
19 expended by the Department for the purpose of awarding grants under
20 the Oklahoma Kids After-School Grant Program. Expenditures from
21 said fund shall be made upon warrants issued by the State Treasurer
22 against claims filed as prescribed by law with the Director of the
23 Office of Management and Enterprise Services for approval and
24 payment.
ENGR. H. B. NO. 2021 Page 2
1 SECTION 4. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
2 Passed the House of Representatives the 12th day of March, 2026.
