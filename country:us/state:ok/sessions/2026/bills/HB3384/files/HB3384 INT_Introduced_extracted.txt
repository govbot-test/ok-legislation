17 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 36 O.S. 2021, Section 3639.1, is
18 amended to read as follows:
19 Section 3639.1. A. No insurer shall cancel, refuse to renew or
20 increase the premium of a homeowner's insurance policy or any other
21 personal residential insurance coverage, which has been in effect
22 more than forty-five (45) days, solely because the insured filed a
23 first claim against the policy. The provisions of this section
24 shall not be construed to prevent the cancellation, nonrenewal or
