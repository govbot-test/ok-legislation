7 An Act relating to sunsets; amending 63 O.S. 2021,

12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 63 O.S. 2021, Section 1-1923, as
14 amended by Section 1, Chapter 197, O.S.L. 2023 (63 O.S. Supp. 2025,
15 Section 1-1923), is amended to read as follows:
16 Section 1-1923. A. There is hereby re-created, to continue
17 until July 1, 2025 2027, in accordance with the provisions of the
18 Oklahoma Sunset Law, a Long-Term Care Facility Advisory Council
19 which shall be composed as follows:
20 1. The Governor shall appoint a thirteen-member Long-Term Care
21 Facility Advisory Council which shall advise the State Commissioner
22 of Health. The Advisory Council shall be comprised of the following
23 persons:

18 Governor shall appoint individuals to fill the remainder of the
19 term.
20 B. The State Department of Health shall provide administrative
21 support to perform designated duties of the Advisory Council. The
22 Department shall also provide space for meetings of the Advisory
23 Council.

1 C. The Advisory Council shall annually elect a chair, vice-
2 chair and secretary-treasurer, shall meet at least quarterly, and
3 may hold such special meetings as may be necessary. The members of
4 the Advisory Council shall be reimbursed as provided for by the
5 State Travel Reimbursement Act.
6 D. The Advisory Council shall have the power and duty to:
7 1. Serve as an advisory body to the Department for the
8 development and improvement of services to and care and treatment of
9 residents of facilities subject to the provisions of the Nursing
10 Home Care Act, homes subject to the provisions of the Residential
11 Care Act and facilities subject to the provisions of the Adult Day
12 Care Act;
13 2. Review and make recommendations regarding quality of
14 services, care, and treatment provided to individuals receiving
15 services from entities that are subject to the Nursing Home Care
16 Act, the Residential Care Act and the Adult Day Care Act; and
17 3. Evaluate and review the standards, practices, and procedures
18 regarding the administration and enforcement of the provisions of
19 the Nursing Home Care Act, the Residential Care Act and the Adult
20 Day Care Act.
21 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
