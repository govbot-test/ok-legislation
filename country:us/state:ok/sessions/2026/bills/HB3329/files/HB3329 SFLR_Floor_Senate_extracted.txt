16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 59 O.S. 2021, Section 1354, as
18 last amended by Section 1, Chapter 265, O.S.L. 2025 (59 O.S. Supp.
19 2025, Section 1354), is amended to read as follows:
20 Section 1354. There is hereby re-created, to continue until
21 July 1, 2028 July 1, 2027, pursuant to the provisions of the
22 Oklahoma Sunset Law, the State Board of Examiners of Psychologists.
23 The Board shall administer the provisions of the Psychologists
24 Licensing Act. The Board shall consist of seven (7) members

(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 appo inted by the Governor. Five members shall be psychologists from
2 various areas in psychology and two members shall be lay persons.
3 At the expiration of the term of each Board member who is a
4 psychologist, the Governor shall appoint a successor from a list of
5 ten licensed psychologists which is provided by the Oklahoma
6 Psychological Association. Members shall serve for a term of four
7 (4) years and until a successor is appointed and qualified. Before
8 entering upon the duties of office, each member of the Board shall
9 take the constitutional oath of office and file it with the
10 Secretary of State.
11 SECTION 2. REPEALER 63 O.S. 2021, Section 1-1923, as
12 amended by Section 1, Chapter 197, O.S.L. 2023 (63 O.S. Supp. 2025,
13 Section 1-1923), is hereby repealed.
14 SECTION 3. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
15 SECTION 4. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
16 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
17 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
18 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
