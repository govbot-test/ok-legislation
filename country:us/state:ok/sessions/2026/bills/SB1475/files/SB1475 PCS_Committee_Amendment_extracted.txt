10 An Act relating to memorial roads and bridges;
designating the Toby Keith Memorial Interchange;
11 providing for codification; and providing an
effective date.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
16 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1698.501 of Title 69, unless
17 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
18 The interchange of Interstate 35 and Indian Hills Road in
19 Cleveland County shall be designated as the “Toby Keith Memorial
20 Interchange”. The Department of Transportation shall cause suitable
21 markers bearing that name to be placed at the interchange.
22 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective February 5, 2027.
