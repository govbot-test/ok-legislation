8 An Act relating to crimes and punishments; amending
21 O.S. 2021, Sections 1767.1, as amended by Section
9 459, Chapter 486, O.S.L. 2025, and 1767.3 (21 O.S.
Supp. 2025, Section 1767.1), which relate to the
10 unlawful use of bombs or explosive devices and
related definitions; modifying elements of certain
11 crimes; providing construing provision; updating
certain defined terms; and providing an effective
12 date.
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 21 O.S. 2021, Section 1767.1, as
16 amended by Section 459, Chapter 486, O.S.L. 2025 (21 O.S. Supp.
17 2025, Section 1767.1), is amended to read as follows:
18 Section 1767.1. A. Any person who shall willfully or
19 maliciously commit any of the following acts shall be deemed guilty
20 of a Class D1 felony offense:
21 1. Place or conspire to place in, upon, under, against or near
22 to any building, car, truck, aircraft, motor or other vehicle,
23 vessel, railroad, railway car, or locomotive or structure, any
24 explosive or incendiary device with unlawful intent to destroy,

3 the destruction of any building, public or private, or any car,
4 truck, aircraft, motor or other vehicle, vessel, railroad, railway
5 car, or locomotive or structure; or
6 2. Place or conspire to place in, upon, under, against or near
7 to any building, car, truck, aircraft, motor or other vehicle,
8 vessel, railroad, railway car, or locomotive or structure, any
9 explosive or incendiary device with intent to destroy, throw down,
10 or injure capable of causing destruction or injury, in whole or in
11 part, under circumstances that, if such intent it were accomplished
12 to function, human life or safety would be endangered thereby; or
13 3. By the explosion Use of any explosive or the igniting of any
14 incendiary device to destroy, throw down, or injure any property of
15 another person, or cause injury to another person, in whole or in
16 part; or
17 4. Manufacture, sell, transport, or possess or conspire to
18 manufacture, sell, transport, or possess any explosive, the
19 component parts of an explosive or ingredients of any homemade
20 explosive, an incendiary device, or simulated bomb with knowledge or
21 intent that it or they will be used when such item or materials are
22 designed, modified, assembled, or possessed in a manner that
23 demonstrates preparation for illegal use, or possessed in a
24 combination and under circumstances indicating intent or readiness

1 to u nlawfully kill, injure or intimidate any person, or unlawfully

3 5. Place or conspire to place in, upon, under, against or near
4 to any building, car, truck, aircraft, motor or other vehicle,
5 vessel, railroad, railway car, or locomotive or structure, any foul,
6 poisonous, offensive or injurious substance or compound, explosive,
7 incendiary device, or simulated bomb with intent to wrongfully
8 injure, molest or coerce another person or to injure or damage the
9 property of another person; or
10 6. Injure, damage or attempt to damage by an explosive or

13 7. Make any threat or convey information known to be false,

17 bomb; or
18 8. Manufacture, sell, deliver, mail or send an explosive,
19 incendiary device, or simulated bomb to another person; or
20 9. While committing or attempting to commit any felony,
21 possess, display, or threaten to use any explosive, incendiary
22 device, or simulated bomb.

24 repeal any laws pertaining to, the acts of mischief of juveniles

3 C. Nothing contained herein shall be construed to prohibit the
4 lawful manufacture, sale, transport, possession, or use of
5 explosives, explosive materials, incendiaries, simulated bombs,
6 their components, or ingredients for a lawful purpose and in
7 compliance with applicable state and federal law.
8 SECTION 2. AMENDATORY 21 O.S. 2021, Section 1767.3, is
9 amended to read as follows:
10 Section 1767.3. As used in Section 1767.1 of this title:
11 1. "Explosive" or "explosives" means any chemical compound,
12 mixture or device, the primary or common purpose of which is to
13 function by explosion or which, although not its primary or common
14 purpose, has been modified, manipulated, altered, enhanced, or
15 otherwise caused to function by explosion (that is, with substantial
16 instantaneous release of gas, heat, debris, or concussive pressure
17 or force, or any combination of such actions), unless such compound,
18 mixture or device is otherwise specifically classified by the United
19 States Department of Transportation. The term "explosive" or
20 "explosives" shall include, but not be limited to, gunpowder,
21 dynamite, any bomb, all materials as defined in paragraphs 1 and 2
22 of Section 121.1 of Title 63 of the Oklahoma Statutes, and all
23 material which is classified as explosives by the United States
24 Department of Transportation;
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - HB4142 SFLR Page 4
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 2. "Person" means any individual or individuals, firm,
2 copartnership, corporation, company, association, joint stock
3 association, and includes any trustee, receiver, assignee or
4 personal representative thereof;
5 3. "Incendiary device" means any chemical compound, mixture or
6 device accelerant, material, or combination thereof, the primary
7 purpose or effect of which is to ignite, initiate combustion, or
8 promote the spread of fire, whether through direct ignition,
9 chemical reaction, or by enhancing the combustibility of other
10 materials. This includes, but is not limited to, devices designed
11 to ignite on impact or as a result of chemical reaction such as a,
12 including "Molotov cocktail cocktails" or "firebomb" which is
13 ignited on impact, causing a mechanical reaction of the container's
14 breaking and permitting the inflammable matter to spread or splatter
15 and is ignited from the burning wick or hypergolic reaction of
16 chemicals "firebombs" and materials deployed without a container,
17 such as poured accelerants intended to cause fire or destruction;
18 4. "Component parts" means separate parts which if assembled
19 would form an explosive device. Component parts of an "incendiary
20 device" shall consist of an inflammable material, a breakable
21 container and a source of ignition; and
22 5. "Simulated bomb" means any device or object that by its
23 design, construction, content, or characteristics appears to be, or
24 to contain, an incendiary device, explosive, or explosives, as
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - HB4142 SFLR Page 5
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 defi ned in this section, but is, in fact, an inoperative facsimile
2 or imitation of such a device or explosive.
3 SECTION 3. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
