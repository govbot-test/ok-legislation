7 An Act relating to the Office of Juvenile Affairs;
making an appropriation; stating purpose; providing
8 an effective date; and declaring an emergency.
9
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. There is hereby appropriated to the Office of
13 Juvenile Affairs, from any monies not otherwise appropriated from
14 the Statewide Recovery Fund (497) of the State Treasury, the sum of
15 Two Million Dollars ($2,000,000.00) or so much thereof as may be
16 necessary to address gaps in the youth services program. Such funds
17 shall be utilized in a manner consistent with the recommendations
18 adopted by the Joint Committee on Pandemic Relief Funding on April
19 15, 2026.
20 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
21 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
22 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
23 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
24 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
