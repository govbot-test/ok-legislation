9 An Act relating to teachers; amending 70 O.S. 2021,
Section 6-101.3, which relates to definitions for
10 teachers; modifying definition for career teacher for

15 amended to read as follows:
16 Section 6-101.3. As used in Section 6-101 et seq. of this
17 title:

21 other administrative or supervisory capacity in the school district;
22 2. "Dismissal" means the discontinuance of the teaching service
23 of an administrator or teacher during the term of a written
24 contract, as provided by law;
