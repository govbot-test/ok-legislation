1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 2nd Session of the 60th Legislature (2026)
3 POLICY COMMITTEE
RECOMMENDATION
4 FOR
5
6
7 POLICY COMMITTEE RECOMMENDATION
8 An Act relating to the Corporation Commission;
amending 51 O.S. 2021, Section 24A.12, which relates
9 to litigation files and investigatory files of
Attorney General, district or municipal attorney;
10 authorizing certain agencies to keep litigation files
confidential; and providing an effective date.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 51 O.S. 2021, Section 24A.12, is
15 amended to read as follows:
16 Section 24A.12. Except as otherwise provided by state or local
17 law, the Attorney General of the State of Oklahoma and agency
18 attorneys authorized by law, the Corporation Commission, the
19 Oklahoma Bureau of Narcotics and Dangerous Drugs Control, the
20 Alcoholic Beverage Laws Enforcement Commission, the Workers'
21 Compensation Commission, the office of the district attorney of any
22 county of the state, and the office of the municipal attorney of any
23 municipality may keep its litigation files and investigatory reports
24 confidential.
Req. No. 16606 Page 1
1 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
