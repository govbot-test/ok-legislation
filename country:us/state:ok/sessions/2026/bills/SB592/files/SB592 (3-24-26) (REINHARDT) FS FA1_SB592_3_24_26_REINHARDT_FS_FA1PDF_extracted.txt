10 An Act relating to alcoholic beverages; amending 37A
O.S. 2021, Section 3-115, which relates to withdrawal
11 of beer from retailer’s stock; allowing certain
distributors to issue certain credit to certain
12 retailer for certain removal of beer or cider;
establishing certain requirements for certain credit
13 to be made; limiting certain number of credits in
calendar year; limiting certain amount of each
14 credit; prohibiting certain credit from being a
refund; prohibiting certain credit from being
15 considered consignment; amending 37A O.S. 2021,
Section 3-123, as amended by Section 3, Chapter 94,
16 O.S.L. 2023 (37A O.S. Supp. 2025, Section 3-123),
which relates to sale of alcoholic beverages to
17 wholesalers, beer distributors, or retailers;
excluding certain licensees from certain inducement
18 provisions; excluding certain actions from being
deemed an inducement or discriminatory action in
19 certain situations; construing certain provisions;
allowing certain licensee to request certain
20 advertising from certain manufacturer; prohibiting
certain entities from reimbursing certain licensees;
21 defining term; updating statutory language; and
providing an effective date.
