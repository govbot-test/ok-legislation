12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
14 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
15 There is hereby appropriated to the Legislative Service Bureau
16 from monies not otherwise appropriated from the ARPA Administrative
17 Costs Fund created in Section 255.5 of Title 62 of the Oklahoma
18 Statutes, the sum of Thirty-four Thousand Six Hundred Ninety-two
19 Dollars ($34,692.00) or so much thereof as may be necessary to pay
20 obligations that may arise out of contracts during Fiscal Year 2027
21 related to the American Rescue Plan Act of 2021.
22 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
23 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
