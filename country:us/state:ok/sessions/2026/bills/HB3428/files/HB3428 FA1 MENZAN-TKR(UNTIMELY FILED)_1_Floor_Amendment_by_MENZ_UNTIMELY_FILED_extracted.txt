FLOOR AMENDMENT

State of Oklahoma
SPEAKER:
CHAIR:
I move to amend HB3428
Page Section Lines
Of the Engrossed Bill
By deleting the content of the entire measure, and by inserting in lieu
thereof the following language:
AMEND TITLE TO CONFORM TO AMENDMENTS
Amendment submitted by: Annie Menz
Adopted: _____________________________
______________________________________
Reading Clerk
1 "SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
2 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 201 of Title 40, unless there is
3 created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
4 A. The Oklahoma Employment Security Commission (OESC) shall
5 consult with the Oklahoma Department of Veterans Affairs (ODVA) to
6 create a veterans benefits and services poster. The OESC shall
7 distribute a digital copy of the poster to all qualifying employers
8 pursuant to this section.
9 B. Such poster shall, at a minimum, include information
10 regarding the following services available to veterans:
11 1. Contact and website information for the Division of Veterans
12 Services and the Department's veterans programs;
13 2. Substance abuse and mental health treatment;
14 3. Educational, workforce, and training resources;
15 4. Tax benefits;
16 5. State of Oklahoma veteran driver licenses and non-driver
17 identification card;
18 6. Eligibility for unemployment insurance benefits under state
19 or federal law;
20 7. Legal services; and
21 8. Contact information for the United States Department of
22 Veterans Affairs Veterans Crisis Line.
23 C. Every employer in the state with more than one hundred full-
24 time-equivalent employees shall display a physical or digital poster
Req. No. 17057 Page 1
1 crea ted pursuant to this section in a conspicuous place accessible
2 to all employees in the workplace."
