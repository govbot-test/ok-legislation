1 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES - FLOOR VERSION
2 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
3 2nd Session of the 60th Legislature (2026)
5 and
6 Wingard of the Senate
7

9 AS INTRODUCED
10 An Act relating to labor; requiring certain employers
to post veterans benefits in the workplace; providing
11 for codification; and providing an effective date.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
16 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 201 of Title 40, unless there is
17 created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
18 A. The Oklahoma Department of Labor shall consult with the
19 Division of Veterans Services to create and distribute a veterans
20 benefits and services poster.
21 B. Such poster shall, at a minimum, include information
22 regarding the following services available to veterans:
23 1. Contact and website information for the Division of Veterans
24 Services and the Department's veterans program;
HB3428 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 2. Substance abuse and mental health treatment;
2 3. Educational, workforce, and training resources;
3 4. Tax benefits;
4 5. State of Oklahoma veteran driver licenses and non-driver
5 identification card;
6 6. Eligibility for unemployment insurance benefits under state
7 or federal law;
8 7. Legal services; and
9 8. Contact information for the United States Department of
10 Veterans Affairs Veterans Crisis Line.
11 C. Every employer in the state with more than fifty (50) full-
12 time equivalent employees shall display the poster created pursuant
13 to this section in a conspicuous place accessible to all employees
14 in the workplace.
15 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
16
