7 A Joint Resolution approving certain proposed
permanent rules of certain energy and agricultural
8 state agencies; and directing distribution.
9
10 WHEREAS, pursuant to Section 308 of Title 75 of the Oklahoma
11 Statutes, the Oklahoma State Legislature shall adopt a joint
12 resolution to approve or disapprove administrative rules.
13 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
14 AND THE SENATE OF THE 2ND SESSION OF THE 60TH OKLAHOMA LEGISLATURE:
15 SECTION 1. The Legislature hereby approves all proposed
16 permanent rules of the Oklahoma Department of Agriculture, Food, and
17 Forestry filed on or before February 1, 2026.
18 SECTION 2. The Legislature hereby approves all proposed
19 permanent rules of the Capitol-Medical Center Improvement and Zoning
20 Commission filed on or before February 1, 2026.
21 SECTION 3. The Legislature hereby approves all proposed
22 permanent rules of the Corporation Commission filed on or before
23 February 1, 2026.
