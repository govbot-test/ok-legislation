9 An Act relating to the Administrative Procedures Act;

10 O.S. 2021, Sections 250.3, as last amended by Section

19 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
20 This act shall be known and may be cited as the "Guidance
21 Transparency Act".
