11 CONCURRING THEREIN:
12 THAT the Oklahoma State Legislature hereby proclaims "Christ is
13 King", recognizing the spiritual significance of this declaration to
14 many of its citizens and honoring the role of faith in the history
15 and culture of Oklahoma.
16 THAT the Oklahoma State Legislature expresses that this
17 resolution is not intended to establish any religion or infringe
18 upon the rights of any individual, but rather to celebrate the
19 voluntary expression of belief that has shaped communities across
20 the state.
