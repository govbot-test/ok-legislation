12 An Act relating to the Office of Management and
Enterprise Services; requiring that portions of
13 certain appropriated funds be used for certain
purposes; mandating certain transfers; requiring
14 utilization in certain manner; providing an effective
date; and declaring an emergency.

18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
19 SECTION 1. From the funds appropriated to the Office of
20 Management and Enterprise Services in Enrolled Senate Bill No. 1177
21 of the 2nd Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature, the sum of One
22 Million Nine Hundred Twenty Thousand Dollars ($1,920,000.00) shall
23 be used to provide for the Pay for Success program. The Office of
24 Management and Enterprise Services shall transfer the sum of Seven
Req. No. 3900 Page 1
1 Hund red Twenty Thousand Dollars ($720,000.00) to the Criminal
2 Justice Pay for Success Revolving Fund, created in Section 510.8c of
3 Title 57 of the Oklahoma Statutes, and the sum of One Million Two
4 Hundred Thousand Dollars ($1,200,000.00) to the Pay for Success
5 Innovation Fund, created in Section 9010.5 of Title 62 of the
6 Oklahoma Statutes.
7 SECTION 2. Of the amount allocated for the Pay for Success
8 program in Section 1 of this act, seventy-five percent (75%) shall
9 be utilized by the Office of Management and Enterprise Services in
10 equal amounts for municipalities of the state with populations of
11 three hundred fifty thousand (350,000) or more as determined by the
12 latest Federal Decennial Census.
13 SECTION 3. There is hereby appropriated to the Central
14 Purchasing Contract Database Revolving Fund, established in Enrolled
15 House Bill No. 3415 of the 2nd Session of the 60th Oklahoma
16 Legislature, from any monies not otherwise appropriated from the
17 General Revenue Fund of the State Treasury for the fiscal year
18 ending June 30, 2027, the sum of Five Hundred Thousand Dollars
19 ($500,000.00) or so much thereof as may be necessary to carry out
20 the provisions of Enrolled House Bill No. 3415 of the 2nd Session of
21 the 60th Oklahoma Legislature.
22 SECTION 4. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
23 SECTION 5. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
24 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
Req. No. 3900 Page 2
1 decl ared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
2 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
