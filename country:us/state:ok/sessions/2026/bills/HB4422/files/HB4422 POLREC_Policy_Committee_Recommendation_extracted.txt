9 An Act relating to poor persons; amending 56 O.S.
2021, Section 230.73, which relates to Temporary
10 Assistance for Needy Families; requiring the use of
SAVE program to determine an applicant's legal
11 status; requiring notice to the Oklahoma Attorney
General; requiring the Attorney General to review
12 cases; requiring notification of Immigration and
Customs Enforcement in certain circumstances;
13 amending 56 O.S. 2021, Section 241, which relates to

14 the use of SAVE program to determine an applicant's

15 Attorney General; requiring the Attorney General to

19 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 56 O.S. 2021, Section 230.73, is
20 amended to read as follows:
21 Section 230.73. A. As used in this section:
22 1. "Legal immigrant" means an individual not born in the United
23 States and not a citizen of the United States whose entrance into
24 the United States has been approved by the Immigration and

1 Natu ralization Service United States Citizenship and Immigration

3 2. "Qualified alien" shall have the same meaning as such term
4 is defined by Section 431(b) of the federal Personal Responsibility
5 and Work Opportunity Reconciliation Act of 1996, P.L. 104-193, as
6 amended.
7 B. 1. The Legislature hereby finds and declares that passage
8 of the federal Personal Responsibility and Work Opportunity
9 Reconciliation Act of 1996, P.L. 104-193, requires the states to
10 make certain decisions concerning legal immigrants and their
11 eligibility for certain types of public assistance.
12 2. The goal of this section is to recognize that foreign-born
13 legal residents of the State of Oklahoma contribute to our society
14 by working in our communities, supporting local businesses, and
15 paying taxes and should be eligible to receive certain types of
16 public assistance under certain conditions. Moreover, the state
17 goal is to provide the types of assistance that will enhance the
18 state's ability to receive federal financial participation, thereby
19 reducing the ultimate burden on the state and local government for
20 emergency health and welfare needs.
21 3. This section is also intended to encourage and support
22 efforts to help foreign-born legal residents of the State of
23 Oklahoma to become citizens of the United States.
