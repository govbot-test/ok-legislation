16 requirements of or is within the jurisdictional areas of
17 environmental responsibility of the Department of Environmental

20 8. Drilling wells and producing groundwater therefrom except in

22 9. Using groundwater for air conditioning or cooling purposes

1 10. Failure Failing to properly plug abandoned water wells in
2 accordance with rules of the Board and file reports thereof; or
3 11. Using groundwater for cooling purposes at a data processing
4 facility or data center through open-air evaporative cooling systems
5 or any other cooling technology that consumes groundwater through
6 evaporation or discharge without recirculation. Groundwater permits
7 for such facilities shall only be issued if the applicant
8 demonstrates that the cooling system utilizes closed-loop,
9 dielectric immersion, or other comparably low-consumptive cooling
10 technologies that substantially recirculate groundwater and minimize
11 consumptive loss as determined by the Board.
12 B. Complaints by any individual in violation of the provisions
13 of subsection A of this section shall be made to the Board and to
14 the local groundwater irrigation district, if such district is
15 established pursuant to the Groundwater Irrigation District Act.

1 dete rmination, the Board shall take action to cease commission of

3 in Section 1020.22 of this title. Such steps shall include, but
4 shall not be limited to pursuing voluntary compliance, obtaining the
5 issuance of a cease and desist order by the Executive Director of
6 the Oklahoma Water Resources Board, imposing progressive fines and
7 penalties related to the severity and frequency of waste, requiring
8 more frequent reporting of usage, instituting action in a court of
9 competent jurisdiction to enjoin the waste, pursuing a suspension of
10 any suspending the permit or other administrative remedies by the
11 Board, and filing a complaint in the district court of the county

13 district attorney of said such county to prosecute such complaint.

15 subsection A of this section, any complaint or investigation, or any
16 enforcement matter other than an individual proceeding involving the
17 suspension of an Oklahoma Water Resources Board permit, shall be
18 referred to and subject to the jurisdiction of the Department of
19 Environmental Quality or other appropriate state environmental
20 agency or state agency with limited environmental responsibility.

5 permitted groundwater users;
6 2. Modernize its data collection system with respect to
7 permitted groundwater users and implement a program to receive and
8 index usage reports by an online or electronic system;
9 3. Investigate any claims of waste pursuant to Section 1020.15
10 of Title 82 of the Oklahoma Statutes; and
11 4. Impose fines and penalties for overuse of water allotments
12 under a permit by any permitted well user or waste pursuant to
13 Section 1020.15 of Title 82 of the Oklahoma Statutes.
