19 petition to be sufficient, or upon the successful appeal of an order
20 determining the petition to be insufficient, the circulators of said
21 such petition shall have forty-five (45) ninety (90) days to obtain
22 a sufficient number of signatures to impanel a grand jury. Failure
23 to obtain the requisite number of signatures within that time period
