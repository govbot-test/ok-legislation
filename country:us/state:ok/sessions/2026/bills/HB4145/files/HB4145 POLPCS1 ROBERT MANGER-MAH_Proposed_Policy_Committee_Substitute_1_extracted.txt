13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 68 O.S. 2021, Section 2889, is
15 amended to read as follows:
16 Section 2889. Homesteads, as defined in Section 2888 of this
17 title, are hereby classified for the purpose of taxation as provided
18 in Section 22 of Article X of the Oklahoma Constitution. All
19 homesteads in this state shall be assessed for taxation the same as
20 other real property therein, except that each homestead, as defined
21 by Section 2801 et seq. of this title, shall be exempted from all
22 forms of ad valorem taxation to the extent of One Thousand Dollars
23 ($1,000.00) Seven Thousand Dollars ($7,000.00) of the assessed
24 valuation.
