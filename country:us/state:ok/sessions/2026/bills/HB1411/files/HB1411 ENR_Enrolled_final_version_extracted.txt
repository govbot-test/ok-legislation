Section 1:
ENROLLED HOUSE
BILL NO. 1411 By: Grego and Hill of the House
and
Hamilton of the Senate
An Act relating to the True Grit Trail; directing the
Department of Transportation to erect signage in
certain locations; requiring the Department of
Transportation to collaborate with the Oklahoma
Tourism and Recreation Department to maintain certain
online resources for the True Grit Trail; providing
source of funding; providing for codification; and
providing an effective date.
SUBJECT: True Grit Trail
BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 4101 of Title 69, unless there
is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
A. The Oklahoma Department of Transportation shall erect
signage designating the following roads as the True Grit Trail:
beginning at U.S. Highway 64 at the Oklahoma-Arkansas state line to
U.S. Highway 59; from U.S. Highway 59 to U.S. Highway 271; from U.S.
Highway 271 to the town of Spiro; from Spiro back to U.S. Highway
59; from U.S. Highway 59 to State Highway 31; from State Highway 31
to U.S. Highway 270; from U.S. Highway 270 to Main Street in
McAlester; from Main Street to East Krebs Avenue; from East Krebs
Avenue and Main Street back to U.S. Highway 270; from U.S. Highway
270 to State Highway 2; from State Highway 2 to Robbers Cave State
Park; from Robbers Cave State Park on State Highway 2 to U.S.
Highway 270; from U.S. Highway 270 to State Highway 82; from State
Highway 82 to U.S. Highway 271; from U.S. Highway 271 to State
Highway 1; and from State Highway 1 and ending at the intersection
of State Highway 1 and U.S. Highway 259.
B. The Department of Transportation shall erect informational
highway signage designed or approved by the Oklahoma Tourism and
Recreation Department to direct visitors to sites of significant
interest along the True Grit Trail, including:

C. The Department of Transportation shall collaborate with the
Oklahoma Tourism and Recreation Department to maintain online
resources for the True Grit Trail, including, but not limited to:

Section 18:

Section 19:
2. Information about each site of significant interest
identified in this act; and

permanent markers to be placed as required by this act.
