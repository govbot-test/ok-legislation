10 An Act relating to business entities; amending 18
O.S. 2021, Sections 2005, as amended by Section 3,
11 Chapter 121, O.S.L. 2024, 2043, and 2055.2, as
amended by Section 21, Chapter 121, O.S.L. 2024 (18
12 O.S. Supp. 2025, Sections 2005 and 2055.2) which
relate to the Oklahoma Limited Liability Company Act;
13 requiring submission of electronic mail address of
certain agents or entities to the Secretary of State;
14 amending 54 O.S. 2021, Sections 500-201A, 500-210A,
and 500-902A, which relate to the Uniform Limited
15 Partnership Act of 2010; requiring submission of
electronic mail address of certain agents or entities
16 to the Secretary of State; updating statutory
references; updating statutory language; and
17 providing an effective date.
18
19
20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
21 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 18 O.S. 2021, Section 2005, as
22 amended by Section 3, Chapter 121, O.S.L. 2024 (18 O.S. Supp. 2025,
23 Section 2005), is amended to read as follows:
24 Section 2005. A. The articles of organization shall set forth:
SB1641 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 1. The name of the limited liability company;
2 2. The term of the existence of the limited liability company
3 which may be perpetual; and
4 3. The street address of its principal place of business,
5 wherever located, and the name, electronic mail address, and street
6 address of its registered agent which shall be identical to its
7 registered office in this state.
8 B. If the limited liability company is to establish two or more
9 series of members, managers or membership interests having separate
10 rights, powers or duties as provided under Section 2054.4 of this
11 title or Section 14 2054.5 of this act title and the debts,
12 liabilities and obligations incurred, contracted for or otherwise
13 existing with respect to a particular series are to be enforceable
14 against the assets of the series only, the articles of organization
15 shall set forth a notice of the limitation on liabilities of the
16 series.
17 C. The articles of organization may set forth any other matters
18 the members determine to include. It is not necessary to set out in
19 the articles of organization any of the powers enumerated in Section
20 2000 et seq. of this title.

1 SECTION 3. AMENDATORY 18 O.S. 2021, Section 2055.2, as
2 amended by Section 21, Chapter 121, O.S.L. 2024 (18 O.S. Supp. 2025,
3 Section 2055.2), is amended to read as follows:
4 Section 2055.2.
5 ANNUAL CERTIFICATE FOR DOMESTIC LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANY AND
6 FOREIGN LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANY
7 A. Every domestic limited liability company and every foreign
8 limited liability company registered to do business in this state
9 shall file a certificate each year in the Office of the Secretary of
10 State, which confirms it is an active business and includes its
11 principal place of business address and electronic mail address, and
12 shall pay an annual certificate fee of Twenty-five Dollars ($25.00).
13 B. The annual certificate shall be due on the anniversary date
14 of filing the articles of organization, articles of registered
15 series, or registration, as the case may be, until cancellation of
16 the articles of organization or articles of registered series or
17 withdrawal of the registration.
18 C. The Secretary of State shall, at least sixty (60) days
19 before the anniversary date of each year, cause a notice of the
20 annual certificate to be sent to each domestic limited liability
21 company and registered series and each foreign limited liability
22 company and registered series required to comply with the provisions
23 of this section to its last known electronic mail address of record
24 with the Secretary of State.

BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 D. A domestic limited liability company or registered series or
2 foreign limited liability company or registered series that fails to
3 file the annual certificate and pay the annual certificate fee
4 within sixty (60) days after the date due shall cease to be in good
5 standing as a domestic limited liability company or registered
6 series or registered as a foreign limited liability company or
7 registered series in this state.
8 E. Except for accepting a resignation of a registered agent
9 when a successor registered agent is not being appointed or an
10 application for reinstatement, the Secretary of State shall not
11 accept for filing any certificate or articles, or issue any
12 certificate of good standing, in respect to any domestic limited
13 liability company or registered series that has ceased to be in good
14 standing or foreign limited liability company or registered series
15 that has ceased to be registered, unless or until the domestic
16 limited liability company or registered series has been reinstated
17 in good standing or the foreign limited liability company or
18 registered series has been reinstated as a foreign limited liability
19 company or registered series duly registered in this state.
20 F. A domestic limited liability company or registered series
21 that has ceased to be in good standing or a foreign limited
22 liability company or registered series that has ceased to be
23 registered in this state may not maintain any action, suit or
24 proceeding in any court of this state until the domestic limited

1 liab ility company or registered series has been reinstated in good
2 standing or the foreign limited liability company or registered
3 series has been reinstated as a foreign limited liability company or
4 registered series duly registered in this state. An action, suit or
5 proceeding may not be maintained in any court of this state by any
6 successor or assignee of the domestic limited liability company or
7 registered series or foreign limited liability company or registered
8 series on any right, claim or demand arising out of the transaction
9 of business by the domestic limited liability company or registered
10 series after it has ceased to be in good standing or a foreign
11 limited liability company or registered series that has ceased to be
12 registered in this state until the domestic limited liability
13 company or registered series or foreign limited liability company or
14 registered series, or any person that has acquired all or
15 substantially all of its assets, has caused the limited liability
16 company or registered series to be reinstated in good standing or as
17 a foreign limited liability company or registered series duly
18 registered in this state, as applicable.
