9 An Act relating to hospitals; imposing certain duties
on hospital in cases of fetal death or miscarriage;
10 requiring the State Department of Health to develop
and publish certain form; providing for codification;
11 and providing an effective date.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
16 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1-702g of Title 63, unless there
17 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
18 A. When a fetal death occurs in a hospital, or when a woman
19 presents to a hospital for treatment of a miscarriage, the hospital
20 shall, prior to discharge:
21 1. Provide the woman or her designee with a physical or digital
22 copy of the form described in subsection B of this section; and
23
24
SB1436 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 2. Ensure that a hospital staff member verbally communicates
2 that the woman or family may request the certificates listed on the
3 form through the process described on the form.
4 B. The State Department of Health shall, for the purposes of
5 this section, develop and publish on its public website a form that:
6 1. Explains the process for requesting a fetal death
7 certificate under Section 1-318 of Title 63 of the Oklahoma Statutes
8 and the process for requesting a certificate of birth resulting in
9 stillbirth under Section 1-318.2 of Title 63 of the Oklahoma
10 Statutes; and
11 2. Provides contact information for the Division of Vital
12 Records of the Department.
13 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
14

OVERSIGHT, dated 04/16/2026 - DO PASS.
