10 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
11 An Act relating to the Department of Mental Health
and Substance Abuse Services; making an
12 appropriation; identifying source; providing amount;
stating purpose; providing for noncodification; and
13 declaring an emergency.
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
18 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
19 There is hereby appropriated to the Department of Mental Health
20 and Substance Abuse Services from any monies not otherwise
21 appropriated from the Rate Preservation Fund of the State Treasury,
22 the sum of Nineteen Million Six Hundred Sixty Thousand Seven Hundred
23 Seventy Dollars ($19,660,770.00) or so much thereof as may be
24 necessary to pay for Title XIX services.

1 SECTION 2. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
2 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
3 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
4 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
