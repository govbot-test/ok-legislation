10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
11 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
12 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 5-107C of Title 70, unless there
13 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
14 Each public school district in this state shall post within one
15 click of the home page of the school district’s website the
16 following information:
17 1. The name, picture, email address, phone number, mailing
18 address, and a short biography for each member of the school
19 district’s board of education. The biography shall include the date
20 the member was elected to office and the date his or her term of
21 office expires;
22 2. The name, picture, email address, phone number, mailing
23 address, and a short biography for the school district
24 superintendent; and

4 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
