16 "An Act relating to groundwater permits; amending 82

22 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
23 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 82 O.S. 2021, Section 1020.7, is
24 amended to read as follows:
ENGR. H. A. to ENGR. S. B. NO. 133 Page 1

1 Section 1020.7. Any person intending to use groundwater shall
2 make application apply to the Oklahoma Water Resources Board for an
3 appropriate permit as provided in Section 1020.11 of this title
4 before commencing any drilling for such purposes and before taking
5 water from any completed well heretofore drilled. Such application
6 to take and use groundwater shall be on a form provided by the Board
7 and pursuant to the rules and regulations established by the Board.
8 Any person intending to use groundwater for a medical marijuana grow
9 facility shall present to the Board a valid medical marijuana
10 commercial grower license issued by the Oklahoma Medical Marijuana
11 Authority pursuant to Section 427.14 of Title 63 of the Oklahoma
12 Statutes and an Oklahoma Bureau of Narcotics Manufacturer
13 Registration.
14 The application heretofore filed with the Board shall be used in
15 granting permits for existing wells and the Board shall publish the
16 notice of the hearing thereon.
17 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
18 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
19 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
20 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
21 be in full force from and after its passage and approval."
