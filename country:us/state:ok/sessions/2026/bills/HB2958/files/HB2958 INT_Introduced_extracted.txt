1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 2nd Session of the 60th Legislature (2026)
3 HOUSE BILL 2958 By: Grego
4
5
6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to state employee compensation;
providing for salary increase for certain state
8 employees; specifying certain employees not eligible
for salary increase; providing for noncodification;
9 providing an effective date; and declaring an
emergency.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
14 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
15 A. Effective July 1, 2026, each state employee who was in full
16 time status as of June 30, 2026, shall receive a salary increase of
17 nine percent (9%).
18 B. The provisions of this section shall not be applicable to
19 any person employed by the Oklahoma State Regents for Higher
20 Education or employed by any college or university within The
21 Oklahoma State System of Higher Education.
22 C. The provisions of this section shall not be applicable to
23 any person employed by a common school district.
