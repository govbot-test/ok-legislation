9 AS INTRODUCED
10 An Act relating to cities and towns; requiring
municipal bond proposals to contain one subject and
11 express subject in ballot title; clarifying that
multiple proposals shall be permitted; providing test
12 to determine whether proposal violates act; providing
for codification; and providing an effective date.
13
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
18 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 13-113 of Title 26, unless there
19 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
20 A. Every municipal bond proposal to be voted on by the citizens
21 of that municipality shall contain only one subject and the subject
22 shall be expressed and defined in its ballot title.
23 B. Multiple bond proposals shall be permitted to be on the same
24 ballot, provided they require separate votes to be cast.

1 C. A bond proposal may violate this act if the monies raised
2 through the bond are appropriated and spent by more than a single
