Section 1:
1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 2nd Session of the 60th Legislature (2026)
4
5
6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to cities and towns; requiring
municipal bond proposals to contain one subject and
8 express subject in ballot title; clarifying that
multiple proposals shall be permitted; providing test
9 to determine whether proposal violates act; providing
for codification; and providing an effective date.
10
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
15 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 13-113 of Title 26, unless there
16 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
17 A. Every municipal bond proposal to be voted on by the citizens
18 of that municipality shall contain only one subject and the subject
19 shall be expressed and defined in its ballot title.
20 B. Multiple bond proposals shall be permitted to be on the same
21 ballot, provided they require separate votes to be cast.
22 C. A bond proposal may violate this act if the monies raised
23 through the bond are appropriated and spent by more than a single
24 agency, department, or director within the municipality.
Req. No. 14084 Page 1
1 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
