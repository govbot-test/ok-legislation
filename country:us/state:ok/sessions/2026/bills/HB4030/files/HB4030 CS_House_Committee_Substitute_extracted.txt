12 Office of Management and Enterprise Services in writing and file a

15 exemption and budget work program revisions with the chair of the

17 of the Appropriations and Budget Committee of the Oklahoma House of
18 Representatives. The chair of the Appropriations Committee of the
19 Oklahoma State Senate and/or the chair of the Appropriations and

21 notify the Office of Management and Enterprise Services of any
22 noncompliance of the request with legislative intent within twelve
23 (12) calendar days of receipt of the exemption request.
24

1 B. The Office of Management and Enterprise Services shall
2 approve the request for exemption unless both the chair and vice
3 chair of the Legislative Oversight Committee on State Budget
4 Performance provide written notification to the Office of Management
5 and Enterprise Services within twelve (12) calendar days of the
6 Committee's receipt of the exemption request that the exemption
7 subverts the intention and objectives of the Legislature in
8 establishing the original limit. The Office of Management and
9 Enterprise Services shall give written notice of approval or
10 disapproval of each exemption to the agency, the Governor, the chair
11 of the Appropriations Committee of the Oklahoma State Senate, and
12 the chair of the Appropriations and Budget Committee of the Oklahoma
13 House of Representatives within eighteen (18) calendar days of
14 receiving the request.
