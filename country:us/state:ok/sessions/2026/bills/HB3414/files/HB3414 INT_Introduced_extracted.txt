Section 1:
1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 2nd Session of the 60th Legislature (2026)
4
5
6
7 AS INTRODUCED
8 An Act relating to public finance; directing the
Office of Management and Enterprise Services (OMES)
9 to create reporting function to assess whether
contract is service-driven or for staff augmentation;
10 directing OMES to amend state accounting manual;
requiring permanent file location for invoices for
11 intangible assets; providing for codification; and
providing an effective date.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
16 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 34.11.11 of Title 62, unless
17 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
18 A. The Office of Management and Enterprise Services (OMES)
19 shall create a reporting function within the state's accounting
20 software to assess whether a contract is service-driven or for staff
21 augmentation.
22 B. OMES shall amend the state accounting manual to require that
23 any invoice for intangible assets include a file path to a permanent
24 file location where the asset will be maintained during post-review.
Req. No. 14281 Page 1
1 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
