11 An Act relating to public finance; directing the
Office of Management and Enterprise Services (OMES)
12 to create reporting function to assess whether
contract is service-driven or for staff augmentation;
13 directing OMES to amend state accounting manual;
requiring permanent file location for invoices for
14 intangible assets; providing for codification; and
providing an effective date.
15
16
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
18 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
19 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 34.11.11 of Title 62, unless
20 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
21 A. The Office of Management and Enterprise Services (OMES)
22 shall create a reporting function within the state's accounting
23 software to assess whether a contract is service-driven or for staff
24 augmentation.

1 B. OMES shall amend the state accounting manual to require that
2 any invoice for intangible assets include a file path to a permanent
