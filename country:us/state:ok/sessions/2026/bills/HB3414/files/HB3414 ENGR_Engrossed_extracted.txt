10 An Act relating to public finance; directing the
Office of Management and Enterprise Services (OMES)
11 to create reporting function to assess whether
contract is service-driven or for staff augmentation;
12 directing OMES to amend state accounting manual;
requiring permanent file location for invoices for
13 intangible assets; providing for codification; and
providing an effective date.
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
18 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 34.11.11 of Title 62, unless
19 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
20 A. The Office of Management and Enterprise Services (OMES)
21 shall create a reporting function within the state's accounting
22 software to assess whether a contract is service-driven or for staff
23 augmentation.
24
ENGR. H. B. NO. 3414 Page 1
1 B. OMES shall amend the state accounting manual to require that
2 any invoice for intangible assets include a file path to a permanent
3 file location where the asset will be maintained during post-review.
4 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
5 Passed the House of Representatives the 24th day of March, 2026.
