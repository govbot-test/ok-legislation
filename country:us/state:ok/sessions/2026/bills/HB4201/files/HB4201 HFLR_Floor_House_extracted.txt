11 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Child Care Facilities
Licensing Act; requiring one master teacher for
12 certain number of enrolled children; providing for
codification; and declaring an emergency.
13
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
18 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 404.4 of Title 10, unless there
19 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
20 The master teacher ratio required for two-, three-, four- and
21 five-star centers shall be one (1) full-time master teacher for
22 every sixty (60) children actually enrolled at a licensed child care
23 facility.
