6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Child Care Facilities
Licensing Act; requiring one master teacher for
8 certain number of enrolled children; providing for
codification; and providing an effective date.
9
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 404.4 of Title 10, unless there
15 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 The master teacher ratio required for two-, three-, four- and
17 five-star centers shall be one (1) full-time master teacher for
18 every sixty (60) children actually enrolled at a licensed child care
19 facility.
20 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
