9 An Act relating to the practice of pharmacy;
authorizing pharmacists to dispense ivermectin upon
10 certain federal approval and subject to certain
conditions; providing certain immunities; authorizing
11 promulgation of rules; providing for codification;
and providing a contingent effective date.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
16 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 353.22.1 of Title 59, unless
17 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
18 A. Upon approval by the federal Food and Drug Administration of
19 ivermectin for over-the-counter use, a pharmacist may dispense
20 ivermectin for human use to a patient without requiring a
21 prescription from a licensed prescriber, in accordance with any
22 written standardized procedures or protocols issued by the State
23 Board of Pharmacy including, if required, providing the patient with
24 instructions on the proper use of ivermectin.
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - SB2014 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 B. Notwithstanding any other law, a pharmacist acting in a
2 reasonably prudent manner is not criminally or civilly liable or
3 subject to professional disciplinary action for dispensing
4 ivermectin in accordance with this section.
5 C. The State Board of Pharmacy may promulgate rules necessary
6 to implement this section.
7 SECTION 2. This act shall only become effective upon the
8 certification by the State Board of Pharmacy that the federal Food
9 and Drug Administration has approved ivermectin for over-the-counter
10 use.
