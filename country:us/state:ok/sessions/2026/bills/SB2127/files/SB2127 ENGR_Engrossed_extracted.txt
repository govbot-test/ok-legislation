6 An Act relating to agriculture; creating the Advisory
Council on Product Classification; providing
7 membership terms and duties; allowing for
reimbursement of travel expenses under the State
8 Travel Reimbursement Act; providing for codification;
and providing an effective date.
9
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
13 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 2-35 of Title 2, unless there is
14 created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
15 A. There is hereby created within the Oklahoma Department of
16 Agriculture, Food, and Forestry, the Advisory Council on Product
17 Classification.
18 B. The Council shall serve solely in an advisory role and shall
19 advise and make recommendations to the Department regarding the
20 classification of agriculture and food products for purposes
21 conducted by the Department.
22 C. The Council shall be composed of five (5) members appointed
23 by the State Board of Agriculture, all of whom shall represent
24 interests affected by product classification. Members appointed to
ENGR. S. B. NO. 2127 Page 1
1 the Council shall serve at the pleasure of the Board. Appointees to
2 the Council shall serve for a term of five (5) years. Members of
3 the Council shall not be compensated for their service but shall
4 qualify for reimbursement for travel expenses under the State Travel
5 Reimbursement Act.
6 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
7 Passed the Senate the 26th day of March, 2026.
