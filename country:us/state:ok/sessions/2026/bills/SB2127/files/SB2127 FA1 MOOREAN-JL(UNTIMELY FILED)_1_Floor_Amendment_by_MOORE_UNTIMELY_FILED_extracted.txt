19 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
20 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified

23 A. To ensure uniformity in pesticide labeling and warnings
24 within this state, the Oklahoma Department of Agriculture, Food, and

1 Fore stry has the exclusive authority to make registration and
2 renewal decisions related to pesticides as provided in this section.
3 B. The health and safety warning label on any pesticide that is
4 sold in this state shall not vary from the health and safety warning
5 label that is registered with the United States Environmental
6 Protection Agency (EPA) at the time of sale.
7 C. This section displaces any common law requirements with
8 respect to pesticide labels or warnings.
