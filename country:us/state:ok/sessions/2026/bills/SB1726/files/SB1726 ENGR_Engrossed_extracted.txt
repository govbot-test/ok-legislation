11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
13 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3235 of Title 70, unless there
14 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
15 A. An institution of higher education within The Oklahoma State
16 System of Higher Education utilizing graduate students as
17 instructors of record or as primary classroom instructors in
18 undergraduate courses shall establish and maintain a formal training
19 program to ensure such graduate instructors are adequately prepared
20 for their instructional responsibilities.
21 B. The training required by subsection A of this section shall
22 include effective teaching practices, classroom management,
23 assessment and grading standards, student accommodations, academic
24 integrity, and applicable institutional, state, and federal
ENGR. S. B. NO. 1726 Page 1
1 poli cies. The training shall also include instruction on the
2 protection of students’ rights under the First Amendment to the
3 United States Constitution including the principles of free speech,
4 free inquiry, and viewpoint neutrality. The training shall
5 emphasize that students shall not be penalized or disadvantaged in
6 grading, participation, or evaluation on the basis of lawful
7 expression.
8 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
9 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
10 of the public peace, health, or safety, an emergency is hereby
11 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
12 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
13 Passed the Senate the 25th day of March, 2026.
