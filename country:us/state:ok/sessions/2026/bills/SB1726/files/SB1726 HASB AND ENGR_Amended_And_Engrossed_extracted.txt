ENGR. H. A. to ENGR. S. B. NO. 1726 Page 1
1 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
2 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3235 of Title 70, unless there
3 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
4 A. An institution of higher education within The Oklahoma State
5 System of Higher Education utilizing graduate students as
6 instructors of record or as primary classroom instructors in
7 undergraduate courses shall establish and maintain a formal training
8 program to ensure such graduate instructors are adequately prepared
9 for their instructional responsibilities.
10 B. The training required by subsection A of this section shall
11 include effective teaching practices, classroom management,
12 assessment and grading standards, student accommodations, academic
13 integrity, and applicable institutional, state, and federal
14 policies. The training shall also include instruction on the
15 protection of students’ rights under the First Amendment to the
16 United States Constitution including the principles of free speech,
17 free inquiry, and viewpoint neutrality. The training shall
18 emphasize that students shall not be penalized or disadvantaged in
19 grading, participation, or evaluation on the basis of lawful
20 expression.
21 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
22 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
23 of the public peace, health, or safety, an emergency is hereby
24
ENGR. H. A. to ENGR. S. B. NO. 1726 Page 2
1 decl ared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
2 be in full force from and after its passage and approval."
