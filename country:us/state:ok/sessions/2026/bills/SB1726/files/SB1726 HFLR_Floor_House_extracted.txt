10 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
11 An Act relating to higher education; requiring
institutions of higher education utilizing graduate
12 students as instructors to establish and maintain
training for graduate student instructors; providing
13 for contents of training; providing for codification;
providing an effective date; and declaring an
14 emergency.
15
16
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
18 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
19 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3235 of Title 70, unless there
20 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
21 A. An institution of higher education within The Oklahoma State
22 System of Higher Education utilizing graduate students as
23 instructors of record or as primary classroom instructors in
24 undergraduate courses shall establish and maintain a formal training
SB1726 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 prog ram to ensure such graduate instructors are adequately prepared
2 for their instructional responsibilities.
3 B. The training required by subsection A of this section shall
4 include effective teaching practices, classroom management,
5 assessment and grading standards, student accommodations, academic
6 integrity, and applicable institutional, state, and federal
7 policies. The training shall also include instruction on the
8 protection of students’ rights under the First Amendment to the
9 United States Constitution including the principles of free speech,
10 free inquiry, and viewpoint neutrality. The training shall
11 emphasize that students shall not be penalized or disadvantaged in
12 grading, participation, or evaluation on the basis of lawful
13 expression.
14 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
15 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
16 of the public peace, health, or safety, an emergency is hereby
17 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
18 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
19
20 COMMITTEE REPORT BY: COMMITTEE ON EDUCATION OVERSIGHT, dated
04/14/2026 - DO PASS, As Amended.
