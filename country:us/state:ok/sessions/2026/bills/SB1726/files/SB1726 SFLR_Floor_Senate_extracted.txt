10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
11 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
12 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3235 of Title 70, unless there
13 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
14 A. An institution of higher education within The Oklahoma State
15 System of Higher Education utilizing graduate students as
16 instructors of record or as primary classroom instructors in
17 undergraduate courses shall establish and maintain a formal training
18 program to ensure such graduate instructors are adequately prepared
19 for their instructional responsibilities.
20 B. The training required by subsection A of this section shall
21 include effective teaching practices, classroom management,
22 assessment and grading standards, student accommodations, academic
23 integrity, and applicable institutional, state, and federal
24 policies. The training shall also include instruction on the
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - SB1726 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 prot ection of students’ rights under the First Amendment to the
2 United States Constitution including the principles of free speech,
3 free inquiry, and viewpoint neutrality. The training shall
4 emphasize that students shall not be penalized or disadvantaged in
5 grading, participation, or evaluation on the basis of lawful
6 expression.
7 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
8 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
9 of the public peace, health, or safety, an emergency is hereby
10 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
11 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
