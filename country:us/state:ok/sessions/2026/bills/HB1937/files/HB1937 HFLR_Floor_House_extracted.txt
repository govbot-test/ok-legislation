12 An Act relating to schools; amending Section 1,
Chapter 297, O.S.L. 2024 (70 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section
13 6-401), which relates to student communication;
defining student; permitting school districts to
14 determine whether to place school personnel on
administrative leave during an investigation;
15 removing requirement that an incident be noted in a
school personnel's employee file; requiring an
16 employee that was put on administrative leave during
an investigation and the investigation finding no
17 misconduct be reinstated and that the finding of no
misconduct be noted in the school personnel's
18 employee file; providing an effective date; and
declaring an emergency.

1 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY Section 1, Chapter 297, O.S.L.
2 2024 (70 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section 6-401), is amended to read as
3 follows:
4 Section 6-401. A. As used in this section:
5 1. "Electronic or digital communication" includes, but is not
6 limited to, emails, text messages, instant messages, direct
7 messages, social media messages, messages sent through software
8 applications, and any other electronic or digital means of
9 communication; and
10 2. "School personnel" means teachers, coaches, administrators,
11 school bus drivers, or any other persons employed full-time or part-
12 time by a public school or charter school; and
13 3. "Student" means a person enrolled in a public school or
14 charter school in this state receiving primary or secondary
15 education to obtain a standard high school diploma or high school
16 equivalency diploma. Student does not mean a person who holds a
17 standard high school diploma or high school equivalency diploma.
18 B. School personnel engaging in electronic or digital
19 communication with an individual student shall include the student's
20 parent or legal guardian in any electronic or digital communication,
21 unless such communication is on a school-approved platform and
22 related to school and academic communications.
23 C. Exceptions to the requirement in subsection B of this
24 section may be made in case of an emergency as determined by the
