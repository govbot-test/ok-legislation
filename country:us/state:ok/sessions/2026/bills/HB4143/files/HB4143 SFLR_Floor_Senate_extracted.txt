9 Section 40-102. A. 1. Every law enforcement officer who, in
10 the regular course of duty, investigates or receives a report of a
11 traffic collision occurring on public roadways and resulting in
12 injury to or death of a person or total property damage to an
13 apparent extent of Five Hundred Dollars ($500.00) Three Thousand
14 Dollars ($3,000.00) or more shall prepare a written report of the
15 collision on the standard collision report form supplied by the
16 Department of Public Safety. The reports shall be forwarded within
17 thirty (30) days of the collision or, if the collision results in
18 the death of any person, then within twenty (20) days of the death
19 of the person, whichever time period is lesser, by the law
20 enforcement agency preparing the report to the Department of Public
21 Safety. No report is required when all parties involved agree to
22 exchange information and there is no injury, no death, and no damage
23 to property other than vehicles.
