12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 68 O.S. 2021, Section 2104, as
14 last amended by Section 1, Chapter 252, O.S.L. 2025 (68 O.S. Supp.
15 2024, Section 2104), is amended to read as follows:
16 Section 2104. A. The value of any motor vehicle, except a
17 manufactured home, for the purposes of the excise tax levied by
18 Section 2103 of this title, shall be determined as of the time the
19 person applying for a certificate of title thereto obtained either
20 ownership or possession of the vehicle, which shall be presumed to
21 be the actual date of the sale or other transfer of ownership, and
22 assignment of the certificate of title.
23 B. The value of any vehicle, for purposes of the excise tax
24 levied by Section 2103 of this title, shall be the actual sales
Req. No. 16953 Page 1
1 pric e of such a vehicle after reducing the sales price by any
2 credits or discounts given for a trade-in vehicle, if a trade-in was
3 made as part of the transaction. The actual sales price of the
4 vehicle, which total shall be the basis of the motor vehicle excise
5 tax, as well as the number of tires on the vehicle and the tire rim
6 diameters, shall be entered on the bill of sale furnished by the
7 seller to the purchaser, or on such other form as may be prescribed
8 by Service Oklahoma.
9 Upon receipt of the properly completed bill of sale or other
10 form as prescribed by Service Oklahoma, and the payment of all
11 applicable taxes and fees, Service Oklahoma or an appointed licensed
12 operator shall issue a vehicle certificate of title in accordance
13 with the provisions of the Oklahoma Vehicle License and Registration
14 Act.
15 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
