13 WHEREAS, Section 26 of Article V of the Oklahoma Constitution
14 establishes the first Monday in February as the day on which the
15 regular legislative session commences, after an organizational day
16 on the first Tuesday after the first Monday in odd-numbered years,
17 and requires that the regular session be finally adjourned sine die
18 not later than the last Friday in May; and
19 WHEREAS, based on these parameters, the legislative calendar for
20 the 2nd Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature began on February
21 2, 2026, and will end not later than May 29, 2026; and
22 WHEREAS, the Oklahoma Legislature will complete its work for the
23 regular legislative session in a timely manner on Thursday, May 14,
24
ENGR. H. C. R. NO. 1027 Page 1
1 2026 , prior to the date required to be finally adjourned sine die;
2 and
3 WHEREAS, the Oklahoma Legislature recognizes that unforeseen
4 circumstances may arise that require the attention of the
5 Legislature prior to sine die adjournment.
6 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
7 OF THE 2ND SESSION OF THE 60TH OKLAHOMA LEGISLATURE, THE SENATE
8 CONCURRING THEREIN:
9 THAT following adjournment of each house on Thursday, May 7,
10 2026, the House of Representatives or the Senate may reconvene at
11 any time prior to 5:00 p.m. on Thursday, May 14, 2026, upon the
12 mutual agreement of the Speaker of the House of Representatives and
13 the President Pro Tempore of the Senate; provided, that members of
14 the respective house shall be provided at least twenty-four (24)
15 hours' notice of the reconvening of said chamber.
16 THAT pursuant to the provisions of Section 30 of Article V of
17 the Oklahoma Constitution, each house shall have the permission of
18 the opposite house to adjourn for more than three (3) days beginning
19 Thursday, May 7, 2026, and ending not later than 5:00 p.m. on
20 Thursday, May 14, 2026.
21 THAT if the House of Representatives or the Senate reconvene
22 pursuant to the provisions of this resolution, the respective
23 chambers will adjourn sine die at such time as may be declared by
24 the Presiding Officer of the respective chambers, but in no case
ENGR. H. C. R. NO. 1027 Page 2
1 late r than 5:00 p.m. on Friday, May 29, 2026; provided, that in the
2 event the House of Representatives or the Senate are not reconvened
3 prior to or on Thursday, May 14, 2026, then the respective chambers
4 of the Oklahoma Legislature shall be deemed to stand adjourned sine
5 die at 5:00 p.m. on Thursday, May 14, 2026.
