18 regular legislative session commences, after an organizational day
19 on the first Tuesday after the first Monday in odd-numbered years,

11 THAT following adjournment of each house on Thursday, May 7,
12 2026, the House of Representatives or the Senate may reconvene at
13 any time prior to 5:00 p.m. on Thursday, May 14, 2026, upon the
14 mutual agreement of the Speaker of the House of Representatives and
15 the President Pro Tempore of the Senate; provided, that members of
16 the respective house shall be provided at least twenty-four (24)
17 hours' notice of the reconvening of said chamber.
18 THAT pursuant to the provisions of Section 30 of Article V of
19 the Oklahoma Constitution, each house shall have the permission of
20 the opposite house to adjourn for more than three days beginning
21 Thursday, May 7, 2026, and ending not later than 5:00 p.m. on
22 Thursday, May 14, 2026.
23 THAT if the House of Representatives or the Senate reconvene
24 pursuant to the provisions of this resolution, the respective
