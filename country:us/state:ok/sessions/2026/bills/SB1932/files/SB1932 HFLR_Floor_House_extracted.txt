10 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Motor Carrier Safety
and Hazardous Materials Transportation Act;
11 authorizing certain representation in certain
administrative hearing; providing for codification;
12 and providing an effective date.

14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 230.19 of Title 47, unless there
18 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 During an Oklahoma Motor Carrier Safety and Hazardous Materials
20 Transportation Act administrative hearing, a motor carrier organized
21 as a corporation, limited liability company, limited liability
22 partnership, or partnership may be represented by an officer,
23 member, manager, or partner of the entity at the administrative
