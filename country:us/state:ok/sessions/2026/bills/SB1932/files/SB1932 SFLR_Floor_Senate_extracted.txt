7 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Motor Carrier Safety
and Hazardous Materials Transportation Act;
8 authorizing certain representation in certain
administrative hearing; providing for codification;
9 and providing an effective date.
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
13 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 230.19 of Title 47, unless there
14 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
15 During an Oklahoma Motor Carrier Safety and Hazardous Materials
16 Transportation Act administrative hearing, a motor carrier organized
17 as a corporation, limited liability company, limited liability
18 partnership, or partnership may be represented by an officer,
19 member, manager, or partner of the entity at the administrative
20 hearing if the motor carrier chooses not to be represented by an
21 attorney.
22 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
