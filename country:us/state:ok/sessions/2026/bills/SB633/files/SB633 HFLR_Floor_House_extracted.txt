10 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Juvenile Code;
amending 10A O.S. 2021, Section 2-7-308, which
11 relates to management information system and access
to confidential records and reports; authorizing
12 certain persons access to certain confidential
records and reports; and providing an effective date.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 10A O.S. 2021, Section 2-7-308, is
17 amended to read as follows:
18 Section 2-7-308. A. The Office of Juvenile Affairs shall
19 implement an agency-wide management information system for all
20 programs and services of the Office of Juvenile Affairs related to
21 children, youth and families.
22 B. The management information system shall:
23
24
SB633 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 1. To the maximum extent possible, be based upon the
2 integration, utilization and modification, as necessary, of existing
3 information systems within the Office of Juvenile Affairs;
4 2. Provide for the security of and limited access to the
5 information;
6 3. Include case-specific information, including outcomes, and
7 have the ability to monitor the status of children and youth
8 receiving services through the Office of Juvenile Affairs;
9 4. Be capable of providing management reports and information
10 regarding the various children and youth programs of the Office of
11 Juvenile Affairs, and of providing aggregate information necessary
12 for planning, monitoring and evaluation of said programs and
13 services; and
14 5. Be designed so that management and analytical reports can be
15 readily generated for those who require them.
16 C. 1. The management information system implemented by the
17 Office of Juvenile Affairs shall be integrated with the child
18 welfare management information system implemented by the Department
19 of Human Services and to the extent possible with the Juvenile
20 Justice Information System.
21 2. The management information system shall be available to the
22 superintendent or designee for the Oklahoma School for the Blind,
23 for the Oklahoma School for the Deaf, and for public school
24 districts, and to any other persons authorized to obtain
SB633 HFLR Page 2
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 conf idential records and reports of the Office of Juvenile Affairs
2 pursuant to Chapter 6 of the Oklahoma Juvenile Code.
3 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
