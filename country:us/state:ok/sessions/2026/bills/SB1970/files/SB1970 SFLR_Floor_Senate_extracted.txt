6 An Act relating to memorial highway and bridge
designations; designating the PV2 Earl Maggart
7 Memorial Bridge; providing for codification; and
providing an effective date.
8
9
10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
11 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
12 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1698.510 of Title 69, unless
13 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
14 The bridge on State Highway 18 about three and one-half (3 1/2)
15 miles south of Pawnee crossing over Camp Creek in Pawnee County
16 shall be designated as the “PV2 Earl Maggart Memorial Bridge”. The
17 Department of Transportation shall cause suitable permanent markers
18 bearing that name to be placed on the bridge.
19 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
20 COMMITTEE REPORT BY: COMMITTEE ON AERONAUTICS AND TRANSPORTATION
