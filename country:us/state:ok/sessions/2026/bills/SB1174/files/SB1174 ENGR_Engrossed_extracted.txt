7 An Act relating to judicial compensation; rejecting
judicial salaries established by the Board on
8 Judicial Compensation; providing for noncodification;
and declaring an emergency.
9
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
13 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
14 Pursuant to the authority provided in Section 3.2 of Title 20 of
15 the Oklahoma Statutes to reject judicial salaries established by the
16 Board on Judicial Compensation, the Oklahoma Legislature hereby
17 rejects the Final Report of the Board on Judicial Compensation
18 issued on September 16, 2025.
19 SECTION 2. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
20 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
21 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
22 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
