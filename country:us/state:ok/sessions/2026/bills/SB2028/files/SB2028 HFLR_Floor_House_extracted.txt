10 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Milk and Milk
Products Act; amending 2 O.S. 2021, Section 7-413,
11 which relates to violations; expanding violations;
requiring warning labels on containers of raw or
12 unpasteurized milk and raw milk products; directing
the Oklahoma Department of Agriculture, Food, and
13 Forestry to develop and produce warning labels;
requiring warning label to be affixed to certain
14 products prior to sale or distribution; authorizing
the Oklahoma Department of Agriculture, Food, and
15 Forestry to promulgate rules; amending 2 O.S. 2021,
Section 7-414, which relates to construction of act;
16 allowing for certain sales of ungraded raw milk and
raw milk products; restricting the advertisement of
17 the sale of ungraded raw milk; increasing average
monthly yield for incidental sales; providing for
18 codification; and declaring an emergency.

6 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
7 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 7-413.1 of Title 2, unless there
8 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
9 A. Any container of raw milk or raw milk products sold, offered
10 for sale, or distributed shall be labeled with a visible warning
11 label and the date the milk was filled in accordance with this
12 section.
13 B. The Oklahoma Department of Agriculture, Food, and Forestry
14 shall produce a warning label and shall make that warning label
15 available to all producers selling raw or unpasteurized milk through
16 the Department’s website. The warning label shall include
17 statements that are substantially similar to the following:
18 1. Raw (unpasteurized) milk and raw milk products are not
19 licensed, regulated, or inspected and may contain harmful bacteria
20 that can cause serious illness; and
21 2. Pregnant women, unborn babies, children, the elderly, and
22 people with weakened immune systems are at higher risk of serious
23 illness from consuming raw milk.
