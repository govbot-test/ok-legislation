I move to amend Senate Bill No. 2028 as follows:
1. On Page 2, Line 7, by inserting after the word “filled” and before the semicolon, the words
“in accordance with Section 2 of this act”;
2. On Page 2, Line 19 ½, by inserting a new SECTION 2 to read as follows, and by
renumbering subsequent sections:
“ SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified in the Oklahoma Statutes
as Section 413.1 of Title 2, unless there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
A. Any container of raw milk or raw milk products sold, offered for sale, or distributed shall be
labeled with a visible warning label and the date the milk was filled in accordance with this
section.
B. The Oklahoma Department of Agriculture, Food, and Forestry shall produce a warning label
and shall make that warning label available to all producers selling raw or unpasteurized milk
through the Department’s website. The warning label shall include statements that are
substantially similar to the following:
1. Raw (unpasteurized) milk and raw milk products are not licensed, regulated, or inspected
and may contain harmful bacteria that can cause serious illness; and
2. Pregnant women, unborn babies, children, the elderly, and people with weakened
immune systems are at higher risk of serious illness from consuming raw milk.
C. Every producer of raw or unpasteurized milk shall affix the warning label described in
subsection B of this section prior to the sale or distribution of such milk or milk products.
D. The Department shall promulgate rules necessary to implement the provisions of this section
including but not limited to the size, placement, and content of the warning label.”; and
3. By amending the title to conform.
