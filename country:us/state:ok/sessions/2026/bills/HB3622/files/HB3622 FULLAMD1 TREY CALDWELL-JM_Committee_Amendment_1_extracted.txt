I move to amend HB3622
Of the printed Bill
Page 1 Section 1 Lines 14-20
Of the Engrossed Bill
By deleting Section 1 in its entirety and inserting in lieu thereof the
following:
"SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified in the
Oklahoma Statutes as Section 5095 of Title 74, unless there is created a
duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving fund for the
Oklahoma Department of Commerce to be designated the "U.S. Decennial
Census Revolving Fund". The fund shall be a continuing fund, not subject
to fiscal year limitations, and shall consist of all monies received by
the Oklahoma Department of Commerce as directed by law. All monies
accruing to the credit of said fund are hereby appropriated and may be
budgeted and expended by the Oklahoma Department of Commerce for the
purpose of preparing for any upcoming federal decennial census, including
the upgrade of required technology. Expenditures from said fund shall be
made upon warrants issued by the State Treasurer against claims filed as
prescribed by law with the Director of the Office of Management and
Enterprise Services for approval and payment.";
And by deleting Sections 2 and 3 in their entirety and inserting in lieu
thereof the following:
"SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026."
