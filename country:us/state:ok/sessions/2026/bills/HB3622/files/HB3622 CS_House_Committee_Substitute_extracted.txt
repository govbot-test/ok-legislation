1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 2nd Session of the 60th Legislature (2026)
3 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
FOR
5
6
7 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
8 An Act relating to the 2030 Decennial Census;
creating the U.S. Decennial Census Revolving Fund;
9 establishing the fund at Commerce; deeming funds
appropriated; providing expenditure and budgeting
10 requirements and limitations; and providing an
effective date.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
15 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 5095 of Title 74, unless there
16 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
17 There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving fund
18 for the Oklahoma Department of Commerce to be designated the "U.S.
19 Decennial Census Revolving Fund". The fund shall be a continuing
20 fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations, and shall consist of
21 all monies received by the Oklahoma Department of Commerce as
22 directed by law. All monies accruing to the credit of said fund are
23 hereby appropriated and may be budgeted and expended by the Oklahoma
24 Department of Commerce for the purpose of preparing for any upcoming
Req. No. 16718 Page 1
1 fede ral decennial census, including the upgrade of required
2 technology. Expenditures from said fund shall be made upon warrants
3 issued by the State Treasurer against claims filed as prescribed by
4 law with the Director of the Office of Management and Enterprise
5 Services for approval and payment.
6 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
