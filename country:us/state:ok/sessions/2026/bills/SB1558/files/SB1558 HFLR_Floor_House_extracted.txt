16 Section 402. As used in the Oklahoma Child Care Facilities
17 Licensing Act:
18 1. “Adult” means an individual eighteen (18) years of age or

20 Office of Juvenile Affairs and jurisdiction has been extended beyond
21 eighteen (18) years of age pursuant to the provisions of the
22 Oklahoma Juvenile Code;
23 2. “Child” or “minor” means any person who has not attained the
24 age of eighteen (18) years, or an individual who is in the legal
