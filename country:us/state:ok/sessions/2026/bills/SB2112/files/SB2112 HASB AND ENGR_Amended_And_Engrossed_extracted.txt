5 Section 116. A. As used in this section:
6 1. “Cattle crossing signage” means a sign in a twenty-four (24)

8 on a yellow background; and
9 2. “Open pasture road” means a road maintained by a county;
10 where the land on both sides of the road is owned or leased by the
11 same owner or lessee; and a such roadway is without fencing.
12 B. Any public road, as defined in Section 232 of Title 69 of
13 the Oklahoma Statutes, in whole or in part, that is maintained by a
14 county and that meets the following conditions by November 1, 2025,
15 shall be designated as an open pasture road:
16 1. Cattle guards have been installed at the access points of
17 such road or part thereof; and
18 2. The land on either side of the road or part thereof to be
19 designated is owned by a single party; and
20 3. There is no fencing erected on such land on either side of
21 the road Cattle are allowed to be unrestrained or to run at large
22 through such roads.
23 It shall be the responsibility of the property owner to notify
24 the board of county commissioners that the roadway is eligible to be
