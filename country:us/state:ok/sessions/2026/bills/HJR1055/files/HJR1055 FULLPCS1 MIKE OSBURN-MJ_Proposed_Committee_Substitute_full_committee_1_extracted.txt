18 A. Beginning November 1, 2034, the State Superintendent of
19 Public Instruction shall be appointed using the following process:
20 1. The Oklahoma State Board of Education (OSBE) shall gather
21 applications, conduct interviews of applicants, and shall provide a
22 list of not less than three, and not more than five, candidates for
23 the Governor to choose from for the position of State Superintendent
24 of Public Instruction. The Governor shall appoint one of the

3 2. The Oklahoma State Senate shall vote whether to confirm the

5 3. Should the Senate fail to confirm the Governor's appointee

1 This measure amends the Oklahoma Constitution in Section 4 of

6 applicants, preparing a list of eligible candidates to be given
7 to the Governor, the Governor selecting a candidate from the
