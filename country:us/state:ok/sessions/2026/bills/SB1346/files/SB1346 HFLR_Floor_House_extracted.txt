19 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
20 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1085.66 of Title 82, unless
21 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
22 A. 1. The Oklahoma Water Resources Board shall establish and
23 administer a Water and Wastewater Infrastructure Investment Program.
24 The program shall include development of competitive loans for

5 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
6 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1085.67 of Title 82, unless
7 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
8 A. 1. There is hereby created in the State Treasury a
9 revolving fund for the Oklahoma Water Resources Board to be
10 designated the “Water and Wastewater Infrastructure Investment
11 Revolving Fund”. The fund shall be a continuing fund, not subject
12 to fiscal year limitations, and shall consist of all monies
13 appropriated to the fund by law. All monies accruing to the credit
14 of the fund are hereby appropriated and may be budgeted and expended
15 by the Board for the purposes provided in this act. The Board may
16 enter into contracts with financial institutions and execute such
17 instruments as may be necessary to hold and disseminate loan funds
18 in accordance with applicable regulations and Board policies.
19 2. Notwithstanding any other provisions of law, income and
20 investment return on fund principal and interest income and
21 repayment of principal on loans made from the balance of the fund
22 shall accrue to the fund.
23 B. 1. The Board shall have full discretion of all monies
24 within the fund for enhancements, leveraging, and reserve capacities
