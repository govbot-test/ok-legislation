I move to amend House Bill No. 1687 as follows:
1. On Page 11, Line 7, by inserting after the word “Nurse” and before the word “or”, the words “, a
physician assistant,”
2. On Page 43, Line 17 ½, by inserting a new subsection H to read as follows:
“H. Nothing in this section shall prevent an individual with capacity from deferring to the
judgment of another person, including a person that the individual has named as an agent.”;
and
3. On Page 61, Line 3, by deleting after the word “effective” and before the period, the date
“November 1, 2026” and inserting the date “July 1, 2027”.
