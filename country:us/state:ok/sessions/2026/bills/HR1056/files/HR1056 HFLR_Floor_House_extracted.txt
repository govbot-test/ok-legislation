13 WHEREAS, the United States Government has declared April as
14 Sexual Assault Awareness Month and the nonprofit organizations Peace

20 individuals, families, and communities, affecting people of all
21 ages, races, national origins, faiths, gender identities, sexual
22 orientations, immigration statuses, and economic backgrounds; and
23 WHEREAS, Sexual Assault Awareness Month and Denim Day were also
24 instituted to call attention to misconceptions and misinformation
