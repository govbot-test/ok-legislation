8 An Act relating to motor vehicles; prohibiting
certain entities from providing certain
9 recommendations; providing certain cause of civil
action for certain violation; prohibiting certain
10 entities from withholding certain grants in certain
circumstance; providing certain cause of civil action
11 for certain violation; providing for codification;
providing an effective date; and declaring an
12 emergency.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 808.1 of Title 47, unless there
18 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 A. 1. No agency, entity, or instrumentality of this state, or
20 entity contracted with such, shall provide specific recommendations
21 in favor of or against a commercial driver training school in this
22 state.
23 2. A violation of the provisions of paragraph 1 of this
24 subsection shall be a cause of civil action for the commercial
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - SB1595 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 driv er training school affected by such violation against the
2 violating agency, entity, or instrumentality, or entity contracted
3 with such.
4 B. 1. No agency, entity, or instrumentality of this state, or
5 entity contracted with such, that administers grants for students of
6 commercial driver training schools in this state shall withhold such
7 grants based on the student’s choice of a specific school.
8 2. A violation of the provisions of paragraph 1 of this
9 subsection shall be a cause of civil action for the commercial
10 driver training school or student affected by such violation against
11 the violating agency, entity, or instrumentality, or entity
12 contracted with such.
13 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
14 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
15 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
16 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
17 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
