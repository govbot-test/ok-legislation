7 An Act relating to district attorneys; directing
district attorneys to submit certain quarterly
8 report; requiring submission of quarterly report to
certain entities; directing the District Attorneys
9 Council to submit annual report with aggregated data;
authorizing the District Attorneys Council to issue
10 standardized reporting requirements; providing for
codification; and providing an effective date.

13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
16 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 215.42 of Title 19, unless there
17 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
18 A. Upon the effective date of this act, the district attorney
19 for each district court judicial district shall provide a quarterly
20 report with the number of cases submitted or referred to the
21 district attorney by state agencies serving children and juveniles
22 including the Oklahoma Department of Human Services and the Office
23 of Juvenile Affairs, that the district attorney declined to file
24 charges and prosecute, to the Legislature, the Office of Management

1 and Enterprise Services, and the appropriate standing committee of
2 the respective body having oversight of the District Attorneys
3 Council.
4 B. The District Attorneys Council shall prepare an annual
5 comprehensive statistical report based upon the data gathered from
6 each district attorney that shall include the types of referrals and
7 stated reason for non-prosecution, when available. The annual
8 report shall be submitted to the Legislature, the Office of
9 Management and Enterprise Services, and the appropriate standing
10 committee of the respective body having oversight of the District
11 Attorneys Council.
12 C. The District Attorneys Council shall be authorized to issue
13 standardized reporting requirements to ensure compliance with the
14 provisions of this section.
15 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
