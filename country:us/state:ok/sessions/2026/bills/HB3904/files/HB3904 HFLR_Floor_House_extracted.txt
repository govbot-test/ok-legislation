11 An Act relating to Medicaid; authorizing flexible
payment structures for prenatal, delivery, and
12 postpartum care; providing for codification; and
providing an effective date.
13
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
18 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 4013 of Title 56, unless there
19 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
20 A. The Oklahoma Medicaid Program shall reimburse for prenatal,
21 delivery, and postpartum services separately in lieu of a global
22 payment or an all-inclusive payment methodology for maternity
23 services.

1 B. Prenatal, delivery, and postpartum services include without
2 limitation:
3 1. Office visits;
4 2. Laboratory fees;
5 3. Physician-ordered testing;
6 4. Blood work;
7 5. Fetal nonstress tests; and
8 6. Continuous glucose monitors or other services for
9 gestational diabetes, when medically necessary.
10 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
