7 An Act relating to Medicaid; authorizing flexible
payment structures for prenatal, delivery, and
8 postpartum care; providing for codification; and
providing an effective date.
9
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 4013 of Title 56, unless there
15 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 A. The Oklahoma Medicaid Program shall reimburse for prenatal,
17 delivery, and postpartum services separately in lieu of a global
18 payment or an all-inclusive payment methodology for maternity
19 services.
20 B. Prenatal, delivery, and postpartum services include without
21 limitation:
22 1. Office visits;
23 2. Laboratory fees;
24 3. Physician-ordered testing;
ENGR. H. B. NO. 3904 Page 1
1 4. Blood work;
2 5. Fetal nonstress tests; and
3 6. Continuous glucose monitors or other services for
4 gestational diabetes, when medically necessary.
5 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
6 Passed the House of Representatives the 25th day of March, 2026.
