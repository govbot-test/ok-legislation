3 SUBCOMMITTEE RECOMMENDATION
FOR
5
6
7 SUBCOMMITTEE RECOMMENDATION
8 An Act relating to Medicaid; authorizing flexible
payment structures for prenatal, delivery, and
9 postpartum care; providing for codification; and
providing an effective date.
10
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
15 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 4013 of Title 56, unless there
16 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
17 A. The Oklahoma Medicaid Program shall reimburse for prenatal,
18 delivery, and postpartum services separately in lieu of a global
19 payment or an all-inclusive payment methodology for maternity
20 services.
21 B. Prenatal, delivery, and postpartum services include without
22 limitation:
23 1. Office visits;
24 2. Laboratory fees;
Req. No. 16444 Page 1
1 3. Physician-ordered testing;
2 4. Blood work;
3 5. Fetal nonstress tests; and
4 6. Continuous glucose monitors or other services for
5 gestational diabetes, when medically necessary.
6 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
