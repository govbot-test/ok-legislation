9 An Act relating to gang-related offenses; amending 21
O.S. 2021, Section 856.3, as last amended by Section
10 25 of Enrolled Senate Bill No. 1627 of the 2nd
Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature, which
11 relates to condition of membership; criminalizing
certain acts related to foreign terrorist
12 organizations; providing for penalties; authorizing a
court to take judicial notice of certain designation;
13 updating statutory language; and providing an
effective date.
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 21 O.S. 2021, Section 856.3, as
18 last amended by Section 25 of Enrolled Senate Bill No. 1627 of the
19 2nd Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature, is amended to read as
20 follows:
21 Section 856.3. A. Any person who attempts or commits a gang-
22 related offense as a condition of membership in a criminal street
23 gang or while in association with any criminal street gang or gang
24 member shall be guilty of a Class B5 felony offense punishable upon
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - HB3764 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 conv iction by imprisonment in the custody of the Department of
2 Corrections for a term of five (5) years, which shall be in addition
3 to any other penalty imposed. For purposes of this section
4 subsection, “criminal street gang” is defined by subsection F of
5 Section 856 of this title and “gang-related offense” means those
6 offenses enumerated in subsection F of Section 856 of this title.
7 B. Any person who attempts or commits a gang-related offense as
8 a condition of membership of, while in association with, acting on
9 behalf of, or knowingly supporting any foreign terrorist
10 organization shall be guilty of a Class B5 felony offense punishable
11 upon conviction by imprisonment in the custody of the Department of
12 Corrections for a term of five (5) years, which shall be in addition
13 to any other penalty imposed. For purposes of this subsection,
14 “foreign terrorist organization” means any organization designated
15 by the United States Department of State as such pursuant to 8
16 U.S.C., Section 1189. The court may take judicial notice of such
17 designation as published in the Federal Register or maintained by
18 the United States Department of State.
19 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
