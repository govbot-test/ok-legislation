6 An Act relating to soldiers and sailors; allowing
Oklahoma Department of Veterans Affairs to hire
7 seasonal employees; exempting such employees from

8 an effective date.

11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
13 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 430 of Title 72, unless there is
14 created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
15 A. For the purposes of this section, seasonal employees shall
16 be unclassified employees employed by the Oklahoma Department of
17 Veterans Affairs who work less than one thousand six hundred ninety-
18 nine (1,699) hours in any twelve-month period.
19 B. The Department may employ seasonal employees throughout the
20 calendar year. Project labor or pro re nata jobs employed by the
21 Department for a period of time necessary to complete a project or
22 fulfill the need shall be in the unclassified service of the state,
23 as provided by the Oklahoma Personnel Act, Section 840-1.1 et seq.
24 of Title 74 of the Oklahoma Statutes. Such employees shall not be

4 C. The Department, in its annual budget request, shall include
5 a summary of the use of project labor, which shall include the
6 number of workers employed under the provisions of this section and
7 the total wages paid to these employees.
8 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
