15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 70 O.S. 2021, Section 6-194, as
16 last amended by Section 1, Chapter 277, O.S.L. 2025 (70 O.S. Supp.
17 2025, Section 6-194), is amended to read as follows:
18 Section 6-194. A. The district boards of education of this
19 state shall establish professional development programs for the
20 certified teachers and administrators of the district. Programs
21 shall be adopted by each board based upon recommendations of a
22 professional development committee appointed by the board of
23 education for the district.

14 professional development needs. The professional development

16 competencies and instructional strategies in the core curriculum

18 1. Increasing the academic performance data scores for the

20 2. Closing achievement gaps among student subgroups;
21 3. Increasing student achievement as demonstrated on state-
22 mandated tests and nationally norm-referenced college entrance

24 4. Increasing high school graduation rates; and
