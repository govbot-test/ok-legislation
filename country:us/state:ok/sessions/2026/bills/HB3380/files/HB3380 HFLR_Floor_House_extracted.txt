3 SECTION 4. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
4 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1-11-104 of Title 10A, unless
5 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
6 The Department of Human Services shall:
7 1. Take appropriate action to address state and local policies
8 and practices that inappropriately prohibit participation in
9 federally-funded child-welfare programs by qualified individuals or
10 organizations based upon their sincerely-held religious beliefs or
11 moral convictions; and
12 2. Take appropriate action to increase partnerships between
13 agencies and faith-based organizations and houses of worship to
14 serve families whose children have been placed in foster care or are
15 at risk of being placed in foster care.
