10 B. 1. Except in cases provided for in Section 983b of this
11 title, when the judgment and sentence of a court, either in whole or
12 in part, imposes court financial obligations upon a defendant, the

14 thereafter until the debt is either paid or waived, shall offer to
15 conduct, contemporaneous with sentencing or at a later date, a
16 hearing to determine the ability of a defendant to pay the court
17 financial obligations. The court may make such determinations at a
18 cost hearing or upon written motion or affidavit by the defendant.
19 The ability of a defendant to pay court financial obligations may
20 not impact the sentence imposed.
21 2. Defendants with court financial obligations who are found by
22 the court to be unable to pay, in whole or in part, or who show
23 sufficient evidence of a presumption pursuant to paragraph 5 of this
24 subsection, shall be relieved of the debt by the court through a

11 ability to generate income or manage resources,
12 g. additional case-related expenses to be paid by the
13 defendant, and
14 h. any other factors relevant to the ability of the
15 defendant to pay.
