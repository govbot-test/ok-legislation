10 An Act relating to criminal procedure; amending 22
O.S. 2021, Section 983, as last amended by Section 2,
11 Chapter 211, O.S.L. 2024 (22 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section
983), which relates to court costs and fees;
12 modifying guidelines and procedures for cost arrest
warrants and the collection of court financial
13 obligations; directing courts to impose fines, courts
costs, fees, and assessments authorized by law;
14 allowing ability-to-pay hearings to be conducted at
time of sentencing; requiring defendants to pay
15 financial obligations; providing exceptions;
providing list of conditions; presuming defendant is
16 unable to pay if certain conditions are satisfied;
providing for the waiver of financial obligations;
17 directing courts to make written findings;
authorizing courts to reduce amounts owed; providing
18 options in lieu of payment; allowing courts to reopen
previous determinations and reduce or waive remaining
19 financial obligations; extending certain deadlines;
prohibiting the imprisonment of defendants or
20 suspension of driving privileges for failing to pay
financial obligations; directing the Administrative
21 Office of the Courts to develop certain forms,
procedural rules, and guidelines; making requirements
22 applicable to sentences after certain date; and
providing an effective date.
