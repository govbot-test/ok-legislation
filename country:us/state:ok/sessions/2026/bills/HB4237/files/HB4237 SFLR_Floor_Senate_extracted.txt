9 An Act relating to criminal procedure; amending 22
O.S. 2021, Section 983, as last amended by Section 2,
10 Chapter 211, O.S.L. 2024 (22 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section
983), which relates to court costs and fees;

12 to make certain determination at cost hearing;
requiring waiver of cost hearings to satisfy certain
13 conditions; and providing an effective date.
