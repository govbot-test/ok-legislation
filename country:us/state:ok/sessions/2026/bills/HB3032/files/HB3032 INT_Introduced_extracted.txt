Section 1:
1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 2nd Session of the 60th Legislature (2026)
4
5
6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to schools; amending Section 1,
Chapter 255, O.S.L. 2024 (70 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section
8 3-119.1), which relates to electronic applications
for free or reduced-price meals; eliminating
9 reference to optional applications; prohibiting minor
children from being enrolled in school unless a
10 parent or guardian fills out an application for free
or reduced-price meals; requiring school district to
11 provide applications during enrollment; allowing
parents or guardians to opt out of the free or
12 reduced-price meals programs; requiring the State
Department to ensure compliance with certain notice
13 mandates; requiring school districts to provide
parents and guardians notice of certain enrollment
14 essentials; providing an effective date; and
declaring an emergency.
15
16
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
18 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY Section 1, Chapter 255, O.S.L.
19 2024 (70 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section 3-119.1), is amended to read as
20 follows:
21 Section 3-119.1. A. The State Department of Education shall
22 make available to school districts an optional electronic universal
23 application for free or reduced-price meals under the National
24 School Lunch Act. The application shall at a minimum:
Req. No. 14011 Page 1
1 1. Enable parents and legal guardians to complete and
2 electronically submit applications for free and reduced-price meals
3 to the school districts in which their students are enrolled
4 enrolling;
5 2. Facilitate school districts in collecting and electronically
6 submitting free and reduced-price meal applications to the State
7 Department of Education; and
8 3. Allow for secure transmission of data necessary for
9 eligibility and enrollment determinations for the Supplemental
10 Nutrition Assistance Program.
11 B. The provisions of this section shall not be construed to
12 require school districts, parents, or legal guardians to use the
13 electronic universal application provided for in subsection A of
14 this section No minor child shall be admitted to any public school
15 operating in this state unless and until an application for free or
16 reduced-price meals under the National School Lunch Act is completed
17 by the child's parent or legal guardian and returned to the
18 applicable school district. Applications required by this
19 subsection shall be provided by the school district to each child's
20 parent or legal guardian during enrollment or re-enrollment. In
21 lieu of an application for free or reduced-price meals, a child's
22 parent or legal guardian may complete a form prescribed by the State
23 Department of Education and provided by the school district to opt
24
Req. No. 14011 Page 2
1 out of the application requirements of this subsection and
2 participation in the free or reduced-price meals programs.
3 C. The State Department of Education shall ensure that each
4 school district in this state provides, on the school district
5 website and in any notice or publication prepared for parents
6 regarding free or reduced-price meals, the following information
7 regarding requirements for school attendance: "For school
8 enrollment each year, a parent or legal guardian shall provide one
9 of the following:
10 1. A completed application for free or reduced-price meals; or
11 2. A completed and signed free or reduced-price meals program
12 opt out form".
13 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
14 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
15 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
16 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
17 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
