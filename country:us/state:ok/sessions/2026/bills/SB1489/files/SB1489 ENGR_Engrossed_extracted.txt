19 disabilities, provided their individualized education program
20 (I.E.P.) (IEP) states the need for extended school year special
21 education and related services. Children with disabilities shall
22 mean children, as defined in the Individuals with Disabilities
23 Education Act (IDEA), P.L. No. 105-17, who are three (3) years of
24 age.
