24 school district.

16 Section 13-101. A. The State Department of Education and the

20 funds to provide special education and related services necessary
21 for children with disabilities. “Children with disabilities” shall
22 mean children, as defined by IDEA, who are three (3) years of age
23 through twenty-one (21) years of age.

2 are hereby authorized to provide special education and related

20 of said such children in special education classes shall be included

3 this title shall provide for such modification and revised

7 shall have the right to:

13 other meeting regarding accommodations for a child with a
14 disability. The parent or legal guardian may agree in writing to a
15 review period of fewer than five (5) business days; and
16 2. Bring any individual of the parent or legal guardian’s
17 choosing including an individual with unique knowledge or expertise
18 regarding the child to any meeting with school personnel including,
19 but not limited to, meetings regarding an IEP, meetings conducted
20 pursuant to Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973, and
21 meetings concerning the academics, behavior, discipline, attendance,
22 health, or any other matter involving the child with a disability.
