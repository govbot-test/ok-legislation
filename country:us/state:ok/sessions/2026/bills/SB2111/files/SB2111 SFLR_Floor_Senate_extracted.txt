3 SENATE BILL NO. 2111 By: Murdock
4
5
6 An Act relating to game and fish; amending 29 O.S.
2021, Section 5-205, which relates to clothing
7 requirements for hunting; creating exception to
certain clothing requirements in commercial hunting
8 areas; and providing an effective date.
9
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 29 O.S. 2021, Section 5-205, is
13 amended to read as follows:
14 Section 5-205. A. No person after January 1, 1980, may hunt
15 any deer or elk with legal firearms unless such person is wearing a
16 head covering and an outer garment above the waistline, both
17 totaling five hundred (500) square inches or more of clothing, both
18 consisting of daylight fluorescent orange color totaling not less
19 than four hundred (400) square inches, and both to be worn
20 conspicuously on the person.
21 B. The clothing requirements set forth in subsection A of this
22 section shall not apply in commercial hunting areas.
23
24
