8 An Act relating to credit cards; amending 14A O.S.
2021, Section 2-211, as amended by Section 1, Chapter
9 410, O.S.L. 2025 (14A O.S. Supp. 2025, Section 2-
211), which relates to discounts inducing payment by
10 cash, check, or similar means; setting the maximum
surcharge amount for a credit card; requiring certain
11 payment to be the greater of a certain amount;
updating statutory language; providing an effective
12 date; and declaring an emergency.
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 14A O.S. 2021, Section 2-211, as
16 amended by Section 1, Chapter 410, O.S.L. 2025 (14A O.S. Supp. 2025,
17 Section 2-211), is amended to read as follows:
18 Section 2-211. A. With respect to all sales, service, and
19 lease transactions including, but not limited to, any consumer
20 credit sales transaction, a discount which a seller offers, allows,
21 or otherwise makes available for the purpose of inducing payment by
22 cash, check, debit card, or similar means rather than by use of a
23 credit card shall not constitute a credit service charge as
24 determined under Section 2-109 of this title if the discount is

8 to pay using a credit card instead of paying by cash, check, debit
9 card, or similar means unless the seller complies with the following

11 1. Notice displaying the amount of the surcharge applicable

13 the point of sale for in-person transactions and the home page and

16 the customer for transactions processed over the phone; and
17 2. No surcharge shall exceed two percent (2%) four percent (4%)
18 of the total transaction or the actual amount to be charged to the
19 person or retailer seller to process the credit card transaction,
20 whichever is less greater. A customer shall not be considered to
21 have chosen to use a credit card as a method of payment under this
22 section if, at the time of the transaction, the person or retailer
23 seller accepts only credit cards as payment.

1 C. A seller who is registered with the United States Department
