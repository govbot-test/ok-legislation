18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
19 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
20 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1265 of Title 19, unless there
21 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
22 There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving fund
23 for the Oklahoma Department of Public Safety to be designated the
24 "Safer Counties Revolving Fund". The fund shall be a continuing
HB3595 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 fund , not subject to fiscal year limitations, and shall consist of
2 all monies received by the Oklahoma Department of Public Safety from
3 monies appropriated by the Legislature. All monies accruing to the
4 credit of said fund are hereby appropriated and may be budgeted and
5 expended by the Oklahoma Department of Public Safety for the purpose
6 of making grant disbursements available to all Oklahoma counties,
7 who shall purchase public safety and traffic barrier equipment with
8 monies received. Counties may not use funds provided from the
9 "Safer Counties Revolving Fund" for any purpose inconsistent with
10 public safety. Expenditures from said fund shall be made upon
11 warrants issued by the State Treasurer against claims filed as
12 prescribed by law with the Director of the Office of Management and
13 Enterprise Services for approval and payment.
14 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
