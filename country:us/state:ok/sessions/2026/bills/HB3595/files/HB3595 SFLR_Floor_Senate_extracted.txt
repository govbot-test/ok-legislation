9 An Act relating to counties and county officers;
creating the Safer Counties Revolving Fund;
10 identifying funding source; providing purpose;
requiring and limiting the utilization of funds;
11 providing for codification; and providing an
effective date.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
16 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1265 of Title 19, unless there
17 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
18 There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving fund
19 for the Oklahoma Department of Public Safety to be designated the
20 "Safer Counties Revolving Fund". The fund shall be a continuing
21 fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations, and shall consist of
22 all monies received by the Oklahoma Department of Public Safety from
23 monies appropriated by the Legislature. All monies accruing to the
24 credit of said fund are hereby appropriated and may be budgeted and
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - HB3595 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 expe nded by the Oklahoma Department of Public Safety for the purpose
2 of making grant disbursements available to all Oklahoma counties,
3 who shall purchase public safety and traffic barrier equipment with
4 monies received. Counties may not use funds provided from the Safer
5 Counties Revolving Fund for any purpose inconsistent with public
6 safety. Expenditures from said fund shall be made upon warrants
7 issued by the State Treasurer against claims filed as prescribed by
8 law with the Director of the Office of Management and Enterprise
9 Services for approval and payment.
10 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
