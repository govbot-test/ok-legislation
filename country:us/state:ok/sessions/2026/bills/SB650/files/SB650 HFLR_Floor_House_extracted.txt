11 adopt a plan for components of maintenance and

public utilities to be in compliance upon approval of

claims; specifying certain accidents and occurrences;
18 providing for the process and amounts awarded under
certain circumstances; expanding exemptions for
19 liability by the state or political subdivisions;
updating statutory references and language; making
20 language gender neutral; providing for codification;
