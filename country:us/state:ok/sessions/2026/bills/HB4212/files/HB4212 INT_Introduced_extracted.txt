Re: RBH No. 15109
RBH No. 15109 would allow the surviving spouse of an OLERS
participant to remarry and continue receiving the survivorship
benefit, provided the surviving spouse had been married to the
participant for at least 30 months or the participant died in the
line of duty.
OPLAAA is amended to define the above change to non fiscal.
RBH No. 15109 is a non fiscal bill as defined by OPLAAA as amended.
I am a member of the American Academy of Actuaries and meet the
Qualification Standards of the American Academy of Actuaries to
render the actuarial opinion herein.

Re: RBH No. 15109
RBH No. 15109 would allow the surviving spouse of an OLERS
participant to remarry and continue receiving the survivorship
benefit, provided the surviving spouse had been married to the
participant for at least 30 months or the participant died in the
line of duty.
OPLAAA is amended to define the above change to non fiscal.
RBH No. 15109 is a non fiscal bill as defined by OPLAAA as amended.
