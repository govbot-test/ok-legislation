16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. There is hereby appropriated to the Oklahoma
18 Department of Transportation from any monies not otherwise
19 appropriated from the Progressing Rural Economic Prosperity Fund of
20 the State Treasury created in Section 256 of Title 62 of the
21 Oklahoma Statutes, the sum of Three Million Five Hundred Thousand
22 Dollars ($3,500,000.00) for the purpose of improving transportation
23 infrastructure with heavy industrial traffic located east of State
24 Highway 99/U.S. Highway 377 and north of State Highway 62.
Req. No. 17297 Page 1
1 SECTION 2. There is hereby appropriated to the Oklahoma
2 Department of Transportation from any monies not otherwise
3 appropriated from the Progressing Rural Economic Prosperity Fund of
4 the State Treasury created in Section 256 of Title 62 of the
5 Oklahoma Statutes, the sum of Four Million Eight Hundred Thousand
6 Dollars ($4,800,000.00) for the purpose of improving transportation
7 infrastructure with heavy industrial traffic located south of U.S.
8 Highway 412 and west of State Highway 385.
9 SECTION 3. There is hereby appropriated to the Oklahoma
10 Department of Transportation from any monies not otherwise
11 appropriated from the Progressing Rural Economic Prosperity Fund of
12 the State Treasury created in Section 256 of Title 62 of the
13 Oklahoma Statutes, the sum of Four Million Seven Hundred Thousand
14 Dollars ($4,700,000.00) for the purpose of improving transportation
15 infrastructure that serves as a main route for emergency services
16 located north of the Northwest Expressway/State Highway 3 and west
17 of State Highway 74.
18 SECTION 4. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
19 SECTION 5. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
20 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
21 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
22 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
