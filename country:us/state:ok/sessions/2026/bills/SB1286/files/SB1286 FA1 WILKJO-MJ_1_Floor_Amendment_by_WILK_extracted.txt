FLOOR AMENDMENT
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
State of Oklahoma
SPEAKER:
CHAIR:
I move to amend SB1286
Page 1 Section 1 Lines 24
Of the Engrossed Bill
By creating a new section 2 which shall read as follows:
"SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified in the
Oklahoma Statutes as Section 4263 of Title 74, unless there is created a
duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
Any candidate for office that is subject to ethics commission
reporting that contributes to or loans to their candidate committee One
Hundred Thousand ($100,000.00) Dollars or more shall cause an audit to be
conducted by the ethics commission to determine and verify the source of
such funds."
AMEND TITLE TO CONFORM TO AMENDMENTS
