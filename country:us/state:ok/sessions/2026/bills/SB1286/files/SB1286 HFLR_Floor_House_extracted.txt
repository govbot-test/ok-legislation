9 An Act relating to elections; amending 26 O.S. 2021,

Section 2:
Section 3-123, which relates to polling places;
10 requiring certain political subdivisions to provide a
polling place; and providing an effective date.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 26 O.S. 2021, Section 3-123, is
15 amended to read as follows:
16 Section 3-123. The board of education of any school district
17 may, and the governing board of any municipality political
18 subdivision that held an election conducted by a county election
19 board on or after January 1, 2023, and that owns, rents, or leases a
20 facility located in or near a precinct without a suitable polling
21 place available shall, furnish a room or rooms in any school
22 building or municipal building the facility for use as a polling
23 place at no cost upon the written request of the secretary of the
24 county election board.
