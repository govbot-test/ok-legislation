4 SENATE BILL NO. 1286 By: Gillespie
5
6 An Act relating to elections; amending 26 O.S. 2021,
Section 3-123, which relates to polling places;
7 requiring certain political subdivisions to provide a
polling place; and providing an effective date.
8
9
10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
11 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 26 O.S. 2021, Section 3-123, is
12 amended to read as follows:
13 Section 3-123. The board of education of any school district
14 may, and the governing board of any municipality political
15 subdivision that held an election conducted by a county election
16 board on or after January 1, 2023, and that owns, rents, or leases a
17 facility located in or near a precinct without a suitable polling
18 place available shall, furnish a room or rooms in any school
19 building or municipal building the facility for use as a polling
20 place at no cost upon the written request of the secretary of the
21 county election board.
22 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
