2 (72 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section 36), is amended to read as follows:
3 Section 36. A. This act shall be known and may be cited as the
4 “Dignity in Burial Act”.
5 B. The Oklahoma Department of Veterans Affairs shall establish
6 a program to ensure a dignified burial for honorably discharged
7 veterans of the United States Armed Forces who, prior to death,
8 lacked the financial capacity to prepare for or to provide for an
9 interment or cremation befitting a veteran of the United States
10 Armed Forces. The program shall aid in obtaining any available
11 burial benefits from the United States Department of Veterans
12 Affairs or through private organizations. The program shall provide
13 a burial grant for any veteran or any person who qualifies for
14 reimbursement, as determined by the Oklahoma Department of Veterans
15 Affairs, for unpaid expenses directly resulting from interment or
16 cremation. The maximum amount of reimbursement, not including
17 private funds, shall not exceed One Thousand Dollars ($1,000.00) per
18 application.

20 follows:

22 Title 72 of the Oklahoma Statutes this title;

24 this state;
