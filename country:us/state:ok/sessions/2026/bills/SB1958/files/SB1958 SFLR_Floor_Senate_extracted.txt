8 An Act relating to memorial highway and bridge
designations; designating the Patrolman Mark Harris
9 Memorial Interchange; providing for codification; and
providing an effective date.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1698.510 of Title 69, unless
15 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 The interchange of Interstate 35 and Flood Avenue in Cleveland
17 County shall be designated as the “Patrolman Mark Harris Memorial
18 Interchange”. The Department of Transportation shall cause suitable
19 permanent markers bearing that name to be placed at the
20 intersection.
21 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
