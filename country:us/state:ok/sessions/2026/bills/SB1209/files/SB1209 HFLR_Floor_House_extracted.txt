10 An Act relating to forcible entry and detainer;
amending 12 O.S. 2021, Section 1148.4, which relates
11 to issuance and return of summons; modifying time
period for certain trial; updating statutory
12 language; making language gender neutral; and

15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 12 O.S. 2021, Section 1148.4, is
17 amended to read as follows:
18 Section 1148.4. The summons shall be issued and returned as in
19 other cases, except that it shall command the sheriff, or other
20 person serving it, to summon the defendant to appear for trial at
21 the time and place specified therein in the summons, which time
22 shall be not less than five (5) days nor more than ten (10) days,
23 exclusive of Sundays and holidays, from the date that the summons is
24 issued. The summons shall apprise the defendant of the nature of
