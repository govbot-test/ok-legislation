8 An Act relating to forcible entry and detainer;
amending 12 O.S. 2021, Section 1148.4, which relates
9 to issuance and return of summons; modifying time
period for certain trial; updating statutory
10 language; making language gender neutral; and
providing an effective date.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 12 O.S. 2021, Section 1148.4, is
15 amended to read as follows:
16 Section 1148.4. The summons shall be issued and returned as in
17 other cases, except that it shall command the sheriff, or other
18 person serving it, to summon the defendant to appear for trial at
19 the time and place specified therein in the summons, which time
20 shall be not less than five (5) days nor more than ten (10) days,
21 exclusive of Sundays and holidays, from the date that the summons is
22 issued. The summons shall apprise the defendant of the nature of
23 the claim that is being asserted against him or her; and there shall
24 be endorsed upon the summons the relief sought and the amount for
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - SB1209 SFLR Page 1

1 whic h the plaintiff will take judgment if the defendant fails to
2 appear. In all cases, pleadings may be amended to conform to the
3 evidence.
4 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
