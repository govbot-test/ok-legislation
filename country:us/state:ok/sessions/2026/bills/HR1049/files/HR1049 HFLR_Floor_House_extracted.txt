1 WHEREAS, the total economic impact of asthma in the United
2 States was $81,900,000,000 per year from 2008 to 2013, which is
3 approximately $115,000,000,000 when adjusted for inflation; and
4 WHEREAS, Black, Hispanic, and Indigenous individuals in the

13 allergies, including about 3,900,000 children; and
14 WHEREAS, food allergy has increased among United States children
15 over the past 20 years, with the greatest increase in Black
16 children; and
17 WHEREAS, children with food allergies are two to four times more
18 likely to have asthma or other allergic diseases; and
19 WHEREAS, May is an appropriate month to designate as "Asthma and
20 Allergy Awareness Month".
