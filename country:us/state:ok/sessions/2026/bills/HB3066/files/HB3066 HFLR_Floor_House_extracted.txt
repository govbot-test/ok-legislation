10 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
11 An Act relating to the Health Care Workforce Training
Commission; providing purpose and intent; creating
12 the Rural Health Transformation Revolving Fund;
providing for promulgation of rules; providing for
13 codification; and providing an effective date.
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
18 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 697.18.1 of Title 70, unless
19 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
20 In furtherance of its mission to increase access to health care
21 workers in the rural and underserved areas of Oklahoma, the Health
22 Care Workforce Training Commission shall be authorized to receive
23 appropriations from the State Legislature; accept federal funds and
24 grants, and use the same in accordance with federal requirements;
HB3066 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 and receive and accept donations, grants, contributions, and gifts
2 from any public or private source.
3 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
4 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 697.18.2 of Title 70, unless
5 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
6 A. There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving
7 fund for the Health Care Workforce Training Commission to be
8 designated the "Rural Health Transformation Revolving Fund". The
9 fund shall be a continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year
10 limitations, and shall consist of all available monies:
11 1. Received by the state as Rural Health Transformation Program
12 Funds pursuant to, including but not limited to, the One Big
13 Beautiful Bill Act of 2025, Section 71401 of P.L. 119-21;
14 2. Directed to the state by the federal government for the
15 purposes of recruiting and retaining clinical workforce talent to
16 rural areas, with commitments to serve rural communities for a
17 minimum of 5 years, or any other lawful purposes established by the
18 One Big Beautiful Bill Act of 2025;
19 3. Derived as an interest and income from deposits and
20 investments of fund assets; and
21 4. Designated for deposit by law.
22 B. 1. All monies accruing to the credit of said fund are
23 hereby appropriated and may be budgeted and expanded by the Health
24 Care Workforce Training Commission for authorized purposes as
HB3066 HFLR Page 2
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 prov ided in paragraph 2 of this subsection. Expenditures from said
2 fund shall be made upon warrants issued by the State Treasurer
3 against claims filed as prescribed by law with the Director of the
4 Office of Management and Enterprise Services for approval and
5 payment.
6 2. All monies accruing to the credit of said fund shall be
7 utilized for the purpose of recruiting and retaining clinical
8 workforce talent to the rural and underserved areas of Oklahoma or
9 any other lawful purpose established by the One Big Beautiful Bill
10 Act of 2025, including the costs and expenses of operating such
11 programs.
12 3. All monies accruing to the credit of the fund shall be
13 budgeted, expended, and transferred in strict accordance with
14 applicable state and federal law and guidance providing for a
15 limiting the utilization of such funds.
16 C. The Health Care Workforce Training Commission shall be
17 authorized to promulgate any additional rules necessary for
18 implementation of the Rural Health Transformation Program.
19 SECTION 3. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
