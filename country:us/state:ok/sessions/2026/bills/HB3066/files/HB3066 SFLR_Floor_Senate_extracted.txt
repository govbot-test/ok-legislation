9 An Act relating to the Health Care Workforce Training
Commission; granting certain powers to the
10 Commission; creating the Workforce Talent Recruitment
Revolving Fund; stating source and allowed uses of
11 monies; providing for budgeting and expenditure of
funds; authorizing promulgation of certain rules;
12 providing for codification; and declaring an
emergency.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 697.5a of Title 70, unless there
18 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 In furtherance of its mission to increase access to health care
20 workers in the rural and underserved areas of this state, the Health
21 Care Workforce Training Commission may:
22 1. Receive appropriations from the Legislature;
23 2. Accept, and use in accordance with federal requirements,
24 federal funds and grants; and
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - HB3066 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 3. Receive and accept donations, grants, contributions, and
2 gifts from any public or private source.
3 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
4 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 697.21c of Title 70, unless
5 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
6 There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving fund
7 for the Health Care Workforce Training Commission to be designated
8 the “Workforce Talent Recruitment Revolving Fund”. The fund shall
9 be a continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations, and
10 shall consist of all available monies awarded to the Health Care
11 Workforce Training Commission by the State Department of Health
12 under the Rural Health Transformation Program established by Section
13 71401 of P.L. No. 119-21. All monies accruing to the credit of the
14 fund are hereby appropriated and may be budgeted and expended by the
15 Health Care Workforce Training Commission for the purpose of
16 recruiting and retaining clinical workforce talent in the rural and
17 underserved areas of this state or any other lawful purpose
18 consistent with any conditions of the award of such monies under the
19 Rural Health Transformation Program. Expenditures from the fund
20 shall be made upon warrants issued by the State Treasurer against
21 claims filed as prescribed by law with the Director of the Office of
22 Management and Enterprise Services for approval and payment.

1 SECTION 3. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
2 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 697.21d of Title 70, unless
3 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
4 The Health Care Workforce Training Commission may promulgate any
5 rules necessary for implementation of the Rural Health
6 Transformation Program established by Section 71401 of P.L. No. 119-
7 21.
8 SECTION 4. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
9 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
10 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
11 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
