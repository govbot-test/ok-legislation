6 An Act relating to higher education; amending 70 O.S.
2021, Section 3242, which relates to resident tuition
7 eligibility for students with certain status;
removing language providing eligibility for students
8 who cannot present certain documentation; removing
language regarding eligibility for certain
9 scholarships or financial aid for certain students;
removing outdated language; amending 70 O.S. 2021,
10 Section 3242.2, which relates to higher education
benefits for certain students; removing exception to
11 prohibition for certain students; removing outdated
language; updating statutory language; providing for
12 promulgation of rules; and declaring an emergency.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 70 O.S. 2021, Section 3242, is
17 amended to read as follows:
18 Section 3242. A. The Oklahoma State Regents for Higher
19 Education may adopt a policy which allows a student to enroll in an
20 institution within The Oklahoma State System of Higher Education and
21 allows a student to be eligible for resident tuition if the student:
22 1. Graduated from a public or private high school in this
23 state; and
24

1 2. Resided in this state with a parent or legal guardian while
2 attending classes at a public or private high school in this state
3 for at least two (2) years prior to graduation.
4 B. To be eligible for the provisions of subsection A of this
5 section, an eligible student shall:
6 1. Satisfy admission standards as determined by the Oklahoma
7 State Regents for Higher Education for the appropriate type of
8 institution and have secured admission to, and enrolled in, an
9 institution within The Oklahoma State System of Higher Education;
10 and
11 2. If the student cannot present to the institution valid
12 documentation of United States nationality or an immigration status
13 permitting study at a postsecondary institution:
14 a. provide to the institution a copy of a true and
15 correct application or petition filed with the United
16 States Citizenship and Immigration Services to
17 legalize the student’s immigration status, or
18 b. file an affidavit with the institution stating that
19 the student will file an application to legalize his
20 or her immigration status at the earliest opportunity
21 the student is eligible to do so, but in no case later
22 than:
23 (1) one (1) year after the date on which the student
24 enrolls for study at the institution, or

1 (2) if there is no formal process to permit children
2 of parents without lawful immigration status to
3 apply for lawful status without risk of

6 Services provide such a formal process, and
7 c. if the student files an affidavit pursuant to
8 subparagraph b of this paragraph, present to the
9 institution a copy of a true and correct application
10 or petition filed with the United States Citizenship

12 (1) one (1) year after the date on which the student
13 enrolls for study at the institution, or
14 (2) if there is no formal process to permit children
15 of parents without lawful immigration status to
16 apply for lawful status without risk of
17 deportation, one (1) year after the date the
18 United States Citizenship and Immigration
19 Services provide such a formal process, which
20 copy shall be maintained in the institution’s
21 records for that student.

24 section, and subparagraph a of paragraph 2 of subsection B of this

1 sect ion shall not be disqualified on the basis of the student’s
2 immigration status from any scholarships or financial aid provided
3 by this state.
4 D. The provisions of this section shall not impose any
5 additional conditions to maintain resident tuition status at a
6 postsecondary educational institution within The Oklahoma State
7 System of Higher Education on a student who was enrolled in a degree
8 program and first received such resident tuition status at that
9 institution during the 2006-2007 school year or any prior year.
10 SECTION 2. AMENDATORY 70 O.S. 2021, Section 3242.2, is
11 amended to read as follows:
12 Section 3242.2. A. Except as otherwise provided in Section
13 3242 of Title 70 of the Oklahoma Statutes, an An individual who is
14 not lawfully present in the United States shall not be eligible on
15 the basis of residence within the this state for:
16 1. Any postsecondary education benefit, including, but not
17 limited to, scholarships or financial aid; or
18 2. Resident tuition.
19 B. The provisions of subsection A of this section shall not
20 apply to a student enrolled in a degree program at a postsecondary
21 educational institution within The Oklahoma State System of Higher
22 Education during the 2006-2007 school year or any prior year who
23 received a resident tuition benefit pursuant to Section 3242 of
24 Title 70 of the Oklahoma Statutes at that institution The Oklahoma

1 Stat e Regents for Higher Education may promulgate rules to implement
2 the provisions of this section.
3 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
4 of the public peace, health, or safety, an emergency is hereby
5 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
6 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
