10 An Act relating to higher education; amending 70 O.S.
2021, Section 3242, which relates to resident tuition
11 eligibility for students with certain status;
removing language providing eligibility for students
12 who cannot present certain documentation; removing
language regarding eligibility for certain
13 scholarships or financial aid for certain students;
removing outdated language; amending 70 O.S. 2021,
14 Section 3242.2, which relates to higher education
benefits for certain students; removing exception to
15 prohibition for certain students; removing outdated
language; updating statutory language; providing for
16 promulgation of rules; and declaring an emergency.
17
18
19 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
20 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 70 O.S. 2021, Section 3242, is
21 amended to read as follows:
22 Section 3242. A. The Oklahoma State Regents for Higher
23 Education may adopt a policy which allows a student to enroll in an
24

1 inst itution within The Oklahoma State System of Higher Education and
2 allows a student to be eligible for resident tuition if the student:
3 1. Graduated from a public or private high school in this
4 state; and
5 2. Resided in this state with a parent or legal guardian while
6 attending classes at a public or private high school in this state
7 for at least two (2) years prior to graduation.
8 B. To be eligible for the provisions of subsection A of this
9 section, an eligible student shall:
10 1. Satisfy admission standards as determined by the Oklahoma
11 State Regents for Higher Education for the appropriate type of
12 institution and have secured admission to, and enrolled in, an
13 institution within The Oklahoma State System of Higher Education;
14 and
15 2. If the student cannot present to the institution valid
16 documentation of United States nationality or an immigration status
17 permitting study at a postsecondary institution:
18 a. provide to the institution a copy of a true and
19 correct application or petition filed with the United
20 States Citizenship and Immigration Services to
21 legalize the student’s immigration status, or
22 b. file an affidavit with the institution stating that
23 the student will file an application to legalize his
24 or her immigration status at the earliest opportunity

1 the student is eligible to do so, but in no case later
2 than:
3 (1) one (1) year after the date on which the student
4 enrolls for study at the institution, or
5 (2) if there is no formal process to permit children
6 of parents without lawful immigration status to
7 apply for lawful status without risk of

12 subparagraph b of this paragraph, present to the

17 enrolls for study at the institution, or
18 (2) if there is no formal process to permit children
19 of parents without lawful immigration status to

1 copy shall be maintained in the institution’s
2 records for that student.
3 C. Any student who completes the required criteria prescribed
4 in subsection A of this section, paragraph 1 of subsection B of this
5 section, and subparagraph a of paragraph 2 of subsection B of this
6 section shall not be disqualified on the basis of the student’s
7 immigration status from any scholarships or financial aid provided
8 by this state.
9 D. The provisions of this section shall not impose any
10 additional conditions to maintain resident tuition status at a
11 postsecondary educational institution within The Oklahoma State
12 System of Higher Education on a student who was enrolled in a degree
13 program and first received such resident tuition status at that
14 institution during the 2006-2007 school year or any prior year.
15 SECTION 2. AMENDATORY 70 O.S. 2021, Section 3242.2, is
16 amended to read as follows:
17 Section 3242.2. A. Except as otherwise provided in Section
18 3242 of Title 70 of the Oklahoma Statutes, an An individual who is
19 not lawfully present in the United States shall not be eligible on
20 the basis of residence within the this state for:
21 1. Any postsecondary education benefit, including, but not
22 limited to, scholarships or financial aid; or
23 2. Resident tuition.
24
SB1633 HFLR Page 4
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 B. The provisions of subsection A of this section shall not
2 apply to a student enrolled in a degree program at a postsecondary
3 educational institution within The Oklahoma State System of Higher
4 Education during the 2006-2007 school year or any prior year who
5 received a resident tuition benefit pursuant to Section 3242 of
6 Title 70 of the Oklahoma Statutes at that institution The Oklahoma
7 State Regents for Higher Education may promulgate rules to implement
8 the provisions of this section.
9 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
10 of the public peace, health, or safety, an emergency is hereby
11 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
12 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
