Section 2232, which relates to the annual statewide

18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
19 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 74 O.S. 2021, Section 2232, is
20 amended to read as follows:
21 Section 2232. The Commission, through the Oklahoma Tourism and
22 Recreation Department, may sponsor, promote, and implement an annual
23 statewide tourism and recreation industry conference to promote the
24 tourism and recreation industry. The Department is authorized to
SB1525 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 may partner with private entities for the administration and
2 execution of the conference. The Department may enter into
3 contracts or agreements pursuant to this section on a fair and
4 reasonable basis in compliance with paragraph 10 of subsection C of
5 Section 85.5 of this title in an amount not to exceed Seventy-five
6 Thousand Dollars ($75,000.00). The Department is hereby authorized
7 to may charge registration and exhibit space fees necessary to cover
8 the costs of the conference and shall deposit the fees plus any
9 other conference proceeds, including donated funds, into an agency
10 special account to be created by the Special Agency Account Board.
11 Expenditure of monies from the agency special account shall be for
12 purposes incidental to the tourism and recreation industry
13 conference, and shall be approved by the Commission Executive
14 Director.
15 SECTION 2. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
16 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
17 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
18 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
