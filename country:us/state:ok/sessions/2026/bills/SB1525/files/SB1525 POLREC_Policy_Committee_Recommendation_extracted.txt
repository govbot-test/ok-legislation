14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 74 O.S. 2021, Section 2232, is
16 amended to read as follows:
17 Section 2232. The Commission, through the Oklahoma Tourism and
18 Recreation Department, may sponsor, promote, and implement an annual
19 statewide tourism and recreation industry conference to promote the
20 tourism and recreation industry. The Department is authorized to
21 may partner with private entities for the administration and
22 execution of the conference. The Department may enter into
23 contracts or agreements pursuant to this section on a fair and
24 reasonable basis in compliance with paragraph 10 of subsection C of
Req. No. 17330 Page 1
1 Sect ion 85.5 of this title in an amount not to exceed Seventy-five
2 Thousand Dollars ($75,000.00). The Department is hereby authorized
3 to may charge registration and exhibit space fees necessary to cover
4 the costs of the conference and shall deposit the fees plus any
5 other conference proceeds, including donated funds, into an agency
6 special account to be created by the Special Agency Account Board.
7 Expenditure of monies from the agency special account shall be for
8 purposes incidental to the tourism and recreation industry
9 conference, and shall be approved by the Commission Executive
10 Director.
11 SECTION 2. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
12 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
13 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
14 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
