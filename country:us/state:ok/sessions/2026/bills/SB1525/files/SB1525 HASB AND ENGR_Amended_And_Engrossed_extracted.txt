Section 2:
Section 2232, which relates to the annual statewide
14 tourism and recreation industry conference;
permitting the Oklahoma Tourism and Recreation
15 Department to enter certain agreements to promote the

16 Department to enter contracts or agreements not to
exceed Seventy-five Thousand Dollars; and declaring
17 an emergency.

19
20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
21 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 74 O.S. 2021, Section 2232, is
22 amended to read as follows:
23 Section 2232. The Commission, through the Oklahoma Tourism and
24 Recreation Department, may sponsor, promote, and implement an annual
ENGR. H. A. to ENGR. S. B. NO. 1525 Page 1
1 stat ewide tourism and recreation industry conference to promote the
2 tourism and recreation industry. The Department is authorized to
3 may partner with private entities for the administration and
4 execution of the conference. The Department may enter into
5 contracts or agreements pursuant to this section on a fair and
6 reasonable basis in compliance with paragraph 10 of subsection C of
7 Section 85.5 of this title in an amount not to exceed Seventy-five
8 Thousand Dollars ($75,000.00). The Department is hereby authorized
9 to may charge registration and exhibit space fees necessary to cover
10 the costs of the conference and shall deposit the fees plus any
11 other conference proceeds, including donated funds, into an agency
12 special account to be created by the Special Agency Account Board.
13 Expenditure of monies from the agency special account shall be for
14 purposes incidental to the tourism and recreation industry
15 conference, and shall be approved by the Commission Executive
16 Director.
17 SECTION 2. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
18 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
19 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
20 be in full force from and after its passage and approval."
