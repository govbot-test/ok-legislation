11 An Act relating to the University Hospitals
Authority; making an appropriation; stating purpose;
12 providing an effective date; and declaring an
emergency.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. There is hereby appropriated to the University
17 Hospitals Authority, from any monies not otherwise appropriated from
18 the Statewide Recovery Fund (497) of the State Treasury, the sum of
19 Fifty Million Dollars ($50,000,000.00) or so much thereof as may be
20 necessary to address gaps in funding for the adolescent behavioral
21 health center. Such funds shall be utilized in a manner consistent
22 with the recommendations adopted by the Joint Committee on Pandemic
23 Relief Funding on April 15, 2026.
24 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
