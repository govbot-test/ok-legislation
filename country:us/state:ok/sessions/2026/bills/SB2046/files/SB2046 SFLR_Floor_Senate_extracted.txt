7 An Act relating to municipalities; defining term;
subjecting campsites on municipal land to certain
8 review by the Department of Environmental Quality and
the Department of Public Safety; providing for the
9 promulgation of rules; defining term; subjecting
campsites on county land to certain review by the
10 Department of Environmental Quality and the
Department of Public Safety; providing for the
11 promulgation of rules; providing for codification;
and providing an effective date.

14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
16 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 43-117 of Title 11, unless there
17 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
18 A. As used in this section, “unauthorized camp” means any tent,
19 shelter, or bedding constructed or arranged for the purpose of or in
20 such a way to permit overnight use on a property not designated as a
21 campsite.
22 B. 1. Any campsite on land within a municipality not
23 designated for use as a campsite shall be subject to evaluation by
24 the Department of Environmental Quality and the Department of Public
