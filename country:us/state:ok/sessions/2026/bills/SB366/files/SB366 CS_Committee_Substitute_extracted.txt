11 An Act relating to school district boards of
education; amending 70 O.S. 2021, Section 5-117,
12 which relates to powers and duties of school district
boards of education; directing a board of education
13 to provide certain notice if a lessee does not timely
elect to purchase certain property; defining term;
14 requiring certain boards of education to provide
certain notice regarding certain proposed
15 transactions; defining term; requiring notice to
include certain information if a board of education
16 decides to accept certain bid, offer, or proposal;
providing certain time period for certain
17 notification; providing for lottery selection if more
than one eligible charter school elects certain
18 purchase or lease; prohibiting the establishment of
certain covenants, policies, or other agreements;
19 providing certain construction; updating statutory
language; providing an effective date; and declaring
20 an emergency.
