1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 2nd Session of the 60th Legislature (2026)
3 HOUSE BILL 3307 By: Steagall
4
5 AS INTRODUCED
6 An Act relating to Veterans Traumatic Brain Injury
Treatment and Recovery Revolving Fund; providing an
7 act relating to appropriations; stating amount and
purpose; providing an effective date; and declaring
8 an emergency.
9
10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
11 SECTION 1. There is hereby appropriated to the Veterans
12 Traumatic Brain Injury Treatment and Recovery Revolving Fund from
13 any monies not otherwise appropriated from the General Revenue Fund
14 of the State Treasury for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2027, the
15 sum of Five Million Dollars ($5,000,000.00) or so much thereof as
16 may be necessary to perform the duties imposed upon the Oklahoma
17 Department of Veterans Affairs by law.
18 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
19 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
20 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
21 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
22 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
