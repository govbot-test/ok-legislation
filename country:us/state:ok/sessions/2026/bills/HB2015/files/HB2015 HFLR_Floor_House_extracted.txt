10 An Act relating to landlord and tenant; amending 41
O.S. 2021, Section 121, as amended by Section 1,
11 Chapter 230, O.S.L. 2022 (41 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section
121), which relates to landlord's breach of a rental
12 agreement; providing a tenant may bring an action to
enforce an obligation of a landlord; providing
13 guidelines on when a tenant can bring an action to
enforce an obligation of a landlord; providing types
14 of relief; providing when a landlord's liability for
damages begins; providing that a tenant may withhold
15 payment of rent in certain circumstances; providing
procedure for withholding payment of rent;
16 prescribing method for recovery of attorney fees; and
providing an effective date.
17
18

22 Section 121), is amended to read as follows:
