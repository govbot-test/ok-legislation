8 POLICY COMMITTEE RECOMMENDATION
9 An Act relating to the Department of Corrections;
amending 57 O.S. 2021, Section 506, which relates to
10 Oklahoma Corrections Act of 1967; modifying
qualifications of the Director of the Department of
11 Corrections; providing term of service; deleting
removal or replacement power of the Governor; and
12 providing an effective date.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 57 O.S. 2021, Section 506, is
17 amended to read as follows:
18 Section 506. There is hereby created the position of Director
19 of the Department of Corrections. The Director shall be qualified
20 for such position by character, knowledge, skill, ability,
21 education, training and successful administrative experience and
22 shall either be certified as a peace officer in this state by the
23 Council on Law Enforcement Education and Training, or shall have
24 five (5) ten (10) years of professional level corrections work. The
Req. No. 16540 Page 1
1 Dire ctor of Corrections shall be appointed by the Governor with the
2 advice and consent of the Senate and shall serve at the pleasure of
3 the Governor and may be removed or replaced without cause for a term
4 of five (5) years. Compensation for the Director shall be
5 determined by the Governor. The Director may be removed from office
6 by a two-thirds (2/3) vote of the members elected to and
7 constituting each chamber of the Legislature.
8 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
