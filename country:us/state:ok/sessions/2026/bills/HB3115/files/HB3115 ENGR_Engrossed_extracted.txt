7 An Act relating to the Department of Corrections;
amending 57 O.S. 2021, Section 506, which relates to
8 Oklahoma Corrections Act of 1967; modifying
qualifications of the Director of the Department of
9 Corrections; providing term of service and
reappointment; deleting removal or replacement power
10 of the Governor; and providing an effective date.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 57 O.S. 2021, Section 506, is
15 amended to read as follows:
16 Section 506. There is hereby created the position of Director
17 of the Department of Corrections. The Director shall be qualified
18 for such position by character, knowledge, skill, ability,
19 education, training and successful administrative experience and
20 shall either be certified as a peace officer in this state by the
21 Council on Law Enforcement Education and Training, or shall have
22 five (5) ten (10) years of professional level corrections work. The
23 Director of Corrections shall be appointed by the Governor with the
24 advice and consent of the Senate and shall serve at the pleasure of
ENGR. H. B. NO. 3115 Page 1
1 the Governor and may be removed or replaced without cause for a term
2 of five (5) years. The Director may serve more than one term and
3 may be reappointed by the Governor with the advice and consent of
4 the Senate. Compensation for the Director shall be determined by
5 the Governor. The Director may be removed from office by a two-
6 thirds (2/3) vote of the members elected to and constituting each
7 chamber of the Legislature.
8 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
9 Passed the House of Representatives the 16th day of March, 2026.
