16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 57 O.S. 2021, Section 506, is
18 amended to read as follows:
19 Section 506. There is hereby created the position of Director
20 of the Department of Corrections. The Director shall be qualified
21 for such position by character, knowledge, skill, ability,
22 education, training and successful administrative experience and
23 shall either be certified as a peace officer in this state by the
24 Council on Law Enforcement Education and Training, or shall have

1 five (5) ten (10) years of professional level corrections work. The
2 Director of Corrections shall be appointed by the Governor with the
3 advice and consent of the Senate and shall serve at the pleasure of
4 the Governor and may be removed or replaced without cause for a term
5 of five (5) years. The Director may serve more than one term and
6 may be reappointed by the Governor with the advice and consent of
7 the Senate. Compensation for the Director shall be determined by
8 the Governor. The Director may be removed from office by a two-
9 thirds (2/3) vote of the members elected to and constituting each
10 chamber of the Legislature.
11 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
