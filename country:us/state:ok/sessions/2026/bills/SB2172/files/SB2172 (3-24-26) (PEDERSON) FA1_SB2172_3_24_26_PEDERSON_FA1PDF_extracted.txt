I move to amend Senate Bill No. 2172 as follows:
1. On Page 3, Line 1, by inserting after the word “facilities” and before the comma, the words

subsequent paragraphs:
“ 2. Any person who maintains a valid United States Department of Agriculture Class A or
B license, if the sole purpose of any possession, sale, transfer, or breeding of a primate is to
supply a research facility, in accordance with paragraph 1 of this subsection;” and
3. On Page 3, Line 14 through Page 4, Line 8, by deleting subparagraphs a, b, and c, and relettering
subsequent subparagraphs.
