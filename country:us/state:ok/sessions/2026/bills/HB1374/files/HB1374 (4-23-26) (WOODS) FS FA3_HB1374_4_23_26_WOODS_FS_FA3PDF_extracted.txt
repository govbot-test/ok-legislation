10 SECTION 4. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
11 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 158.14 of Title 17, unless there
12 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
13 A. Except as provided in subsection B of this section, a person
14 who negligently, recklessly, or intentionally causes or spreads a
15 wildland fire shall be liable to a property owner for injury or
16 damage occurring to the owner’s property that resulted from such
17 wildland fire. A person liable under this subsection shall be
18 liable regardless of whether the fire begins on federal, tribal,
19 state-owned, or private land.
20 B. In any civil action or other legal proceeding by a property
21 owner seeking to recover damages occurring to the owner’s property
22 that resulted from a wildland fire caused by an electric utility, an
23 award for damages to a property owner for injury or damages that

3 C. The lack of an applicable electrical wildland fire

17 encompassing regions affected by wildland fires of at least forty
18 thousand (40,000) acres within the past ten (10) years.
19 C. The Commission shall use data and input from relevant

22 D. The Commission shall conduct periodic evaluations of the
23 programs developed under this section to determine the effectiveness
