8 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Department of
Commerce; appropriating certain funds in certain
9 amount for certain purpose; directing utilization of
certain amounts for certain purposes; providing for
10 noncodification; providing an effective date; and
declaring an emergency.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
15 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
16 There is hereby appropriated to the Oklahoma Department of
17 Commerce from any monies not otherwise appropriated from the General
18 Revenue Fund of the State Treasury for the fiscal year ending June
19 30, 2027, the sum of Five Hundred Thousand Dollars ($500,000.00) or
20 so much thereof as may be necessary to perform the duties imposed
21 upon the Oklahoma Department of Commerce by law.
22 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
23 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
24
ENGR. H. B. NO. 4042 Page 1
1 Funds appropriated by Section 1 of this act shall be utilized as
2 follows:
3 Five Hundred Thousand Dollars ($500,000.00) to be transferred to
4 the U.S. Decennial Census Revolving Fund created by Enrolled House
5 Bill No. 3622 of the 2nd Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature.
6 SECTION 3. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
7 SECTION 4. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
8 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
9 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
10 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
11 Passed the House of Representatives the 14th day of April, 2026.
