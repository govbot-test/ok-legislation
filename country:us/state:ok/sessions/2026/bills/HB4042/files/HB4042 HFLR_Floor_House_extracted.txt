17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
18 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
19 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
20 There is hereby appropriated to the Oklahoma Department of
21 Commerce from any monies not otherwise appropriated from the General
22 Revenue Fund of the State Treasury for the fiscal year ending June
23 30, 2027, the sum of Five Hundred Thousand Dollars ($500,000.00) or

1 so m uch thereof as may be necessary to perform the duties imposed

7 Five Hundred Thousand Dollars ($500,000.00) to be transferred to

9 Bill No. 3622 of the 2nd Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature.
