14 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 27A O.S. 2021, Section 2-10-901,
15 is amended to read as follows:
16 Section 2-10-901. A. All incorporated cities and towns may
17 directly or through a public trust of which it is a beneficiary
18 develop a plan, subject to the approval of the Department of
19 Environmental Quality, to provide a solid waste management system
20 and shall adequately provide for the collection and disposal of
21 solid waste generated or existing within the incorporated limits of
22 such city or town or in the area to be served thereby at one or more
23 disposal sites. The governing body of the city or town may enter
24 into agreements with a county or counties, with one or more other
