15 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES AND THE SENATE OF THE
16 2ND SESSION OF THE 60TH OKLAHOMA LEGISLATURE:
17 SECTION 1. The Secretary of State shall refer to the people for
18 their approval or rejection, as and in the manner provided by law,
19 the following proposed amendment to Section 3 of Article VI of the
20 Constitution of the State of Oklahoma to read as follows:
21 Section 3. A. No person shall be eligible to the office of
22 Governor, Lieutenant Governor, Secretary of State, State Auditor and
23 Inspector, Attorney General, State Treasurer, or Superintendent of
24 Public Instruction except a citizen of the United States of the age
Req. No. 16235 Page 1
1 of n ot less than thirty-one (31) years and who shall have been ten
2 (10) years next preceding his or her election, or appointment, a
3 qualified elector of this state.
4 B. In addition to the eligibility requirements in subsection A
5 of this section, no person shall be eligible to the office of
6 Attorney General except an attorney licensed to practice law in the
7 State of Oklahoma who has been practicing law for at least five (5)
8 years.
9 SECTION 2. The Ballot Title for the proposed Constitutional
10 amendment as set forth in SECTION 1 of this resolution shall be in
11 the following form:
12 BALLOT TITLE
13 Legislative Referendum No. ____ State Question No. ____
14 THE GIST OF THE PROPOSITION IS AS FOLLOWS:
15 This proposition amends Section 3 of Article 6 of the Oklahoma
16 Constitution. Section 3 provides the eligibility requirements
17 for certain state offices including the office of Attorney
18 General. This proposition would amend the section to also
19 require that for a person to be eligible to be Attorney General
20 the person must be an attorney licensed to practice law in the
21 State of Oklahoma who has been practicing law for at least five
22 (5) years.
23 SHALL THE PROPOSAL BE APPROVED?
24 FOR THE PROPOSAL — YES _____________
