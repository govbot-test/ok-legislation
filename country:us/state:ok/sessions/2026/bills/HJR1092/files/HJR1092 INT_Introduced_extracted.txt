8 Building Code Commission; and directing distribution.
9
10
11 WHEREAS, pursuant to Section 308 of Title 75 of the Oklahoma
12 Statutes, the Oklahoma State Legislature shall adopt a joint
13 resolution to approve or disapprove administrative rules.
14 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
15 AND THE SENATE OF THE 2ND SESSION OF THE 60TH OKLAHOMA LEGISLATURE:
16 SECTION 1. The Legislature hereby approves all of the proposed
17 changes to Chapter 20 of Title 748 of the Oklahoma Administrative
18 Code filed by the Oklahoma Uniform Building Code Commission on or
19 before February 1, 2026.
20 SECTION 2. The Oklahoma Secretary of State is hereby directed
21 to distribute copies of this resolution to the Governor and the
22 editor of "The Oklahoma Register".
