9 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Teachers' Retirement
System; defining terms; directing the Board of
10 Trustees to adopt and maintain an assumed rate of
investment return; prohibiting the assumed rate from
11 being reduced; providing exception; directing the
Board to review compliance; directing the Board to
12 certify compliance; prohibiting the calculation from
being modified for compliance; providing for
13 codification; and providing an effective date.

18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
19 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
20 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 17-116.2E of Title 70, unless
21 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
22 A. For purposes of this section:
23
24
Req. No. 16495 Page 1
RBH No. 16495
1 1. "Annual net investment return" means the total investment
2 return of the Oklahoma Teachers' Retirement System, net of fees and
3 expenses, expressed as a percentage; and
4 2. "Fiscal year" means the fiscal year of the System.
5 B. The Board of Trustees of the Oklahoma Teachers’ Retirement
6 System shall adopt and maintain an assumed rate of investment return
7 for actuarial valuations.
8 C. The assumed rate of investment return shall not be reduced
9 below the lowest annual net investment return actually earned by the
10 System during the immediately preceding twenty (20) fiscal years
11 unless expressly authorized by an act of the Legislature.
12 D. The Board shall review compliance with this section annually
13 and certify such compliance to the Governor, the President Pro
14 Tempore of the Oklahoma State Senate, and the Speaker of the
15 Oklahoma House of Representatives.
16 E. The calculation of annual net investment return used for
17 purposes of this section shall be based on the same methodology
18 consistently applied in the System's audited financial statements
19 and shall not be modified for the purpose of compliance with this
20 section.
21 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
