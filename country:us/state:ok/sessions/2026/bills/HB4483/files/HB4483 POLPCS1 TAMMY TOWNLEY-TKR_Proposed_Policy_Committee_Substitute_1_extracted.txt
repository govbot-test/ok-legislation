8 PROPOSED POLICY COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
9 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Tourism and
Recreation Department; increasing employee salaries;
10 providing for noncodification; providing an effective
date; and declaring an emergency.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
15 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
16 A. Effective July 1, 2026, the compensation paid to each
17 employee of the Oklahoma Tourism and Recreation Department shall be
18 increased as follows:
19 1. Seven percent (7%) for employees with an annual salary of
20 less than Forty Thousand Dollars ($40,000.00);
21 2. Five percent (5%) for employees with an annual salary equal
22 to or greater than Forty Thousand Dollars ($40,000.00) but less than
23 Sixty Thousand Dollars ($60,000.00); and
24
Req. No. 15977 Page 1
1 3. Three percent (3%) for employees with an annual salary equal
2 to or greater than Sixty Thousand Dollars ($60,000.00) but less than
3 One Hundred Thousand Dollars ($100,000.00).
4 B. Employees with an annual salary equal to or greater than One
5 Hundred Thousand Dollars ($100,000.00) shall not receive an increase
6 pursuant to this act.
7 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
8 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
9 of the public peace, resources, health or safety, an emergency is
10 hereby declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take
11 effect and be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
