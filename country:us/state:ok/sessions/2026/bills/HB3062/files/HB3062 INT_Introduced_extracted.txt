6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to firearms; amending 20 O.S. 2021,
Section 129, which relates to the authority of judges
8 to carry firearms; extending authority to carry
firearms to retired municipal judges; and providing
9 an effective date.

11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 20 O.S. 2021, Section 129, is
15 amended to read as follows:
16 Section 129. A judge of the district court, municipal judge, or
17 retired judge of the district court, or retired municipal judge, who
18 receives a statement from the appropriate retirement system
19 verifying the status of the person as a retired judge of the
20 district court or retired municipal judge, may carry a firearm on
21 his or her person anywhere in throughout the state to use only for
22 personal protection if the person has successfully completed a
23 handgun qualification course for court officials developed by the
24 Council on Law Enforcement Education and Training. The Council on

1 Law Enforcement Education and Training may provide for an
2 identification card to be issued to the judge of the district court,
3 retired judge of the district court, or municipal judge, or retired
4 municipal judge and may provide application forms. If the person
5 issued an identification card is no longer eligible, that person
6 shall immediately return the identification card to the Council on
7 Law Enforcement Education and Training.
8 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
