9 A Concurrent Resolution honoring Chuck Norris, a

13 WHEREAS, Carlos Ray "Chuck" Norris was born on March 10, 1940,
14 in Ryan, Oklahoma, and rose from humble beginnings to achieve
15 international recognition; and
16 WHEREAS, Chuck Norris served honorably in the United States Air
17 Force, demonstrating early his commitment to service and discipline;
18 and
19 WHEREAS, he became a world-renowned martial artist, earning
20 multiple championships and helping to popularize martial arts in the
21 United States; and

23 in numerous films and television series, including Walker, Texas
24 Ranger, becoming a cultural icon; and

13 THAT the Legislature commends his contributions to the arts,
