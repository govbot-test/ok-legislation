10 WHEREAS, Carlos Ray "Chuck" Norris was born on March 10, 1940,
11 in Ryan, Oklahoma, and rose from humble beginnings to achieve
12 international recognition; and
13 WHEREAS, Chuck Norris served honorably in the United States Air
14 Force, demonstrating early his commitment to service and discipline;
15 and
16 WHEREAS, he became a world-renowned martial artist, earning
17 multiple championships and helping to popularize martial arts in the
18 United States; and
19 WHEREAS, Chuck Norris achieved global fame as an actor, starring
20 in numerous films and television series, including Walker, Texas
21 Ranger, becoming a cultural icon; and
22 WHEREAS, beyond his entertainment career, he has dedicated
23 significant efforts to philanthropy, including the founding of
24 programs that support youth development and character education; and

2 integrity, and service that reflect great credit upon the State of
3 Oklahoma.

5 OF THE 2ND SESSION OF THE 60TH OKLAHOMA LEGISLATURE, THE SENATE
6 CONCURRING THEREIN:
7 THAT the Oklahoma Legislature hereby honors Chuck Norris for his
8 lifetime of achievement and proudly recognizes him as a
9 distinguished native son of Ryan, Oklahoma.
10 THAT the Legislature commends his contributions to the arts,

12 positive example he set for citizens of Oklahoma and beyond.
