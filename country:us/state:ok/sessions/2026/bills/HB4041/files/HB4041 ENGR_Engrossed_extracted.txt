8 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Office of the
Attorney General; appropriating certain funds in
9 certain amount for certain purpose; directing
utilization of certain amounts for certain purposes;
10 providing for noncodification; providing an effective
date; and declaring an emergency.
11
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
16 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
17 There is hereby appropriated to the Office of the Attorney
18 General from any monies not otherwise appropriated from the General
19 Revenue Fund of the State Treasury for the fiscal year ending June
20 30, 2027, the sum of Two Million Two Hundred Fifty Thousand Dollars
21 ($2,250,000.00) or so much thereof as may be necessary to perform
22 the duties imposed upon the Office of the Attorney General by law.
23 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
24 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
ENGR. H. B. NO. 4041 Page 1
1 Funds appropriated by Section 1 of this act shall be utilized as
2 follows:
3 1. Two Million Dollars ($2,000,000.00) to implement the
4 provisions of Enrolled Senate Bill No. 1379 of the 2nd Session of
5 the 60th Oklahoma Legislature; and
6 2. Two Hundred Fifty Thousand Dollars ($250,000.00) to be
7 transferred to the Public Safety Technology Revolving Fund created
8 by Enrolled House Bill No. 1250 of the 2nd Session of the 60th
9 Oklahoma Legislature.
10 SECTION 3. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
11 SECTION 4. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
12 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
13 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
14 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
15 Passed the House of Representatives the 14th day of April, 2026.
