10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
11 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 70 O.S. 2021, Section 1210.704, as
12 amended by Section 20, Chapter 323, O.S.L. 2023 (70 O.S. Supp. 2025,
13 Section 1210.704), is amended to read as follows:
14 Section 1210.704. A. Beginning with the 2024–2025 school year,
15 all public high schools in this state shall make a minimum of four
16 advanced placement courses available to students.
17 B. School district boards of education shall be responsible for
18 ensuring annually that all high school students have access to
19 advanced placement courses beginning in the 2024-2025 school year.
20 Such access may be provided through enrollment in courses offered
21 through:
22 1. A school site or sites within the district;
23 2. A technology center school within the district;

4 C. Each public school district in this state shall make
5 advanced placement examinations available each school year to any

11 procedures to register for the examination.

13 learning platform to provide high quality online learning
14 opportunities for Oklahoma students that are aligned with the
15 subject matter standards adopted by the State Board of Education
16 pursuant to Section 11-103.6 of this title. The Board shall
17 implement online courses, with an emphasis on science, technology,
18 engineering, and math (STEM) courses, foreign language courses, and
19 advanced placement courses. The online platform shall be available
20 to all Oklahoma school districts students in this state.
21 D. E. The State Department of Education shall provide
22 information to all boards of education, to be distributed to their
23 students and parents, on available opportunities and the enrollment
24 process for students to take advanced placement courses. The
