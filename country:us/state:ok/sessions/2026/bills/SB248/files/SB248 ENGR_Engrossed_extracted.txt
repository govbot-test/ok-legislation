9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
10 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 74 O.S. 2021, Section 2251, is
11 amended to read as follows:
12 Section 2251. A. There is hereby created in the State Treasury
13 a revolving fund for the Oklahoma Tourism and Recreation Department
14 to be designated the “Oklahoma Tourism and Recreation Department
15 Revolving Fund”. The fund shall be a continuing fund, not subject
16 to fiscal year limitations, and shall consist of all monies received
17 by the Department pursuant to the provisions of this act, monies
18 derived from the sale or conveyance of real property under the
19 jurisdiction of the Commission, and interest attributable to
20 investment of money in the fund. The revolving fund shall be a
21 continuing fund not subject to fiscal year limitations and shall be
22 under the administrative direction of the Department. Expenditures
23 from the fund created by this section shall be for the
24 administration, operation, and maintenance expenses of the
ENGR. S. B. NO. 248 Page 1
1 Depa rtment, for purchase of real property, and shall be made
2 pursuant to the laws of this state and the statutes relating to the
3 Department. Expenditures from said fund shall be made upon warrants
4 issued by the State Treasurer against claims filed as prescribed by
5 law with the Director of the Office of Management and Enterprise
6 Services for approval and payment the Oklahoma Tourism, Parks and
7 Recreation Enhancement Act. All monies accruing to the credit of
8 the fund are hereby appropriated and may be budgeted and expended by
9 the Department for the administration, operation, and maintenance
10 expenses of the Department, for purchase of real property, and shall
11 be made pursuant to the laws of this state. Expenditures from the
12 fund shall be made upon warrants issued by the State Treasurer
13 against claims filed as prescribed by law with the Director of the
14 Office of Management and Enterprise Services for approval and
15 payment.
16 B. Notwithstanding any provision of law, all interest and
17 income derived from deposits to the Oklahoma Tourism and Recreation
18 Department Revolving Fund shall be credited to the Oklahoma Tourism
19 and Recreation Department Revolving Fund.
20 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
21 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 2251.1 of Title 74, unless there
22 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
23 A. There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving
24 fund for the Oklahoma Tourism and Recreation Department to be
ENGR. S. B. NO. 248 Page 2
1 desi gnated the “Real Property Proceeds Revolving Fund”. The fund
2 shall be a continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations,
3 and shall consist of all monies derived from the sale, transfer,
4 lease, or other conveyance of real property under the jurisdiction
5 of the Oklahoma Tourism and Recreation Commission, except those
6 leases entered into pursuant to a concessionaire agreement. All
7 monies accruing to the fund are hereby appropriated and may be
8 expended by the Department for maintenance and capital projects
9 included on the annually updated five-year plan approved by the
10 Long-Range Capital Planning Commission pursuant to Section 188A of
11 Title 73 of the Oklahoma Statutes; provided, such monies shall not
12 be expended for compensation of any employee of a state agency. No
13 monies from the fund shall be transferred for any purpose to any
14 other state agency. Expenditures from the fund shall be made upon
15 warrants issued by the State Treasurer against claims filed as
16 prescribed by law with the Director of the Office of Management and
17 Enterprise Services for approval and payment.
18 B. Notwithstanding any provision of law, all interest and
19 income derived from deposits to the Real Property Proceeds Revolving
20 Fund shall be credited to the Real Property Proceeds Revolving Fund.
21 SECTION 3. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
