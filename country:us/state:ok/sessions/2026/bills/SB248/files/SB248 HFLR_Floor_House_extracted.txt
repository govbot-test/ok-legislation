5 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
6 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 2251.1 of Title 74, unless there
7 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
8 A. There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving
9 fund for the Oklahoma Tourism and Recreation Department to be
10 designated the “Real Property Proceeds Revolving Fund”. The fund
11 shall be a continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations,
12 and shall consist of all monies derived from the sale, transfer,
13 lease, or other conveyance of real property under the jurisdiction
14 of the Oklahoma Tourism and Recreation Commission, except those
15 leases entered into pursuant to a concessionaire agreement. All
16 monies accruing to the fund are hereby appropriated and may be
17 expended by the Department for maintenance and capital projects
18 included on the annually updated five-year plan approved by the
19 Long-Range Capital Planning Commission pursuant to Section 188A of
20 Title 73 of the Oklahoma Statutes; provided, such monies shall not
21 be expended for compensation of any employee of a state agency. No
22 monies from the fund shall be transferred for any purpose to any
23 other state agency. Expenditures from the fund shall be made upon
24 warrants issued by the State Treasurer against claims filed as
