13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 74 O.S. 2021, Section 2251, is
15 amended to read as follows:
16 Section 2251. A. There is hereby created in the State Treasury
17 a revolving fund for the Oklahoma Tourism and Recreation Department
18 to be designated the “Oklahoma Tourism and Recreation Department
19 Revolving Fund”. The fund shall be a continuing fund, not subject
20 to fiscal year limitations, and shall consist of all monies received
21 by the Department pursuant to the provisions of this act, monies
22 derived from the sale or conveyance of real property under the
23 jurisdiction of the Commission, and interest attributable to
24 investment of money in the fund. The revolving fund shall be a
Req. No. 3619 Page 1
1 cont inuing fund not subject to fiscal year limitations and shall be
2 under the administrative direction of the Department. Expenditures
3 from the fund created by this section shall be for the
4 administration, operation, and maintenance expenses of the
5 Department, for purchase of real property, and shall be made
6 pursuant to the laws of this state and the statutes relating to the
7 Department. Expenditures from said fund shall be made upon warrants
8 issued by the State Treasurer against claims filed as prescribed by
9 law with the Director of the Office of Management and Enterprise
10 Services for approval and payment the Oklahoma Tourism, Parks and
11 Recreation Enhancement Act. All monies accruing to the credit of
12 the fund are hereby appropriated and may be budgeted and expended by
13 the Department for the administration, operation, and maintenance
14 expenses of the Department, for purchase of real property, and shall
15 be made pursuant to the laws of this state. Expenditures from the
16 fund shall be made upon warrants issued by the State Treasurer
17 against claims filed as prescribed by law with the Director of the
18 Office of Management and Enterprise Services for approval and
19 payment.
20 B. Notwithstanding any provision of law, all interest and
21 income derived from deposits to the Oklahoma Tourism and Recreation
22 Department Revolving Fund shall be credited to the Oklahoma Tourism
23 and Recreation Department Revolving Fund.
24
Req. No. 3619 Page 2
1 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
2 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 2251.1 of Title 74, unless there
3 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
4 A. There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving
5 fund for the Oklahoma Tourism and Recreation Department to be
6 designated the “Real Property Proceeds Revolving Fund”. The fund
7 shall be a continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations,
8 and shall consist of all monies derived from the sale, transfer,
9 lease, or other conveyance of real property under the jurisdiction
10 of the Oklahoma Tourism and Recreation Commission, except those
11 leases entered into pursuant to a concessionaire agreement. All
12 monies accruing to the fund are hereby appropriated and may be
13 expended by the Department for maintenance and capital projects
14 included on the annually updated five-year plan approved by the
15 Long-Range Capital Planning Commission pursuant to Section 188A of
16 Title 73 of the Oklahoma Statutes; provided, such monies shall not
17 be expended for compensation of any employee of a state agency. No
18 monies from the fund shall be transferred for any purpose to any
19 other state agency. Expenditures from the fund shall be made upon
20 warrants issued by the State Treasurer against claims filed as
21 prescribed by law with the Director of the Office of Management and
22 Enterprise Services for approval and payment.
23
24
Req. No. 3619 Page 3
1 B. Notwithstanding any provision of law, all interest and
2 income derived from deposits to the Real Property Proceeds Revolving
3 Fund shall be credited to the Real Property Proceeds Revolving Fund.
4 SECTION 3. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
