I move to amend Senate Bill No. 1926 on Page 5, Line 16 ½ by inserting a new subsection “H.” to
read as follows and amending the title to conform:
“H. Notice shall be given by a sheriff or deputy sheriff. A plaintiff may elect to contract a person
licensed to make service of process if relief is sought outside of the county in which a plaintiff or
defendant resides or the county in which the alleged event occurred pursuant to paragraph 1 of
subsection A of this section. Any costs associated with relief sought under this subsection shall be
paid by the requesting party.”
