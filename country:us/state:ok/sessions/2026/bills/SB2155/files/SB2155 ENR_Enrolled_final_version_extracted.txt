ENROLLED SENATE
BILL NO. 2155 By: Thompson of the Senate
and
Lawson and Hill of the
House
An Act relating to the Oklahoma Route 66 Commission;
amending Section 2, Chapter 412, O.S.L. 2022, as
amended by Section 2, Chapter 80, O.S.L. 2025 (69
O.S. Supp. 2025, Section 4041), which relates to the
Oklahoma Route 66 Commission Revolving Fund; granting
certain power; updating statutory language; updating
statutory reference; and providing an effective date.
SUBJECT: Oklahoma Route 66 Commission
BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. AMENDATORY Section 2, Chapter 412, O.S.L.
2022, as amended by Section 2, Chapter 80, O.S.L. 2025 (69 O.S.
Supp. 2025, Section 4041), is amended to read as follows:

Section 3:
Section 4041. A. There is hereby created in the State Treasury
a revolving fund to be designated as the “Oklahoma Route 66
Commission Revolving Fund”, which shall consist of all monies
appropriated, transferred, or otherwise designated to the fund by
law. The revolving fund shall be a continuing fund, not subject to
fiscal year limitations, and shall be under the administration of
the Oklahoma Route 66 Commission. All monies accruing to the credit
of said the fund are hereby appropriated, and shall be budgeted and
expended in accordance with the provisions of subsection B of this

Section 4:
section for the purpose of preserving and developing Route 66 and
commemorating the Route 66 Centennial.
B. Funds shall be budgeted or expended from the Oklahoma Route
66 Commission Revolving Fund in accordance with rules adopted by the
Commission.
C. The Oklahoma Route 66 Commission shall promulgate, pursuant
to the provisions of the Administrative Procedures Act, permanent
rules subject to approval by the Legislature, to implement:

Section 5:
1. Application procedures for projects submitting requests for
funds;

Section 6:
2. Processes for awarding of funding applications;

Section 7:
3. Details of the Commission’s general duties;

Section 8:
4. Reporting requirements of the Commission to the Legislature;
and

Section 9:
5. Discretionary powers to execute memorandums of understanding
with state agencies for administrative services; and

Section 10:
6. Any other rules necessary to carry out the purpose and
mission of the Commission as expressed in this act section, Section
4040 of this title, and Section 1353 of Title 68 of the Oklahoma
Statutes.
D. All unencumbered funds remaining in the Oklahoma Route 66
Commission Revolving Fund upon the expiration of the Oklahoma Route
66 Commission shall be transferred to the State Treasurer for
deposit in the General Revenue Fund.

Section 11:
SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.

An Act
ENROLLED SENATE
BILL NO. 2155 By: Thompson of the Senate
and
Lawson and Hill of the
House
An Act relating to the Oklahoma Route 66 Commission;
amending Section 2, Chapter 412, O.S.L. 2022, as
amended by Section 2, Chapter 80, O.S.L. 2025 (69
O.S. Supp. 2025, Section 4041), which relates to the
Oklahoma Route 66 Commission Revolving Fund; granting
certain power; updating statutory language; updating
statutory reference; and providing an effective date.
SUBJECT: Oklahoma Route 66 Commission
BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
SECTION 1. AMENDATORY Section 2, Chapter 412, O.S.L.
2022, as amended by Section 2, Chapter 80, O.S.L. 2025 (69 O.S.
Supp. 2025, Section 4041), is amended to read as follows:
Section 4041. A. There is hereby created in the State Treasury
a revolving fund to be designated as the “Oklahoma Route 66
Commission Revolving Fund”, which shall consist of all monies
appropriated, transferred, or otherwise designated to the fund by
law. The revolving fund shall be a continuing fund, not subject to
fiscal year limitations, and shall be under the administration of
the Oklahoma Route 66 Commission. All monies accruing to the credit
of said the fund are hereby appropriated, and shall be budgeted and
expended in accordance with the provisions of subsection B of this
section for the purpose of preserving and developing Route 66 and
commemorating the Route 66 Centennial.

B. Funds shall be budgeted or expended from the Oklahoma Route
66 Commission Revolving Fund in accordance with rules adopted by the
Commission.
C. The Oklahoma Route 66 Commission shall promulgate, pursuant
to the provisions of the Administrative Procedures Act, permanent
rules subject to approval by the Legislature, to implement:
1. Application procedures for projects submitting requests for
funds;
2. Processes for awarding of funding applications;
3. Details of the Commission’s general duties;
4. Reporting requirements of the Commission to the Legislature;
and
5. Discretionary powers to execute memorandums of understanding
with state agencies for administrative services; and
6. Any other rules necessary to carry out the purpose and
mission of the Commission as expressed in this act section, Section
4040 of this title, and Section 1353 of Title 68 of the Oklahoma
Statutes.
D. All unencumbered funds remaining in the Oklahoma Route 66
Commission Revolving Fund upon the expiration of the Oklahoma Route
66 Commission shall be transferred to the State Treasurer for
deposit in the General Revenue Fund.
SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
