11 statutory reference; and providing an effective date.

14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY Section 2, Chapter 412, O.S.L.
16 2022, as amended by Section 2, Chapter 80, O.S.L. 2025 (69 O.S.
17 Supp. 2025, Section 4041), is amended to read as follows:
18 Section 4041. A. There is hereby created in the State Treasury
19 a revolving fund to be designated as the “Oklahoma Route 66
20 Commission Revolving Fund”, which shall consist of all monies
21 appropriated, transferred, or otherwise designated to the fund by
22 law. The revolving fund shall be a continuing fund, not subject to
23 fiscal year limitations, and shall be under the administration of
24 the Oklahoma Route 66 Commission. All monies accruing to the credit

1 of s aid the fund are hereby appropriated, and shall be budgeted and
2 expended in accordance with the provisions of subsection B of this
3 section for the purpose of preserving and developing Route 66 and
4 commemorating the Route 66 Centennial.
5 B. Funds shall be budgeted or expended from the Oklahoma Route
6 66 Commission Revolving Fund in accordance with rules adopted by the
7 Commission.
8 C. The Oklahoma Route 66 Commission shall promulgate, pursuant
9 to the provisions of the Administrative Procedures Act, permanent
10 rules subject to approval by the Legislature, to implement:
11 1. Application procedures for projects submitting requests for

14 3. Details of the Commission’s general duties;
15 4. Reporting requirements of the Commission to the Legislature;

19 6. Any other rules necessary to carry out the purpose and

21 4040 of this title, and Section 1353 of Title 68 of the Oklahoma
22 Statutes.

24 Commission Revolving Fund upon the expiration of the Oklahoma Route
