10 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Route 66 Commission;
amending Section 2, Chapter 412, O.S.L. 2022, as
11 amended by Section 2, Chapter 80, O.S.L. 2025 (69
O.S. Supp. 2025, Section 4041), which relates to the
12 Oklahoma Route 66 Commission Revolving Fund; granting
certain power; updating statutory language; updating
13 statutory reference; and providing an effective date.
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY Section 2, Chapter 412, O.S.L.
18 2022, as amended by Section 2, Chapter 80, O.S.L. 2025 (69 O.S.
19 Supp. 2025, Section 4041), is amended to read as follows:
20 Section 4041. A. There is hereby created in the State Treasury
21 a revolving fund to be designated as the “Oklahoma Route 66
22 Commission Revolving Fund”, which shall consist of all monies
23 appropriated, transferred, or otherwise designated to the fund by
24 law. The revolving fund shall be a continuing fund, not subject to
SB2155 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 fisc al year limitations, and shall be under the administration of
2 the Oklahoma Route 66 Commission. All monies accruing to the credit
3 of said the fund are hereby appropriated, and shall be budgeted and
4 expended in accordance with the provisions of subsection B of this
5 section for the purpose of preserving and developing Route 66 and
6 commemorating the Route 66 Centennial.
7 B. Funds shall be budgeted or expended from the Oklahoma Route
8 66 Commission Revolving Fund in accordance with rules adopted by the
9 Commission.
10 C. The Oklahoma Route 66 Commission shall promulgate, pursuant
11 to the provisions of the Administrative Procedures Act, permanent
12 rules subject to approval by the Legislature, to implement:
13 1. Application procedures for projects submitting requests for
14 funds;
15 2. Processes for awarding of funding applications;
16 3. Details of the Commission’s general duties;
17 4. Reporting requirements of the Commission to the Legislature;
18 and
19 5. Discretionary powers to execute memorandums of understanding
20 with state agencies for administrative services; and
21 6. Any other rules necessary to carry out the purpose and
22 mission of the Commission as expressed in this act section, Section
23 4040 of this title, and Section 1353 of Title 68 of the Oklahoma
24 Statutes.

BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 D. All unencumbered funds remaining in the Oklahoma Route 66
2 Commission Revolving Fund upon the expiration of the Oklahoma Route

5 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
