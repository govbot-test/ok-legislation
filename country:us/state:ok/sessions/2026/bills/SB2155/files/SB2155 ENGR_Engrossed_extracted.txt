13 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY Section 2, Chapter 412, O.S.L.
14 2022, as amended by Section 2, Chapter 80, O.S.L. 2025 (69 O.S.
15 Supp. 2025, Section 4041), is amended to read as follows:
16 Section 4041. A. There is hereby created in the State Treasury
17 a revolving fund to be designated as the “Oklahoma Route 66
18 Commission Revolving Fund”, which shall consist of all monies
19 appropriated, transferred, or otherwise designated to the fund by
20 law. The revolving fund shall be a continuing fund, not subject to
21 fiscal year limitations, and shall be under the administration of
22 the Oklahoma Route 66 Commission. All monies accruing to the credit
23 of said the fund are hereby appropriated, and shall be budgeted and
24 expended in accordance with the provisions of subsection B of this

1 sect ion for the purpose of preserving and developing Route 66 and
2 commemorating the Route 66 Centennial.
3 B. Funds shall be budgeted or expended from the Oklahoma Route
4 66 Commission Revolving Fund in accordance with rules adopted by the
5 Commission.

7 to the provisions of the Administrative Procedures Act, permanent
8 rules subject to approval by the Legislature, to implement:
9 1. Application procedures for projects submitting requests for

11 2. Processes for awarding of funding applications;

13 4. Reporting requirements of the Commission to the Legislature;
14 and
15 5. Discretionary powers to execute memorandums of understanding
16 with state agencies for administrative services; and
17 6. Any other rules necessary to carry out the purpose and
18 mission of the Commission as expressed in this act section, Section
19 4040 of this title, and Section 1353 of Title 68 of the Oklahoma
20 Statutes.
21 D. All unencumbered funds remaining in the Oklahoma Route 66
22 Commission Revolving Fund upon the expiration of the Oklahoma Route
23 66 Commission shall be transferred to the State Treasurer for
24 deposit in the General Revenue Fund.
