Req. No. 17255
1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 2nd Session of the 60th Legislature (2026)
3 HOUSE CONCURRENT
RESOLUTION 1022 By: Dobrinski
4
5
6
7 AS INTRODUCED
8 A Concurrent Resolution recognizing National Lineman
Appreciation Day; praising linemen from Oklahoma
9 electric providers; and celebrating their work to
power homes, businesses, and localities across the
10 state.
11
12 WHEREAS, Oklahoma linemen are the true heroes of the electric
13 power industry because they are responsible for maintaining and
14 repairing electrical power grids 24 hours a day, 365 days a year;
15 and
16 WHEREAS, these dedicated professionals are often the first to
17 respond during storms and other catastrophic weather events so other
18 first responders can get to the scene safely; and
19 WHEREAS, linemen operate under dangerous conditions, risking
20 their lives high atop power lines to keep electricity flowing; and
21 WHEREAS, linemen also play a vital role in Oklahoma's economy by
22 maintaining the energy infrastructure that powers homes, businesses,
23 and localities across the state; and
24 WHEREAS, linemen deserve recognition for their hard work to
Req. No. 17255 Page 1
1 powe r local communities and keep people safe during emergencies.
2 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
3 OF THE 2ND SESSION OF THE 60TH OKLAHOMA LEGISLATURE, THE SENATE
4 CONCURRING THEREIN:
5 THAT the Oklahoma Legislature recognizes April 21, 2026, as
6 National Lineman Appreciation Day.
7 THAT the Oklahoma Legislature extends its sincere gratitude to
8 linemen from Public Service Company of Oklahoma, Oklahoma Gas and
9 Electric Company, Oklahoma Municipal Power Authority, the Grand
10 River Dam Authority, local electric cooperatives, Liberty Utilities,
11 and all other electric providers for their dedicated service and
12 swift response to restore power during emergency situations.
13 THAT the Oklahoma Legislature commends local linemen for their
14 vital role in powering homes, businesses, and localities across the
15 state.
16 THAT the Oklahoma Legislature honors linemen for their critical
17 service in all types of weather, recognizing them as essential first
18 responders who risk their lives to ensure the safety and well-being
19 of all communities.
