9 A Concurrent Resolution recognizing National Lineman
Appreciation Day; praising linemen from Oklahoma
10 electric providers; and celebrating their work to
power homes, businesses, and localities across the
11 state.
12
13 WHEREAS, Oklahoma linemen are the true heroes of the electric
14 power industry because they are responsible for maintaining and
15 repairing electrical power grids 24 hours a day, 365 days a year;
16 and
17 WHEREAS, these dedicated professionals are often the first to
18 respond during storms and other catastrophic weather events so other
19 first responders can get to the scene safely; and
20 WHEREAS, linemen operate under dangerous conditions, risking
21 their lives high atop power lines to keep electricity flowing; and
22 WHEREAS, linemen also play a vital role in Oklahoma's economy by
23 maintaining the energy infrastructure that powers homes, businesses,
24 and localities across the state; and
ENGR. H. C. R. NO. 1022 Page 1

1 WHEREAS, linemen deserve recognition for their hard work to
2 power local communities and keep people safe during emergencies.
3 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
4 OF THE 2ND SESSION OF THE 60TH OKLAHOMA LEGISLATURE, THE SENATE
5 CONCURRING THEREIN:
6 THAT the Oklahoma Legislature recognizes April 21, 2026, as
7 National Lineman Appreciation Day.
8 THAT the Oklahoma Legislature extends its sincere gratitude to
9 linemen from Public Service Company of Oklahoma, Oklahoma Gas and
10 Electric Company, Oklahoma Municipal Power Authority, the Grand
11 River Dam Authority, local electric cooperatives, Liberty Utilities,
12 and all other electric providers for their dedicated service and
13 swift response to restore power during emergency situations.
14 THAT the Oklahoma Legislature commends local linemen for their
15 vital role in powering homes, businesses, and localities across the
16 state.
17 THAT the Oklahoma Legislature honors linemen for their critical
18 service in all types of weather, recognizing them as essential first
19 responders who risk their lives to ensure the safety and well-being
20 of all communities.
