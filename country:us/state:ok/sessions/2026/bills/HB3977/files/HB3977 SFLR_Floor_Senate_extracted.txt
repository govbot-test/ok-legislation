14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 2 O.S. 2021, Section 6-1, is
16 amended to read as follows:

18 1. Hold a current license to practice veterinary medicine in

22 3. Be appointed by the State Board of Agriculture.
