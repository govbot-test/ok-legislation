7 FLOOR SUBSTITUTE
8 An Act relating to criminal procedure; amending 22
O.S. 2021, Section 1053, as amended by Section 2,
9 Chapter 209, O.S.L. 2022 (22 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section
1053), which relates to appeals taken by the state or
10 municipality; providing clarification for certain
cases taken up on appeal; and providing an effective
11 date.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 22 O.S. 2021, Section 1053, as
16 amended by Section 2, Chapter 209, O.S.L. 2022 (22 O.S. Supp. 2025,
17 Section 1053), is amended to read as follows:
18 Section 1053. Appeals to the Court of Criminal Appeals may be
19 taken by the state or a municipality in the following cases only:
20 1. Upon judgment for the defendant on quashing or setting aside
21 an indictment or information;
22 2. Upon an order of the court arresting the judgment;
23 3. Upon a question reserved by the state or a municipality;
24
Req. No. 16938 Page 1
1 4. Upon judgment for the defendant on a motion to quash for
2 insufficient evidence in a felony matter;
3 5. Upon a final pretrial order, decision, or judgment:
4 a. suppressing evidence on constitutional grounds, or
5 b. excluding evidence on any other grounds,
6 where appellate review of the issue would be in the best interests
7 of justice;
8 6. Upon a final pretrial order, decision or judgment:
9 a. suppressing evidence on constitutional grounds, or
10 b. excluding evidence on any other grounds,
11 in cases alleging a violation of any of the provisions of Section
12 13.1 of Title 21 of the Oklahoma Statutes; and
13 7. Upon an order, decision or judgment finding that a defendant
14 is immune from or not subject to criminal prosecution.
15 Priority shall be given to appeals taken pursuant to paragraph
16 5, 6, or 7 of this section, and an order staying proceedings shall
17 be entered pending the outcome of the appeal.
18 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
