16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
18 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
19 There is hereby appropriated to the Supreme Court from any
20 monies not otherwise appropriated from the General Revenue Fund of
21 the State Treasury for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2027, the sum
22 of One Hundred Forty-two Thousand One Hundred Thirty-seven Dollars
23 ($142,137.00) or so much thereof as may be necessary to perform the
24 duties imposed upon the district courts by law.
