12 An Act relating to Supreme Court; appropriating
certain funds in certain amount for certain purpose;
13 directing utilization of certain amounts for certain
purposes; providing for noncodification; providing an
14 effective date; and declaring an emergency.
15
16
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
18 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
19 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
20 There is hereby appropriated to the Supreme Court from any
21 monies not otherwise appropriated from the General Revenue Fund of
22 the State Treasury for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2027, the sum
23 of One Hundred Forty-two Thousand One Hundred Thirty-seven Dollars
24
HB4034 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 ($14 2,137.00) or so much thereof as may be necessary to perform the
2 duties imposed upon the district courts by law.
3 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
4 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
5 Funds appropriated by Section 1 of this act shall be utilized as
6 follows: One Hundred Forty-two Thousand One Hundred Thirty-seven
7 Dollars ($142,137.00) to fund salary increases to certified
8 shorthand reporters.
9 SECTION 3. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
10 SECTION 4. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
11 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
12 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
13 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
14
15 COMMITTEE REPORT BY: COMMITTEE ON JOINT COMMITTEE ON APPROPRIATIONS
AND BUDGET, dated 04/13/2026 - DO PASS, As Amended and Coauthored.
