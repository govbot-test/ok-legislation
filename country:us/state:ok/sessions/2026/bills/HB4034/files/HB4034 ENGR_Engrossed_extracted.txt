8 An Act relating to Supreme Court; appropriating
certain funds in certain amount for certain purpose;
9 directing utilization of certain amounts for certain
purposes; providing for noncodification; providing an
10 effective date; and declaring an emergency.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
15 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
16 There is hereby appropriated to the Supreme Court from any
17 monies not otherwise appropriated from the General Revenue Fund of
18 the State Treasury for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2027, the sum
19 of One Hundred Forty-two Thousand One Hundred Thirty-seven Dollars
20 ($142,137.00) or so much thereof as may be necessary to perform the
21 duties imposed upon the district courts by law.
22 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
23 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
24
ENGR. H. B. NO. 4034 Page 1
1 Funds appropriated by Section 1 of this act shall be utilized as
2 follows: One Hundred Forty-two Thousand One Hundred Thirty-seven
3 Dollars ($142,137.00) to fund salary increases to certified
4 shorthand reporters.
5 SECTION 3. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
6 SECTION 4. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
7 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
8 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
9 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
10 Passed the House of Representatives the 14th day of April, 2026.
