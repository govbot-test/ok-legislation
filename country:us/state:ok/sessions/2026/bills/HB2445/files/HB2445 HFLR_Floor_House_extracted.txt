1 SECTION 2. AMENDATORY 69 O.S. 2021, Section 4020.1, is
2 amended to read as follows:
3 Section 4020.1. Route 66 is one of the original highways within
4 the U.S. Highway System. Officially, the numerical designation 66
5 was assigned to the Chicago-to-Los Angeles route on November 11,
6 1926. From the outset, public road planners intended U.S. 66 to
7 connect the main streets of rural and urban communities along its
8 course for the most practical of reasons; most small towns had no
9 prior access to a major national thoroughfare. This was certainly
10 true in Oklahoma. Oklahoma's stretch of Route 66 runs from Miami
11 through Tulsa and Oklahoma City to Erick, passing through cities and
12 towns of all sizes and vast expanses of rural landscape, before
13 entering Texas and continuing on its way to Santa Monica,
14 California. The Oklahoma Route 66 Centennial Forward Commission is
15 established to plan, coordinate and implement programs and special
16 events to celebrate the historic highway's centennial in 2026 with
17 the support of the Oklahoma Historical Society.
18 The centennial is an opportunity to celebrate the important
19 history of Route 66 in Oklahoma through commemorative, educational
20 and community events, including programs and exhibits about the
21 history of how local communities grew and changed with construction
22 of the highway; the development of a modern transportation system;
23 the cultural impact of Route 66 both within the United States and
24 internationally; the portrayal of Route 66 in music, artwork and

BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 folk lore; and how we maintain the mystique and appeal of Route 66
2 for future generations.
3 The study, preservation and maintenance of Route-66-related
4 resources has been a public/private partnership in Oklahoma for
5 decades. Government agencies, private organizations and individual
6 citizens have played critical roles in these efforts, and the
7 Oklahoma Route 66 Centennial Forward Commission shall seek input
8 from these partners and the general public as it develops plans for
9 the celebration and encourages their participation.
10 The significance of the Oklahoma stretch of Route 66 is
11 demonstrated through its status as both a state and a National
12 Scenic Byway and as a segment of the Route 66 National Historic
13 Trail (legislation for NHT designation is pending in the U.S.
14 Congress). Dozens of Oklahoma properties are related to Route 66,
15 such as roadbed segments, bridges, commercial buildings, service
16 stations and tourist attractions, and are listed in the National
17 Register of Historic Places. The significance of the highway is
18 further recognized through the National Trust for Historic
19 Preservation's selection of the entire length of the highway as one
20 of its National Treasures.
