13

15 "An Act relating to state government; creating the
Olympics in Oklahoma Revolving Fund; establishing the
16 revolving fund characteristics; declaring the funds
appropriated; providing for codification; providing
17 an effective date; and declaring an emergency.
18
19
20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
21 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
22 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 5013.4 of Title 74, unless there
23 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
24
ENGR. H. A. to ENGR. S. B. NO. 1378 Page 1
1 A. There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving
2 fund for the Oklahoma Department of Commerce to be designated the
3 “Olympics in Oklahoma Revolving Fund”. The fund shall be a
4 continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations, and shall
5 consist of all monies received by the Department from
6 appropriations, donations, grants, and other sources of funding for
7 the purpose of hosting the 2028 Olympic Games. Expenditures from
8 the fund shall be made upon warrants issued by the State Treasurer
9 against claims filed as prescribed by law with the Director of the
10 Office of Management and Enterprise Services for approval and
11 payment.
12 B. Subject to approval by the Oklahoma Department of Commerce,
13 no less than five percent (5%) of all funds dispensed from the fund
14 shall be awarded to contracts fulfilled by Oklahoma-based
15 businesses.
16 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
17 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
18 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
19 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
20 be in full force from and after its passage and approval."
