6 An Act relating to school employees; amending 70 O.S.
2021, Section 509.11, which relates to equal access
7 to employees of a school district; modifying what
constitutes access regarding statewide professional
8 educators' associations; prohibiting equal access
from being conditional on certain provisions or
9 services; directing certain communications to be
classified as promotional events; permitting
10 distribution of certain materials in school
mailboxes; prohibiting a school district from
11 requiring a fee or other payment from statewide
professional educators' associations for certain
12 activities; requiring certain notification if certain
equal access is denied; requiring notification of the
13 State Board of Education if equal access is not
granted within certain time period; updating
14 statutory language; allowing school district
employees to terminate membership in certain
15 organization at any time; prohibiting an organization
from taking certain actions; prohibiting certain
16 policy or agreement from placing certain conditions
or requirements on certain initiation or termination;
17 providing certain construction; and providing for
codification.

8 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
9 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 509.13 of Title 70, unless there
10 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
11 A. A school district employee who is a member of an employee
12 organization designated pursuant to Section 509.2 of Title 70 of the
13 Oklahoma Statutes may terminate membership in the employee
14 organization at any time. Upon receipt of a school district
15 employee's termination of membership via mail or electronic mail,
16 the employee organization shall terminate the employee's membership
17 and cease collection of dues.
18 B. An employee organization designated pursuant to Section
19 509.2 of Title 70 of the Oklahoma Statutes shall be prohibited from:
20 1. Limiting a school district employee's right to terminate his
21 or her membership only during certain dates or by using specific
22 forms; and
23 2. Requiring a school district employee to disclose the reason
24 for his or her membership termination.
