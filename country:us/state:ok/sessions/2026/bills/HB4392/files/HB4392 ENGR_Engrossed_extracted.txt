3 SECTION 3. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
4 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 602 of Title 3, unless there is
5 created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
6 A. The Oklahoma Department of Aerospace and Aeronautics shall
7 designate not less than five sites as Advanced Air Mobility (AAM)
8 pilot sites within the state. Such sites shall be eligible for AAM-
9 specific support and regulatory assistance; provided, the site meets
10 program guidance as issued by the Department. Of the designated
11 sites, not less than two sites shall be designated as supporting
12 airports and demonstrate a commitment to publicly operated AAM
13 infrastructure, the public benefit of such technology, and a pathway
14 to financial stability.
15 B. The Department may approve and provide funding opportunities
16 at approved sites for infrastructure, equipment, software, or other
17 needs the site may have to work with AAM companies, operators, and
18 supportive industries. The Department shall not approve funding for
19 research and development opportunities nor the purchase of
20 nontraditional aircraft or small uncrewed aerial systems (SUAS).
21 The Department shall retain ownership of any asset it invests in.
22 Sites shall be required to sign agreements acknowledging that the
23 Department retains the ownership of the asset. The Department may
24 enter into agreements with the site or approved operators to recoup

1 Prog ram (SEA SIP) Revolving Fund". The fund shall be a continuing
2 fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations, and shall consist of
3 all monies received by the Department as directed by law, from
4 federal and state grants or appropriations, from private donations,
5 and from revenues or fees recouped from assets in which the
6 Department invests. All monies accruing to the credit of said fund
7 are hereby appropriated and may be utilized in accordance with the
8 provisions of the Sustainable Emerging Aviation Services Investment
9 Program. Expenditures from said fund shall be made upon warrants
10 issued by the State Treasurer against claims filed as prescribed by
11 law with the Director of the Office of Management and Enterprise
12 Services for approval and payment.
