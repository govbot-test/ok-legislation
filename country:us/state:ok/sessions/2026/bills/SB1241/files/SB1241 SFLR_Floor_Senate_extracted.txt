4 SECTION 4. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
5 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 774.2 of Title 15, unless there
6 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
7 A. A primary ticket seller, secondary ticket exchange, or
8 reseller shall clearly and conspicuously disclose at the point of
9 selection:
10 1. The total price, including all mandatory fees and taxes;
11 2. Itemization of base price, face value, service fees, and
12 taxes; and
13 3. Seat location or section, row, and seat number, if
14 applicable.
15 B. 1. No reseller shall offer or sell a speculative ticket or
16 more than one copy of the same ticket for an event.
17 2. Admission tickets shall not be resold prior to the initial
18 public sale. Complimentary admission tickets shall not be resold by
19 ticket exchanges.
20 3. Artists, venues, and private ticketing providers may
21 restrict transferability if terms and conditions are clearly
22 disclosed and acknowledged by the purchaser prior to sale.
23 4. Tickets shall be considered licenses. Venue operators may
24 enforce policies regarding conduct, behavior, public health, safety,

1 or a ge, and may establish limits on the quantity of tickets
2 purchased.
3 C. 1. A primary ticket seller, secondary ticket exchange, or
4 reseller shall issue a full refund within ten (10) days if the:
5 a. event is canceled,
6 b. ticket is counterfeit or invalid,
7 c. ticket location does not conform to the description
