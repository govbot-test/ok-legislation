7 An Act relating to school employees; amending Section
1, Chapter 291, O.S.L. 2023, as amended by Section 3,
8 Chapter 57, O.S.L. 2025 (70 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section
6-104.8), which relates to paid maternity leave;
9 expanding eligibility of paid maternity leave to
employment at any school district or technology
10 center school district in this state for at least a
year; providing an effective date; and declaring an
11 emergency.
