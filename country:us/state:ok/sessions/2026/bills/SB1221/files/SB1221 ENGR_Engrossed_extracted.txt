6 An Act relating to driver licenses; requiring Service
Oklahoma to enact certain license tracking system by
7 or before certain date; requiring Service Oklahoma to
enact certain expedited delivery of credentials by or
8 before certain date; providing for codification;
providing an effective date; and declaring an
9 emergency.

12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 6-101.6 of Title 47, unless
15 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 A. By or before January 1, 2027, Service Oklahoma shall enact a
17 system that enables an individual to track the mailing status of a
18 driver license or identification card issued to such individual by
19 Service Oklahoma. Such system shall consist of an update via
20 electronic communication sent directly to an individual about a
21 change in the mailing status of a driver license or identification
22 card or a web portal where an individual may register to view the
23 current mailing status of a driver license.
24 B. By or before January 1, 2027, Service Oklahoma shall enact a

1 syst em that enables an applicant for a credential to be issued by
2 Service Oklahoma, including REAL ID Compliant and REAL ID
3 Noncompliant driver licenses and identification cards, to request

8 delivery fee shall be deposited to the credit of the Service
9 Oklahoma Revolving Fund.
10 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
11 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
