14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 68 O.S. 2021, Section 3634, is
16 amended to read as follows:
17 Section 3634. A. The total amount of rebate payments
18 conditionally pre-qualified by the Department of Commerce each
19 fiscal year with respect to any of the incentives authorized
20 pursuant to this act shall not exceed Thirty Million Dollars
21 ($30,000,000.00). Projects not conditionally pre-qualified for
22 rebate payments due to the limitations provided in this section may
23 be conditionally pre-qualified by the Department in subsequent
24 fiscal years.
