1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 2nd Session of the 60th Legislature (2026)
3 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
FOR
5
6
7 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
8 An Act relating to revenue and taxation; amending 68
O.S. 2021, Section 3634, which relates to the Filmed
9 in Oklahoma Act of 2021; modifying provisions related
to expenditure requirements for incentive
10 eligibility; and providing an effective date.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 68 O.S. 2021, Section 3634, is
15 amended to read as follows:
16 Section 3634. A. The total amount of rebate payments
17 conditionally pre-qualified by the Department of Commerce each
18 fiscal year with respect to any of the incentives authorized
19 pursuant to this act shall not exceed Thirty Million Dollars
20 ($30,000,000.00). Projects not conditionally pre-qualified for
21 rebate payments due to the limitations provided in this section may
22 be conditionally pre-qualified by the Department in subsequent
23 fiscal years.
24
Req. No. 17023 Page 1
1 B. The amount of rebate payments conditionally pre-qualified by
2 the Department of Commerce each fiscal year with respect to any of
3 the incentives authorized pursuant to this act shall not exceed:
4 1. Seven Million Five Hundred Thousand Dollars ($7,500,000.00)
5 for production projects with total expenditures of less than Seven
6 Million Five Hundred Thousand Dollars ($7,500,000.00); and
7 2. Twenty-two Million Five Hundred Thousand Dollars
8 ($22,500,000.00) for production projects with total expenditures of
9 Seven Million Five Hundred Thousand Dollars ($7,500,000.00) or more.
10 C. A production project shall have total expenditures of Fifty
11 Thousand Dollars ($50,000.00) or more to be eligible for the rebate
12 payments provided in this act or Twenty Thousand Dollars
13 ($20,000.00) for projects filmed outside this state as provided for
14 in Section 3636 of this title.
15 D. In any fiscal year if the amount of rebate payments
16 conditionally pre-qualified by the Department of Commerce are less
17 than the limitations provided in this section, the excess rebate
18 payments not utilized shall be added to subsequent fiscal year's
19 limitation.
20 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
