1 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
2 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1-630 of Title 63, unless there
3 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
4 Each hospital with an emergency department and each ambulatory
5 surgical center shall:
6 1. Develop and implement policies and procedures for the
7 rendering of appropriate medical attention for persons at risk of
8 forming venous thromboembolisms which reflect evidence-based best
9 practices relating to, at a minimum:
10 a. assessing patients for risk of venous thromboembolism
11 using a nationally recognized risk assessment tool,
12 and
13 b. treatment options for a patient diagnosed with venous
14 thromboembolism; and
15 2. Train all nonphysician personnel at least annually on the
16 policies and procedures developed under this section. For purposes
17 of this section, the term "nonphysician personnel" means all
18 personnel of the licensed facility working in clinical areas and
19 providing patient care, except those persons licensed as health care
20 practitioners.
