1 Educ ation. Expenditures from the Teacher Empowerment Revolving Fund

3 this section.

5 for the program:
6 1. To support public school districts that elect to participate
7 in assigning advanced, lead, and master teacher certificates as
8 outlined in Section 6-190 of this title. Funding shall be allocated
9 to school districts to provide the stipend amounts indicated for
10 advanced, lead, and master certificates as provided for in
11 subsection I of Section 6-190 of this title on a first-come, first-
12 served basis until the cash is exhausted for the school year; and
13 2. To support the compensation of teachers certified in special
14 education as provided for in Section 13-110 of this title, subject
15 to the stipend amounts required by paragraph 1 of this subsection
16 being fully funded, until the cash is exhausted for the school year.
17 SECTION 2. AMENDATORY 70 O.S. 2021, Section 13-110, is
18 amended to read as follows:
19 Section 13-110. A. Qualified and properly certified teachers
20 of special education shall be paid a minimum of five percent (5%)
21 ten percent (10%) above the prevailing wage paid teachers of
22 children who are nondisabled do not have disabilities in the same
23 public school district. Of the ten percent (10%) amount required by
24 this subsection, five percent (5%) shall be provided by the
Req. No. 2411 Page 2
1 empl oying school district, and five percent (5%) shall be provided
2 pursuant to subsection B of this section.
3 B. The State Department of Education shall allocate funds from
4 the Teacher Empowerment Revolving Fund created pursuant to Section
5 6-190.2 of this title to each school district in this state to
6 implement the state matching fund requirement of subsection A of
7 this section. Funding shall be allocated to each school district to
8 provide the five percent (5%) amount calculated pursuant to
9 subsection A of this section for every teacher certified in special
10 education.
11 SECTION 3. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
12 SECTION 4. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
13 of the public peace, health, or safety, an emergency is hereby
14 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
15 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
