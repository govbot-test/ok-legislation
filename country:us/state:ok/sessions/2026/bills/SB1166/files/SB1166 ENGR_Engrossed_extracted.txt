7 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Department of
Agriculture, Food, and Forestry; requiring that
8 portions of certain appropriated funds be used for
certain purposes; providing an effective date; and
9 declaring an emergency.
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. From the funds appropriated to the Oklahoma
13 Department of Agriculture, Food, and Forestry in Enrolled Senate
14 Bill No. 1177 of the 2nd Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature,
15 the sum of One Million Dollars ($1,000,000.00) shall be used for the
16 Oklahoma State University Robert M. Kerr Food and Agricultural
17 Products Center for operational expenses.
18 SECTION 2. From the funds appropriated to the Oklahoma
19 Department of Agriculture, Food, and Forestry in Enrolled Senate
20 Bill No. 1177 of the 2nd Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature,
21 the sum of Five Million Two Hundred Thousand Dollars ($5,200,000.00)
22 shall be used for the extension offices that are affiliated with the
23 Oklahoma State University Cooperative Extension Service.
24
ENGR. S. B. NO. 1166 Page 1
1 SECTION 3. From the funds appropriated to the Oklahoma
2 Department of Agriculture, Food, and Forestry in Enrolled Senate
3 Bill No. 1177 of the 2nd Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature,
4 the sum of One Million Three Hundred Thousand Dollars
5 ($1,300,000.00) shall be used for research centers that are
6 affiliated with Oklahoma State University Agriculture Experiment
7 Stations.
8 SECTION 4. From the funds appropriated to the Oklahoma
9 Department of Agriculture, Food, and Forestry in Enrolled Senate
10 Bill No. 1177 of the 2nd Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature,
11 the sum of Eight Million Five Hundred Thousand Dollars
12 ($8,500,000.00) shall be used for the cooperative extension and
13 agriculture research programs that are affiliated with Langston
14 University.
15 SECTION 5. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
16 SECTION 6. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
17 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
18 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
19 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
