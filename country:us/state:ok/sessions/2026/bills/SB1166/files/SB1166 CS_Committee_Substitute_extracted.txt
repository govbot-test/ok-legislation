10 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Department of
Agriculture, Food, and Forestry; requiring that
11 portions of certain appropriated funds be used for
certain purposes; providing an effective date; and
12 declaring an emergency.
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. From the funds appropriated to the Oklahoma
16 Department of Agriculture, Food, and Forestry in Enrolled Senate
17 Bill No. 1177 of the 2nd Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature,
18 the sum of One Million Dollars ($1,000,000.00) shall be used for the
19 Oklahoma State University Robert M. Kerr Food and Agricultural
20 Products Center for operational expenses.
21 SECTION 2. From the funds appropriated to the Oklahoma
22 Department of Agriculture, Food, and Forestry in Enrolled Senate
23 Bill No. 1177 of the 2nd Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature,
24 the sum of Five Million Two Hundred Thousand Dollars ($5,200,000.00)
Req. No. 3895 Page 1
1 shal l be used for the extension offices that are affiliated with the
2 Oklahoma State University Cooperative Extension Service.
3 SECTION 3. From the funds appropriated to the Oklahoma
4 Department of Agriculture, Food, and Forestry in Enrolled Senate
5 Bill No. 1177 of the 2nd Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature,
6 the sum of One Million Three Hundred Thousand Dollars
7 ($1,300,000.00) shall be used for research centers that are
8 affiliated with Oklahoma State University Agriculture Experiment
9 Stations.
10 SECTION 4. From the funds appropriated to the Oklahoma
11 Department of Agriculture, Food, and Forestry in Enrolled Senate
12 Bill No. 1177 of the 2nd Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature,
13 the sum of Eight Million Five Hundred Thousand Dollars
14 ($8,500,000.00) shall be used for the cooperative extension and
15 agriculture research programs that are affiliated with Langston
16 University.
17 SECTION 5. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
18 SECTION 6. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
19 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
20 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
21 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
