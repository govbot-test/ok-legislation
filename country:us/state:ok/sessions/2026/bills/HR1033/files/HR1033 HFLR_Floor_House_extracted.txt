8 AS INTRODUCED
9 A Resolution declaring February 18, 2026 as Scout Day
at the Oklahoma Capitol; recognizing the history,
10 importance, and work of Scouting America.
11
12
13 WHEREAS, Scouting America was first established by Englishman
14 Lord Robert Baden-Powell in 1908, and the Boy Scouts of America, the
15 first scouting organization in the United States, was
16 established in the United States on February 8, 1910; and
17 WHEREAS, since day one (1), Scouting America has espoused the
18 Scout Oath and the twelve (12) points of the Scout Law, establishing
19 that a Scout is Trustworthy, Loyal, Helpful, Friendly, Courteous,
20 Kind, Obedient, Cheerful, Thrifty, Brave, Clean, and Reverent; and
21 WHEREAS, Scouting America includes character development
22 programs such as Cub Scouts, Scouts BSA, Venturing, Sea Scouts, and
23 Exploring that prepare young people through career exploration and
24 skills enrichment programs; and

17 OF THE 2nd SESSION OF THE 60TH OKLAHOMA LEGISLATURE:
18 THAT the Oklahoma House of Representatives recognizes the long
19 history of Scouting America.
20 THAT the Oklahoma House of Representatives recognizes the

22 THAT the Oklahoma House of Representatives recognizes the
