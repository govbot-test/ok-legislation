8 An Act relating to Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis;
providing an act relating to appropriations; relating
9 to the Barbara Weber Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis
(ALS) Grant Program; stating amount and purpose;
10 providing funding for program; providing an effective
date; and declaring an emergency.
11
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. There is hereby appropriated to the Barbara Weber
16 Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis (ALS) Grant Program from any monies
17 not otherwise appropriated from the General Revenue Fund of the
18 State Treasury for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2027, the sum of
19 Zero Dollars ($0.00) or so much thereof as may be necessary to
20 perform the duties imposed upon the Oklahoma State Department of
21 Health by law.
22 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
23 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
24 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
