8 POLICY COMMITTEE RECOMMENDATION
9 An Act relating to poor persons; directing the
Director of the Oklahoma Health Care Authority to
10 appoint advisory committee; providing purpose of
committee; providing for membership of committee;
11 providing for codification; and providing an
effective date.
12
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 4002.16 of Title 56, unless
18 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 A. The Director of the Oklahoma Health Care Authority shall
20 appoint an advisory committee to advise the Director on matters
21 relating to service delivery for dental care of Medicaid patients.
22 B. Such advisory committee shall include among its members
23 representatives of the Oklahoma Dental Association, dental service
24 organizations, and the Oklahoma Board of Dentistry.
Req. No. 16561 Page 1
1 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
