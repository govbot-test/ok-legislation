8 An Act relating to legislative referendums; directing
special election on proposed amendments to the
9 Constitution and state statutes be held; specifying
certain date; referencing House Bill No. 4440 of the
10 60th Oklahoma Legislature; referencing House Joint
Resolution No. 1024 of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature;
11 directing filing; and declaring an emergency.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
16 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
17 Pursuant to Section 3 of Article V of the Oklahoma Constitution,
18 a special election is hereby ordered to be held throughout the State
19 of Oklahoma on Tuesday, August 25, 2026, at which time the proposed
20 referendums, as set forth in Enrolled House Bill No. 4440 of the 2nd
21 Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature and Enrolled House Joint
22 Resolution No. 1024 of the 2nd Session of the 60th Oklahoma
23 Legislature, shall be submitted to the people of Oklahoma for their
24 approval or rejection as and in the manner provided by law.
ENGR. H. B. NO. 4063 Page 1
1 SECTION 2. The Chief Clerk of the House of Representatives,
2 immediately after the passage of this act, shall prepare and file
3 one copy thereof in accordance with Section 3 of Article V of the
4 Oklahoma Constitution, one copy with the Secretary of State, and one
5 copy with the Attorney General.
6 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
7 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
8 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
9 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
10 Passed the House of Representatives the 21st day of April, 2026.
