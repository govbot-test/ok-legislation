17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
18 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
19 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
20 Pursuant to Section 3 of Article V of the Oklahoma Constitution,
21 a special election is hereby ordered to be held throughout the State
22 of Oklahoma on Tuesday, August 25, 2026, at which time the proposed
23 referendums, as set forth in Enrolled House Bill No. 4440 of the 2nd
24 Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature and Enrolled House Joint
Req. No. 17506 Page 1
1 Reso lution No. 1024 of the 2nd Session of the 60th Oklahoma
2 Legislature, shall be submitted to the people of Oklahoma for their
3 approval or rejection as and in the manner provided by law.
4 SECTION 2. The Chief Clerk of the House of Representatives,
5 immediately after the passage of this act, shall prepare and file
6 one copy thereof in accordance with Section 3 of Article V of the
7 Oklahoma Constitution one copy with the Secretary of State and one
8 copy with the Attorney General.
9 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
10 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
11 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
12 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
