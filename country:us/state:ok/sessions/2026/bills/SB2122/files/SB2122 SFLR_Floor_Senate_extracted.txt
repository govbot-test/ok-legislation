8 An Act relating to agriculture; amending 2 O.S. 2021,

Section 2:
Section 5-60.6, which relates to authority to
9 promulgate rules by the Oklahoma Department of
Agriculture, Food, and Forestry; requiring rules to
10 allow out-of-state poultry processors to be used; and
providing an effective date.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 2 O.S. 2021, Section 5-60.6, is
15 amended to read as follows:
16 Section 5-60.6. The Oklahoma Department of Agriculture, Food,
17 and Forestry is authorized to promulgate rules as necessary to carry
18 out the provisions of this act, including rules that permit the
19 utilization of out-of-state poultry processing plants if in-state
20 options are not available.
21 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
