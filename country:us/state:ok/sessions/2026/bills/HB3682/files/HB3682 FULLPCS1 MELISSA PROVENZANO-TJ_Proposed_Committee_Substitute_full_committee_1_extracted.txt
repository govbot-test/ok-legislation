8 PROPOSED COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
9 An Act relating to smoking; creating the Oklahoma
Clean Indoor Air Act; defining terms; prohibiting
10 smoking in certain places; providing exceptions;
mandating signage; providing for promulgation of
11 rules from the State Department of Health; repealing
63 O.S. 2021, Section 1-1527, which relates to
12 preemption; providing for noncodification; providing
for codification; and providing an effective date.
13
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
18 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
19 This act shall be known and may be cited as the "Oklahoma Clean
20 Indoor Air Act".
21 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
22 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1-1527.10 of Title 63, unless
23 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
24 A. As used in this section:
Req. No. 16604 Page 1
1 1. "Cigar bar" means an establishment that exclusively occupies
2 an enclosed indoor space and is primarily engaged in the retail sale
3 of tobacco products for consumption by customers on the premises;
4 2. "Enclosed area" means a space with a roof and more than two
5 walls;
6 3. "Public place" means any enclosed area to which the public
7 is invited or permitted;
8 4. "Smoking" means cigarettes, cigars, pipes, and electronic
9 smoking devices, including but not limited to vapes; and
10 5. "Workplace" means any indoor area under the control of an
11 employer where employees perform duties.
12 B. Smoking shall be prohibited in all the following places:
13 1. Indoor workplaces, including but not limited to, offices and
14 factories;
15 2. Government buildings;
16 3. Restaurants and bars;
17 4. Public transportation;
18 5. Health care facilities;
19 6. Educational institutions;
20 7. Retail stores and malls; and
21 8. Sports arenas and stadiums.
22 C. Smoking shall be allowed:
23 1. In tobacco shops or cigar bars, with proper ventilation and
24 signage;
Req. No. 16604 Page 2
1 2. Private residences, unless used for childcare or health care
2 services;
3 3. Designated smoking rooms in hotels, with a limited
4 percentage of rooms; and
5 4. On tribal lands and reservations.
6 D. Establishments shall have clear signage stating "No smoking"
7 at entrances and throughout facilities.
8 E. The State Department of Health shall promulgate rules to
9 enforce the provisions of this act.
10 F. Local governments or municipalities may enact stricter
11 ordinances.
12 SECTION 3. REPEALER 63 O.S. 2021, Section 1-1527, is
13 hereby repealed.
14 SECTION 4. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
