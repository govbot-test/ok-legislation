9 An Act relating to law libraries; requiring law
libraries be represented by district attorney;
10 providing for the Office of the Attorney General to
represent law libraries in certain circumstance;
11 providing for codification; and providing an
effective date.
12
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1228 of Title 20, unless there
18 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 A. A county law library, its board of trustees, or any
20 employees of such shall be represented without charge in any legal
21 matter that relates to the official duties or operations of such law
22 library by the district attorney in whose district such law library
23 is located.
24

1 B. If the district attorney is unable to represent a county law
2 library due to a conflict of interest, the Office of the Attorney
3 General shall, without charge, provide any required legal
4 representation.
5 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
