15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3218.7-2 of Title 70, unless
18 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 Within The Oklahoma State System of Higher Education, no
20 resident tuition or nonresident tuition shall be charged to the
21 spouse or children of a Gold Star recipient who was killed in the
22 line of duty in any branch of the United States Armed Forces,
23 provided that either:
24
HB2961 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 1. The Gold Star recipient was a legal resident of the State of
2 Oklahoma at the time of death; or
3 2. The spouse or child maintains residency in the State of
4 Oklahoma during enrollment.
5 SECTION 2. AMENDATORY 70 O.S. 2021, Section 3218.7-1, as
6 amended by Section 2, Chapter 382, O.S.L. 2024 (70 O.S. Supp. 2025,
7 Section 3218.7-1), is amended to read as follows:
8 Section 3218.7-1. A. Within The Oklahoma State System of
9 Higher Education, no fees nor room and board shall be charged to:
10 1. Children of Oklahoma peace officers as defined by Section
11 648 of Title 21 of the Oklahoma Statutes who have given their lives
12 in the line of duty;
13 2. Children of Oklahoma firefighters who have given their lives
14 in the line of duty;
15 3. Children of commissioned members of the Oklahoma Law
16 Enforcement Retirement System, who have given their lives in the
17 line of duty; and
18 4. Children of Oklahoma emergency medical technicians who have
19 given their lives in the line of duty; and
20 5. The spouse or children of a Gold Star recipient who was
21 killed in the line of duty in any branch of the United States Armed
22 Forces, provided that either:
23 a. the Gold Star recipient was a legal resident of the
24 State of Oklahoma at the time of death, or
HB2961 HFLR Page 2
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 b. the spouse or child maintains residency in the State
2 of Oklahoma during enrollment.
3 B. Such waiver of fees and room and board shall be limited to a
4 period of five (5) years.
5 C. Such waiver of fees and room and board shall be a service
6 benefit of a commissioned member of the Oklahoma Law Enforcement
7 Retirement System.
8 D. For purposes of this section:
9 1. "Firefighter" means a volunteer firefighter or a permanent
10 salaried professional member of any fire department within the State
11 of Oklahoma; and
12 2. "Emergency medical technician" means a person volunteering
13 or employed as an emergency medical technician and who is licensed
14 as an emergency medical technician pursuant to Section 1-2505 of
15 Title 63 of the Oklahoma Statutes.
16 SECTION 3. AMENDATORY 70 O.S. 2021, Section 14-134, is
17 amended to read as follows:
18 Section 14-134. A. Within the system of career technology
19 districts, no resident tuition or nonresident tuition shall be
20 charged to the:
21 1. Children of Oklahoma peace officers as defined by Section
22 648 of Title 21 of the Oklahoma Statutes who have given their lives
23 in the line of duty;
24

2 in the line of duty;
3 3. Children of members of the Oklahoma Law Enforcement

5 whose disability is by means of personal and traumatic injury of a
6 catastrophic nature, as defined by Section 2-300 of Title 47 of the
7 Oklahoma Statutes, and occurred in the line of duty; and
8 4. Children of Oklahoma emergency medical technicians who have
9 given their lives in the line of duty; and

19 C. Such waiver of resident tuition or nonresident tuition to
20 the children of deceased peace officers and to the children of
21 deceased firefighters as provided for in this section shall be a
22 service benefit of each Oklahoma peace officer and Oklahoma
23 firefighter.
