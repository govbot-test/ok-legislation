13 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 27A O.S. 2021, Section 2-11-401.1,
14 is amended to read as follows:
15 Section 2-11-401.1. As used in the Oklahoma Used Tire Recycling
16 Act:
17 1. "ASTM" means the American Society for Testing and Materials;
18 2. "Automobile" means every motor vehicle of the type
19 constructed and used for the transportation of ten persons or less,
20 including the driver, or used for the transportation of property.
21 Provided, however, that the automobile's gross vehicle weight rating
22 does not exceed sixteen thousand (16,000) pounds;
23 3. "Automotive dismantler and parts recycler" means the same as
24 defined in Section 591.2 of Title 47 of the Oklahoma Statutes;

1 13. "Priority cleanup list" means a list, created and
2 maintained by the Department, of:
3 a. unpermitted dumps which did not exist when the owner

7 b. such other tire dumps designated by the Department

9 14. "Reusable tire" means a tire that has been previously used
10 on a vehicle, not currently mounted on a vehicle, but can be legally
11 placed into service for vehicle use in Oklahoma;
12 15. "Semitrailer" means the same as defined in Section 1-162 of
13 Title 47 of the Oklahoma Statutes;
14 16. "Tire" means any solid or air-filled covering for vehicle
15 wheels;

20 18. "Tire dealer" means any person engaged in the business of
21 selling new and used tires to final consumers, not for resale;
22 19. "Tire-derived aggregate" means pieces of scrap tires that
23 have a basic geometrical shape and are generally between twelve (12)
24 millimeters (0.47 inches) and three hundred five (305) millimeters

1 24. "Trailer" means the same as defined in Section 1-180 of
2 Title 47 of the Oklahoma Statutes;
3 25. "Used tire" means an unprocessed whole tire or tire part
4 that can no longer be used for its originally intended purpose but
5 can be beneficially reused as approved by the Department. Any used
6 tire collected in accordance with the requirements of the Oklahoma
7 Used Tire Recycling Act is not considered to be discarded. A tire
8 that can be used, reused or legally modified to be reused for its
9 original intended purpose shall not be a used tire;
10 26. "Used tire processing" means altering the form of whole
11 used tires by shredding, chipping or other method approved by the
12 Department, except baling and pyrolysis. For purposes of this act
13 only, tire pyrolysis shall, for a five-year period ending on July 1,
14 2031, meet the definition of used tire processing until July 1,
15 2031, but not thereafter. Any tire pyrolysis facility shall not be
16 considered an advanced recycling facility but rather a used tire
17 recycling facility and shall be subject to all applicable statutes
18 and rules for such facilities;
19 27. "Used tire recycling facility" means any place which is
20 permitted as a solid waste disposal site, in accordance with the
21 Oklahoma Solid Waste Management Act, at which used tires are
22 processed; and
23 28. "Vehicle" means the same as defined in Section 1-186 of
24 Title 47 of the Oklahoma Statutes.
