SENATE CHAMBER

COMMITTEE AMENDMENT
(Date)
I move to amend Senate Bill No. 1714 as follows:
1. On Page 1, Line 21 1/2 by inserting, the following language and renumbering subsequent
subsections:
“B. All agencies shall provide a notification of the implementation of the State Employee
Suggestion Program via email to all agency employees and on an annual basis from the date
of the implementation of the program shall provide a description of the program to all agency
employees. All agencies shall prominently display in all agency break areas a written
description of the State Employee Suggestion Program.”;
2. On Page 2, Line 2, by inserting, after the period the words “Suggestions shall be made in
writing and dated with the date of submission.”;
3. On Page 5, Line 22, by inserting, after the period and before the word “Staff”, the words
“All meetings of the Committee shall be subject to the provisions of the Oklahoma Open
Meeting Act.”;
4. On Page 5, Line 24 ½ by inserting a new Section 5 to read as follows and renumbering
subsequent sections:
“Section 5. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified in the Oklahoma Statutes as
Section 1608 of Title 74, unless there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
If a state agency determines an employee's or employees’ suggestion would not result in a
cost savings to the agency or would result in a cost savings of less than what is described in
the employee’s suggestion, the employee or employees may file a written appeal to the
Incentive Awards for State Employees Committee. Upon receipt of an appeal, the Committee
shall direct the state agency to file a response to the employee's or employees’ appeal and
shall review the appeal and response at the next meeting of the Committee. If the Committee
determines the employee's or employees’ suggestion would result in the described cost
savings to the agency or a cost savings different than that of the employee’s or employees’
suggestion and the agency’s determination, the Committee may order the agency to
implement the employee's or employees’ suggestion and award the employee or employees
based on the Committee’s cost savings determination in accordance with Section 1604 of this
title.”; and
5. Amending the title to conform.
