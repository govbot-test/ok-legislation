16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
18 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 330.99 of Title 63, unless there
19 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
20 A. The Oklahoma Task Force 1 (OK-TF1) shall be recognized as
21 the official deployment asset and urban search and rescue team of
22 the State of Oklahoma. The OK-TF1 shall be the first deployed asset
23 for any Emergency Management Assistance Compact-related requests
24 outside of the State of Oklahoma during any declared disasters.
