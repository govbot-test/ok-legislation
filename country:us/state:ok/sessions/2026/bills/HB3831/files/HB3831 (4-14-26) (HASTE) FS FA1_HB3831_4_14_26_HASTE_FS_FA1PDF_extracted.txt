15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 330.99 of Title 63, unless there
18 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 A. Oklahoma Task Force 1 (OK-TF1) shall be recognized as the
20 official deployment asset team for urban search and rescue missions
21 and for floods and disasters in this state; provided, however, OK-
22 TF1 shall not be deployed for wildland firefighting. OK-TF1 shall
23 be the first deployed asset for any Emergency Management Assistance
24

1 Comp act-related requests outside of this state during any declared
2 disasters.
3 B. The Oklahoma Department of Emergency Management is
4 authorized to promulgate and enforce rules governing the
5 administration, allowable uses, and oversight of the Oklahoma Task
6 Force 1 Revolving Fund created in subsection C of this section. The
7 funds in the Oklahoma Task Force 1 Revolving Fund shall be divided
8 equally between the Oklahoma City and Tulsa teams of OK-TF1.
9 C. There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving
10 fund for the Oklahoma Department of Emergency Management, to be
11 designated the “Oklahoma Task Force 1 Revolving Fund”. The fund
12 shall be a continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations,
13 and shall consist of all monies deposited to the credit of the fund
14 by law. All monies accruing to the credit of the fund are hereby
15 appropriated and may be budgeted and expended by the Oklahoma
16 Department of Emergency Management for the direct reimbursement of
17 participating agencies of Oklahoma Task Force 1. Expenditures from
18 the fund shall be made upon warrants issued by the State Treasurer
19 against claims filed as prescribed by law with the Director of the
20 Office of Management and Enterprise Services for approval and
21 payment.
22 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
