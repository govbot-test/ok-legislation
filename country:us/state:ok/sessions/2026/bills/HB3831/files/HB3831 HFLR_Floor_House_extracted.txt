21 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
22 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 330.99 of Title 63, unless there
23 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:

1 A. Oklahoma Task Force 1 (OK-TF1) shall be recognized as the
2 official deployment asset team for urban search and rescue missions
3 and for floods and disasters in the State of Oklahoma; provided,
4 however, OK-TF1 shall not be deployed for wildland firefighting.
5 OK-TF1 shall be the first deployed asset for any Emergency
6 Management Assistance Compact-related requests outside of the State
7 of Oklahoma during any declared disasters.
8 B. The Oklahoma Department of Emergency Management is
9 authorized to promulgate and enforce rules governing the
10 administration, allowable uses, and oversight of the Oklahoma Task
11 Force 1 Revolving Fund.
12 C. There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving
13 fund for the Oklahoma Department of Emergency Management, to be
14 designated the "Oklahoma Task Force 1 Revolving Fund". The fund
15 shall be a continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations,
16 and shall consist of all monies deposited to the credit of the fund
17 by law. All monies accruing to the credit of the fund are hereby
18 appropriated and may be budgeted and expended by the Department of
19 Emergency Management for the direct reimbursement of participating
20 agencies of Oklahoma Task Force 1. Expenditures from the fund shall
21 be made upon warrants issued by the State Treasurer against claims
22 filed as prescribed by law with the Director of the Office of
23 Management and Enterprise Services for approval and payment.
