8 An Act relating to emergency management services;
recognizing Oklahoma Task Force 1 as the official
9 deployment asset team for specific purposes;
authorizing the Oklahoma Department of Emergency
10 Management to promulgate rules; creating the Oklahoma
Task Force 1 Revolving Fund; providing for the
11 continuation of the fund; making fund not subject to

12 appropriation and budgeting of funds; requiring the

19 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
20 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
21 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 330.99 of Title 63, unless there
22 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
23 A. Oklahoma Task Force 1 (OK-TF1) shall be recognized as the
24 official deployment asset team for urban search and rescue missions
Req. No. 16476 Page 1

1 and for floods and disasters in the State of Oklahoma; provided,
2 however, OK-TF1 shall not be deployed for wildland firefighting.
3 OK-TF1 shall be the first deployed asset for any Emergency
4 Management Assistance Compact-related requests outside of the State
5 of Oklahoma during any declared disasters.
6 B. The Oklahoma Department of Emergency Management is
7 authorized to promulgate and enforce rules governing the
8 administration, allowable uses, and oversight of the Oklahoma Task
9 Force 1 Revolving Fund.
10 C. There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving
11 fund for the Oklahoma Department of Emergency Management, to be
12 designated the "Oklahoma Task Force 1 Revolving Fund". The fund
13 shall be a continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations,
14 and shall consist of all monies deposited to the credit of the fund
15 by law. All monies accruing to the credit of the fund are hereby
16 appropriated and may be budgeted and expended by the Department of
17 Emergency Management for the direct reimbursement of participating
18 agencies of Oklahoma Task Force 1. Expenditures from the fund shall
19 be made upon warrants issued by the State Treasurer against claims
20 filed as prescribed by law with the Director of the Office of
21 Management and Enterprise Services for approval and payment.
22 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
23 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
