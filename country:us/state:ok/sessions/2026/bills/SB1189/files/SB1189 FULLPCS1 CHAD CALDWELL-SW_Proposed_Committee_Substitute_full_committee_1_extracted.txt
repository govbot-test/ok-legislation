9 PROPOSED COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
10 An Act relating to schools; amending Section 2,
Chapter 281, O.S.L. 2023, as amended by Section 2,
11 Chapter 408, O.S.L. 2024 (70 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section
5-148.2), which relates to the School Security
12 Revolving Fund; adding applicability of fund to the
Oklahoma School of Science and Mathematics, the
13 Oklahoma School for the Blind, and the Oklahoma
School for the Deaf; removing expenditure limitation;
14 adding certain schools to allocation of funds;
requiring certain amount of funds to be allocated
15 from the fund each fiscal year for three fiscal years
beginning on certain date, subject to available
16 funding; providing an effective date; and declaring
an emergency.
17
18
19 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
20 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY Section 2, Chapter 281, O.S.L.
21 2023, as amended by Section 2, Chapter 408, O.S.L. 2024 (70 O.S.
22 Supp. 2025, Section 5-148.2), is amended to read as follows:
23 Section 5-148.2. A. There is hereby created in the State
24 Treasury a revolving fund for the State Department of Education to
Req. No. 17368 Page 1
1 be d esignated the “School Security Revolving Fund”. The fund shall
2 be a continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations, and
3 shall consist of all monies received by the State Department of
4 Education from:
5 1. Reimbursements, grants, or other monies received from other
6 state agencies and entities for school security;
7 2. Reimbursements, grants, or other monies received from the
8 United States government obligated to school security projects;
9 3. Gifts, donations, and bequests; and
10 4. Monies appropriated or apportioned by the Legislature.
11 B. The provisions of this section shall also apply to the
12 Oklahoma School of Science and Mathematics created in Section
13 1210.401 of this title, the Oklahoma School for the Blind created in
14 Section 1721 of this title, and the Oklahoma School for the Deaf
15 created in Section 1731 of this title.
16 C. All monies accruing to the credit of the School Security
17 Revolving Fund are hereby appropriated and may be budgeted and
18 expended by the State Department of Education, the Oklahoma School
19 of Science and Mathematics, the Oklahoma School for the Blind, and
20 the Oklahoma School for the Deaf for the purposes of:
21 1. Establishing and maintaining a School Resource Officer
22 Program; and
23 2. Providing physical security enhancements for schools
24 including, but not limited to, school resource officers, cameras,
Req. No. 17368 Page 2
1 gate s, lighting, locks, doors, windows, security geofencing,
2 ballistic storm shelters, and mobile panic alert systems.
3 Monies in the School Security Revolving Fund shall supplement
4 and not supplant existing school security funding.
5 C. D. Expenditures from the School Security Revolving Fund
6 shall be made upon warrants issued by the State Treasurer against
7 claims filed as prescribed by law with the Director of the Office of
8 Management and Enterprise Services for approval and payment.
9 Expenditures from the School Security Revolving Fund shall not
10 exceed Subject to the availability of funding, Fifty Million Dollars
11 ($50,000,000.00) in any fiscal year and shall be allocated from the
12 School Security Revolving Fund and divided equally among every
13 public school district in the this state, the Oklahoma School of
14 Science and Mathematics, the Oklahoma School for the Blind, and the
15 Oklahoma School for the Deaf each fiscal year for three (3) fiscal
16 years beginning July 1, 2026.
17 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
18 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
19 of the public peace, health, or safety, an emergency is hereby
20 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
21 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
