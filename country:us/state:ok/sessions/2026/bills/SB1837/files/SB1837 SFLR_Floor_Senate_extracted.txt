9 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
10 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
11 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1018.1 of Title 56, unless there
12 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
13 A. The Oklahoma Health Care Authority shall submit a request to
14 the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services for an exemption from
15 the provisions of 42 C.F.R., Section 441.301(c)(1)(vi) to allow
16 providers of Medicaid home- and community-based services in rural
17 counties of this state to provide case management and develop the
18 required person-centered plan. As used in this subsection, “rural
19 counties” means counties with a population of not more than sixty
20 thousand (60,000), according to the most recent Federal Decennial
21 Census.
22 B. For providers exempted under subsection A of this section,
23 the Authority shall devise conflict of interest protections in
24 accordance with 42 C.F.R., Section 441.301(c)(1)(vi), including
