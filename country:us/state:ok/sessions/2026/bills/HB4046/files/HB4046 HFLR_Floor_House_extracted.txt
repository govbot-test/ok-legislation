18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
19 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
20 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
21 A. Of the funds appropriated to the Military Department of the
22 State of Oklahoma in Section 158 of Enrolled House Bill No. 2766 of
23 the 1st Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature, Twenty-one Million
24 Dollars ($21,000,000.00) or so much as may be necessary shall be

1 tran sferred to the Military Readiness, Innovation, Education, and
2 Aviation Revolving Fund.
3 B. Of the funds transferred, Six Million Two Hundred Thousand
4 Dollars ($6,200,000.00) or so much thereof as may be necessary,
5 shall be for the purpose of constructing or improving common
6 education facilities on a military base located in Southwest
7 Oklahoma.
8 C. Of the funds transferred, Five Million Dollars
9 ($5,000,000.00) or so much thereof as may be necessary, shall be for
10 the purpose of constructing or improving common education facilities
11 on a military base located in Northwest Oklahoma.
12 D. Of the funds transferred, Nine Million Eight Hundred
13 Thousand ($9,800,000.00) or so much thereof as may be necessary,
14 shall be for the purpose of constructing or improving cargo access
15 to a military facility in Southeast Oklahoma, including access by
16 rail.
17 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
18 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
19 There is hereby appropriated to the Military Readiness,

21 not otherwise appropriated from the Progressing Rural Economic
22 Prosperity Fund of the State Treasury created in Section 256 of
23 Title 62 of the Oklahoma Statutes, the sum of Four Million Dollars
24 ($4,000,000.00) for the purpose of constructing or improving cargo

3 SECTION 3. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
4 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
5 There is hereby appropriated to the Military Readiness,
6 Innovation, Education, and Aviation Revolving Fund from any monies
7 not otherwise appropriated from the Progressing Rural Economic
8 Prosperity Fund of the State Treasury created in Section 256 of
9 Title 62 of the Oklahoma Statutes, the sum of Ten Million Dollars
10 ($10,000,000.00) for the purpose of constructing a new building
11 attached to an innovation center that is dedicated to research and
12 education projects supporting national defense. Such funds shall
13 not be released until additional funding sources for the project are
14 obtained and a tenant or tenants are secured.
15 SECTION 4. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
16 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
17 The Oklahoma Military Department is hereby authorized to
18 transfer the balance of the Base Infrastructure Needs and
19 Development – Schools Revolving Fund, created in Section 301A of
20 Title 44 of the Oklahoma Statutes, to the Military Readiness,
21 Innovation, Education, and Aviation Revolving Fund created in
22 Enrolled House Bill No. 4045 of the 2nd Session of the 60th Oklahoma
23 Legislature.
