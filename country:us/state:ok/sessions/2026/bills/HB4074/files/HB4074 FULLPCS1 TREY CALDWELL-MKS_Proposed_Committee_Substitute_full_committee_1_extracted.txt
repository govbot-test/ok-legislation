10 PROPOSED COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
11 An Act relating to the Health Care Workforce Training
Commission; identifying funds appropriated to the
12 Health Care Workforce Revolving Fund; identifying
funds appropriated to the Health Care Workforce
13 Training Commission; authorizing the Health Care
Workforce Training Commission to transfer and
14 allocate between funds as needed for certain
purposes; providing for noncodification; providing an
15 effective date; and declaring an emergency.
16
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
18 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
19 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
20 Of the funds appropriated to the Health Care Workforce Revolving
21 Fund or to the Health Care Workforce Training Commission pursuant
22 to:
23 1. Enrolled Senate Bill No. 1458 of the 2nd Regular Session of
24 the 58th Oklahoma Legislature;
Req. No. 17472 Page 1
1 2. Enrolled Senate Bill No. 8 of the 2nd Extraordinary Session
2 of the 58th Oklahoma Legislature;
3 3. Enrolled Senate Bill No. 9 of the 2nd Extraordinary Session
4 of the Oklahoma Legislature;
5 4. Enrolled Senate Bill No. 10 of the 2nd Extraordinary Session
6 of the 58th Oklahoma Legislature;
7 5. Enrolled Senate Bill No. 17 of the 2nd Extraordinary Session
8 of the 58th Oklahoma Legislature;
9 6. Enrolled Senate Bill No. 1188 of the 1st Regular Session of
10 59th Oklahoma Legislature; and
11 7. Enrolled Senate Bill No. 39 of the 1st Extraordinary Session
12 of the 59th Oklahoma Legislature;
13 the Health Care Workforce Training Commission is hereby authorized
14 to transfer and allocate amounts between such funds as needed, in
15 order to complete any such project and purpose listed in measures
16 described in paragraphs 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, and 6 of this section.
17 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
18 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
19 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
20 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
21 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
