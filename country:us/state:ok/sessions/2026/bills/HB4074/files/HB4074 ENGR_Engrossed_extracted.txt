7 An Act relating to the Health Care Workforce Training
Commission; identifying funds appropriated to the
8 Health Care Workforce Revolving Fund; identifying
funds appropriated to the Health Care Workforce
9 Training Commission; authorizing the Health Care
Workforce Training Commission to transfer and
10 allocate between funds as needed for certain
purposes; providing for noncodification; and
11 declaring an emergency.
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
15 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
16 Of the funds appropriated to the Health Care Workforce Revolving
17 Fund or to the Health Care Workforce Training Commission pursuant
18 to:
19 1. Enrolled Senate Bill No. 1458 of the 2nd Regular Session of
20 the 58th Oklahoma Legislature;
21 2. Enrolled Senate Bill No. 8 of the 2nd Extraordinary Session
22 of the 58th Oklahoma Legislature;
23 3. Enrolled Senate Bill No. 9 of the 2nd Extraordinary Session
24 of the 58th Oklahoma Legislature;
ENGR. H. B. NO. 4074 Page 1
1 4. Enrolled Senate Bill No. 10 of the 2nd Extraordinary Session
2 of the 58th Oklahoma Legislature;
3 5. Enrolled Senate Bill No. 17 of the 2nd Extraordinary Session
4 of the 58th Oklahoma Legislature;
5 6. Enrolled Senate Bill No. 1188 of the 1st Regular Session of
6 the 59th Oklahoma Legislature; and
7 7. Enrolled Senate Bill No. 39 of the 1st Extraordinary Session
8 of the 59th Oklahoma Legislature,
9 the Health Care Workforce Training Commission is hereby authorized
10 to transfer and allocate amounts between such funds as needed, in
11 order to complete any such project and purpose listed in measures
12 described in paragraphs 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, and 7 of this section.
13 SECTION 2. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
14 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
15 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
16 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
