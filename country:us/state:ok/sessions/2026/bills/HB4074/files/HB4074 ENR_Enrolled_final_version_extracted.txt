ENROLLED HOUSE
BILL NO. 4074 By: Caldwell (Trey) and Kane of
the House
and
Hall and Haste of the
Senate
An Act relating to the Health Care Workforce Training
Commission; identifying funds appropriated to the
Health Care Workforce Revolving Fund; identifying
funds appropriated to the Health Care Workforce
Training Commission; authorizing the Health Care
Workforce Training Commission to transfer and
allocate between funds as needed for certain
purposes; providing for noncodification; and
declaring an emergency.
SUBJECT: Health Care Workforce Training Commission
BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
Of the funds appropriated to the Health Care Workforce Revolving
Fund or to the Health Care Workforce Training Commission pursuant
to:

Section 3:
1. Enrolled Senate Bill No. 1458 of the 2nd Regular Session of
the 58th Oklahoma Legislature;

Section 4:
2. Enrolled Senate Bill No. 8 of the 2nd Extraordinary Session
of the 58th Oklahoma Legislature;

Section 5:
3. Enrolled Senate Bill No. 9 of the 2nd Extraordinary Session
of the 58th Oklahoma Legislature;

Section 6:
4. Enrolled Senate Bill No. 10 of the 2nd Extraordinary Session
of the 58th Oklahoma Legislature;

Section 7:
5. Enrolled Senate Bill No. 17 of the 2nd Extraordinary Session
of the 58th Oklahoma Legislature;

Section 8:
6. Enrolled Senate Bill No. 1188 of the 1st Regular Session of
the 59th Oklahoma Legislature; and

Section 9:
7. Enrolled Senate Bill No. 39 of the 1st Extraordinary Session
of the 59th Oklahoma Legislature,
the Health Care Workforce Training Commission is hereby authorized
to transfer and allocate amounts between such funds as needed, in
order to complete any such project and purpose listed in measures
described in paragraphs 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, and 7 of this section.

Section 10:
SECTION 2. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
be in full force from and after its passage and approval.

An Act
ENROLLED HOUSE
BILL NO. 4074 By: Caldwell (Trey) and Kane of
the House
and
Hall and Haste of the
Senate
An Act relating to the Health Care Workforce Training
Commission; identifying funds appropriated to the
Health Care Workforce Revolving Fund; identifying
funds appropriated to the Health Care Workforce
Training Commission; authorizing the Health Care
Workforce Training Commission to transfer and
allocate between funds as needed for certain
purposes; providing for noncodification; and
declaring an emergency.
SUBJECT: Health Care Workforce Training Commission
BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
Of the funds appropriated to the Health Care Workforce Revolving
Fund or to the Health Care Workforce Training Commission pursuant
to:
1. Enrolled Senate Bill No. 1458 of the 2nd Regular Session of
the 58th Oklahoma Legislature;
2. Enrolled Senate Bill No. 8 of the 2nd Extraordinary Session
of the 58th Oklahoma Legislature;
3. Enrolled Senate Bill No. 9 of the 2nd Extraordinary Session
of the 58th Oklahoma Legislature;

4. Enrolled Senate Bill No. 10 of the 2nd Extraordinary Session
of the 58th Oklahoma Legislature;
5. Enrolled Senate Bill No. 17 of the 2nd Extraordinary Session
of the 58th Oklahoma Legislature;
6. Enrolled Senate Bill No. 1188 of the 1st Regular Session of
the 59th Oklahoma Legislature; and
7. Enrolled Senate Bill No. 39 of the 1st Extraordinary Session
of the 59th Oklahoma Legislature,
the Health Care Workforce Training Commission is hereby authorized
to transfer and allocate amounts between such funds as needed, in
order to complete any such project and purpose listed in measures
described in paragraphs 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, and 7 of this section.
SECTION 2. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
