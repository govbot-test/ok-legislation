8 An Act relating to the State Department of Health;
making an appropriation; identifying source;
9 providing amount; stating purpose; providing for
noncodification; and declaring an emergency.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
14 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
15 There is hereby appropriated to the State Department of Health
16 from any monies not otherwise appropriated from the General Revenue
17 Fund of the State Treasury for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2025,
18 the sum of Two Million Dollars ($2,000,000.00) or so much thereof as
19 may be necessary to pay for legacy contracts associated with the
20 Choosing Childbirth Program.
21 SECTION 2. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
22 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
23 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
24 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
