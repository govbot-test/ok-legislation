11 An Act relating to the State Department of Health;

12 providing amount; stating purpose; providing for

14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
17 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
18 There is hereby appropriated to the State Department of Health
19 from any monies not otherwise appropriated from the General Revenue
20 Fund of the State Treasury for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2025,
21 the sum of Two Million Dollars ($2,000,000.00) or so much thereof as
22 may be necessary to pay for legacy contracts associated with the
23 Choosing Childbirth Program.
