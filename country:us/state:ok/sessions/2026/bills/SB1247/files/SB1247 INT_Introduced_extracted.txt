6 AS INTRODUCED
6
7 An Act relating to higher education; amending 70 O.S.
7 2021, Section 3242.2, which relates to higher
8 education benefits for certain students; removing
8 exception to prohibition for certain students;
9 removing outdated language; updating statutory
9 language; providing for promulgation of rules;
10 repealing 70 O.S. 2021, Section 3242, which relates
10 to resident tuition eligibility for certain students;
11 providing an effective date; and declaring an
11 emergency.
12
12
13
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14
15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 70 O.S. 2021, Section 3242.2, is
15
16 amended to read as follows:
16
17 Section 3242.2. A. Except as otherwise provided in Section
17
18 3242 of Title 70 of the Oklahoma Statutes, an An individual who is
18
19 not lawfully present in the United States shall not be eligible on
19
20 the basis of residence within the this state for:
20
21 1. Any postsecondary education benefit, including, but not
21
22 limited to, scholarships or financial aid; or
22
23 2. Resident tuition.
23
24
24
Req. No. 2211 Page 1
1 B. The provisions of subsection A of this section shall not
1
2 apply to a student enrolled in a degree program at a postsecondary
2
3 educational institution within The Oklahoma State System of Higher
3
4 Education during the 2006-2007 school year or any prior year who
4
5 received a resident tuition benefit pursuant to Section 3242 of
5
6 Title 70 of the Oklahoma Statutes at that institution The Oklahoma
6
7 State Regents for Higher Education may promulgate rules to implement
7
8 the provisions of this section.
8
9 SECTION 2. REPEALER 70 O.S. 2021, Section 3242, is
9
10 hereby repealed.
10
11 SECTION 3. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
11
12 SECTION 4. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
12
13 of the public peace, health, or safety, an emergency is hereby
13
14 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
14
15 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
