6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to counties and county officers;
amending 2 O.S. 2021, Section 15-68, which relates to
8 the board of directors for the county free fair
association; allowing the board of county
9 commissioners to choose between a five-member or
nine-member board; providing schedule for election of
10 members; and providing an effective date.

13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 2 O.S. 2021, Section 15-68, is
15 amended to read as follows:
16 Section 15-68. A. There is hereby constituted a board of
17 directors for the free fair association.
18 B. 1. The board of directors shall be composed of either five
19 (5) members or nine (9) members as determined by the board of county
20 commissioners, who shall be by written ballot elected for a term of
21 three (3) years. Three For nine-member boards, three members shall
22 be elected annually, one from each county commissioner's district of
23 the counties, at a public meeting or convention of the qualified
24 electors of each commissioner's district in the counties. For five-
