9 An Act relating to counties and county officers;
amending 2 O.S. 2021, Section 15-68, which relates to
10 the board of directors for county free fair
association; allowing board of county commissioners
11 to vote to have a five-member board; providing manner
in which five-member board shall be elected; and
12 providing an effective date.

16 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 2 O.S. 2021, Section 15-68, is
17 amended to read as follows:
18 Section 15-68. A. There is hereby constituted a board of
19 directors for the free fair association.
20 B. 1. The board of directors shall be composed of nine (9)
21 members, unless the board of county commissioners determines by vote
22 to form a five-member board of directors under subsection I of this
23 section, who shall be by written ballot elected for a term of three
24 (3) years. Three members shall be elected annually, one from each

16 subsection or otherwise indicated in this section. The members
