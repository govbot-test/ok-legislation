7 An Act relating to schools; amending 70 O.S. 2021,

8 Transparency Act; requiring school districts to post
percentage of instructional expenditures on the home
9 page of its website; providing definition of
instructional expenses; providing exclusions to
10 instructional expenses; amending Section 1, Chapter
157, O.S.L. 2023 (70 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section 15-
11 110), which relates to bond elections; requiring
school districts to include percentage of
12 instructional expenditures with any bond proposal;
clarifying applicability; amending 70 O.S. 2021,
13 Section 15-204, which relates to bond guarantee

14 percentage of instructional expenditures with any

15 date; and declaring an emergency.
