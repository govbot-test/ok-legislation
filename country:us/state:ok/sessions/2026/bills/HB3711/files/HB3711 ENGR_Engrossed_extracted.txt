8 Section 15-110. A. When a school district board of education
9 calls an election for the purpose of issuing bonds pursuant to
10 Section 15-102 of Title 70 of the Oklahoma Statutes this title, the
11 school district shall, at least thirty (30) days prior to the
12 election, post on its website a copy of the bond proposal, including

16 instructional expenses in the last fiscal year. "Instructional
17 expenditures" means instruction expenditures as defined by the
18 National Center for Education Statistics, a federal entity within

21 expenditures shall have the same meaning as provided by an
