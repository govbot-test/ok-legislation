11 An Act relating to the Ethics Commission; amending 74
O.S. 2021, Section 4258, which relates to the Ethics
12 Commission Fund; increasing deposit cap amount;
updating reference; providing an effective date; and
13 declaring an emergency.
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 74 O.S. 2021, Section 4258, is
18 amended to read as follows:
19 Section 4258. A. There is hereby created in the State Treasury
20 a revolving fund for the Ethics Commission to be designated the
21 "Ethics Commission Fund". The Ethics Commission Fund shall be a
22 continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations, and
23 deposits to the fund shall consist of the first One Hundred Fifty
24 Thousand Dollars ($150,000.00) Two Hundred Fifty Thousand Dollars
Req. No. 17399 Page 1
1 ($25 0,000.00) of fees received by the Commission each fiscal year.
2 Fees collected each fiscal year in excess of One Hundred Fifty
3 Thousand Dollars ($150,000.00) Two Hundred Fifty Thousand Dollars
4 ($250,000.00) shall be placed to the credit of the Ethics Commission
5 Online Filing Revolving Fund created pursuant to Section 2 4258.2 of
6 this act title.
7 B. All monies accruing to the credit of the Ethics Commission
8 Fund are hereby appropriated and may be budgeted and expended by the
9 Commission for any expenses incurred in the implementation of its
10 duties as provided by law. Expenditures from the fund shall be made
11 upon warrants issued by the State Treasurer against claims filed as
12 prescribed by law with the Director of the Office of Management and
13 Enterprise Services for approval and payment.
14 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
15 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
16 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
17 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
18 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
