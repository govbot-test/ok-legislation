11 PROPOSED COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
12 An Act relating to the Ethics Commission; amending 74
O.S. 2021, Section 4258, which relates to the Ethics
13 Commission Fund; increasing deposit cap amount;
updating reference; providing an effective date; and
14 declaring an emergency.
15
16
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
18 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 74 O.S. 2021, Section 4258, is
19 amended to read as follows:
20 Section 4258. A. There is hereby created in the State Treasury
21 a revolving fund for the Ethics Commission to be designated the
22 "Ethics Commission Fund". The Ethics Commission Fund shall be a
23 continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations, and
24 deposits to the fund shall consist of the first One Hundred Fifty
Req. No. 17374 Page 1
1 Thou sand Dollars ($150,000.00) Two Hundred Fifty Thousand Dollars
2 ($250,000.00) of fees received by the Commission each fiscal year.
3 Fees collected each fiscal year in excess of One Hundred Fifty
4 Thousand Dollars ($150,000.00) Two Hundred Fifty Thousand Dollars
5 ($250,000.00) shall be placed to the credit of the Ethics Commission
6 Online Filing Revolving Fund created pursuant to Section 2 4258.2 of
7 this act title.
8 B. All monies accruing to the credit of the Ethics Commission
9 Fund are hereby appropriated and may be budgeted and expended by the
10 Commission for any expenses incurred in the implementation of its
11 duties as provided by law. Expenditures from the fund shall be made
12 upon warrants issued by the State Treasurer against claims filed as
13 prescribed by law with the Director of the Office of Management and
14 Enterprise Services for approval and payment.
15 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
16 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
17 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
18 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
19 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
