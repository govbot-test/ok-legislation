9 An Act relating to counties and county officers;
amending 19 O.S. 2021, Section 130.1, as amended by
10 Section 1, Chapter 86, O.S.L. 2023 (19 O.S. Supp.
2025, Section 130.1), which relates to the Commission
11 on County Government Personnel Education and
Training; extending sunset date; providing an
12 effective date; and declaring an emergency.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 19 O.S. 2021, Section 130.1, as
17 amended by Section 1, Chapter 86, O.S.L. 2023 (19 O.S. Supp. 2025,
18 Section 130.1), is amended to read as follows:
19 Section 130.1. There is hereby re-created, to continue until
20 July 1, 2026 2031, in accordance with the provisions of the Oklahoma
21 Sunset Law, the Commission on County Government Personnel Education
22 and Training, hereinafter called the "Commission". The Commission
23 shall be composed of the following five (5) members: The President
24 of Oklahoma State University or designee; the State Auditor and
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - HB3002 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 Insp ector or designee; the Director of the Department of
2 Transportation or designee; the Chairman of the Oklahoma Tax
3 Commission or designee; and the President of the Oklahoma County
4 Officers Association or designee.
5 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
6 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
7 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
8 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
9 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
