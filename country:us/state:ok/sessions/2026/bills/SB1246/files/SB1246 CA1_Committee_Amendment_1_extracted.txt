Section 2:
1. On Page 2, Line 3 ½, by inserting a new paragraph 2 to read as follows and renumbering
subsequent paragraphs:
“2. “Contiguous property owner” means the record owner of each immediately contiguous
parcel of land to the land where the proposed permitted facility or activity is located that is not
owned or controlled by the permit applicant. For the purposes of this paragraph, “immediately
contiguous parcel” includes parcels that are on the opposite side of a road or highway, if such road
or highway is on the property boundary, but does not include contiguous parcels that are more than
one-quarter (1/4) mile from the permitted activity that is the primary focus of a permit, such as the
smokestack where proposed air emissions will be emitted, or from the outfall point of a proposed
wastewater discharge, or from any location where proposed solid waste disposal or land application
will occur”; and

Section 3:
2. On Page 6, Line 9, by deleting after the word “all” and before the word “property”, the word
“adjacent” and inserting the word “contiguous”.

I move to amend Senate Bill No. 1246 as follows:
1. On Page 2, Line 3 ½, by inserting a new paragraph 2 to read as follows and renumbering
subsequent paragraphs:
“2. “Contiguous property owner” means the record owner of each immediately contiguous
parcel of land to the land where the proposed permitted facility or activity is located that is not
owned or controlled by the permit applicant. For the purposes of this paragraph, “immediately
contiguous parcel” includes parcels that are on the opposite side of a road or highway, if such road
or highway is on the property boundary, but does not include contiguous parcels that are more than
one-quarter (1/4) mile from the permitted activity that is the primary focus of a permit, such as the
smokestack where proposed air emissions will be emitted, or from the outfall point of a proposed
wastewater discharge, or from any location where proposed solid waste disposal or land application
will occur”; and
2. On Page 6, Line 9, by deleting after the word “all” and before the word “property”, the word
“adjacent” and inserting the word “contiguous”.
