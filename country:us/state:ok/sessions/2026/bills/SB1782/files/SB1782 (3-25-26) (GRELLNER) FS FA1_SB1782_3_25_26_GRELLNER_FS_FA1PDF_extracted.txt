15 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3260 of Title 70, unless there
16 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
17 A. By July 31, 2028, and by July 31 each year thereafter, each
18 institution of higher education within The Oklahoma State System of
19 Higher Education shall electronically submit to the Oklahoma State
20 Regents for Higher Education the following information for the
21 preceding academic year:
