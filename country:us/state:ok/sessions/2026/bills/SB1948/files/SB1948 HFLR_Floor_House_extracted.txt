11 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
12 An Act relating to fireworks; creating the Rocket's
Red Glare Act; amending 68 O.S. 2021, Section 1623,
13 which relates to conditions for storage,
transportation, sale, and use; updating statutory
14 language; removing certain requirements to sell
fireworks within certain time frame; prohibiting
15 counties from restricting certain firework display
under certain circumstances; amending 68 O.S. 2021,
16 Section 1624, as amended by Section 1, Chapter 144,
O.S.L. 2024 (68 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section 1624), which
17 relates to the distribution of skyrockets; removing
the prohibition of certain skyrockets; providing for
18 noncodification; and declaring an emergency.

21 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
22 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
23 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
