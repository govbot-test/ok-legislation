Section 1:
SB1948 FULLAMD3 Scott Fetgatter-MJ
4/13/2026 4:00:06 pm
COMMITTEE AMENDMENT
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
State of Oklahoma
SPEAKER:
CHAIR:
I move to amend SB1948
Page 3 Section 1 Line 9
Of the Engrossed Bill
By deleting the words "a private outdoor firework display" and inserting
in lieu thereof the following:
"private outdoor consumer fireworks"
On Page 3, Section 1, Line 11, by deleting the word "and".
On Page 3, Section 1, Line 13, by inserting after the words "Oklahoma
Statutes" the following language:
"; and 3. There are no red flag fire warnings in effect, meaning:
a. no sustained winds greater than twenty (20) miles per hour,
b. the relative humidity is above twenty-five percent (25%), and
c. the ten (10) hour dead fuel is above nine percent (9%) moisture."
On Page 3, Section 1, Line 14, by inserting a new subsection G which shall
read as follows:
"G. Persons lighting fireworks under the conditions listed in paragraph 3
of subsection H of this section shall notify the local responding fire
department before lighting any fireworks."
AMEND TITLE TO CONFORM TO AMENDMENTS
Amendment submitted by: Scott Fetgatter
Adopted: _____________________________

Reading Clerk
