6 An Act relating to fireworks; amending 68 O.S. 2021,
Section 1623, which relates to conditions for
7 storage, transportation, sale, and use; updating
statutory language; removing certain requirements to
8 sell fireworks within certain time frame; prohibiting
counties from restricting certain firework display
9 under certain circumstances; and providing an
effective date.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 68 O.S. 2021, Section 1623, is
14 amended to read as follows:
15 Section 1623. A. Consumer Fireworks fireworks may be legally
16 stored, transported, sold, and used in this state with the
17 exceptions and conditions specified under the provisions of Section
18 1621 et seq. of this title and consistent with Section 22-110 of
19 Title 11 of the Oklahoma Statutes. Novelties may be legally stored,
20 transported, sold, and used in this state.
21 B. All fireworks storage and sales areas shall be conspicuously
22 posted with signs reading “FIREWORKS-NO SMOKING”.
23 C. Fireworks offered for retail sale must shall be sold
24 according to the National Fire Protection Association (NFPA) 1124

1 G. Nothing in Section 1621 et seq. of this title shall be
