10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
11 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
12 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3-104.12 of Title 70, unless
13 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
14 There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving fund
15 for the State Department of Education to be designated the “Career
16 Counselor Revolving Fund”. The fund shall be a continuing fund, not
17 subject to fiscal year limitations, and shall consist of all monies
18 received by the Department from appropriations, gifts, grants,
19 donations, and bequests provided for the purpose of providing
20 support for career counselors in school districts in this state.
21 All monies accruing to the credit of the fund are hereby
22 appropriated and may be budgeted and expended by the Department for
23 the purpose provided for in this subsection. Expenditures from the
24 fund shall be made upon warrants issued by the State Treasurer
