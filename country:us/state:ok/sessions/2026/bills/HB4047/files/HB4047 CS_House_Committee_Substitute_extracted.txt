11 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Department of
Commerce; making appropriations; identifying sources;
12 stating purposes; providing for noncodification;
providing an effective date; and declaring an
13 emergency.
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
17 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
18 There is hereby appropriated to the Oklahoma Department of
19 Commerce from any monies not otherwise appropriated from the
20 Progressing Rural Economic Prosperity Fund of the State Treasury
21 created in Section 256 of Title 62 of the Oklahoma Statutes, the sum
22 of Two Hundred Fifty Thousand Dollars ($250,000.00) for the purpose
23 of providing resources to an economic development organization with
24 an office located in a town with a population of thirty-five
Req. No. 17408 Page 1
1 thou sand (35,000) people or less per the latest Federal Decennial
2 Census, and located in the central part of the state, to construct
3 and operate a transitional living facility to serve those aging out
4 of foster care or those who are low income.
5 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
6 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
7 There is hereby appropriated to the Oklahoma Department of
8 Commerce from any monies not otherwise appropriated from the
9 Progressing Rural Economic Prosperity Fund of the State Treasury
10 created in Section 256 of Title 62 of the Oklahoma Statutes, the sum
11 of Two Million Dollars ($2,000,000.00) for the purpose of
12 constructing needed facility upgrades, such as fairground arena
13 connectors, electrical infrastructure, and facilities associated
14 with livestock events at a location where statewide events commonly
15 referred to as a "state fair" occurs in the central region of the
16 state.
17 SECTION 3. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
18 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
19 There is hereby appropriated to the Oklahoma Department of
20 Commerce from any monies not otherwise appropriated from the
21 Progressing Rural Economic Prosperity Fund of the State Treasury
22 created in Section 256 of Title 62 of the Oklahoma Statutes, the sum
23 of One Million Seven Hundred Fifty Thousand Dollars ($1,750,000.00)
24 for the purpose of creating energy efficiencies at a university
Req. No. 17408 Page 2
1 camp us located in Western Oklahoma with a student population of less
2 than one thousand one hundred (1,100) students.
3 SECTION 4. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
4 SECTION 5. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
5 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
6 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
7 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
