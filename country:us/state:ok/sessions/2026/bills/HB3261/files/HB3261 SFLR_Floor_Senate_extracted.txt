12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 6-103.16 of Title 70, unless
15 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 A. The State Department of Education shall create and manage a
17 database for registering all school support employees as defined in
18 Section 1-116 of Title 70 of the Oklahoma Statutes. The database
19 may be administered within or integrated with existing educator
20 certification or personnel tracking systems of the Department.
21 B. For each school support employee, the database shall
22 include:
23 1. The full legal name of the support employee;

1 2. The name of the employing school district and school site
2 including the employee’s position or role; and
3 3. A unique, permanent identification number issued by the
4 Department that shall be used to identify the support employee for
5 employment verification and record continuity across school
6 districts and other employment records maintained by the Department.
7 The identification number shall not be reassigned or altered due to
8 a change in employing school district or subsequent employment
9 within the public education system of this state.
10 C. No later than October 1, 2027, and by October 1 annually
11 thereafter, each school district shall submit the information
12 required in paragraphs 1 and 2 of subsection B of this section to
13 the Department for all employed support personnel. Corrections and
14 updates may be made until October 15 annually when the submitted
15 information shall be certified by the school district
16 superintendent.
17 D. Each school district shall update the information required
18 by this section within thirty (30) days of hiring a new support
19 employee and within thirty (30) days of a support employee’s
20 termination or other separation from employment.
21 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
