12 An Act relating to schools; requiring the State
Department of Education to create and manage a
13 database of all school support employees; providing
database content requirements; requiring school
14 districts to submit to the Department certain
information on employed support personnel; mandating
15 school district updates; providing for codification;
and providing an effective date.

20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
21 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
22 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 6-103.16 of Title 70, unless
23 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:

1 A. The State Department of Education shall create and manage a
2 database for registering all school support employees as defined in
3 Section 1-116 of this title. The database may be administered
4 within or integrated with existing educator certification or
5 personnel tracking systems of the Department.
6 B. For each school support employee, the database shall
7 include:
8 1. The full legal name of the support employee;
9 2. The name of the employing school district and school site,
10 including the employee's position or role; and
11 3. A unique, permanent identification number issued by the
12 Department that shall be used to identify the support employee for
13 employment verification and record continuity across school
14 districts and other employment records maintained by the Department,
15 and which shall not be reassigned or altered due to a change in
16 employing school district or subsequent employment within the public
17 education system of this state.
18 C. Not later than October 1, 2026, each school district shall
19 submit the information required in paragraphs 1 and 2 of subsection
20 B of this section to the Department for all employed support
21 personnel.
22 D. Each school district shall update the Department with the
23 information required in this act within thirty (30) days of a new
