updating statutory references; and providing an
9 effective date.

12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 49 O.S. 2021, Section 6.1, is
14 amended to read as follows:
15 Section 6.1. Any individual convicted of violating subsection B
16 of Section 6 of Title 49 of the Oklahoma Statutes this title shall
17 be guilty of a misdemeanor and shall be subject to a fine not to
18 exceed One Thousand Dollars ($1,000.00), imprisonment in the county
19 jail for up to one (1) year, or both such fine and imprisonment.
20 Upon receipt of a final judgment against a notary public for a
21 violation of subsection B of Section 6 of Title 49 of the Oklahoma
22 Statutes this title from a district court of this state or its
23 equivalent from a foreign jurisdiction, the Secretary of State shall
