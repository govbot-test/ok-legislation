6 An Act relating to notaries public; amending 49 O.S.
2021, Section 6.1, which relates to provision of
7 legal advice; modifying allowable punishment;
updating statutory references; and providing an
8 effective date.
9
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 49 O.S. 2021, Section 6.1, is
13 amended to read as follows:
14 Section 6.1. Any individual convicted of violating subsection B
15 of Section 6 of Title 49 of the Oklahoma Statutes this title shall
16 be guilty of a misdemeanor and shall be subject to a fine not to
17 exceed One Thousand Dollars ($1,000.00), imprisonment in the county
18 jail for up to one (1) year, or both such fine and imprisonment.
19 Upon receipt of a final judgment against a notary public for a
20 violation of subsection B of Section 6 of Title 49 of the Oklahoma
21 Statutes this title from a district court of this state or its
22 equivalent from a foreign jurisdiction, the Secretary of State shall
23 revoke the appointment of the notary for a period of eight (8)
24 years.

1 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
