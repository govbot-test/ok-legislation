9 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
10 An Act relating to waters and water rights; amending
27A O.S. 2021, Section 2-6-103.1, which relates to
11 contracts for technical assistance programs;
authorizing the Department of Environmental Quality
12 to contract with certain vendors; authorizing certain
use of funds; stating how contracts shall be awarded;
13 authorizing promulgation of rules and regulations;
and providing an effective date.
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 27A O.S. 2021, Section 2-6-103.1,
18 is amended to read as follows:
19 Section 2-6-103.1. A. The Department of Environmental Quality
20 may, to the extent funds are available, contract with the Oklahoma
21 Rural Water Association and technically qualified vendors, as
22 defined in the Department’s regulations, for technical assistance
23 programs to rural water and wastewater system operators throughout
24 the state. The Department may use a portion of the funds
Req. No. 3887 Page 1
1 appr opriated for grants and assistance programs to rural water and
2 small systems to contract with technically qualified vendors to
3 provide technical assistance programs to rural water and wastewater
4 system operators throughout the state. Assistance in response to
5 drinking water or wastewater emergencies is also eligible. The
6 Department may contract with other nonprofit entities in addition to
7 the Oklahoma Rural Water Association or technically qualified
8 vendors for this purpose if the Department, in its discretion,
9 determines that those entities can and will provide equally
10 effective and efficient technical assistance services to rural water
11 and wastewater system operators throughout the state. Contracts
12 shall be awarded to the lowest and best, or best value, bidder or
13 bidders. The exceptions and exemptions to the Oklahoma Central
14 Purchasing Act shall not apply to the contracts awarded under this
15 section.
16 B. The Environmental Quality Board shall promulgate rules and
17 regulations for the Department of Environmental Quality that define
18 the technical qualifications of vendors and to carry out the
19 provisions of this section.
20 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
