11 An Act relating to waters and water rights; amending
27A O.S. 2021, Section 2-6-103.1, which relates to
12 contracts for technical assistance programs;
authorizing the Department of Environmental Quality
13 to contract with certain suppliers; authorizing the
Department to use a portion of certain funds to
14 contract with certain parties; and providing an
effective date.
15
16
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
18 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 27A O.S. 2021, Section 2-6-103.1,
19 is amended to read as follows:
20 Section 2-6-103.1. The Department of Environmental Quality may,
21 to the extent funds are available, contract with the Oklahoma Rural
22 Water Association or qualified state suppliers for technical
23 assistance programs to rural water and wastewater system operators
24 throughout the state. The Department may use a portion of the funds
HB4246 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 appr opriated for grants and assistance programs to rural water and
2 small systems to contract with qualified state suppliers to provide
3 technical assistance programs to rural water and wastewater systems
4 operators throughout the state. Assistance in response to drinking
5 water or wastewater emergencies is also eligible. The Department
6 may contract with other nonprofit entities in addition to the
7 Oklahoma Rural Water Association or qualified state suppliers for
8 this purpose if the Department, in its discretion, determines that
9 those entities can and will provide equally effective and efficient
10 technical assistance services to rural water and wastewater system
11 operators throughout the state.
12 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
