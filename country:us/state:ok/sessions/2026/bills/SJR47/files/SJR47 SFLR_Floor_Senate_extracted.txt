17 SECTION 1. The Secretary of State shall refer to the people for
18 their approval or rejection, as and in the manner provided by law,
19 the following proposed amendment to the Oklahoma Constitution by
20 adding a new Section 6 to Article III to read as follows:
21 Section 6. Each voter shall present proof of identity to the
22 appropriate election official when voting at any election authorized
23 by law and conducted by a county election board or the State
24 Election Board. Proof of identity shall be required for any method
