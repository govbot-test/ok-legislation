8 PROPOSED COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
9 An Act relating to state government; amending 74 O.S.
2021, Section 5401, which relates to the Oklahoma
10 Strategic Military Planning Commission; re-creating
the Commission; providing a date of dissolution; and
11 declaring an emergency.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 74 O.S. 2021, Section 5401, is
16 amended to read as follows:
17 Section 5401. A. There is hereby re-created the Oklahoma
18 Strategic Military Planning Commission.
19 B. The Oklahoma Strategic Military Planning Commission shall
20 consist of nine (9) members as follows:
21 1. Five persons to be appointed by the Governor, each of whom
22 shall represent, respectively, the interests of communities that
23 would be affected by realignment or closure of the following
24 military installations:
Req. No. 16079 Page 1
1 a. Altus Air Force Base,
2 b. Vance Air Force Base,
3 c. Fort Sill,
4 d. the Army Ammunition Plant located near McAlester, and
5 e. Tinker Air Force Base;
6 2. One person to be appointed by the Speaker of the House of
7 Representatives from the membership of the House and one person to
8 be appointed by the President Pro Tempore of the Senate from the
9 membership of the Senate. The members appointed pursuant to this
10 paragraph shall be ex officio and nonvoting members of the
11 Commission; and
12 3. The Secretary of Veterans Affairs, or successor member of
13 the Governor's Executive Cabinet who has responsibility for Veterans
14 Affairs, and the Adjutant General of the Oklahoma Army and Air
15 National Guard. The members appointed pursuant to this paragraph
16 shall be ex officio and nonvoting members of the Commission.
17 C. The members of the Commission shall serve at the pleasure of
18 the appointing authority. The members of the Commission shall
19 select from among their membership a chair and vice-chair. The
20 chair and vice-chair shall serve for a period of one (1) year and
21 may succeed themselves in office.
22 D. The Commission shall be subject to the provisions of the
23 Oklahoma Open Meeting Act and the Oklahoma Open Records Act.
24
Req. No. 16079 Page 2
1 E. The Commission shall be authorized to meet at such times as
2 may be required in order to fulfill the duties and responsibilities
3 imposed upon it by law.
4 F. The provisions of this section shall cease to have the force
5 and effect of law and the Commission shall be dissolved by operation
6 of law on December 31, 2025 2030.
7 G. The members of the Commission shall not be deemed to be
8 officers or to hold public office for purposes of Section 6 of Title
9 51 of the Oklahoma Statutes.
10 SECTION 2. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
11 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
12 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
13 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
