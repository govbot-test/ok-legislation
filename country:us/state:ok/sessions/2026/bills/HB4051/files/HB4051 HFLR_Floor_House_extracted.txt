16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 63 O.S. 2021, Section 5020A, is
18 amended to read as follows:
19 Section 5020A. A. There is hereby created in the State
20 Treasury a fund for the Oklahoma Health Care Authority to be
21 designated the "Rate Preservation Fund". The fund shall be a
22 continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations, and shall
23 consist of monies designated to the fund by law. All monies
24 accruing to the credit of the fund are hereby appropriated and may
