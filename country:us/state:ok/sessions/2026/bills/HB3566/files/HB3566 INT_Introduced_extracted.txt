4 pursuant to Section 2103 of Title 68 of the Oklahoma Statutes for
5 each electric vehicle but such amount shall not be in addition to
6 any other amount otherwise authorized by this section to be retained
7 with respect to a vehicle; and

16 B. Through June 30, 2025, for each certificate of registration
17 issued for boats and motors, each licensed operator shall be
18 entitled to retain the greater of One Dollar and twenty-five cents
19 ($1.25) or an amount to be determined by Service Oklahoma according
20 to the provisions of this subsection. At the end of fiscal year
21 1997 and each fiscal year thereafter, Service Oklahoma shall compute
22 the average amount of registration fees for all boats and motors
23 registered in this state during the fiscal year and shall multiply
24 the result by six and twenty-two one-hundredths percent (6.22%).

1 The resulting product shall be the amount which may be retained by
2 each licensed operator for each certificate of registration for
3 boats and motors issued during the following calendar year.
4 C. When an application for registration is made with Service
5 Oklahoma, the Corporation Commission or a licensed operator, a
6 registration fee of One Dollar and seventy-five cents ($1.75) shall
7 be collected for each license plate or decal issued. Such fees
8 shall be in addition to the registration fees on motor vehicles and
9 when an application for registration is made to the licensed
10 operator, such licensed operator shall retain a fee as provided in
11 Section 1141.1 of this title through June 30, 2023. Beginning July
12 1, 2023, the fee shall be retained by the licensed operator pursuant
13 to subsection E of Section 1141.1 of this title. When the fee is
14 paid by a person making application directly with Service Oklahoma
15 or the Corporation Commission, as applicable, the registration fees
16 shall be in the same amount as provided for licensed operators and
17 the fee provided by this section shall be deposited in the Service
18 Oklahoma Revolving Fund or as provided in Section 1167 of this
19 title, as applicable. Service Oklahoma shall prepare schedules of
20 registration fees and charges for titles, which shall include the
21 fees for licensed operators, and all fees and charges paid by a
22 person shall be listed separately on the application and
23 registration and totaled on the application and registration. The
24 licensed operators shall charge only such fees as are specifically
Req. No. 15302 Page 6
1 prov ided for by law, and all such authorized fees shall be posted in
2 such a manner that any person shall have notice of all fees that are
3 imposed by law.
4 D. Unless otherwise provided, beginning July 1, 2025, the
5 provisions related to the reimbursement, retention, apportionment,
6 or distribution of funds to or by licensed operators as outlined in
7 this section shall be retained by the licensed operator pursuant to
8 subsection E of this section.
9 E. Beginning July 1, 2023, unless otherwise provided, licensed
10 operator compensation shall be fixed by Service Oklahoma pursuant to
11 Section 3-103 of this title.
12 1. For fiscal year beginning July 1, 2023, through the fiscal
13 year ending on June 30, 2025:
14 a. licensed operators shall be entitled to retain
15 nineteen percent (19%) of all fees collected related
16 to registrations provided by the Oklahoma Vehicle
17 License and Registration Act,
18 b. licensed operators shall be entitled to retain forty
19 percent (40%) of all fees collected related to the
20 issuance of Class A, Class B, Class C, and Class D
21 driver licenses, permits, and identification cards,
22 including REAL ID Compliant and REAL ID Noncompliant
23 credentials, pursuant to Section 6-101 et seq. of this
24 title, and
Req. No. 15302 Page 7
1 c. licensed operators shall be entitled to retain Three
2 Dollars and fifty-six cents ($3.56) for the annual
3 renewal of each frac tank, construction machinery,
4 rental trailer, commercial trailer or semitrailer
5 registered pursuant to Section 1133 of this title.
6 2. All other fees directed by this section shall default back
7 to the apportionment outlined in Section 1104 of this title.
8 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
