9 An Act relating to schools; requiring the State
Department of Education to create a four-year plan to
10 address Department programming needs; providing plan
requirements; requiring annual plan submission to the
11 Governor and Legislature; requiring the Department of
Education to promulgate rules; and providing an
12 effective date.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3-104.12 of Title 70, unless
18 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 A. The State Department of Education shall prepare and adopt a
20 comprehensive four-year plan for the purpose of addressing the State
21 Department of Education's programming needs and development. Such
22 plan shall be a continuous plan, reviewed and updated annually.
23 B. The plan shall include, but not be limited to:
24
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - HB3029 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 1. A statement of the State Department of Education's long-term
2 goals and short-term objectives for current and future programs;
3 2. A description of proposed new programs, including how such
4 programs would operate;
5 3. An analysis of cost implications and measurement criteria
6 for current program needs and future program development; and
7 4. Specific action plans, target dates, strategies, and methods
8 of implementation for achieving the identified goals and objectives.
9 C. Before December 1 of each year, the comprehensive four-year
10 plan shall be submitted to the Governor, the Speaker of the Oklahoma
11 House of Representatives, and the President Pro Tempore of the
12 Oklahoma State Senate.
13 D. The State Department of Education shall promulgate rules to
14 implement the provisions of this act.
15 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective September 1, 2026.
16 COMMITTEE REPORT BY: COMMITTEE ON EDUCATION
