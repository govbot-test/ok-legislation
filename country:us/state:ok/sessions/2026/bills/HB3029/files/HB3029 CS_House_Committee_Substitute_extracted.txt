8 An Act relating to schools; requiring the State
Department of Education to create a four-year plan to
9 address Department programming needs; providing plan
requirements; requiring annual plan submission to the
10 Governor and Legislature; requiring the Department of
Education to promulgate rules; and providing an
11 effective date.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
16 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3-104.12 of Title 70, unless
17 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
18 A. The State Department of Education shall prepare and adopt a
19 comprehensive four-year plan for the purpose of addressing the State
20 Department of Education's programing needs and development. Such
21 plan shall be a continuous plan, reviewed and updated annually.
22 B. The plan shall include, but not be limited to:
23 1. A statement of the State Department of Education's long-term
24 goals and short-term objectives for current and future programs;
Req. No. 17008 Page 1

1 2. A description of proposed new programs, including how such
2 programs would operate;
3 3. An analysis of cost implications and measurement criteria
4 for current program needs and future program development; and
5 4. Specific action plans, target dates, strategies, and methods
6 of implementation for achieving the identified goals and objectives.
7 C. Before December 1 of each year, the comprehensive four-year
8 plan shall be submitted to the Governor, the Speaker of the Oklahoma
9 House of Representatives, and the President Pro Tempore of the
10 Oklahoma State Senate.
11 D. The State Department of Education shall promulgate rules to
12 implement the provisions of this act.
13 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective September 1, 2026.
