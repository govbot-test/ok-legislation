6 Section 1686.1. A. The Construction Industries Board is hereby
7 authorized to establish a license category for contractors who

11 wiring for a structure or electrical facility that is not directly
12 involved in the operation of the environmentally controlled poultry
13 house wiring system. In addition to other statutory authority, the
14 Construction Industries Board shall have the power to exercise all
15 incidental powers and duties necessary to effectuate the provisions
16 of the poultry house contractor license, conduct investigations
17 involving compliance with the licensing requirements, and enforce
18 the licensing provisions of this section. However, the Construction
19 Industries Board is not authorized to conduct an electrical code
20 inspection of an environmentally controlled poultry house premise
21 wiring system for purposes of compliance with electrical code
22 installation standards established for the Electrical License Act or
23 to issue administrative citations or fines concerning electrical

7 contracting only for wiring of environmentally controlled poultry
8 house systems and is not intended to disallow or exclude unlimited

11 house contractor license.
