An Act relating to retirement; amending 74 O.S. 2021,
Section 920, which relates to the Oklahoma Public
Employees Retirement System; modifying employer
contribution amount for certain time frame; providing
an effective date; and declaring an emergency.
SUBJECT: Retirement
BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 74 O.S. 2021, Section 920, is
amended to read as follows:
Section 920. (1) Effective July 1, 1994, every state agency
which is a participating employer shall contribute to the System an
amount equal to eleven and one-half percent (11 1/2%) of the monthly
compensation of each member, but not in excess of Forty Thousand
Dollars ($40,000.00).
(2) Effective July 1, 1995, every state agency which is a
participating employer shall contribute to the System an amount
equal to eleven and one-half percent (11 1/2%) of the monthly
compensation of each member, not to exceed the allowable annual
compensation as defined in paragraph (9) of Section 902 of this
title.
(3) Effective July 1, 1996, every state agency which is a
participating employer shall contribute to the System an amount
equal to twelve percent (12%) of the monthly compensation of each
member, not to exceed the allowable annual compensation defined in
paragraph (9) of Section 902 of this title.

(4) Effective July 1, 1999, and through the fiscal year ending
June 30, 2005, every state agency which is a participating employer
shall contribute to the System an amount equal to ten percent (10%)
of the monthly compensation of each member, not to exceed the
allowable annual compensation defined in paragraph (9) of Section
902 of this title.
(5) Effective July 1, 2005, except as otherwise provided by
subsection (11) (13) of this section, every state agency which is a
participating employer shall contribute an amount to the System
equal to a percentage of monthly compensation of each member, not to
exceed the allowable annual compensation defined in paragraph (9) of
Section 902 of this title as follows:
July 1, 2005 – June 30, 2006 11 1/2%
July 1, 2006 – June 30, 2007 12 1/2%
July 1, 2007 – June 30, 2008 13 1/2%
July 1, 2008 – June 30, 2009 14 1/2%
July 1, 2009 – June 30, 2011 15 1/2%
July 1, 2011 – June 30, 2012 2026
and each year thereafter 16 1/2%
(6) Beginning July 1, 2026, through June 30, 2031, except as
otherwise provided by subsection (13) of this section, every state
agency which is a participating employer shall contribute an amount
to the System equal to nine and one-half percent (9 1/2%) of monthly
compensation of each member, not to exceed the allowable annual
compensation defined in paragraph (9) of Section 902 of this title.
(7) Beginning July 1, 2031, except as otherwise provided by
subsection (13) of this section, every state agency which is a
participating employer shall contribute an amount to the System
equal to sixteen and one-half percent (16 1/2%) of monthly
compensation of each member, not to exceed the allowable annual
compensation defined in paragraph (9) of Section 902 of this title.
(8) The Board shall certify, on or before November 1 of each
year, to the Office of Management and Enterprise Services an

(11) (13) Effective November 1, 2015, an employer shall be
required to make payment to the Oklahoma Public Employees Retirement
System of the amount described by subsection A of Section 10 935.10
of this act title with respect to any employee who is a participant
in the defined contribution system created pursuant to the
provisions of Sections 1 935.1 through 11 935.11 of this act title.
The employer shall be required to make the required matching
contribution amount for all employees that participate in the
defined contribution system and to remit the difference between such
amount and the amount the employer would otherwise have paid
pursuant to the provisions of this section to the Oklahoma Public
Employees Retirement System.
SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
