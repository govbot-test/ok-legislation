8 An Act relating to damages; amending 12 O.S. 2021,
Section 3009.1, which relates to personal injury
9 suits; defining terms; prohibiting certain damages

10 evidence to establish damages; requiring disclosure

11 date.

22 drugs, prescriptions, devices, or products provided or applied to a
23 plaintiff by a health care provider or consumed by a plaintiff at a
24 health care provider’s direction.
