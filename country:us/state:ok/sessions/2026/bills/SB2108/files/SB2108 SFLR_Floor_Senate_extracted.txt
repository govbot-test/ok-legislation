8 An Act relating to feral swine; amending 2 O.S. 2021,
Section 6-604, which relates to the removal of feral
9 swine; requiring permission from landowner or lessee
to remove or take feral swine on certain property;
10 prohibiting the transportation, importation, and
release of live feral swine; providing certain
11 exemptions; providing penalties; amending 29 O.S.
2021, Sections 4-107.2, as amended by Section 1,
12 Chapter 77, O.S.L. 2018, 4-135, as last amended by
Section 1, Chapter 198, O.S.L. 2023, and Section 3,
13 Chapter 171, O.S.L. 2022 (29 O.S. Supp. 2025,
Sections 4-135 and 4-135.2), which relate to control
14 of nuisance wildlife; removing ability to maintain
certain firearm for nuisance control; permitting the
15 nuisance control without permit under certain
circumstances; prohibiting certain times to shoot
16 feral swine; authorizing revocation of license upon
conviction; making language gender neutral; updating
17 statutory language and references; repealing 2 O.S.
2021, Section 6-605, which relates to special permit
18 for private property owner to kill feral swine;
providing for codification; and providing an
19 effective date.

10 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
11 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 6-615 of Title 2, unless there
12 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
13 A. As used in this section, the terms “feral swine,” “remove,”
14 and “Judas pig tagging system” shall have the same meanings as
15 provided in Section 6-603 of Title 2 of the Oklahoma Statutes.
16 B. It shall be unlawful for any person to:
17 1. Transport or relocate any live feral swine within this
18 state;
19 2. Import any live feral swine into this state; or
20 3. Intentionally release any feral swine to live in a wild or
21 feral state upon any public or private land.
22 C. The prohibitions in subsection B of this section shall not
23 apply to:

20 Dollars ($2,000.00) nor more than Five Thousand Dollars ($5,000.00),

23 imprisonment. In addition, upon conviction, the court may order the
