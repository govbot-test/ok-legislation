Section 1:
1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 2nd Session of the 60th Legislature (2026)
4
5
6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to school bonds; creating one-year
waiting period for new school bonds; clarifying one-
8 year waiting period does not apply to nondebt capital
options; exempting destroyed school facilities;
9 providing for codification; and providing an
effective date.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 15-102A of Title 70, unless
15 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 A. No election for the purpose of issuing bonds pursuant to
17 Section 15-102 of Title 70 of the Oklahoma Statutes shall be called
18 within three hundred sixty-five (365) days after the date which all
19 bonded indebtedness has been certified by the district treasurer as
20 retired and no longer outstanding. All bonds issued pursuant to
21 Section 26 of Article X of the Oklahoma Constitution on or before
22 the effective date of this act shall be considered existing
23 indebtedness for the purposes of this section.
24
Req. No. 14082 Page 1
1 B. The waiting period in subsection A of this section shall not
2 apply to or prevent a school district from pursuing nondebt capital
3 options including, but not limited to:
4 1. Private donations;
5 2. Lease-purchase agreements not subject to Section 26 of
6 Article X of the Oklahoma Constitution; and
7 3. Public-private construction partnerships as permitted by
8 law.
9 C. The waiting period in subsection A of this section shall not
10 apply where the school facilities have been destroyed by an act of
11 God.
12 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
