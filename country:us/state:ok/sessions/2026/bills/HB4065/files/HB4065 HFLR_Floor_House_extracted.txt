12 An Act relating to the Oklahoma School of Science and
Mathematics; appropriating certain funds in certain
13 amount for certain purpose; directing utilization of
certain amounts for certain purposes; providing for
14 noncodification; providing an effective date; and
declaring an emergency.

16
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
18 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
19 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
20 There is hereby appropriated to the Oklahoma School of Science
21 and Mathematics from any monies not otherwise appropriated from the
22 General Revenue Fund of the State Treasury for the fiscal year
23 ending June 30, 2027, the sum of Ninety-three Thousand Dollars
24 ($93,000.00) or so much thereof as may be necessary to perform the

1 duti es imposed upon the Oklahoma School of Science and Mathematics
2 by law.
3 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
4 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
5 Funds appropriated by Section 1 of this act shall be utilized as
6 follows: Ninety-three Thousand Dollars ($93,000.00) to establish
7 and maintain a School Resource Officer Program or provide physical
8 security enhancements including, but not limited to, school resource
9 officers, cameras, gates, lighting, locks, doors, windows, security
10 geofencing, ballistic storm shelters, and mobile panic alert
11 systems.
12 SECTION 3. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
13 SECTION 4. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
14 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
15 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
16 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
