6 An Act relating to counties; amending 19 O.S. 2021,

Section 2:
Section 270, which relates to confidentiality of
7 Department of Defense Form 214; authorizing
grandchildren of veterans to view certain military
8 discharge papers; updating statutory reference; and
providing an effective date.
9
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 19 O.S. 2021, Section 270, is
13 amended to read as follows:
14 Section 270. A. The county clerk shall keep any Department of
15 Defense Form 214 (DD Form 214) military discharge papers filed with
16 the clerk, including any DD Form 214 military discharge papers that
17 was were filed before the effective date of this act July 1, 2002,
18 separate from records available for public inspection and shall not
19 make the DD Form 214 military discharge papers available to any
20 person except as provided in this section. A record of the names of
21 veterans who have filed DD Forms 214 military discharge papers with
22 the clerk shall be made available for public inspection.
23 B. The county clerk may authorize the viewing or copying of a
24 veteran’s DD Form 214 military discharge papers only by the veteran,
ENGR. S. B. NO. 2026 Page 1
1 the veteran’s spouse or, child, or grandchild, a guardian for or a
2 person having power of attorney for the veteran, spouse, or child,
3 or grandchild upon presentation of a court order showing the
4 appointment, the executor or administrator of the estate of the
5 veteran or spouse upon presentation of a court order showing the
6 appointment, a representative of the United States Department of
7 Veterans Affairs, a funeral director upon presentation of a contract
8 signed by the person responsible for the funeral costs for the
9 veteran, or a person authorized by the court to view or copy the DD
10 Form 214 military discharge papers upon presentation of the court
11 order. The clerk shall record the names and addresses of all
12 persons viewing or copying a DD Form 214 military discharge papers.
13 C. A county clerk shall not be liable or responsible for any
14 harm or damages that may occur as a result of any person obtaining,
15 copying or viewing a DD Form 214 military discharge papers.
16 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
