Section 2:
Section 270, which relates to confidentiality of
10 Department of Defense Form 214; authorizing
grandchildren of veterans to view certain military
11 discharge papers; updating statutory reference; and
providing an effective date.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 19 O.S. 2021, Section 270, is
16 amended to read as follows:
17 Section 270. A. The county clerk shall keep any Department of
18 Defense Form 214 (DD Form 214) military discharge papers filed with
19 the clerk, including any DD Form 214 military discharge papers that
20 was were filed before the effective date of this act July 1, 2002,
21 separate from records available for public inspection and shall not
22 make the DD Form 214 military discharge papers available to any
23 person except as provided in this section. A record of the names of
24

BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 vete rans who have filed DD Forms 214 military discharge papers with
2 the clerk shall be made available for public inspection.
3 B. The county clerk may authorize the viewing or copying of a
4 veteran’s DD Form 214 military discharge papers only by the veteran,
5 the veteran’s spouse or, child, or grandchild, a guardian for or a
6 person having power of attorney for the veteran, spouse, or child,
7 or grandchild upon presentation of a court order showing the
8 appointment, the executor or administrator of the estate of the
9 veteran or spouse upon presentation of a court order showing the
10 appointment, a representative of the United States Department of
11 Veterans Affairs, a funeral director upon presentation of a contract
12 signed by the person responsible for the funeral costs for the
13 veteran, or a person authorized by the court to view or copy the DD
14 Form 214 military discharge papers upon presentation of the court
15 order. The clerk shall record the names and addresses of all
16 persons viewing or copying a DD Form 214 military discharge papers.
17 C. A county clerk shall not be liable or responsible for any
18 harm or damages that may occur as a result of any person obtaining,
19 copying or viewing a DD Form 214 military discharge papers.
20 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
