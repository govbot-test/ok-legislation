7 An Act relating to Medicaid; directing the Oklahoma

12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 4012 of Title 56, unless there
15 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 A. The Oklahoma Health Care Authority shall file a Medicaid
17 waiver or state plan amendment, as necessary, on or before January
18 1, 2027, to impose a cost-sharing requirement of Thirty-five Dollars
19 ($35.00) per provision of care, items, or services for expansion
20 enrollees, subject to federal limitations on cost-sharing and
21 eligibility limitations under 42 U.S.C. Section 1396a. The Oklahoma
22 Health Care Authority shall not reduce the cost-sharing requirement
23 for an expansion enrollee's nonemergency use of emergency
24 departments below Thirty-five Dollars ($35.00). The total aggregate

1 amou nt of cost-sharing charges imposed under the state plan for all
2 individuals in the family may not exceed five percent (5%) of the
3 family income, as applied on a quarterly basis.
4 B. A provider participating under the state plan may require as
5 a condition for the provision of care, items, or services to an
6 expansion enrollee, payment of cost-sharing charges under the state
7 plan pursuant to subsection A of this section. The amount of any
8 required payment of cost-sharing for an expansion enrollee shall be
9 deducted from the payment to a participating provider, regardless of
10 whether that provider elects to require payment of such charges.
11 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
