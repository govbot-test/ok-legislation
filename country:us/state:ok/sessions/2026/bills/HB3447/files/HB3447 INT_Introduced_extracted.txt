Section 1:
1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 2nd Session of the 60th Legislature (2026)
4
5
6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to education; directing the board of
education of any school district furnishing
8 transportation to furnish transportation to child
care facilities that meet certain requirements;
9 providing condition; permitting districts to enter
into agreements; providing how transportation expense
10 may be paid; prohibiting money from being chargeable
or becoming part of the district's finances;
11 providing for codification; and providing an
effective date.
12
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 9-120 of Title 70, unless there
18 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 A. The board of education of any school district furnishing
20 transportation is hereby directed to furnish transportation to child
21 care facilities within the district for children attending the
22 schools of that district. Provided, however, that such child care
23 facility shall be on a transportation route already taken.
24
Req. No. 14119 Page 1
1 B. Two or more districts may enter into agreements for the
2 furnishing of such transportation.
3 C. The expense of any such additional transportation shall be
4 paid by the children so transported or from other private sources.
5 Money so collected shall not be chargeable to or become a part of
6 the school district's finances.
7 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
