1 THE GIST OF THE PROPOSITION IS AS FOLLOWS:
2 This measure amends Section 8B of Article 10 of the Oklahoma
3 Constitution. This measure decreases the limit on the annual
4 increase of the fair cash value of certain real property for ad
5 valorem tax purposes. Currently in the Constitution, the fair
6 cash value of real property that is used for agricultural
7 purposes or that qualifies for a homestead exemption may not
8 increase by more than three percent (3%) each year. This
9 measure would reduce that annual increase to two percent (2%).
