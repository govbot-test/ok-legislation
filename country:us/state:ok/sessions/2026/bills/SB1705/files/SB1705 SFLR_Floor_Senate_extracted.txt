6 An Act relating to cities and towns; prohibiting
political subdivisions from spending public funds to
7 pay nonprofit organizations to pay defendants’ bail;
providing for injunctive relief; providing for award
8 of attorney fees and costs; providing for
codification; and providing an effective date.
9
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
13 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 27-117.2 of Title 11, unless
14 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
15 A. A political subdivision may not spend public funds to pay a
16 nonprofit organization that accepts and uses donations from the
17 public to deposit money with a court in the amount of a defendant’s
18 bail bond.
19 B. If a political subdivision engages in an activity prohibited
20 by subsection A of this section, a taxpayer or resident of the
21 political subdivision is entitled to appropriate injunctive relief
22 to prevent further activity prohibited by subsection A of this
23 section and further payment of public funds related to that
24 activity.
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - SB1705 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 C. A party that prevails in an action under subsection B of
2 this section is entitled to recover the party’s reasonable attorney
3 fees and court costs.
4 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
