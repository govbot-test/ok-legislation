1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 2nd Session of the 60th Legislature (2026)
3 HOUSE BILL 3205 By: Humphrey
4
5
6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to state government; requiring the
Attorney General to take action to protect certain
8 agricultural and property rights; providing for
codification; and providing an effective date.
9
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
13 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 20.1 of Title 74, unless there
14 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
15 The Attorney General shall, to the best of his or her ability,
16 take appropriate action, which shall include pursuing criminal and
17 civil actions against individuals, organizations, companies, and any
18 other entity that the Attorney General reasonably believes is
19 harming, disrupting, stopping or interfering with Oklahoma ranchers,
20 livestock production, and farmer and rancher property rights.
21 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
