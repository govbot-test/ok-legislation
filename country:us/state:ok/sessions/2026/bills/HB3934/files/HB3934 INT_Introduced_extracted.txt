6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to professions and occupations;
creating extended care licensed dental hygienists;
8 providing for qualifications; creating three levels
of licensure; limiting number of extended permits by
9 a supervising dentist; describing scope of practice;
requiring continuing education credits; allowing for
10 Medicaid coverage; providing for codification; and
providing an effective date.
