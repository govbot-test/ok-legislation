8 An Act relating to torts; amending 76 O.S. 2021,

Section 2:
Section 52.1, which relates to liability of firearm
9 manufacturers; expanding scope of liability exemption
to include component parts; and providing an
10 effective date.
11
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 76 O.S. 2021, Section 52.1, is
16 amended to read as follows:
17 Section 52.1. No firearm manufacturer, distributor, or seller
18 who lawfully manufactures, distributes, or sells a firearm or
19 component parts for a firearm is liable to any person or entity, or
20 to the estate, successors, or survivors of either, for any injury
21 suffered, including wrongful death and property damage, because of
22 use of such firearm by another or because aftermarket modifications
23 using component parts were made by another to such firearm.
24 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
