11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
13 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 11-203 of Title 70, unless there
14 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
15 A. As used in this section:
16 1. “Harmful to minors” has the same meaning as provided in
17 Section 1040.75 of Title 21 of the Oklahoma Statutes;
18 2. “Library” means a school library, media program, classroom
19 library, or any other collection of books or other materials, print
20 or digital, that are maintained by a school district or charter
21 school or its employees for use by students and that do not qualify
22 as textbooks approved by the State Textbook Committee pursuant to
23 Sections 16-101 through 16-124 of Title 70 of the Oklahoma Statutes;

23 including the time, date, and location of the alleged violation and
24 the identity of any person involved.
