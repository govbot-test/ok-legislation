13 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES AND THE SENATE OF THE
14 2ND SESSION OF THE 60TH OKLAHOMA LEGISLATURE:
15 SECTION 1. The Secretary of State shall refer to the people for
16 their approval or rejection, as and in the manner provided by law,
17 the following proposed amendment to the Constitution of the State of
18 Oklahoma by adding a new Section 44 to Article X thereof, to read as
19 follows:
20 This section shall be known and may be cited as the "Funding the
21 Future Resolution of 2026".
22 SECTION 2. The Ballot Title for the proposed Constitutional
23 amendment as set forth in SECTION 1 of this resolution shall be in
24 the following form:

1 BALLOT TITLE

3 THE GIST OF THE PROPOSITION IS AS FOLLOWS:
4 This measure creates the Funding the Future Resolution of 2026.
5 SHALL THE PROPOSAL BE APPROVED?
6 FOR THE PROPOSAL — YES _____________
7 AGAINST THE PROPOSAL — NO _____________
8 SECTION 3. The Chief Clerk of the House of Representatives,
9 immediately after the passage of this resolution, shall prepare and
10 file one copy thereof, including the Ballot Title set forth in
11 SECTION 2 hereof, with the Secretary of State and one copy with the
12 Attorney General.
