11 POLICY COMMITTEE RECOMMENDATION
12 An Act relating to alcoholic beverage licenses;
amending 37A O.S. 2021, Section 2-115, which relates
13 to special event licenses; requiring certain
applicant to hold certain liability insurance;
14 updating statutory language; and providing an
effective date.

16

18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
19 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 37A O.S. 2021, Section 2-115, is
20 amended to read as follows:
21 Section 2-115. A. A special event license may be issued to an
22 organization, association, or nonprofit corporation organized for
23 political, fraternal, charitable, religious, or social purposes.
24 The holder of a special event license is authorized to sell and
Req. No. 17345 Page 1

1 dist ribute alcoholic beverage beverages on the premises for which
2 the license is issued.
3 B. The ABLE Alcoholic Beverage Laws Enforcement (ABLE)
4 Commission shall promulgate rules governing the application for and
5 the issuance of special event licenses. Such application shall
6 require the applicant to hold general liability insurance, including
7 liquor liability coverage, in an amount not less than One Million
8 Dollars ($1,000,000.00) per occurrence and Two Million Dollars
9 ($2,000,000.00) aggregate.
10 C. The restrictions and rules which apply to the sale of mixed
11 beverages on the premises of a mixed beverage licensee also apply to
12 the sale of such beverages under the authority of a special event
13 license. Any act which if done on the premises of a mixed beverage
14 licensee would be a ground for revocation or suspension of the mixed
15 beverage license is a ground for revocation or suspension of a
16 special event license.
17 D. No special event license may be issued for any premises
18 already licensed by the ABLE Commission.
19 E. No special event license shall be required for an
20 organization, association, or nonprofit corporation which is an
21 economic development chamber or similar entity, provided the event
22 is not conducted primarily for fundraising purposes, and provided
23 the services of a licensed caterer are used to provide and
24 distribute the alcoholic beverages at the event.
