7 AS INTRODUCED
8 A Resolution designating the second week in April as
Lung Cancer Action Week; and expressing the
9 significance of lung cancer awareness.

11
12 WHEREAS, the American Lung Association has been working to
13 ensure that fewer people are diagnosed with lung cancer and those
14 living with the disease live longer; and
15 WHEREAS, lung cancer is the leading cause of cancer deaths; and
16 WHEREAS, lung cancer screening saves lives, and advocacy and
17 increased awareness will result in more high-risk individuals
18 getting screened; and
19 WHEREAS, an individual is deemed high-risk if they are aged 50-
20 80 years old and have a smoking history of 20 or more pack years;
21 and
22 WHEREAS, public support for research funding will lead to new
23 treatments and better methods of early detection.
