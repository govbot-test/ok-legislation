RESOLUTION 1045 By: Roe
4
5
6 AS INTRODUCED
Lung Cancer Action Week; and expressing the
8 significance of lung cancer awareness.
9
10
11 WHEREAS, the American Lung Association has been working to
12 ensure that fewer people are diagnosed with lung cancer and those
13 living with the disease live longer; and
14 WHEREAS, lung cancer is the leading cause of cancer deaths; and
15 WHEREAS, lung cancer screening saves lives, and advocacy and
16 increased awareness will result in more high-risk individuals
17 getting screened; and
18 WHEREAS, an individual is deemed high-risk if they are aged 50-
19 80 years old and have a smoking history of 20 or more pack years;
20 and
21 WHEREAS, public support for research funding will lead to new
22 treatments and better methods of early detection.
23 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
24 OF THE 2ND SESSION OF THE 60TH OKLAHOMA LEGISLATURE:
Req. No. 17248 Page 1
1 THAT the Oklahoma House of Representatives hereby designates the
2 second week in April as Lung Cancer Action Week throughout the state
3 and encourages all residents of Oklahoma to learn more about lung
4 cancer, risk factors, early detection, and to take action.
