14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY Section 1, Chapter 453, O.S.L.
16 2025 (44 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section 301B), is amended to read as
17 follows:
18 Section 301B. A. There is hereby created in the State Treasury
19 a revolving fund for the Oklahoma Military Department to be
20 designated the "Base Infrastructure Needs and Development -
21 Technology Revolving Fund", which may also be cited as "BIND –
22 Technology Fund" "Military Readiness, Innovation, Education, and
23 Aviation Revolving Fund". The fund shall be a continuing fund, not
24 subject to fiscal year limitations, and shall consist of all monies

1 dire cted for deposit to the fund by law. All monies accruing to the
2 credit of said fund are hereby appropriated and may be budgeted and
3 expended by the Oklahoma Military Department for the purpose
4 established in subsection B of this section. Expenditures from said
5 fund shall be made upon warrants issued by the State Treasurer
6 against claims filed as prescribed by law with the Director of the
7 Office of Management and Enterprise Services for approval and
8 payment.
9 B. There is hereby established within the Oklahoma Military
10 Department the "Base Infrastructure Needs and Development –
11 Technology Program", which may also be cited as the "BIND –
12 Technology Program" "Military Readiness, Technology, Education, and
13 Innovation Program". The Department shall utilize funds from the
14 BIND – Technology Fund Military Readiness, Innovation, Education,
15 and Aviation Revolving Fund created in subsection A of this section,
16 alone or in combination with other available funding, to make
17 infrastructure, equipment, or technology investments, including, but
18 not limited to, the purchase of licenses and software associated
19 with military simulation training, improved access onto or off a
20 military facility, construct or improve common education facilities
21 located on military bases, or to grow local industries that will
22 either:
23 1. Decrease the likelihood of military base and other military
24 infrastructure closures in this state; or

1 2. Increase the likelihood of military base and other military
2 infrastructure expansions in this state.
3 C. In fiscal years where funds are expended from the Military
4 Readiness, Innovation, Education, and Aviation Revolving Fund, the
5 Oklahoma Military Department shall file a report within forty-five

8 Tempore of the Oklahoma State Senate, the Appropriations and Budget
9 Chair of the Oklahoma House of Representatives, and the
10 Appropriations Chair of the Oklahoma State Senate, stating how the
11 funds were used.
