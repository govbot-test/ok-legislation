6 An Act relating to controlled dangerous substances;
amending 63 O.S. 2021, Sections 2-315 and 2-508,
7 which relate to destruction of controlled dangerous
substances and disposition of seized property;
8 modifying and removing requirements and procedures
related to destruction; modifying terminology;
9 requiring compliance with federal regulation;
allowing ultimate user to submit own drugs for
10 destruction under certain conditions; permitting
destruction of decedent’s prescribed drugs under
11 certain conditions; authorizing qualified hospice
program to destroy decedent’s prescribed drugs under
12 certain conditions; requiring completion and
submission of certain form; defining terms; removing
13 requirement for certain destruction site; updating
statutory language; updating statutory references;
14 and providing an effective date.
