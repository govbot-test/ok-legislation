1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 2nd Session of the 60th Legislature (2026)
4
5
6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to medical micropigmentation;
amending 63 O.S. 2021, Section 1-1452, which relates
8 to who can perform micropigmentation; authorizing
independent authority once certain criteria are met;
9 and providing an effective date.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 63 O.S. 2021, Section 1-1452, is
14 amended to read as follows:
15 Section 1-1452. On and after May 1, 2002, medical
16 micropigmentation may only be performed in a physician's office by:
17 1. A physician as defined by the Oklahoma Medical
18 Micropigmentation Regulation Act;
19 2. A person licensed to practice registered nursing by the
20 Oklahoma Board of Nursing who holds a current certificate issued by
21 the State Commissioner of Health pursuant to the provisions of the
22 Oklahoma Medical Micropigmentation Regulation Act while working
23 under supervision of a physician. The level of supervision shall be
24
Req. No. 14364 Page 1
1 dete rmined by the physician in whose office medical
2 micropigmentation is being performed; and
3 3. A person who holds a current certificate issued by the State
4 Commissioner of Health pursuant to the provisions of the Oklahoma
5 Medical Micropigmentation Regulation Act while working under
6 supervision of a physician. Supervision may cease once a person has
7 been supervised for six thousand two hundred forty (6,240) or more
8 hours and who has reported those hours to the State Board of Health.
9 The level of supervision shall be determined by the physician in
10 whose office medical micropigmentation is being performed.
11 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
