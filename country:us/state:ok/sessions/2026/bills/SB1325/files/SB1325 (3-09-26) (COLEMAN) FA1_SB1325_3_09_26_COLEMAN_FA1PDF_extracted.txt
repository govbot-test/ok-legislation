I move to amend Senate Bill No. 1325 as follows:
1. On Page 3, Line 16, by deleting after the word “without” and before the acronym “GPS”, the
word “a”;
2. On Page 3, Lines 16-17, by deleting all new language after the word “monitoring” on Line 16 and
before the period “.” on Page 17;
3. On Page 3, Line 17, by inserting after the word “device” and before the word “shall”, the words
“and monitoring service”;
4. On Page 3, Lines 20-23, by deleting all new language after the word “shall” on Line 20 and
before the period “.” on Line 23, and inserting the words “provide a list of appropriate GPS
monitoring programs or systems for use by the courts and provide minimum requirements for
jurisdictions that do not use the programs on the list”;
5. On Page 4, Line 2, by deleting after the word “acquitted,” and before the word “incarcerated,” the
words “is to be”; and
6. By amending the title to conform.
