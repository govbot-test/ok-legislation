9 An Act relating to the Protection from Domestic Abuse
Act; amending 22 O.S. 2021, Section 60.17, which
10 relates to victim protective orders; updating
statutory language; requiring defendants charged with
11 certain domestic violence offenses to use a Global
Positioning System (GPS) device when released on
12 bond; requiring defendant to pay certain costs;
requiring monitoring by the Department of
13 Corrections; authorizing removal under certain

14 by victim; and providing an effective date.
