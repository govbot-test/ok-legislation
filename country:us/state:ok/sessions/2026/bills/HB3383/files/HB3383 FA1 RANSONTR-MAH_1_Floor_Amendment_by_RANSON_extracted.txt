FLOOR AMENDMENT
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
State of Oklahoma
SPEAKER:
CHAIR:
I move to amend HB3383
Page Section Lines
Of the Engrossed Bill
On page 1, line 21, by deleting the word "No" and inserting in lieu
thereof the following language:
"Except as provided by subsection G of this section, no";
On page 2, line 14 1/2 by inserting the following language:
"G. The provisions of subsection A of this section shall not be
applicable to the employees of the Oklahoma House of Representatives, the
Oklahoma State Senate or to employees of any entity that provides direct
support services to either chamber of the Legislature including, but not
limited to, the Legislative Service Bureau. The provisions of subsection
A of this section shall be fully applicable to any entity organized within
the executive branch or department of state government."
AMEND TITLE TO CONFORM TO AMENDMENTS
Amendment submitted by: Trish Ranson
