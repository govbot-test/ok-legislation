9 An Act relating to agriculture; requiring operation
of an Ag in the Classroom Education Program within
10 the Oklahoma Department of Agriculture, Food, and
Forestry; providing for development of curriculum for
11 K-12 programs in rural and urban schools; providing
for cooperation with certain agencies for operation
12 of program; authorizing the Oklahoma Department of
Agriculture, Food, and Forestry to provide
13 incentives; providing for funding sources; providing
for codification; and providing an effective date.
14
15
16
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
18 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
19 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 5-13.1 of Title 2, unless there
20 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
21 A. The Oklahoma Department of Agriculture, Food, and Forestry
22 shall operate an Ag in the Classroom Education Program for the
23 purpose of educating students about agriculture within this state.
24 B. The Ag in the Classroom Education Program shall:
SB1410 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 1. Develop curriculum for students in grades kindergarten
2 through twelve for Ag in the Classroom programs in urban and rural
3 schools;
4 2. Provide training and workshops for teachers who utilize Ag
5 in the Classroom curriculum;
6 3. Utilize existing Department resources and related programs;
7 and
8 4. Cooperate with the State Department of Education and the
9 Oklahoma State University Oklahoma Cooperative Extension Service as
10 needed in development and operation of the program; provided,
11 however, the Oklahoma Department of Agriculture, Food, and Forestry
12 shall have sole authority over the program.
13 C. The Oklahoma Department of Agriculture, Food, and Forestry
14 may provide incentives if funds are available to teachers who
15 participate in the Ag in the Classroom Education Program and utilize
16 curriculum provided by the program. Teachers who complete
17 eligibility requirements for the incentives as promulgated by rules
18 of the State Board of Agriculture may receive incentives. The
19 incentives may include but not be limited to:
20 1. Teacher recognition in newsletters, the Ag in the Classroom
21 website, social media, or other methods of recognition for
22 completing eligibility requirements;
23 2. A monetary award to be used for agriculture-related class
24 projects or field trips;
SB1410 HFLR Page 2
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 3. Entering a teacher into a drawing for Ag in the Classroom
2 resources or other items; and
3 4. Awarding a teacher an Ag in the Classroom certificate.
4 D. The Department shall directly receive all funds provided for
5 the Ag in the Classroom Education Program pursuant to Section 1104.3
6 of Title 47 of the Oklahoma Statutes. The Department may apply for,
7 accept, and use any gift, grant, or bequest from any source for the
8 purpose of discharging its duties.
9 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
10
11 COMMITTEE REPORT BY: COMMITTEE ON EDUCATION OVERSIGHT, dated
05/5/2026 - DO PASS.
