17 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
18 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 898 of Title 82, unless there is
19 created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
20 This act shall be known and may be cited as the “Southeast
21 Scenic Rivers Act”.
22 SECTION 3. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
23 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 898.1 of Title 82, unless there
24 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
Req. No. 3790 Page 5

1 The Legislature finds that certain free-flowing streams and
2 rivers of Oklahoma possess such unique natural scenic beauty, water,
3 fish, wildlife, and outdoor recreation value of present and future
4 benefit to the people of the state that it is the policy of the
5 Legislature to preserve those areas for the benefit of the people of
6 Oklahoma. For this purpose, the following are hereby designated as
7 southeast scenic rivers:
8 1. The Glover River in McCurtain County;
9 2. The Kiamichi River in Choctaw, Pushmataha, and Le Flore
10 Counties;
11 3. The Little River in McCurtain, Pushmataha, and Le Flore
12 Counties; and
13 4. The Mountain Fork River in Le Flore and McCurtain Counties.

17 A. For the purposes of this section:

21 extraction or washing of minerals or other sources of energy such as

1 The Southeast Scenic Rivers Commission or a board of county

3 authorize the removal of aggregate from the middle section of a

18 municipalities located in the counties or the immediate vicinity of
19 the scenic river for their municipal or domestic water supply if
20 such uses and structures will not significantly interfere with the
21 preservation of the stream as a scenic free-flowing system.
