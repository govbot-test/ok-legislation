I move to amend Senate Bill No. 1332, on Page 5, Line 22, by inserting a new Section 4 to read as
follows, renumbering subsequent section, and by amending the title to conform.
“Section 4. The provisions of this act shall only go into effect upon a determination by the US
Department of Homeland Security or the American Community Survey offered by the Census
Bureau that the amount of immigration & foreign-born residents of Oklahoma is below 0.5% of the
population.”
