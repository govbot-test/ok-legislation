7 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Turnpike Authority;
amending 69 O.S. 2021, Section 1705, as amended by
8 Section 572, Chapter 486, O.S.L. 2025 (69 O.S. Supp.
2025, Section 1705), which relates to the powers and
9 duties of the Oklahoma Turnpike Authority; removing
authorization for Authority for construction at
10 certain locations; altering location of certain
authorized construction; requiring Authority to hold
11 certain public meetings; requiring submission of
certain reports; allowing Authority to hold certain
12 additional public meetings; requiring Authority to
submit certain approval of the Transportation
13 Commission; providing that legislative authorization
for new turnpike locations terminates after certain
14 time period unless Authority takes certain action;
allowing for certain property owners to receive
15 certain compensation; allowing for the Authority to
promulgate certain rules; updating statutory
16 language; and providing an effective date.
