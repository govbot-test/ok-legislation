5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
6 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
7 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1-7-112a of Title 10A, unless
8 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
9 A. The Department of Human Services shall implement an
10 extension of foster care services to support individuals
11 transitioning into adulthood who meet the requirements provided by
12 this section. Participation in extended services under this section
13 shall be a voluntary election by the qualifying individual.
14 Services shall be available to any individual in this state who
15 meets the requirements of subsection B of this section and who:
16 1. Was in the custody of the Department at the time of the
17 individual’s eighteenth birthday but is less than twenty-one (21)
18 years of age; or
19 2. Achieved legal permanency by guardianship or adoption at
20 sixteen (16) years of age or older but is less than twenty-one (21)
21 years of age and the individual does not receive care from the
22 parent or legal guardian.
23
24

1 B. Any individual described in subsection A of this section may
2 receive extended services from the Department under this section if
3 the individual is:
4 1. Completing secondary education or a program leading to an
5 equivalent credential;
6 2. Enrolled in an institution that provides postsecondary or
7 vocational education;
8 3. Participating in a program or activity designed to promote,
9 or remove barriers to, employment;
10 4. Employed for at least eighty (80) hours per month; or
11 5. Incapable of doing any of the activities described in
12 paragraphs 1 through 4 of this subsection due to a medical
13 condition, which incapability is supported by regularly updated
14 information in the case plan of the individual. In such a case, the
15 individual shall be in compliance with a course of treatment as
16 recommended by the Department.
17 C. 1. The Director of the Department of Human Services shall
18 promulgate rules to implement the provisions of this section.
19 2. The Department of Human Services shall seek available
20 funding to implement the provisions of this section.
