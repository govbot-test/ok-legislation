Section 1:
SB206 FA1 NewtonCa-GRS(Untimely Filed)
4/30/2026 12:52:22 pm
FLOOR AMENDMENT
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
State of Oklahoma
SPEAKER:
CHAIR:
I move to amend SB206
Page 2 Section 1 Lines 9-10
Of the Engrossed Bill
By deleting all language beginning with the word "licensed" on Line 9
through the word "services" on Line 10 and inserting in lieu thereof, the
following language "provided by an ambulance service managed by a
municipality, county, ambulance service district, tribal entity, or other
public entity shall be considered essential services"; and
Page 2, Section 1, Line 10 1/2: By inserting the following subparagraphs:
"a. Any ambulance service licensed in this state for nine-one-one
emergency response that has a contract with an entity listed in paragraph
3 of this section, that is in good standing with the listed entities, and
has a contract to provide emergency nine-one-one services in its
jurisdictional boundaries, as defined by the contract.
b. Any ambulance service under subparagraph a of this paragraph
shall use any grant funding obtained by this act, shall only be used for
grant awards in the district and for the grant application defined by the
contract with the entities listed in paragraph 3 of this section."
AMEND TITLE TO CONFORM TO AMENDMENTS
Amendment submitted by: Carl Newton
