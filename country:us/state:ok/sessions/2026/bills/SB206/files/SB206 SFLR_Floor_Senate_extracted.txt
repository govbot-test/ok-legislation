8 An Act relating to emergency medical services;
amending 63 O.S. 2021, Section 1-2502, which relates
9 to legislative findings and declarations; declaring
emergency medical services to be considered essential
10 services for certain purpose; and declaring an
emergency.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 63 O.S. 2021, Section 1-2502, is
15 amended to read as follows:
16 Section 1-2502. The Legislature hereby finds and declares that:
17 1. There is a critical shortage of providers of emergency care
18 for:
19 a. the delivery of fast, efficient emergency medical care
20 for the sick and injured at the scene of a medical
21 emergency and during transport to a health care
22 facility, and
23 b. the delivery of stabilizing and definitive care at a
24 health care facility; and
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - SB206 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 2. Improved emergency service is required to reduce the
2 mortality rate during the first critical minutes immediately
3 following the onset of a medical emergency; and
4 3. Solely for purposes of eligibility for federal funding,
5 emergency medical services provided by an ambulance service managed
6 by a municipality, county, ambulance service district, tribal
7 entity, or other public entity shall be considered essential
8 services.
9 SECTION 2. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
10 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
11 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
12 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
13 COMMITTEE REPORT BY: COMMITTEE ON HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES
February 23, 2026 - DO PASS AS AMENDED
