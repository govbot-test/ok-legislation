8 An Act relating to emergency medical services;
amending 63 O.S. 2021, Section 1-2502, which relates
9 to legislative findings and declarations; declaring
emergency medical services to be considered essential
10 services for certain purpose; and declaring an
emergency.
11
12
13
14 AUTHORS: Add the following House Coauthor: Hays.
15 AMENDMENT NO. 1. Strike the title, enacting clause, and entire bill
and insert:
16
17
18 "An Act relating to emergency medical services;
amending 63 O.S. 2021, Section 1-2502, which relates
19 to legislative findings and declarations; declaring
emergency medical services to be considered essential
20 services for certain purpose; and declaring an
emergency.
21
22
23 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
24
ENGR. H. A. to ENGR. S. B. NO. 206 Page 1
1 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 63 O.S. 2021, Section 1-2502, is
2 amended to read as follows:
3 Section 1-2502. The Legislature hereby finds and declares that:
4 1. There is a critical shortage of providers of emergency care
5 for:
6 a. the delivery of fast, efficient emergency medical care
7 for the sick and injured at the scene of a medical
8 emergency and during transport to a health care
9 facility, and
10 b. the delivery of stabilizing and definitive care at a
11 health care facility; and
12 2. Improved emergency service is required to reduce the
13 mortality rate during the first critical minutes immediately
14 following the onset of a medical emergency; and
15 3. Solely for purposes of eligibility for federal funding,
16 emergency medical services provided by an ambulance service managed
17 by a municipality, county, ambulance service district, tribal
18 entity, or other public entity shall be considered essential
19 services.
20 a. Any ambulance service licensed in this state for nine-
21 one-one emergency response that has a contract with an
22 entity listed in this paragraph, that is in good
23 standing with the listed entities, and has a contract
24
ENGR. H. A. to ENGR. S. B. NO. 206 Page 2
1 to provide emergency nine-one-one services in its
2 jurisdictional boundaries, as defined by the contract.
3 b. Any ambulance service under subparagraph a of this
4 paragraph shall use any grant funding obtained by this
5 act, shall only be used for grant awards in the
6 district and for the grant application defined by the
7 contract with the entities listed in this paragraph.
8 SECTION 2. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
9 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
10 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
11 be in full force from and after its passage and approval."
12 Passed the House of Representatives the 4th day of May, 2026.
