7 PROPOSED POLICY COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
8 An Act relating to elected officers; prohibiting
persons who are a national of any country other than
9 the United States from filing and running for or
holding elected office; providing for codification;
10 and providing an effective date.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
15 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 9 of Title 74, unless there is
16 created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
17 No person, without regard to whether that person is a United
18 States national, may file and run for or hold any elected municipal,
19 county, state, or other government office in the State of Oklahoma
20 if that person is a national of any country other than the United
21 States.
22 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
