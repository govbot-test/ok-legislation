Section 1:
1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 2nd Session of the 60th Legislature (2026)
4
5
6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to elections; prohibiting persons who
are a national of any country other than the United
8 States from holding elected office; providing for
codification; and providing an effective date.
9
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
13 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1-115 of Title 26, unless there
14 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
15 No persons, without regard to whether that person is a United
16 States national, may hold elected municipal, county, state, or other
17 government office in the State of Oklahoma if that person is a
18 national of any country other than the United States.
19 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
