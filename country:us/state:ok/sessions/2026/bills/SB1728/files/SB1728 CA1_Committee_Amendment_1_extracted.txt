I move to amend Senate Bill No. 1728 as follows:
1. On Page 1, Line 23, by deleting after the word “intentionally” and before the word “a”, the word
“isolating” and inserting the words “preventing contact of”;
2. On Page 2, Line 9, by inserting before the word “threats”, the word “malicious”;
3. On Page 2, Lines 9 through 13, by deleting after the word “threats” on Line 9 and before the
comma on Line 13, all language and inserting the words “of legal prosecution for conduct that is
permitted by law”; and
4. On Page 2, Line 21, by deleting after the word “aiding” and before the word “in”, the word
“another” and inserting the words “an intimate partner or family member”.
