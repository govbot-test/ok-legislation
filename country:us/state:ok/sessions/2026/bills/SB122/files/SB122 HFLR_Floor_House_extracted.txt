14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. There is hereby appropriated to the Weigh Station
16 Improvement Revolving Fund, created pursuant to Section 1167 of

21 perform the duties imposed upon the Department of Transportation by
22 law.
