5 SECTION 3. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
6 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 427.5b of Title 63, unless there
7 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
8 There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving fund
9 for the Oklahoma Medical Marijuana Authority to be designated the
10 “Oklahoma Medical Marijuana Land Reclamation Revolving Fund”. The
11 fund shall be a continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year
12 limitations, and shall consist of all monies received by the
13 Authority from land reclamation fees collected pursuant to the
14 Oklahoma Medical Marijuana and Patient Protection Act. All monies
15 accruing to the credit of the fund are hereby appropriated and may
16 be budgeted and expended by the Oklahoma Medical Marijuana Authority
17 for the purposes provided for in this section. Expenditures from
18 the fund shall be made upon warrants issued by the State Treasurer
19 against claims filed as prescribed by law with the Director of the
20 Office of Management and Enterprise Services for approval and
21 payment. Up to Five Million Dollars ($5,000,000.00) of the funds
22 received by and credited to the fund may be used and expended by the
23 Oklahoma Medical Marijuana Authority for environmental remediation
