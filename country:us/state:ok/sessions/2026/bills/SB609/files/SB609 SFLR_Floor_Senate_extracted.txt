8 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Police Pension and
Retirement System; amending 11 O.S. 2021, Section 50-
9 111.2, which relates to transfer of credited service;
and allowing for credited service from certain out-
10 of-state public retirement systems.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 11 O.S. 2021, Section 50-111.2, is
15 amended to read as follows:
16 Section 50-111.2. A. A member of the Oklahoma Police Pension
17 and Retirement System may receive up to five (5) years of credited
18 service accumulated by the member while a member of the Oklahoma
19 Firefighters Pension and Retirement System, the Oklahoma Law
20 Enforcement Retirement System, the Teacher’s Retirement System of
21 Oklahoma, the Oklahoma Public Employees Retirement System, or a
22 county retirement system created pursuant to Section 951 of Title 19
23 of the Oklahoma Statutes, or an Oklahoma municipal retirement
24 system, or a state, county, or municipal retirement system from a
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - SB609 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
RBS No. 430
1 stat e other than Oklahoma, if the member is not receiving or
2 eligible to receive retirement credit or benefits from said service
3 in any other public retirement system. The member shall decide the
4 number of years of credited service, not to exceed five (5) years,
5 to purchase. The State Board shall determine the amount for the
6 purchase pursuant to Section 50-111.4 of this title. The amount may
7 be paid through a trustee-to-trustee transfer to the Oklahoma Police
8 Pension and Retirement System from another system designated in this
9 section, and/or through payments made by the member. The
10 transferred credited service of the member from another retirement
11 system pursuant to this section shall not alter the member’s normal
12 retirement date or vesting requirements. The transferred credited
13 service will be added after the member reaches normal retirement
14 date or vesting date.
15 B. The Oklahoma Police Pension and Retirement System shall
16 transfer credited service to another Oklahoma state retirement
17 system upon request of former members. Upon transfer, the former
18 member shall have forfeited all rights in the Oklahoma Police
19 Pension and Retirement System. Employee and city contributions of
20 the former municipal retirement systems prior to January 1, 1981,
21 are not transferable.
22 COMMITTEE REPORT BY: COMMITTEE ON RETIREMENT AND GOVERNMENT
