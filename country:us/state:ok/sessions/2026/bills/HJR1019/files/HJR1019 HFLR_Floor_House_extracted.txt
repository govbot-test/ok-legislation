6 Oklahoma by adding a new Section 8 to Article III thereof, to read
7 as follows:
8 A. In all elections for federal, state, and county officers,
9 except for judicial branch officers, the following shall be
10 required:
11 1. Each political party recognized by the laws of this state
12 shall have the opportunity to nominate a candidate for the General
13 Election for federal, state, and county officers in the manner
14 provided by law. The candidate must have filed during the timeframe
15 designated by Oklahoma law and become the political party's nominee
16 by winning the party's primary outright or by winning the party's
17 runoff, or by winning as an unopposed candidate in the manner
18 provided by law.
19 2. Nothing in this section shall be construed to limit the
20 authority of the Legislature to enact a mandatory primary system as
21 provided in Section 3 of Article III of this Constitution.
22 B. 1. This section shall remain in effect unless and until it
23 is repealed by a vote of the people by initiative or referendum as
24 provided in this Constitution.
