6 An Act relating to service of process; amending 36
O.S. 2021, Sections 621 and 622, which relate to
7 service of process on foreign insurers; modifying
persons that may be appointed as agent; modifying
8 revocability of agent appointment; requiring certain
agent information be submitted to the Insurance
9 Commissioner; providing for alternate service of
process in certain circumstances; modifying manner of
10 service of process; updating statutory language; and
providing an effective date.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 36 O.S. 2021, Section 621, is
15 amended to read as follows:
16 Section 621. A. Each authorized foreign or alien insurer shall
17 appoint the Insurance Commissioner, a resident of this state, or a
18 person who is authorized to do business in this state as its agent
19 to receive service of legal process, other than a subpoena, issued
20 against it in this state upon any cause of action arising from its
21 transaction of business in this state. The appointment shall be
22 irrevocable, shall bind any successor and shall remain in effect as
23 long as there is in force in this state any contract made by the
24 insurer or obligations arising therefrom.
