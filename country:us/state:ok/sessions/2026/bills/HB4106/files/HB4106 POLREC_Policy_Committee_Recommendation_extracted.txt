7 POLICY COMMITTEE RECOMMENDATION
8 An Act relating to crimes and punishments; amending
21 O.S. 2021, Sections 1704 and 1706, which relate to
9 grand and petit larceny; decreasing threshold amount
for grand larceny offenses; providing felony penalty
10 for third and subsequent petit larceny convictions;
and providing an effective date.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 21 O.S. 2021, Section 1704, is
15 amended to read as follows:
16 Section 1704. Grand larceny is larceny committed in either of
17 the following cases:
18 1. When the property taken is of a value of One Thousand
19 Dollars ($1,000.00) Nine Hundred Dollars ($900.00) or greater; or
20 2. When such property, although not of a value of One Thousand
21 Dollars ($1,000.00) Nine Hundred Dollars ($900.00) or greater, is
22 taken from the person of another.
23 Larceny in other cases is petit larceny.
24
Req. No. 16255 Page 1
1 SECTION 2. AMENDATORY 21 O.S. 2021, Section 1706, is
2 amended to read as follows:
3 Section 1706. A. Petit larceny shall be punishable by a fine
4 of not less than Ten Dollars ($10.00) or more than Five Hundred
5 Dollars ($500.00), or imprisonment in the county jail not to six (6)
6 months, or by both such fine and imprisonment, at the discretion of
7 the court.
8 B. Any person convicted of a third or subsequent offense of
9 petit larceny shall be guilty of a felony, punishable by
10 imprisonment in the custody of the Department of Corrections for a
11 term not exceeding two (2) years, or a fine not exceeding One
12 Thousand Dollars ($1,000.00), or by both such fine and imprisonment.
13 SECTION 3. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
14
