8 [ crimes and punishments - grand and petit larceny -
threshold amount - felony penalty - effective date ]
9
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 21 O.S. 2021, Section 1704, is
13 amended to read as follows:
14 Section 1704. Grand larceny is larceny committed in either of
15 the following cases:
16 1. When the property taken is of a value of One Thousand
17 Dollars ($1,000.00) Nine Hundred Dollars ($900.00) or greater; or
18 2. When such property, although not of a value of One Thousand
19 Dollars ($1,000.00) Nine Hundred Dollars ($900.00) or greater, is
20 taken from the person of another.
21 Larceny in other cases is petit larceny.
22 SECTION 2. AMENDATORY 21 O.S. 2021, Section 1706, is
23 amended to read as follows:
24
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - HB4106 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 Section 1706. A. Petit larceny shall be punishable by a fine
2 of not less than Ten Dollars ($10.00) or more than Five Hundred
3 Dollars ($500.00), or imprisonment in the county jail not to six (6)
4 months, or by both such fine and imprisonment, at the discretion of
5 the court.
6 B. When three or more separate offenses of petit larceny are
7 committed within a one-hundred-eighty-day period and the aggregate
8 value of the property taken exceeds Nine Hundred Dollars ($900.00),
9 the person shall, upon conviction, be guilty of a Class D3 felony
10 offense and shall be punished by imprisonment as provided for in
11 subsections B through F of Section 20P of this title, or by a fine
12 not exceeding One Thousand Dollars ($1,000.00), or by both such fine
13 and imprisonment.
14 SECTION 3. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
15 COMMITTEE REPORT BY: COMMITTEE ON PUBLIC SAFETY
April 14, 2026 - DO PASS AS AMENDED
