9 An Act relating to rural water districts; amending 82
O.S. 2021, Section 1324.31, which relates to
10 organization of a district; removing certain
limitations prohibiting the organizing of a district;
11 making certain exclusion; and declaring an emergency.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 82 O.S. 2021, Section 1324.31, is
16 amended to read as follows:
17 Section 1324.31. Pursuant to the provisions of this act, any
18 corporation which was formed prior to December 1, 1988, may organize
19 and constitute a district for the purpose of providing rural water
20 service subject to the provisions of the Rural Water, Sewer, Gas and
21 Solid Waste Management Districts Act. Provided, however, that
22 nothing in this section shall authorize the organization or
23 reorganization of a solid waste management district pursuant to this
24 section.
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - HB4316 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 SECTION 2. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
2 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
3 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
4 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
