12 An Act relating to rural water districts; amending 82
O.S. 2021, Section 1324.31, which relates to
13 organization of a district; removing certain
limitations prohibiting the organizing of a district;
14 making certain exclusion; and declaring an emergency.
15
16
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
18 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 82 O.S. 2021, Section 1324.31, is
19 amended to read as follows:
20 Section 1324.31. Pursuant to the provisions of this act, any
21 corporation which was formed prior to December 1, 1988, may organize
22 and constitute a district for the purpose of providing rural water
23 service subject to the provisions of the Rural Water, Sewer, Gas and
24 Solid Waste Management Districts Act. Provided, however, that

1 noth ing in this section shall authorize the organization or
2 reorganization of a solid waste management district pursuant to this

4 SECTION 2. It being immediately necessary for the preservation

7 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
