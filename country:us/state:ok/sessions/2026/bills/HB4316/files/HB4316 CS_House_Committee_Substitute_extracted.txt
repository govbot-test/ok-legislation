10 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
11 An Act relating to rural water districts; amending 82
O.S. 2021, Section 1324.31, which relates to
12 organization of a district; removing certain
limitations prohibiting the organizing of a district;
13 making certain exclusion; and declaring an emergency.
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 82 O.S. 2021, Section 1324.31, is
18 amended to read as follows:
19 Section 1324.31. Pursuant to the provisions of this act, any
20 corporation which was formed prior to December 1, 1988, may organize
21 and constitute a district for the purpose of providing rural water
22 service subject to the provisions of the Rural Water, Sewer, Gas and
23 Solid Waste Management Districts Act. Provided, however, that
24 nothing in this section shall authorize the organization or
Req. No. 16737 Page 1
1 reor ganization of a solid waste management district pursuant to this
2 section.
3 SECTION 2. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
4 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
5 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
6 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
