9 An Act relating to staffing contracts; defining
terms; prohibiting certain staffing contracts with
10 temporary agencies for certain facilities; providing
for codification; and providing an effective date.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
15 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 2-7-301.1 of Title 10A, unless
16 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
17 A. As used in this section:
18 1. “Contracting organization” means an organization which
19 operates under a written agreement signed between the Office of
20 Juvenile Affairs and the organization which allows such organization
21 to provide contract workers, rather than full-time direct-hire
22 staff, to detention facilities; and
23
24

BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 2. “Temporary agency” means a staffing agency, part-time
2 staffing agency, recruiting agency, or other organization that
3 provides employees on a temporary basis.
4 B. No group home, post-adjudication treatment facility, or any
5 other facility holding youth who are in the custody of the Office of
6 Juvenile Affairs shall utilize, employ, or enter into an agreement
7 to staff such detention facility by utilizing a contracting
8 organization or temporary agency that provides full- or part-time
9 team members on behalf of the Office on a temporary or contract
10 basis.
11 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
12 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 452.3 of Title 19, unless there
13 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
14 A. As used in this section:
15 1. “Contracting organization” means an organization which
16 operates under a written agreement signed between the county and the
17 organization which allows such organization to provide contract
18 workers, rather than full-time direct-hire staff, to detention
19 facilities; and
20 2. “Temporary agency” means a staffing agency, part-time
21 staffing agency, recruiting agency, or other organization that
22 provides employees on a temporary basis.
23 B. No entity that operates a juvenile detention facility shall
24 utilize, employ, or enter into an agreement to staff such facility

1 by u tilizing a contracting organization or temporary agency that
2 provides full-time team members to the county on a contract basis.
3 SECTION 3. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
4 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 530.9 of Title 56, unless there
5 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
6 A. As used in this section:
7 1. “Contracting organization” means an organization which
8 operates under a written agreement signed between an adult companion
9 home and the organization which allows such organization to provide
10 contract workers, rather than full-time direct-hire staff, to the
11 adult companion home; and
12 2. “Temporary agency” means a staffing agency, part-time
13 staffing agency, recruiting agency, or other organization that
14 provides employees on a temporary basis.
15 B. No adult companion home certified by the Department of Human
16 Services shall utilize, employ, or enter into an agreement to staff
17 such detention facility by utilizing a contracting organization or
18 temporary agency that provides full- or part-time team members to
19 the Department on a contract basis.
20 SECTION 4. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
