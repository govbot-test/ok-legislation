6 An Act relating to staffing contracts; defining
terms; prohibiting certain staffing contracts with
7 temporary agencies for certain facilities; providing
for codification; and providing an effective date.
8
9
10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
11 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
12 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 2-7-301.1 of Title 10A, unless
13 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
14 A. As used in this section:
15 1. “Contracting organization” means an organization which
16 operates under a written agreement signed between the Office of
17 Juvenile Affairs and the organization which allows such organization
18 to provide contract workers, rather than full-time direct-hire
19 staff, to detention facilities; and
20 2. “Temporary agency” means a staffing agency, part-time
21 staffing agency, recruiting agency, or other organization that
22 provides employees on a temporary basis.
23 B. No group home, post-adjudication treatment facility, or any
24 other facility holding youth who are in the custody of the Office of
ENGR. S. B. NO. 1805 Page 1
1 Juve nile Affairs shall utilize, employ, or enter into an agreement
2 to staff such detention facility by utilizing a contracting
3 organization or temporary agency that provides full- or part-time
4 team members on behalf of the Office on a temporary or contract
5 basis.
6 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
7 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 452.3 of Title 19, unless there
8 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
9 A. As used in this section:
10 1. “Contracting organization” means an organization which
11 operates under a written agreement signed between the county and the
12 organization which allows such organization to provide contract
13 workers, rather than full-time direct-hire staff, to detention
14 facilities; and
15 2. “Temporary agency” means a staffing agency, part-time
16 staffing agency, recruiting agency, or other organization that
17 provides employees on a temporary basis.
18 B. No entity that operates a juvenile detention facility shall
19 utilize, employ, or enter into an agreement to staff such facility
20 by utilizing a contracting organization or temporary agency that
21 provides full-time team members to the county on a contract basis.
22 SECTION 3. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
23 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 530.9 of Title 56, unless there
24 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
ENGR. S. B. NO. 1805 Page 2
1 A. As used in this section:
2 1. “Contracting organization” means an organization which
3 operates under a written agreement signed between an adult companion
4 home and the organization which allows such organization to provide
5 contract workers, rather than full-time direct-hire staff, to the
6 adult companion home; and
7 2. “Temporary agency” means a staffing agency, part-time
8 staffing agency, recruiting agency, or other organization that
9 provides employees on a temporary basis.
10 B. No adult companion home certified by the Department of Human
11 Services shall utilize, employ, or enter into an agreement to staff
12 such detention facility by utilizing a contracting organization or
13 temporary agency that provides full- or part-time team members to
14 the Department on a contract basis.
15 SECTION 4. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
