8 An Act relating to staffing contracts; defining
terms; prohibiting certain staffing contracts with
9 temporary agencies for certain facilities; providing
for codification; and providing an effective date.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 2-7-301.1 of Title 10A, unless
15 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 A. As used in this section:
17 1. “Contracting organization” means an organization which
18 operates under a written agreement signed between the Office of
19 Juvenile Affairs and the organization which allows such organization
20 to provide contract workers, rather than full-time direct-hire
21 staff, to detention facilities; and
22 2. “Temporary agency” means a staffing agency, part-time
23 staffing agency, recruiting agency, or other organization that
24 provides employees on a temporary basis.

1 B. No group home, post-adjudication treatment facility, or any
2 other facility holding youth who are in the custody of the Office of
3 Juvenile Affairs shall utilize, employ, or enter into an agreement
4 to staff such detention facility by utilizing a contracting
5 organization or temporary agency that provides full- or part-time
6 team members on behalf of the Office on a temporary or contract
7 basis.
8 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
9 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 452.3 of Title 19, unless there
10 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
11 A. As used in this section:
12 1. “Contracting organization” means an organization which
13 operates under a written agreement signed between the county and the
14 organization which allows such organization to provide contract
15 workers, rather than full-time direct-hire staff, to detention
16 facilities; and
17 2. “Temporary agency” means a staffing agency, part-time
18 staffing agency, recruiting agency, or other organization that
19 provides employees on a temporary basis.
20 B. No entity that operates a juvenile detention facility shall
21 utilize, employ, or enter into an agreement to staff such facility
22 by utilizing a contracting organization or temporary agency that
23 provides full-time team members to the county on a contract basis.

1 SECTION 3. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
2 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 530.9 of Title 56, unless there
3 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
4 A. As used in this section:
5 1. “Contracting organization” means an organization which
6 operates under a written agreement signed between an adult companion
7 home and the organization which allows such organization to provide
8 contract workers, rather than full-time direct-hire staff, to the
9 adult companion home; and
10 2. “Temporary agency” means a staffing agency, part-time
11 staffing agency, recruiting agency, or other organization that
12 provides employees on a temporary basis.
13 B. No adult companion home certified by the Department of Human
14 Services shall utilize, employ, or enter into an agreement to staff
15 such detention facility by utilizing a contracting organization or
16 temporary agency that provides full- or part-time team members to
17 the Department on a contract basis.
18 SECTION 4. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
