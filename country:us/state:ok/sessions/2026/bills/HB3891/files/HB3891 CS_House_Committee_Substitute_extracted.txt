14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 19 O.S. 2021, Section
16 180.62, as amended by Section 3, Chapter 394, O.S.L. 2024 (19
17 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section 180.62), is amended to read as
18 follows:
19 Section 180.62 A. The basic salaries of county officers
20 upon which all salaries and future increases or decreases
21 thereof shall not be less than Nineteen Thousand Dollars
22 ($19,000.00) per annum nor shall they exceed Forty-nine
23 Thousand Five Hundred Dollars ($49,500.00) per annum,
24 provided the basic salary for the sheriff and county
Req. No. 17100 Page 1
1 comm issioners, which shall be no less than other salaries for
2 county officers controlled by this section, shall not be less
3 than Forty-four Thousand Dollars ($44,000.00) per annum nor
4 shall it exceed Seventy-four Thousand Five Hundred Dollars
5 ($74,500.00) per annum.
6 B. The board of county commissioners, or if the county has
7 elected to come under the County Budget Act, the budget board
8 members of the county, shall set the salaries for all elected county
9 officials within the limits allowed by law.
10 C. The annual salaries fixed by this act shall be paid either
11 monthly or twice a month, by order of the board of county
12 commissioners, for each month or fraction thereof the incumbent
13 lawfully occupies and holds title to such office.
14 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
