14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
15 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
16 This act shall be known and may be cited as the "Good Faith
17 Reporting for Child Protection Act".
18 SECTION 2. AMENDATORY 76 O.S. 2021, Section 31, is
19 amended to read as follows:
20 Section 31. A. Any volunteer shall be immune from liability in
21 a civil action on the basis of any act or omission of the volunteer
22 resulting in damage or injury if:

3 D. E. Definitions.
4 1. For the purposes of this section, the term "volunteer" means
5 a person who enters into a service or undertaking of the person's
6 free will without compensation or expectation of compensation in
7 money or other thing of value in order to provide a service, care,
8 assistance, advice, or other benefit; provided, being legally
9 entitled to receive compensation for the service or undertaking
10 performed shall not preclude a person from being considered a
11 volunteer.
12 2. For the purposes of this section, the term "charitable
13 organization" means any benevolent, philanthropic, patriotic,
14 eleemosynary, educational, social, civic, recreational, religious
15 group or association, or any other person performing or purporting
16 to perform acts beneficial to the public.
17 3. For the purposes of this section, the term "not-for-profit
18 corporation" means a corporation formed for a purpose not involving
19 pecuniary gain to its shareholders or members, paying no dividends
20 or other pecuniary remuneration, directly or indirectly, to its
21 shareholders or members as such, and having no capital stock.
22 4. For purposes of this section, the term "employee" means a
23 person who performs ministerial, supervisory, or staff services for
24 a charitable organization and is subject to the organization's

6 E. F. The provisions of this section shall not affect the
7 liability that any person may have which arises from the operation

15 event shall the limitation on liability provided under this
16 subsection apply to a person who does not possess a current motor
17 vehicle policy when providing transportation.
18 F. G. The immunity from civil liability provided for by
19 subsection A of this section shall extend only to the actions taken
20 by a person rendering the service, care, assistance, advice, or
21 other benefit as a volunteer, where such actions are agreed upon in
22 advance by all involved persons to be provided on a volunteer basis.
23 This section shall not be construed to confer any immunity to any
