8 PROPOSED COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
9 An Act relating to Medicaid; creating the ADvantage
Waiver Home and Community-based Services for Seniors
10 Revolving Fund; stating the purpose; providing for
codification; providing an effective date; and
11 declaring an emergency.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
16 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1019 of Title 56, unless there
17 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
18 There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving fund
19 for the Department of Human Services to be designated the "ADvantage
20 Waiver Home and Community-based Services for Seniors Revolving
21 Fund". The fund shall be a continuing fund, not subject to fiscal
22 year limitations, and shall consist of all monies received by the
23 Department of Human Services from state-appropriated funds, federal
24 funds, donations, grants, and contributions from any public or
Req. No. 16211 Page 1
1 priv ate source and designated for the purpose set forth in this
2 section. All monies accruing to the credit of said fund are hereby
3 appropriated and may be budgeted and expended by the Department of
4 Human Services for the purpose of providing home and community-based
5 services to persons at risk of placement in a nursing facility but
6 who elect to be served in a home and community-based setting in lieu
7 of nursing facility services. Expenditures from said fund shall be
8 made upon warrants issued by the State Treasurer against claims
9 filed as prescribed by law with the Director of the Office of
10 Management and Enterprise Services for approval and payment.
11 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
12 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
13 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
14 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
15 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
