14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
16 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1-111 of Title 43A, unless there
17 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
18 There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving fund
19 for the Department of Mental Health and Substance Abuse Services to
20 be designated the “9-8-8 Lifeline Revolving Fund”. The fund shall
21 be a continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations, and
22 shall consist of all monies received by the Department from
23 legislative appropriations provided for the purpose of sustaining
24 and expanding operations of a 9-8-8 suicide and crisis lifeline in
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - SB1369 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 this state. All monies accruing to the credit of the fund are
2 hereby appropriated and may be budgeted and expended by the
3 Department for the purpose provided for in this section.
4 Expenditures from the fund shall be made upon warrants issued by the
5 State Treasurer against claims filed as prescribed by law with the
6 Director of the Office of Management and Enterprise Services for
7 approval and payment.
8 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
9 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
10 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
11 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
12 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
