9 An Act relating to motor vehicles; amending 47 O.S.
2021, Sections 10-103 and 10-106, which relate to
10 accidents; expanding criteria by which a person must
remain at scene or make notification of certain
11 accident; making language gender neutral; updating
statutory language; and providing an effective date.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 47 O.S. 2021, Section 10-103, is
16 amended to read as follows:
17 Section 10-103. The driver of any a vehicle involved in an
18 accident resulting only in damage to property or a vehicle which
19 that is driven or attended by any person shall immediately stop such
20 vehicle at the scene of such the accident or as close thereto to the
21 scene of the accident as possible but or shall forthwith immediately
22 return to and in every event the scene of the accident. The driver
23 shall remain at the scene of such the accident until he or she has
24 fulfilled the requirements of Section 10-104 of this title. Every
SB1226 HFLR Page 1

1 such stop shall be made without obstructing traffic more than is
2 necessary. Any person failing to stop or comply with said the
3 requirements under such circumstances of this section shall, upon
4 conviction, be guilty of a misdemeanor and upon conviction thereof
5 shall be punished punishable by a fine not to exceed Five Hundred
6 Dollars ($500.00), or by imprisonment in the county jail for not
7 more than one (1) year, or by both such fine and imprisonment. In
8 addition to the criminal penalties imposed by this section, any
9 person violating the provisions of this section shall be subject to
10 liability for damages in an amount equal to three times the value of
11 the damage caused by the accident. Said Such damages shall be
12 recoverable in a civil action. Nothing in this section shall
13 prevent a judge from ordering restitution for any damage caused by a
14 driver involved in an accident provided for in this section.
15 SECTION 2. AMENDATORY 47 O.S. 2021, Section 10-106, is
16 amended to read as follows:
17 Section 10-106. The driver of any vehicle involved in an
18 accident resulting only in damage to fixtures, fences, or other
19 property legally upon or adjacent to a public or private road,
20 street, or highway shall take reasonable steps to locate and notify
21 the owner or person in charge of such property of such fact and of
22 the driver’s name and address and of the registration number of the
23 vehicle being driven and shall upon request exhibit a driver license
24 and security verification form, as defined in Section 7-600 of this

7 one (1) year, or by both such fine and imprisonment. Nothing in
8 this section shall prevent a judge from ordering restitution for any
9 damage caused by a driver involved in an accident provided for in
