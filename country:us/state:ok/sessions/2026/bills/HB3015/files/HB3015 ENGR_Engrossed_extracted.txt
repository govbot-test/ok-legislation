3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
4 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
5 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 110A of Title 47, unless there
6 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
7 "Credential" shall mean a driver license, permit, or
8 identification card issued by Service Oklahoma.
9 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
10 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 110B of Title 47, unless there
11 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
12 "Credential holder" shall mean an individual that has been
13 issued a physical or electronic credential.
14 SECTION 3. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
15 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 114.1 of Title 47, unless there
16 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
17 "Electronic credential" shall mean an electronic extension of
18 the physical credential issued by Service Oklahoma that conveys
19 identity and driving privilege information.
20 SECTION 4. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
21 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 114.2 of Title 47, unless there
22 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
23 "Electronic credential system" means a digital process that
24 includes a method for provisioning electronic credentials,

4 SECTION 9. AMENDATORY 47 O.S. 2021, Section 1-114, as
5 amended by Section 24, Chapter 282, O.S.L. 2022 (47 O.S. Supp. 2025,
6 Section 1-114), is amended to read as follows:
7 Section 1-114. Driver and driver license.
8 A. "Driver" means any person who drives, operates or is in
9 actual physical control of a vehicle.
10 B. "Driver license" means a document issued by Service Oklahoma
11 or the driver licensing agency of another state or country which
12 grants to the person named thereon the privilege to drive, operate
13 or be in actual physical control of a motor vehicle. The term shall
14 include an intermediate Class D driver license, a learner permit and
15 commercial learner permit. The term shall also include both the
16 physical document or the digital version of the document approved by
17 Service Oklahoma.
