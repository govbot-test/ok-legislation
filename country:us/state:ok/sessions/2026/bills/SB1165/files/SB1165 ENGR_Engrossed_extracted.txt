7 An Act relating to the Department of Public Safety;
requiring that portions of certain appropriated funds
8 be used for certain purposes; providing an effective
date; and declaring an emergency.
9
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. From the funds appropriated to the Department of
13 Public Safety in Enrolled Senate Bill No. 1177 of the 2nd Session of
14 the 60th Oklahoma Legislature, the Department of Public Safety is
15 hereby authorized to expend Two Million Three Hundred Fifty Thousand
16 Dollars ($2,350,000.00) for any necessary expenditures related to
17 the operation and maintenance of the School Secure Program.
18 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
19 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
20 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
21 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
22 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
