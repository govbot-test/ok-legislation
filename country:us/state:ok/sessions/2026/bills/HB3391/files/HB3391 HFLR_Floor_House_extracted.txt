10 An Act relating to animals; requiring commercial pet
breeders to list license number on advertisements;
11 directing the Oklahoma Department of Agriculture,
Food, and Forestry to promulgate rules and set fines;
12 providing for codification; and providing an
effective date.
13
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
18 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 30.17 of Title 4, unless there
19 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
20 A. All commercial pet breeders under this title shall list
21 their commercial pet breeder's license number, as issued by the
22 Oklahoma Department of Agriculture, Food, and Forestry, on all
23 advertisements for pet sales.
