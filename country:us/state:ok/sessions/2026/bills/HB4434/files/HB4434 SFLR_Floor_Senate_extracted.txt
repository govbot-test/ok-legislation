8 An Act relating to state government; requiring the
Governor to provide certain notification; requiring
9 notification to indicate start of absence and
anticipated return; directing notice be made twenty-
10 four hours before absence except for emergencies;
providing for codification; and providing an
11 effective date.
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
15 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 8.20 of Title 74, unless there
16 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
17 Notification, as provided in this section, shall be required
18 when the Governor is absent from the state. Except in the event of
19 an emergency, at least twenty-four (24) hours prior to the Governor
20 being absent from the state, the Governor shall provide electronic
21 notification to the Lieutenant Governor of his or her plans to be
22 absent from the state, indicating the start date and time and the
23 anticipated return date and time in such notification. The
24 notification required by this section shall also be required of the
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - HB4434 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 acti ng Governor, when he or she is absent from the state, and follow
2 the line of succession provided in Section 15 of Article VI of the
3 Oklahoma Constitution or as may be provided by law. In the event of
4 an emergency, notice shall be provided prior to being absent from
5 the state.
6 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective January 11, 2027.
