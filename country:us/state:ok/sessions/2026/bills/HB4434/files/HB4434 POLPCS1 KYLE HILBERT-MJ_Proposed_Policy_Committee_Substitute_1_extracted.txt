7 PROPOSED POLICY COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
8 An Act relating to state government; requiring
certain notification to successor; requiring
9 notification to indicate start of absence and
anticipated return; directing notice to be made
10 twenty-four hours before absence except for
emergencies; providing for codification; and
11 providing an effective date.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
16 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 8.20 of Title 74, unless there
17 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
18 Notification, as provided in this section, shall be required
19 when the Governor is absent from the state. Except in the event of
20 an emergency, at least twenty-four (24) hours prior to the Governor
21 being absent from the state, the Governor shall provide electronic
22 notification to the Lieutenant Governor of his or her plans to be
23 absent from the state, indicating the start date and time and the
24 anticipated return date and time in such notification. The
Req. No. 16214 Page 1
1 noti fication required by this section shall also be required of the
2 acting Governor, when he or she is absent from the state, and follow
3 the line of succession provided in Section 15 of Article VI of the
4 Oklahoma Constitution or as may be provided by law. In the event of
5 an emergency, notice shall be provided prior to being absent from
6 the state.
7 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective January 11, 2027.
