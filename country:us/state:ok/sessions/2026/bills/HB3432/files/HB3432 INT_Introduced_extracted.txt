7 An Act relating to soldiers and sailors; enacting
free public school sporting event admission for
8 veterans; providing for codification; and providing

11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
13 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 8 of Title 72, unless there is
14 created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
15 A. All honorably discharged veterans who are currently Oklahoma
16 residents shall be allowed free admission to all sporting events
17 hosted by a public school. A valid Oklahoma driver license or state
18 photo identification card and military discharge papers (DD Form
19 214) or similar evidence of an honorable discharge from the United
20 States Armed Forces are required for free admission.
21 B. A valid Oklahoma driver license or identification card
22 issued by the Department of Public Safety with a flag emblem and the
23 word "veteran" in the upper left-hand corner of the front of the
