2 2nd Session of the 60th Legislature (2026)
3 HOUSE
RESOLUTION 1039 By: West (Josh)
4
5
6 AS INTRODUCED
7 A Resolution congratulating the University of
8 National Collegiate Athletic Association's women's
gymnastics national title.
9
10
11 WHEREAS, the 2025 Oklahoma women’s gymnastics team posted a
12 198.0125 score at the NCAA Championships to claim the program’s
13 seventh national title in 11 seasons;
14 WHEREAS, OU finished the year with a 33-2 overall record and
15 recorded seven scores of 198-plus;
16 WHEREAS, the Sooners moved into a third-place tie with their
17 seventh all-time national team title;
18 WHEREAS, OU won its first SEC regular season title with a 7-1
19 record in its first year of SEC competition;
20 WHEREAS, the Sooners won their 15th straight NCAA regional title
21 to advance to their 21st straight NCAA Championships;
22 WHEREAS, Jordan Bowers, Addison Fatta, Elle Mueller, Lily
23 Pederson, Danielle Sievers and Faith Torrez combined for 15 All-
24 America honors at the NCAA Championships;
Req. No. 17200 Page 1
1 WHEREAS, Bowers won the individual all-around national title,
2 the 22nd individual championship in program history;
3 WHEREAS, Bowers and Audrey Davis were named finalists for the
4 AAI Award and Honda Sport Award, recognizing the top gymnast in the
5 nation;
6 WHEREAS, Faith Torrez won two SEC individual titles and four
7 individual NCAA regional titles;
8 WHEREAS, Bowers was honored as the SEC Gymnast of the Year and
9 Torrez as the South Central Region Gymnast of the Year;
10 WHEREAS, Bowers was named the Honda Sport Award Winner for
11 Women’s Gymnastics, the fourth such honor in program history; and
12 WHEREAS, the Sooners set a single-season home attendance record
13 of 43,803 fans.
14 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
15 OF THE 2ND SESSION OF THE 60TH OKLAHOMA LEGISLATURE:
16 THAT the House of Representatives expresses profound
17 appreciation to 2025 OU women's gymnastics team for the excitement
18 and pride they bring to the University of Oklahoma, the State of
19 Oklahoma and to Sooners everywhere, and for the exemplary manner in
20 which they represented the University of Oklahoma and added to its
21 tradition of excellence.
