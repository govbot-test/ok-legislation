15 WHEREAS, the 2025 Oklahoma women’s gymnastics team posted a
16 198.0125 score at the NCAA Championships to claim the program’s
17 seventh national title in 11 seasons;
18 WHEREAS, OU finished the year with a 33-2 overall record and
19 recorded seven scores of 198-plus;
20 WHEREAS, the Sooners moved into a third-place tie with their
21 seventh all-time national team title;
22 WHEREAS, OU won its first SEC regular season title with a 7-1
23 record in its first year of SEC competition;

1 WHEREAS, the Sooners won their 15th straight NCAA regional title
2 to advance to their 21st straight NCAA Championships;
3 WHEREAS, Jordan Bowers, Addison Fatta, Elle Mueller, Lily
4 Pederson, Danielle Sievers and Faith Torrez combined for 15 All-
5 America honors at the NCAA Championships;
6 WHEREAS, Bowers won the individual all-around national title,
7 the 22nd individual championship in program history;
8 WHEREAS, Bowers and Audrey Davis were named finalists for the
9 AAI Award and Honda Sport Award, recognizing the top gymnast in the
10 nation;
11 WHEREAS, Faith Torrez won two SEC individual titles and four
12 individual NCAA regional titles;
13 WHEREAS, Bowers was honored as the SEC Gymnast of the Year and
14 Torrez as the South Central Region Gymnast of the Year;
15 WHEREAS, Bowers was named the Honda Sport Award Winner for
16 Women’s Gymnastics, the fourth such honor in program history; and
17 WHEREAS, the Sooners set a single-season home attendance record
18 of 43,803 fans.
19 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
20 OF THE 2ND SESSION OF THE 60TH OKLAHOMA LEGISLATURE:
21 THAT the House of Representatives expresses profound
22 appreciation to 2025 OU women's gymnastics team for the excitement
23 and pride they bring to the University of Oklahoma, the State of
24 Oklahoma and to Sooners everywhere, and for the exemplary manner in
