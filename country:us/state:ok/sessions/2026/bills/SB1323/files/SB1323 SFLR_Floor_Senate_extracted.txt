6 An Act relating to memorial highway and bridge
designations; designating the Sheriff Marty Grisham
7 Memorial Interchange; providing for codification; and
providing an effective date.
8
9
10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
11 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
12 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1698.510 of Title 69, unless
13 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
14 The interchange of Interstate 35 and State Highway 32 in
15 Marietta, Love County, shall be designated as the “Sheriff Marty
16 Grisham Memorial Interchange”. The Department of Transportation
17 shall cause suitable permanent markers bearing that name to be
18 placed at the interchange.
19 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
