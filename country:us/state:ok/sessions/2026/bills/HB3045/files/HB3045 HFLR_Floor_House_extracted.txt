11 An Act relating to motor vehicles; amending 47 O.S.
2021, Section 10-118, which relates to accident
12 response fee prohibited; prohibiting the charging of
certain fee for accident response by certain
13 entities; making certain exception; and providing an
effective date.
14
15
16
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
18 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 47 O.S. 2021, Section 10-118, is
19 amended to read as follows:
20 Section 10-118. A. Notwithstanding any other section of law to
21 the contrary, no person or entity shall impose an accident response
22 fee for the response or investigation of a motor vehicle accident by
23 law enforcement, fire department, or municipality; provided,
24

BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 howe ver, that this prohibition shall not apply to a volunteer fire
2 department.
3 B. For purposes of this section, “accident response fee” means
4 a fee imposed for the response or investigation of a motor vehicle
5 accident and does not mean any fee otherwise specifically authorized
6 by law.
7 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
