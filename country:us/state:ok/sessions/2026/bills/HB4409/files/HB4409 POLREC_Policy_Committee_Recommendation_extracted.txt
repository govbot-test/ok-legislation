8 POLICY COMMITTEE RECOMMENDATION
9 An Act relating to housing; creating the Legislative
Workforce Housing Committee; directing Committee to
10 increase access to affordable housing; granting
rulemaking authority; directing Committee to work
11 with the Oklahoma Housing Finance Agency; providing
for codification; providing for a sunset date; and
12 providing an effective date.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 2904 of Title 74, unless there
18 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 A. There is hereby created the Legislative Workforce Housing
20 Committee for the purpose of assisting in carrying out the goals of
21 the Oklahoma Housing Stability Program in Section 2903 of this title
22 and providing policy improvements to make the program meet the
23 changing needs of the state.
24
Req. No. 16528 Page 1
1 B. The Committee shall be bicameral and bipartisan and consist
2 of the following:
3 1. One co-chair appointed by the Speaker of the Oklahoma House
4 of Representatives;
5 2. One co-chair appointed by the President Pro Tempore of the
6 Oklahoma State Senate;
7 3. One vice-chair appointed by the Minority Party Leader of the
8 Oklahoma House of Representatives;
9 4. One vice-chair appointed by the Minority Party Leader of the
10 Oklahoma State Senate; and
11 5. The Speaker of the Oklahoma House of Representatives and the
12 President Pro Tempore of the Oklahoma State Senate shall appoint the
13 Committee members in cooperation with the Committee chairs and vice-
14 chairs. There shall be an equal number of additional members from
15 the House and Senate and shall be equal in party affiliation.
16 C. The rules and meeting schedule shall be established by the
17 chairs and vice-chairs of the Committee and shall be approved by
18 members.
19 D. The Committee shall work with the Oklahoma Housing Finance
20 Agency to jointly administer the Oklahoma Housing Stability Program
21 and improve access to affordable housing throughout the state. The
22 Committee may seek input from a variety of stakeholders throughout
23 the state and if needed and desired, bring in expertise from out of
24 state to advise and counsel the Committee members on potential
Req. No. 16528 Page 2
1 acti ons, policy changes, and statutes that assist to achieve the
2 Committee's objectives.
3 E. The provisions of this section shall cease to have the force
4 and effect of law on November 1, 2031.
5 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
