Section 1:
1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 2nd Session of the 60th Legislature (2026)
4
5
6
7 AS INTRODUCED
8 An Act relating to housing; creating the Legislative
Workforce Housing Committee; directing Committee to
9 increase access to affordable housing; granting
rulemaking authority; directing Committee to work
10 with the Oklahoma Housing Finance Agency; providing
for codification; and providing an effective date.
11
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
16 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 2904 of Title 74, unless there
17 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
18 A. There is hereby created the Legislative Workforce Housing
19 Committee for the purpose of assisting in carrying out the goals of
20 the Oklahoma Housing Stability Program in Section 2903 of this title
21 and providing policy improvements to make the program meet the
22 changing needs of the state.
23 B. The Committee shall be bicameral and bipartisan and consist
24 of the following:
Req. No. 14273 Page 1
1 1. One co-chair appointed by the Speaker of the Oklahoma House
2 of Representatives;
3 2. One co-chair appointed by the President Pro Tempore of the
4 Oklahoma State Senate;
5 3. One vice-chair appointed by the Minority Party Leader of the
6 Oklahoma House of Representatives;
7 4. One vice-chair appointed by the Minority Party Leader of the
8 Oklahoma State Senate; and
9 5. The Speaker of the Oklahoma House of Representatives and the
10 President Pro Tempore of the Oklahoma State Senate shall appoint the
11 Committee members in cooperation with the Committee chairs and vice-
12 chairs. There shall be an equal number of additional members from
13 the House and Senate and shall be equal in party affiliation.
14 C. The rules and meeting schedule shall be established by the
15 chairs and vice-chairs of the Committee and shall be approved by
16 members.
17 D. The Committee shall work with the Oklahoma Housing Finance
18 Agency to jointly administer the Oklahoma Housing Stability Program
19 and improve access to affordable housing throughout the state. The
20 Committee may seek input from a variety of stakeholders throughout
21 the state and if needed and desired, bring in expertise from out of
22 state to advise and counsel the Committee members on potential
23 actions, policy changes, and statutes that assist to achieve the
24 Committee's objectives.
Req. No. 14273 Page 2
1 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
