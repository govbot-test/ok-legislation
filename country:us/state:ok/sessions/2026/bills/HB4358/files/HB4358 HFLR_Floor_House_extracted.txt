10 AS INTRODUCED
11 An Act relating to schools; limiting screen time for
prekindergarten through fifth grade public school
12 students to one hour per school day; defining term;
providing exceptions; providing for codification;
13 providing an effective date; and declaring an
emergency.
14
15
16
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
18 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
19 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 11-103.17 of Title 70, unless
20 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
21 A. Beginning with the 2026-2027 school year, public school
22 students in prekindergarten through fifth grade shall be limited to
23 one hour of screen time per school day, inclusive of all subjects.
24 Screen time includes teacher-directed or child-selected activities
HB4358 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 that utilize a computer, tablet, television, smart device,
2 Chromebook, or other similar device with an electronic display.
3 B. The screentime limitations set forth in subsection A of this
4 section shall not apply to the following:
5 1. Special-education needs documented in an individualized
6 education program (IEP) or a 504 plan created under the
7 Rehabilitation Act of 1973, 29 U.S.C., Section 504; and
8 2. Assistive or adaptive technology required for student
9 access.
10 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
11 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
12 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
13 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
14 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
