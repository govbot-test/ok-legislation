12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 11-103.17 of Title 70, unless
15 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 A. Beginning with the 2026-2027 school year, public school
17 students in prekindergarten through fifth grade shall be limited to
18 one (1) hour of screen time per school day, inclusive of all
19 subjects. Screen time includes teacher-directed or child-selected
20 activities that utilize a computer, tablet, television, smart
21 device, Chromebook, or other similar device with an electronic
22 display.
23 B. The screentime limitations set forth in subsection A of this
24 section shall not apply to the following:
