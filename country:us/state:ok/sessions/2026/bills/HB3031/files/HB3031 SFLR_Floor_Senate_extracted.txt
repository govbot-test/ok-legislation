8 An Act relating to economic development and workforce
investment; stating legislative findings and intent;
9 creating the North Pointe Workforce Development
Initiative Revolving Fund; providing for source of
10 funds; and providing for noncodification.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
15 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
16 The Legislature finds that:
17 1. Workforce participation and targeted job training are
18 critical to strengthening Oklahoma's economy;
19 2. Strategic investment in workforce-first development
20 initiatives produces measurable employment, training, and wage
21 outcomes;
22 3. The North Pointe Workforce Development Initiative is
23 designed to prioritize local hiring, job readiness, skills training,
24 and long-term economic sustainability; and
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - HB3031 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 4. A one-time capital and programmatic investment may be
2 necessary to accelerate workforce training and job placement
3 outcomes.
4 It is therefore the intent of the Legislature to authorize a
5 one-time appropriation, subject to available funds and legislative
6 approval through the annual budgeting process, to support the North
7 Pointe Workforce Development Initiative.
8 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
9 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
10 There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving fund
11 for the Oklahoma Department of Commerce to be designated the North
12 Pointe Workforce Development Initiative Revolving Fund. The fund
13 shall be a continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations,
14 and shall consist of all monies received by the Oklahoma Department
15 of Commerce from such sources as may be provided by law. All monies
16 accruing to the credit of said fund are hereby appropriated and may
17 be budgeted and expended by the Oklahoma Department of Commerce as
18 may be necessary to carry out the objectives of the North Pointe
19 Workforce Development Initiative. Expenditures from said fund shall
20 be made upon warrants issued by the State Treasurer against claims
21 filed as prescribed by law with the Director of the Office of
22 Management and Enterprise Services for approval and payment.
23 COMMITTEE REPORT BY: COMMITTEE ON ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT, WORKFORCE
AND TOURISM
