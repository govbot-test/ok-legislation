9 FLOOR SUBSTITUTE
10 An Act relating to economic development and workforce
investment; stating legislative findings and intent;
11 creating the North Pointe Workforce Development
Initiative Revolving Fund; providing for source of
12 funds; providing for noncodification; and declaring
an emergency.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
17 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
18 The Legislature finds that:
19 1. Workforce participation and targeted job training are
20 critical to strengthening Oklahoma's economy;
21 2. Strategic investment in workforce-first development
22 initiatives produces measurable employment, training, and wage
23 outcomes;
24
Req. No. 17168 Page 1
1 3. The North Pointe workforce development initiative is
2 designed to prioritize local hiring, job readiness, skills training,
3 and long-term economic sustainability; and
4 4. A one-time capital and programmatic investment may be
5 necessary to accelerate workforce training and job placement
6 outcomes.
7 It is therefore the intent of the Legislature to authorize a
8 one-time appropriation, subject to available funds and legislative
9 approval through the annual budgeting process, to support the North
10 Pointe workforce development initiative.
11 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
12 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
13 There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving fund
14 for the Oklahoma Department of Commerce to be designated the "North
15 Pointe Workforce Development Initiative Revolving Fund". The fund
16 shall be a continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations,
17 and shall consist of all monies received by the Oklahoma Department
18 of Commerce from such sources as may be provided by law. All monies
19 accruing to the credit of said fund are hereby appropriated and may
20 be budgeted and expended by the Oklahoma Department of Commerce as
21 may be necessary to carry out the objectives of the North Pointe
22 workforce development initiative. Expenditures from said fund shall
23 be made upon warrants issued by the State Treasurer against claims
24
Req. No. 17168 Page 2
1 file d as prescribed by law with the Director of the Office of
2 Management and Enterprise Services for approval and payment.
3 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
4 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
5 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
6 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
