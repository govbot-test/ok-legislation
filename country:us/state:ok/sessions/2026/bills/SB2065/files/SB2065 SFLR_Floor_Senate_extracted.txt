6 An Act relating to state symbols; designating the
European honeybee as the state agricultural
7 pollinator; designating the American bumblebee as the
state native insect; designating the Carolina mantis
8 as the state predator insect; designating the rainbow
scarab as the state soil conservation insect;
9 providing for codification; and providing an
effective date.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 98.26 of Title 25, unless there
15 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 The European honeybee, Apis mellifera, is hereby designated and
17 adopted as the state agricultural pollinator of the State of
18 Oklahoma.
19 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
20 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 98.27 of Title 25, unless there
21 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
22 The American bumblebee, Bombus pensylvanicus, is hereby
23 designated and adopted as the state native insect of the State of
24 Oklahoma.
