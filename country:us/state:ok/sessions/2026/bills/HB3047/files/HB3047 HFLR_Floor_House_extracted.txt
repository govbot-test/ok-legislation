9 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
10 An Act relating to state government; amending 74 O.S.
2021, Section 464, which relates to certain
11 electronic filing; requiring filing of agency reports
with the Legislative Office of Fiscal Transparency;
12 prescribing procedures related to indexing of
reports; and providing an effective date.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 74 O.S. 2021, Section 464, is
17 amended to read as follows:
18 Section 464. Whenever any provision of law directs that a
19 report, administrative rule, budget work program, budget request, or
20 any other document be filed with the Governor, President Pro Tempore
21 of the Senate or the Speaker of the House of Representatives, such
22 documents shall be filed electronically, except as otherwise
23 provided in this section. The Governor, President Pro Tempore of
24 the Senate and Speaker of the House of Representatives shall each
HB3047 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 crea te or cause to be created on the official websites for the
2 Governor, Senate and House of Representatives, respectively, a
3 mechanism for such filings to be made, with an electronic return
4 receipt provided to the person making the filing. If for any reason
5 the person required to file such document determines that it cannot
6 be filed electronically, the person shall file a printed copy in
7 lieu of such electronic filing and shall include an explanation of
8 the reason that the document could not be filed electronically. Any
9 report that an agency is required by statute to provide to the
10 Legislature shall be electronically filed with the Legislative
11 Office of Fiscal Transparency, which is hereby directed to establish
12 an online filing system for the receipt of reports. The Legislative
13 Office of Fiscal Transparency shall maintain an index of all reports
14 that are statutorily required to be provided by agencies to the
15 Legislature, to include the submittal status of each report. The
16 Legislative Office of Fiscal Transparency shall also notify the
17 Legislature of the availability of reports in a centralized location
18 and maintain electronic copies of the reports.
19 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
20
21 COMMITTEE REPORT BY: COMMITTEE ON APPROPRIATIONS AND BUDGET, dated
03/05/2026 - DO PASS, As Amended and Coauthored.
