6 An Act relating to state government; amending 74 O.S.
2021, Section 464, which relates to certain
7 electronic filing; requiring filing of agency reports
with the Legislative Office of Fiscal Transparency;
8 prescribing procedures related to indexing of
reports; and providing an effective date.
9
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 74 O.S. 2021, Section 464, is
13 amended to read as follows:
14 Section 464. Whenever any provision of law directs that a
15 report, administrative rule, budget work program, budget request, or
16 any other document be filed with the Governor, President Pro Tempore
17 of the Senate or the Speaker of the House of Representatives, such
18 documents shall be filed electronically, except as otherwise
19 provided in this section. The Governor, President Pro Tempore of
20 the Senate and Speaker of the House of Representatives shall each
21 create or cause to be created on the official websites for the
22 Governor, Senate and House of Representatives, respectively, a
23 mechanism for such filings to be made, with an electronic return
24 receipt provided to the person making the filing. If for any reason
ENGR. H. B. NO. 3047 Page 1
1 the person required to file such document determines that it cannot
2 be filed electronically, the person shall file a printed copy in
3 lieu of such electronic filing and shall include an explanation of
4 the reason that the document could not be filed electronically. Any
5 report that an agency is required by statute to provide to the
6 Legislature shall be electronically filed with the Legislative
7 Office of Fiscal Transparency, which is hereby directed to establish
8 an online filing system for the receipt of reports. The Legislative
9 Office of Fiscal Transparency shall maintain an index of all reports
10 that are statutorily required to be provided by agencies to the
11 Legislature, including the submittal status of each report. The
12 Legislative Office of Fiscal Transparency shall also notify the
13 Legislature of the availability of the reports in a centralized
14 location and shall maintain electronic copies of the reports.
15 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
16 Passed the House of Representatives the 23rd day of March, 2026.
