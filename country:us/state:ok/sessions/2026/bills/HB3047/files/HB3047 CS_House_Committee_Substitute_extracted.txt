14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 74 O.S. 2021, Section 464, is
16 amended to read as follows:
17 Section 464. Whenever any provision of law directs that a
18 report, administrative rule, budget work program, budget request, or
19 any other document be filed with the Governor, President Pro Tempore
20 of the Senate or the Speaker of the House of Representatives, such
21 documents shall be filed electronically, except as otherwise
22 provided in this section. The Governor, President Pro Tempore of
23 the Senate and Speaker of the House of Representatives shall each
24 create or cause to be created on the official websites for the

1 Gove rnor, Senate and House of Representatives, respectively, a
2 mechanism for such filings to be made, with an electronic return
3 receipt provided to the person making the filing. If for any reason
4 the person required to file such document determines that it cannot
5 be filed electronically, the person shall file a printed copy in
6 lieu of such electronic filing and shall include an explanation of
7 the reason that the document could not be filed electronically. Any
8 report that an agency is required by statute to provide to the
9 Legislature shall be electronically filed with the Legislative
10 Office of Fiscal Transparency, which is hereby directed to establish
11 an online filing system for the receipt of reports. The Legislative
12 Office of Fiscal Transparency shall maintain an index of all reports
13 that are statutorily required to be provided by agencies to the
14 Legislature, to include the submittal status of each report. The
15 Legislative Office of Fiscal Transparency shall also notify the
16 Legislature of the availability of reports in a centralized location
17 and maintain electronic copies of the reports.
18 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
