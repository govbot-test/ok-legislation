15 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
16 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 127 of Title 85A, unless there
17 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
18 ACUTE MYOCARDIAL INFARCTION OR STROKE.
19 A. A firefighter, peace officer, or emergency medical
20 technician who suffers an acute myocardial infarction or stroke
21 resulting in disability or death is presumed to have suffered the
22 disability or death during the course and scope of employment as a
23 firefighter, peace officer, or emergency medical technician if:

1 1. While on duty, the firefighter, peace officer, or emergency
2 medical technician:
3 a. was engaged in a situation that involved stressful or
4 strenuous physical activity involving fire
5 suppression, rescue, hazardous material response,
6 emergency medical services, law enforcement, or other
7 emergency response activity, or
8 b. participated in a training exercise that involved
9 stressful or strenuous physical activity; and
10 2. The acute myocardial infarction or stroke occurred not later
11 than eight (8) hours after the end of a shift in which the
12 firefighter, peace officer, or emergency medical technician was
13 engaged in the activity described in paragraph 1 of this subsection.
14 B. There shall be a rebuttable presumption that the medical
15 events described in subsection A of this section arose out of the
16 employment performed by the person and occurred while the employee
17 was acting within the scope of employment. This presumption may be
18 overcome by clear and convincing evidence.
19 C. For purposes of this section, "stressful or strenuous
20 physical activity" does not include clerical, administrative, or
21 nonmanual activities.
22 D. For purposes of this section, "firefighter", "peace
23 officer", and "emergency medical technician" mean only those
