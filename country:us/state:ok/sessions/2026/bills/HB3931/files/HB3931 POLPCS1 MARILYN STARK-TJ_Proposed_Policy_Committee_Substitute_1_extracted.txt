19 Section 1-321. A. A certificate or record registered under
20 this article may be amended only in accordance with this article and

6 certificates within one (1) year after the date of birth without the
7 certificate being considered as amended. Certificates shall be
8 marked as "amended" for minor corrections made one (1) year after

11 court of competent jurisdiction, changing the name of a person born

13 guardian or legal representative, the Commissioner of Health shall
14 amend the certificate of birth to reflect the new name.

19 1. Upon request and receipt of a sworn acknowledgment of

21 2. Upon receipt of a certified copy of a court order

4 E. For a child born out of wedlock, the Commissioner shall also
5 change the surname of the child on the certificate:
6 1. To the specified surname upon receipt of acknowledgment of
