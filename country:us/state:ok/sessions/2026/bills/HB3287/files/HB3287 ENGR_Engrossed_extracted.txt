5 An Act relating to public health and safety;

11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
13 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1-114.4 of Title 63, unless
14 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
15 A. Upon the effective date of this act, every hospital and
16 health care facility in this state shall be required to post
17 domestic violence and human trafficking signage in inconspicuous
18 areas throughout the hospital and health care facility and in areas
19 deemed private and restricted for the use of patients and health
20 care providers only. The signage shall contain the following
21 information:
22 "If you are a victim of domestic abuse or if you are being
23 forced or have been forced to perform any sexual act or are a victim

2 immediately."
3 B. Hospitals and health care facilities shall develop protocols
4 and procedures to be used for reporting alleged or confirmed cases
5 of domestic abuse and human trafficking for commercial sex to
6 appropriate law enforcement agencies if requested to do so by the
7 victim prior to discharge of the victim from the hospital or release
8 from a health care facility. The protocols shall also include
9 procedures for assisting law enforcement and the victim of domestic
10 abuse or human trafficking in securing said discharge or release
11 without interference by or notification to the alleged perpetrator
12 of domestic abuse or human trafficking.
13 C. The State Department of Health shall promulgate rules as
14 necessary to coordinate the placement, size, format, and
15 specifications of the required signage.
16 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
