3 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
FOR

7 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
8 An Act relating to public health and safety;
directing hospitals and health care facilities to
9 post certain signage; stating signage requirements;
directing hospitals and health care facilities to
10 develop certain protocols and procedures; directing
the State Department of Health to promulgate rules;
11 providing for codification; and providing an
effective date.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
16 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1-114.4 of Title 63, unless
17 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
18 A. Upon the effective date of this act, every hospital and
19 health care facility in this state shall be required to post
20 domestic violence and human trafficking signage in inconspicuous
21 areas throughout the hospital and health care facility and in areas
22 deemed private and restricted for the use of patients and healthcare
23 providers only. The signage shall contain the following
24 information:
Req. No. 16777 Page 1
CORRECTED
1 "If you are a victim of domestic abuse or if you are being
2 forced or have been forced to perform any sexual act or are a victim
3 of human trafficking, please notify the staff of this facility
4 immediately."
5 B. Hospitals and health care facilities shall develop protocols
6 and procedures to be used for reporting alleged or confirmed cases
7 of domestic abuse and human trafficking for commercial sex to
8 appropriate law enforcement agencies if requested to do so by the
9 victim prior to discharge of the victim from the hospital or release
10 from a health care facility. The protocols shall also include
11 procedures for assisting law enforcement and the victim of domestic
12 abuse or human trafficking in securing said discharge or release
13 without interference by or notification to the alleged perpetrator
14 of domestic abuse or human trafficking.
15 C. The State Department of Health shall make the signage
16 available for print on the public website of the Department.
17 Hospitals and health care facilities may request that the signage be
18 mailed to the hospital or health care facility for the cost of
19 printing and first class postage.
20 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
