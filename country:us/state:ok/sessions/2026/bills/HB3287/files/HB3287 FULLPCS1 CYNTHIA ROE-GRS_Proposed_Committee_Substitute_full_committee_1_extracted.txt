8 PROPOSED OVERSIGHT COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
9 An Act relating to public health and safety;
directing hospitals and health care facilities to
10 post certain signage; stating signage requirements;
directing hospitals and health care facilities to
11 develop certain protocols and procedures; directing
the State Department of Health to promulgate rules;
12 providing for codification; and providing an
effective date.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1-114.4 of Title 63, unless
18 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 A. Upon the effective date of this act, every hospital and
20 health care facility in this state shall be required to post
21 domestic violence and human trafficking signage in inconspicuous
22 areas throughout the hospital and health care facility and in areas
23 deemed private and restricted for the use of patients and healthcare
24
Req. No. 16665 Page 1
1 prov iders only. The signage shall contain the following
2 information:
3 "If you are a victim of domestic abuse or if you are being
4 forced or have been forced to perform any sexual act or are a victim
5 of human trafficking, please notify the staff of this facility
6 immediately."
7 B. Hospitals and health care facilities shall develop protocols
8 and procedures to be used for reporting alleged or confirmed cases
9 of domestic abuse and human trafficking for commercial sex to
10 appropriate law enforcement agencies if requested to do so by the
11 victim prior to discharge of the victim from the hospital or release
12 from a health care facility. The protocols shall also include
13 procedures for assisting law enforcement and the victim of domestic
14 abuse or human trafficking in securing said discharge or release
15 without interference by or notification to the alleged perpetrator
16 of domestic abuse or human trafficking.
17 C. The State Department of Health shall promulgate rules as
18 necessary to coordinate the placement, size, format, and
19 specifications of the required signage.
20 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
