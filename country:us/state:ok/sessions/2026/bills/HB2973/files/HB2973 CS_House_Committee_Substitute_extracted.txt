2 2nd Session of the 60th Legislature (2026)
3 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
FOR
5
6
7 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
8 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Cost Accounting
System; requiring school districts to report all
9 state-appropriated spending using a specific data
code; directing the State Board of Education to adopt
10 a data code in compliance with the required
reporting; prohibiting the use of the adopted data
11 code for any other type of funding; providing for
codification; and providing an effective date.
12
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 5-135.5 of Title 70, unless
18 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 A. Beginning with the 2026-2027 school year and each school
20 year thereafter, every public school district shall, in addition to
21 any other applicable data code, use a data code in the Oklahoma Cost
22 Accounting System, adopted by the State Board of Education, to
23 report the expenditure of any state-appropriated funding for any
24 purpose by the public school district.
Req. No. 16955 Page 1
1 B. The State Board of Education shall adopt a data code for the
2 Oklahoma Cost Accounting System which shall be used to comply with
3 the reporting required by this section. The adopted data code shall
4 not be used for any expenditures that are not paid for using state-
5 appropriated funds.
6 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
