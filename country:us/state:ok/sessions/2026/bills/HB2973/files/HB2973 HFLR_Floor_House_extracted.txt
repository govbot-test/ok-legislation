10 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
11 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Cost Accounting
System; requiring school districts to report all
12 state-appropriated spending using a specific data
code; directing the State Board of Education to adopt
13 a data code in compliance with the required
reporting; prohibiting the use of the adopted data
14 code for any other type of funding; providing for
codification; and providing an effective date.
15
16
17
18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
19 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
20 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 5-135.5 of Title 70, unless
21 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
22 A. Beginning with the 2026-2027 school year and each school
23 year thereafter, every public school district shall, in addition to
24 any other applicable data code, use a data code in the Oklahoma Cost
HB2973 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 Acco unting System, adopted by the State Board of Education, to
2 report the expenditure of any state-appropriated funding for any
3 purpose by the public school district.
4 B. The State Board of Education shall adopt a data code for the
5 Oklahoma Cost Accounting System which shall be used to comply with
6 the reporting required by this section. The adopted data code shall
7 not be used for any expenditures that are not paid for using state-
8 appropriated funds.
9 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
