9 An Act relating to transportation; requiring the
Department of Transportation to submit certain
10 notification to the Transportation Commission;
requiring Department to receive certain approval
11 before taking certain action; providing for
codification; and providing an effective date.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
16 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1105 of Title 69, unless there
17 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
18 The Department of Transportation shall, prior to making a claim
19 against a surety company for financial loss due to the
20 nonperformance of a contractor engaged in contract with the
21 Department, notify the Transportation Commission of its intent to
22 make such claim. The Transportation Commission shall review the
23 information submitted by the Department and approve or disapprove
24

1 the making of such claim. The Department shall only proceed with
2 making such claim if the Transportation Commission approves of such.
3 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
