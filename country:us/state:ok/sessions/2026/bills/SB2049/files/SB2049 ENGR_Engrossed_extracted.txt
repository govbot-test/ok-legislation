6 An Act relating to transportation; requiring the
Department of Transportation to submit certain
7 notification to the Transportation Commission;
requiring Department to receive certain approval
8 before taking certain action; providing for
codification; and providing an effective date.
9
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
13 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1105 of Title 69, unless there
14 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
15 The Department of Transportation shall, prior to making a claim
16 against a surety company for financial loss due to the
17 nonperformance of a contractor engaged in contract with the
18 Department, notify the Transportation Commission of its intent to
19 make such claim. The Transportation Commission shall review the
20 information submitted by the Department and approve or disapprove
21 the making of such claim. The Department shall only proceed with
22 making such claim if the Transportation Commission approves of such.
23 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
24
