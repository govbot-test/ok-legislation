I move to amend Senate Bill No. 1421 as follows:
1. On Page 2, Line 8, by deleting after the semicolon “;”, the word “and”;
2. On Page 2, Line 10, by deleting after the word “training”, the period “.” and inserting the
following language: “; and”;
3. On Page 2, Line 10 ½, by inserting the following new paragraph 3:
“3. Shall be prohibited from affirming a gender different than the sex of the child assigned at
birth matching his or her physical anatomy. For the purposes of this paragraph, “affirming” shall
include, but not be limited to, using pronouns, names, or other identifying information different than
that which corresponds with the physical sex of the individual.”; and
4. By amending the title to conform.
