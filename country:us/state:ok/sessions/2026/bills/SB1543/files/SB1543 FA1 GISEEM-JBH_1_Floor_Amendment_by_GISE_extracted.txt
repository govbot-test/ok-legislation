FLOOR AMENDMENT
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
State of Oklahoma
SPEAKER:
CHAIR:
I move to amend SB1543
Page 14 Section 1 Lines 19
Of the Engrossed Bill
by deleting the following phrase "paragraph 2 of subsection C of"; and
On page 31, Section 2, line 19 by deleting the word "may" and inserting in
lieu thereof the word "shall"; and
On page 31, Section 2, Line 20-21 by deleting the phrase "any county in
which any of the underlying violations occurred." and inserting in lieu
thereof the following language "the county in which the last violation
occurred. Upon filing of an aggregated charge, any pending charge for an
underlying violation in another county shall be dismissed without
prejudice.".
AMEND TITLE TO CONFORM TO AMENDMENTS
Amendment submitted by: Emily Gise
