6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to the District Attorneys Council;
making an appropriation; requiring appropriated funds
8 be utilized for certain purposes; providing for
noncodification; providing an effective date; and
9 declaring an emergency.
10
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
15 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
16 There is hereby appropriated to the District Attorneys Council
17 from any monies not otherwise appropriated from the General Revenue
18 Fund of the State Treasury for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2027,
19 the sum of Two Hundred Fifty Thousand Dollars ($250,000.00) or so
20 much thereof as may be necessary for the purpose of hiring a Sexual
21 Assault Nurse Examiner (SANE) Statewide Coordinator and related
22 program expenditures pursuant to the provisions of Section 215.41 of
23 Title 19 of the Oklahoma Statutes.
24 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
