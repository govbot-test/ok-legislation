9 An Act relating to sexual assault nurse examiners;

10 Supp. 2025, Section 215.41), which relates to sexual

17 2023 (19 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section 215.41), is amended to read as
18 follows:
19 Section 215.41. Subject to availability of funding, there is
20 established until October 1, 2025, within the District Attorneys
21 Council under the direction of the Executive Coordinator of the
22 District Attorneys Council, the position of Sexual Assault Nurse
23 Examiner (SANE) Statewide Coordinator. The Statewide Coordinator
24 shall oversee forensic medical examination training throughout the

5 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
6 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
7 There is hereby appropriated to the District Attorneys Council
8 from any monies not otherwise appropriated from the General Revenue
9 Fund of the State Treasury for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2027,

11 much thereof as may be necessary for the purpose of hiring a Sexual
12 Assault Nurse Examiner (SANE) Statewide Coordinator and related
13 program expenditures pursuant to the provisions of Section 215.41 of
14 Title 19 of the Oklahoma Statutes.
