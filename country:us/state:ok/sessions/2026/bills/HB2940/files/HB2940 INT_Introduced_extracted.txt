1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 2nd Session of the 60th Legislature (2026)
3 HOUSE BILL 2940 By: Olsen
4

6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Open Meeting Act;
creating the First Amendment Open Meetings Protection
8 Act; amending 25 O.S. 2021, Section 312, which
relates to recording public meeting proceedings;
9 clarifying that personal recordings shall not be
prohibited unless they interfere with the public
10 meeting; including the Legislature and legislative
committee meetings in recording permissions; allowing
11 monetary damages for unlawful prohibitions of
recordings; providing for noncodification; and
12 providing an effective date.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
17 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
18 This act shall be known and may be cited as the "First Amendment
19 Open Meetings Protection Act".
20 SECTION 2. AMENDATORY 25 O.S. 2021, Section 312, is
21 amended to read as follows:
22 Section 312. A. The proceedings of a public body shall be kept
23 by a person so designated by such public body in the form of written
24 minutes which shall be an official summary of the proceedings

17 title, the Oklahoma Legislature shall be considered a public body
18 solely for the purposes of this subsection, and legislative
