8 An Act relating to human trafficking; amending 21
O.S. 2021, Section 748, as last amended by Section
9 20, Chapter 486, O.S.L. 2025 (21 O.S. Supp. 2025,
Section 748), which relates to human trafficking;
10 requiring admissibility of certain expert testimony;
amending 74 O.S. 2021, Sections 18p-1, as amended by
11 Section 2, Chapter 453, O.S.L. 2024, 18p-3, 18p-4,
18p-5, 18p-6, 18p-7, and 18p-8 (74 O.S. Supp. 2025,
12 Section 18p-1), which relate to domestic violence and
sexual assault programs and services; modifying
13 definition; authorizing Attorney General to enter
into agreements for services for victims of human
14 trafficking; providing for confidentiality of certain
information; authorizing human trafficking shelters
15 to provide certain services; expanding certain
telephone communication service to victims of human
16 trafficking; requiring Attorney General to promulgate
rules for certification of human trafficking programs
17 and services; requiring certification of certain
shelters and programs by the Attorney General;
18 authorizing Attorney General or district attorney to
bring certain actions; authorizing Attorney General
19 to collect certain information; updating statutory
language; and providing an effective date.
