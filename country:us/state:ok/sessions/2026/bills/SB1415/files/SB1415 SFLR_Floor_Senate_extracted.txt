6 An Act relating to state government; providing for
certain nondisclosure agreements to be void and
7 unenforceable; providing certain exception; providing
for codification; and providing an effective date.
8
9
10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
11 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
12 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 840-2.11a of Title 74, unless
13 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
14 A. Except as provided in subsection B of this section,
15 nondisclosure agreements between a state employee and the state
16 agency or entity that employs such shall be deemed void and
17 unenforceable upon the:
18 1. Resignation of the employee from employment with such agency
19 or entity; or
20 2. Termination of employment of the employee by such agency or
21 entity.
22 B. The provisions of subsection A of this section shall not be
23 construed as applying to any state employee that has a mandated
24 professional privilege of confidentiality.
