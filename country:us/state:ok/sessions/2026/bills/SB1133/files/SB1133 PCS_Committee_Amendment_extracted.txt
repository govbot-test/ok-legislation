15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. There is hereby appropriated to the Department of
17 Mental Health and Substance Abuse Services, from any monies not
18 otherwise appropriated from the Statewide Recovery Fund (497) of the
19 State Treasury, the sum of Fifteen Million Dollars ($15,000,000.00)
20 or so much thereof as may be necessary to address gaps in the
21 project to replace capacity of Griffin Memorial Hospital. Such
22 funds shall be utilized in a manner consistent with the
23 recommendations adopted by the Joint Committee on Pandemic Relief
24 Funding on April 15, 2026.

5 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
