11 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
12 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 160.20.1 of Title 17, unless
13 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
14 A. Commercial wind energy equipment shall:
15 1. Not be located within two and one-half times the tip height
16 from the outer wall of a dwelling unless waived in writing by the
17 owner of the dwelling and filed of record with the county clerk in
18 the county where the tract or tracts are located; and
19 2. Not be located within one and one-half times the tip height
20 of a nonparticipating property line unless waived in writing by the
21 owner of the relevant nonparticipating property and filed of record
22 with the county clerk in the county where the tract or tracts are
23 located.

1 B. Setbacks shall be measured from the closest part of the wind
2 turbine base to the outer wall of the dwelling or nonparticipating
3 property line using standard industry practices, and no extra
4 setback or buffer is required.
5 C. The provisions of this section shall not apply to small or
6 community-scale wind systems serving on-site load with a rated
7 capacity of less than one (1) megawatt.
8 D. The provisions of this section shall apply to wind energy
9 facilities and applications for which a lease has been signed, the
10 entirety of the site control for the project is obtained, and the
11 developer has filed for a queue position with the Southwest Power
12 Pool.
13 E. The provisions of this section shall be in effect until
14 November 1, 2028.
