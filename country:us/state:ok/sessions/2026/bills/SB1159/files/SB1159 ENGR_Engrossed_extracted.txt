7 An Act relating to the Corporation Commission; making
an appropriation; providing an effective date; and
8 declaring an emergency.
9
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. There is hereby appropriated to the Remediation
13 Assistance Revolving Fund from any monies not otherwise appropriated
14 from the General Revenue Fund of the State Treasury for the fiscal
15 year ending June 30, 2027, the sum of Five Hundred Thousand Dollars
16 ($500,000.00) or so much thereof as may be necessary to carry out
17 the provisions of Enrolled Senate Bill No. 1319 of the 2nd Session
18 of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature.
19 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
20 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
21 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
22 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
23 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
