16 amended to read as follows:
17 Section 246. A. This act shall be known and may be cited as
18 the “Act to Restore Hope, Opportunity and Prosperity for Everyone”
19 or the “HOPE Act”.
20 B. Prior to awarding assistance approving coverage under the
21 state Medicaid program, the Oklahoma Health Care Authority shall

1 G. H. If the Authority receives information concerning an

5 1. If the information does not result in the Authority finding
