7 POLICY COMMITTEE RECOMMENDATION
8 An Act relating to alcoholic beverages; amending 37A
O.S. 2021, Section 5-120, which relates to books and
9 records of licensees; requiring licensee to provide
certain books and records within a certain timeframe;
10 and providing an effective date.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 37A O.S. 2021, Section 5-120, is
15 amended to read as follows:
16 Section 5-120. All licensees under the Oklahoma Alcoholic
17 Beverage Control Act shall keep books and records with regard to
18 alcoholic beverages which shall contain such information and
19 itemization thereof as the ABLE Commission may prescribe by rule.
20 All books, records, inventories, invoices and other accounting
21 documents required by the Oklahoma Alcoholic Beverage Control Act
22 shall be kept for three (3) years and shall at all times be
23 available for inspection, upon request, by duly authorized
24

1 repr esentatives of the ABLE Commission or the Oklahoma Tax
2 Commission within ten (10) business days after being requested.
3 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
