10 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
11 An Act relating to alcoholic beverages; amending 37A
O.S. 2021, Section 5-120, which relates to books and
12 records of licensees; requiring licensee to provide
certain books and records within a certain timeframe;
13 and providing an effective date.
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 37A O.S. 2021, Section 5-120, is
18 amended to read as follows:
19 Section 5-120. All licensees under the Oklahoma Alcoholic
20 Beverage Control Act shall keep books and records with regard to
21 alcoholic beverages which shall contain such information and
22 itemization thereof as the ABLE Commission may prescribe by rule.
23 All books, records, inventories, invoices and other accounting
24 documents required by the Oklahoma Alcoholic Beverage Control Act
HB3530 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 shal l be kept for three (3) years and shall at all times be
2 available for inspection, upon request, by duly authorized
3 representatives of the ABLE Commission or the Oklahoma Tax
4 Commission within ten (10) business days after being requested.
5 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
6
7 COMMITTEE REPORT BY: COMMITTEE ON HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES
