1 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
2 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 34.102A-1 of Title 62, unless
3 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
4 A. Sections 1 through 5 of this act shall be known and may be
5 cited as the "Taxpayer Endowment Trust Fund Act".
6 B. 1. The Legislature finds that Oklahoma’s long-term economic
7 growth and fiscal stability are best served by policies that promote
8 savings, investment, and reduced reliance on income taxation;
9 2. The Legislature further finds that states with an abundance
10 of energy resources that preserve and grow a portion of those
11 revenues through investments enjoy lower taxes and budget stability;
12 3. Establishing a sovereign wealth fund enables states to
13 convert public funds into a permanent financial asset that generates
14 long-term investment income for public purposes;
15 4. It is therefore the intent of the Legislature to create a
16 trust fund that produces a significant and sustainable revenue
17 stream, preserves and protects its principal, and over time,
18 provides taxpayer relief by replacing income tax revenue through the
19 power of recurring investment and compounding interest;
20 5. The trust fund should provide a means of conserving a
21 portion of the state’s revenues, especially driven by energy
22 resources, to benefit all generations of Oklahomans;
23 6. The trust fund should work in concert with efforts to reduce
24 and eliminate income tax;
HB4072 HFLR Page 3
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 7. The trust fund’s performance goal should seek market returns
2 during the fund’s growth phase lockout period; and
3 8. After the lockout phase, the trust fund’s performance goal
4 should be to maintain safety of principal while maximizing the total
5 return.

6 SECTION 3. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
7 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 34.102A-3 of Title 62, unless
8 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
9 A. There is hereby created in the State Treasury a trust fund
10 to be known and designated as the "Taxpayer Endowment Trust Fund".
11 The members of the Invest in Oklahoma Board shall be considered
12 trustees of the fund. The fund shall be a continuing fund, not
13 subject to fiscal year limitations, and shall consist of all monies
14 directed for deposit to the fund pursuant to legal transfers,
15 apportionments, or appropriations, as well as any interest or
16 investment income derived from such deposits. All monies accruing
17 to the credit of the said fund are hereby appropriated and may be
18 utilized in accordance with the provisions of the Taxpayer Endowment
19 Trust Fund Act.
20 B. Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, all
21 interest and income derived from deposits to the Taxpayer Endowment
22 Trust Fund, shall be credited to the Trust Fund.

1 SECTION 4. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
2 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 34102A-4 of Title 62, unless
3 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
4 A. The Taxpayer Endowment Trust Fund shall be administered,
5 managed, and overseen by the Invest in Oklahoma Board, with
6 administrative assistance provided by the State Treasurer and any
7 other individual or entity designated by the Board. The Board shall
8 invest the assets of the Trust Fund with the care, skill, prudence,
9 and diligence under the circumstances then prevailing that a prudent
10 investor acting in a like capacity and familiar with such matters
11 would use in the conduct of an enterprise of a like character and
12 with like aims. Additionally, the Board shall diversify the
13 investments of the Trust Fund so as to minimize the risk of large
14 losses, unless under the circumstances it is clearly prudent not to
15 do so. The Board may take or delegate all actions reasonably
16 necessary to administer, manage, and protect the Trust Fund but only
17 in a manner that meets the standard provided in this subsection.
18 B. Notwithstanding the provisions of Section 89.2 of Title 62
19 of the Oklahoma Statutes, the Board may invest and reinvest assets
20 of the Trust Fund in any manner not prohibited by law, including but
21 not limited to real property assets, stock, and other equity
22 investments.
23 C. Subject to Board approval, the State Treasurer shall:

1 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
2 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 34.102A-1 of Title 62, unless
3 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
4 A. Sections 1 through 5 of this act shall be known and may be
5 cited as the "Taxpayer Endowment Trust Fund Act".
6 B. 1. The Legislature finds that Oklahoma’s long-term economic
7 growth and fiscal stability are best served by policies that promote
8 savings, investment, and reduced reliance on income taxation;
9 2. The Legislature further finds that states with an abundance
10 of energy resources that preserve and grow a portion of those
11 revenues through investments enjoy lower taxes and budget stability;
12 3. Establishing a sovereign wealth fund enables states to
13 convert public funds into a permanent financial asset that generates
14 long-term investment income for public purposes;
15 4. It is therefore the intent of the Legislature to create a
16 trust fund that produces a significant and sustainable revenue
17 stream, preserves and protects its principal, and over time,
18 provides taxpayer relief by replacing income tax revenue through the
19 power of recurring investment and compounding interest;
20 5. The trust fund should provide a means of conserving a
21 portion of the state’s revenues, especially driven by energy
22 resources, to benefit all generations of Oklahomans;
23 6. The trust fund should work in concert with efforts to reduce
24 and eliminate income tax;

2 during the fund’s growth phase lockout period; and
3 8. After the lockout phase, the trust fund’s performance goal

6 SECTION 3. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
7 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 34.102A-3 of Title 62, unless
8 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
9 A. There is hereby created in the State Treasury a trust fund
10 to be known and designated as the "Taxpayer Endowment Trust Fund".
11 The members of the Invest in Oklahoma Board shall be considered
12 trustees of the fund. The fund shall be a continuing fund, not
13 subject to fiscal year limitations, and shall consist of all monies
14 directed for deposit to the fund pursuant to legal transfers,

19 Trust Fund Act.
20 B. Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, all
21 interest and income derived from deposits to the Taxpayer Endowment
22 Trust Fund, shall be credited to the Trust Fund.
