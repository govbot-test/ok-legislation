9 An Act relating to school employees; amending 70 O.S.
2021, Section 6-104, as amended by Section 2, Chapter
10 57, O.S.L. 2025 (70 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section 6-104),
which relates to sick leave for teachers and support
11 personnel; requiring school districts to grant
certain amount of paid bereavement leave to certain
12 teachers and school support employees; directing
bereavement leave to be in addition to certain leave
13 due to miscarriage; prohibiting certain leave from
negating certain negotiated leave policies; amending
14 Section 7, Chapter 291, O.S.L. 2023 (70 O.S. Supp.
2025, Section 6-104.9), which relates to the Public
15 School Paid Maternity Leave Revolving Fund; allowing
fund to be used for expenses related to paid
16 bereavement leave; updating statutory language;
updating statutory references; providing an effective
17 date; and declaring an emergency.
