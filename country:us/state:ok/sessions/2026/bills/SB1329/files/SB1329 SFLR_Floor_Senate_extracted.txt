8 An Act relating to the state Medicaid program;
defining term; requiring Medicaid providers to
9 conduct certain screening; providing for
reimbursement; requiring the Oklahoma Health Care
10 Authority to seek certain federal approval; directing
promulgation of rules; providing for codification;
11 and providing an effective date.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
16 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 5029.2 of Title 63, unless there
17 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
18 A. As used in this section, “postpartum period” means the
19 period beginning when a woman gives birth and ending one (1) year
20 after the woman gives birth.
21 B. A health care provider participating in the state Medicaid
22 program who conducts a visit with a woman who is a Medicaid member
23 or her infant during the postpartum period shall conduct a mental
24

(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 heal th screening of the woman to check for signs of postpartum
2 depression.
3 C. The screening described in subsection B of this section
4 shall be reimbursable under the state Medicaid program.
5 D. 1. The Oklahoma Health Care Authority shall seek any
6 federal approval necessary to implement the provisions of this
7 section.
8 2. The Oklahoma Health Care Authority Board shall promulgate
9 rules as necessary to implement the provisions of this section.
10 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
