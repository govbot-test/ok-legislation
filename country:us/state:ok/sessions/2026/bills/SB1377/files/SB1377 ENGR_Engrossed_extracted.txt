6 An Act relating to foster care; requiring and
authorizing the Department of Human Services to
7 provide certain items to specified foster children;
authorizing promulgation of rules; providing for
8 codification; providing an effective date; and
declaring an emergency.
9
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
13 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1-9-126 of Title 10A, unless
14 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
15 A. The Department of Human Services, either directly or through
16 a child-placing agency, shall provide a duffel bag to children in
17 foster care who lack a suitcase or other adequate bag in which to
18 pack personal belongings. As funds allow, the Department may
19 include personal hygiene items or other personal supplies in the
20 duffel bags described in this subsection.
21 B. The Director of Human Services may promulgate rules as
22 necessary to implement the provisions of this section.
23 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
