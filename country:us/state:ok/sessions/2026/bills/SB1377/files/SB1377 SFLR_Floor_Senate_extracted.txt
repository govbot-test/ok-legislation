12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1-9-126 of Title 10A, unless
15 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 A. The Department of Human Services, either directly or through
17 a child-placing agency, shall provide a duffel bag to children in
18 foster care who lack a suitcase or other adequate bag in which to
19 pack personal belongings. As funds allow, the Department may
20 include personal hygiene items or other personal supplies in the
21 duffel bags described in this subsection.
22 B. The Director of Human Services may promulgate rules as
23 necessary to implement the provisions of this section.
24 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
