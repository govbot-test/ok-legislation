10 An Act relating to foster care; requiring and
authorizing the Department of Human Services to
11 provide certain items to specified foster children;
authorizing promulgation of rules; providing for
12 codification; providing an effective date; and
declaring an emergency.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1-9-126 of Title 10A, unless
18 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 A. The Department of Human Services, either directly or through
20 a child-placing agency, shall provide a duffel bag to children in
21 foster care who lack a suitcase or other adequate bag in which to
22 pack personal belongings. As funds allow, the Department may
23 include personal hygiene items or other personal supplies in the
24 duffel bags described in this subsection.
