Section 1:
1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 2nd Session of the 60th Legislature (2026)
4
5
6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to veterans; requiring one hundred
percent disability benefits to meet qualifications
8 established by federal law; providing for
codification; and providing an effective date.
9
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
13 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 500 of Title 72, unless there is
14 created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
15 Any state benefits available to one-hundred-percent disabled
16 veterans shall meet the benefit qualifications provided under 38
17 U.S.C., Section 1151.
18 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
