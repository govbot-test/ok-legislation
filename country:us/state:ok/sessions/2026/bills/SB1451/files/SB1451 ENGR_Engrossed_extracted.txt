6 An Act relating to voter registration; amending 26
O.S. 2021, Section 3-129, which relates to
7 publication of election information; modifying
information that may be published; making language
8 gender neutral; requiring the Secretary of the State
Election Board to annually issue report with certain
9 statistics; amending 26 O.S. 2021, Sections 4-112, as

5 SECTION 3. AMENDATORY 26 O.S. 2021, Section 4-120.6, is
6 amended to read as follows:
7 Section 4-120.6. The secretary of each county election board
8 shall cancel the registration of all registered voters who have
9 registered in another county in Oklahoma this state, or in another
