1 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES - FLOOR VERSION
2 STATE OF OKLAHOMA

4 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
FOR
6

8 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
9 An Act relating to perinatal health; creating the
Perinatal Quality Improvement Revolving Fund; stating
10 the purpose; providing for codification; and
providing an effective date.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
15 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1-245 of Title 63, unless there
16 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
17 There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving fund
18 for the State Department of Health to be designated the "Perinatal
19 Quality Improvement Revolving Fund". The fund shall be a continuing
20 fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations, and shall consist of
21 all monies received by the State Department of Health from state-
22 appropriated funds, federal funds, donations, grants, and
23 contributions from any public or private source and designated for
24 the purpose set forth in this section. All monies accruing to the
HB4285 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 cred it of said fund are hereby appropriated and may be budgeted and
2 expended by the State Department of Health for the purpose of
3 working collaboratively with quality improvement initiatives and
4 entities operating or founded in Oklahoma by reducing preventable
5 newborn to infant morbidity and mortality, reducing preventable
6 maternal morbidity and mortality, and improving maternal safety and
7 overall outcomes. Expenditures from said fund shall be made upon
8 warrants issued by the State Treasurer against claims filed as
9 prescribed by law with the Director of the Office of Management and
10 Enterprise Services for approval and payment.
11 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
12
13 COMMITTEE REPORT BY: COMMITTEE ON APPROPRIATIONS AND BUDGET, dated
03/09/2026 - DO PASS, As Amended.
