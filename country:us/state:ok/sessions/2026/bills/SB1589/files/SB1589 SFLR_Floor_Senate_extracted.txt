8 An Act relating to crimes and punishments; amending
21 O.S. 2021, Section 941, as amended by Section 226,
9 Chapter 486, O.S.L. 2025 (21 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section
941), which relates to gambling; defining terms;
10 modifying scope of certain offense; updating
statutory language; updating statutory reference; and
11 providing an effective date.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 21 O.S. 2021, Section 941, as
16 amended by Section 226, Chapter 486, O.S.L. 2025 (21 O.S. Supp.
17 2025, Section 941), is amended to read as follows:
18 Section 941. A. For purposes of this section:
19 1. “Online casino games” means any gambling game that allows a
20 person to access the Internet, World Wide Web, or any part thereof
21 by way of a computer, computer system, computer network, computer
22 software, computer terminal, or similar device including, but not
23 limited to, a cellular or mobile telephone, a smart or electronic
24 watch, or a tablet that, upon risking any representative of value,

1 simu lates any gambling game described in this section or any other

3 defined in Section 964 of this title, a lottery as defined in
4 subsection A of Section 1051 of this title, a bingo game or U-PIK-EM
5 bingo game as defined in paragraphs 1 and 22 of Section 402 of Title
6 3A of the Oklahoma Statutes, or any prohibited game as described in
7 subsection H of Section 262 of Title 3A of the Oklahoma Statutes;
8 and
9 2. “Representative of value” includes any and all currency used
10 as part of a dual-currency system of payment that allows a person to
11 exchange such currency for any prize, award, cash, or cash
12 equivalent, or any chance to win any prize, award, cash, or cash
13 equivalent.
14 B. Except as provided in the Oklahoma Charity Games Act or as
15 otherwise authorized to be conducted on Indian lands in compliance
16 with the Indian Gaming Regulatory Act, 25 U.S.C., Section 2701 et
17 seq., every person who opens, or causes to be opened, or who
18 conducts, whether for hire or not, or carries on either poker,
19 roulette, craps, or any banking or percentage, or any gambling game
20 played with dice, cards, or any device including, but not limited
21 to, online casino games, for money, checks, credits, or any
22 representatives of value, or who either as owner or employee,
23 geolocation provider, gaming supplier, platform provider, promoter,
24 or media affiliate, whether for hire or not, deals or provides

5 as provided for in subsections B through F E of Section 20M of this
6 title.
7 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
