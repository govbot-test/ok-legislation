I move to amend Senate Bill No. 1427 as follows:
1. On Page 1, Line 16, by deleting after the word “Every”, all language;
2. On Page 1, Line 17, by inserting after the word “provider” and before the word “for”, the
words “in this state shall offer pediatric screenings”;
3. On Page 1, Lines 17 through 20, by deleting after the word “diabetes” on Line 17 and
before the word “in” on Line 20, all language;
4. On Page 1, Line 21, by inserting after the word “Health” and before the period “.”, the
words “and subject to the informed consent of the child’s parent or legal guardian”; and
5. By amending the title to conform.
