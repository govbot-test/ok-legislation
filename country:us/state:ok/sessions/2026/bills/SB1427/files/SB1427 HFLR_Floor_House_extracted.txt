9 An Act relating to public health; authorizing health
care providers to conduct pediatric screenings for
10 type 1 diabetes subject to parental consent;
providing for certain reimbursement; directing the
11 State Department of Health to seek certain funding;
directing promulgation of rules; providing for
12 codification; and providing an effective date.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1-581 of Title 63, unless there
18 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 A. Every health care provider in this state may conduct
20 pediatric screenings for type 1 diabetes in accordance with accepted
21 medical practices and subject to the informed consent of the child’s
22 parent or legal guardian.
23 B. The screenings described in subsection A of this section
24 shall be reimbursable under the state Medicaid program, subject to
SB1427 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 rece ipt of any necessary federal approval and in accordance with
2 rules promulgated by the Oklahoma Health Care Authority Board.
3 C. The State Department of Health shall seek grants and other
4 funding opportunities to support the provision of pediatric
5 screenings for type 1 diabetes as described in subsection A of this
6 section.
7 D. The Oklahoma Health Care Authority Board shall promulgate
8 rules necessary to implement subsection B of this section.
9 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
