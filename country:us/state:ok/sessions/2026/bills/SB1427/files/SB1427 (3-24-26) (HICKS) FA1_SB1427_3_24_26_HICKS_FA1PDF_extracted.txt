I move to amend Senate Bill No. 1427 as follows:
1. On Page 1, Line 18, by deleting after the word “Every” and before the word “health”, the
word “primary”;
2. On Page 1, Line 18, by deleting after the word “state”, the words “shall offer” and
inserting the words “may conduct”;
3. On Page 1, Lines 20 and 21, by deleting after the word “practices” on Line 20 and before
the word “and” on Line 21, all language;
4. On Page 2, Line 3, by inserting after the word “approval” and before the period “.”, the
following language: “and in accordance with rules promulgated by the Oklahoma Health Care
Authority Board”;
5. On Page 2, Lines 8 through 10, by deleting after the letter “D.” on Line 8 and before the
word “The” on Line 10, all language; and
6. By amending the title to conform.
