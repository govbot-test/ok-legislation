7 An Act relating to charitable organizations; amending
3A O.S. 2021, Section 607, which relates to costs and
8 fees for permits and licenses from the Oklahoma State
Athletic Commission; exempting charity events from
9 being charged costs and fees for permits and
licenses; defining charity event; and providing an
10 effective date.

13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 3A O.S. 2021, Section 607, is
15 amended to read as follows:
16 Section 607. A. The Oklahoma State Athletic Commission shall
17 promulgate rules necessary to implement processes for issuing
18 sanctioning permits for professional combative sports events,
19 amateur mixed martial arts events, and amateur kickboxing events
20 held or given in this state and for issuing licenses for
21 participants of sanctioned contests and exhibitions, except those
22 events and persons specifically exempt by the provisions of the
23 Oklahoma State Athletic Commission Act.

13 C. An application for a sanctioning permit or a license shall
14 be on such form and require such information as shall be prescribed
15 by the Commission.

18 associated with issuing sanctioning permits and licenses. All costs
19 and fees for sanctioning an event shall be payable by the promoter
20 making application for a sanctioning permit. Costs and fees for a
21 license may be paid by the participant making application or by the
22 promoter of an event sanctioned by the Commission.

1 part icipating in a charity event. For the purposes of this
2 paragraph, "charity event" means any event hosted by a charitable

4 E. A sanctioning permit shall be in effect upon the date issued
5 by the Commission and shall expire upon the conclusion of the event,
6 unless the sanctioning permit is suspended or revoked for just cause
7 by the Commission. Licenses shall be in effect upon the date issued

10 F. Only promoters whose professional combative sports events,
11 amateur mixed martial arts events and amateur kickboxing events that

15 advertising, tickets, programs, posters, souvenirs, wearing apparel,
16 billboards, marquees and promotional signs inside and outside the

20 pictures of the event. The notice of sanction shall substantially
