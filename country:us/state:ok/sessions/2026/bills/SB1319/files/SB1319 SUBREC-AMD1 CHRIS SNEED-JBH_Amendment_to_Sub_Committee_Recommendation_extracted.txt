SB1319 SUBREC-AMD1 Chris Sneed-JBH
4/13/2026 1:01:39 pm
COMMITTEE AMENDMENT
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
State of Oklahoma
SPEAKER:
CHAIR:
I move to amend SB1319
Page 1 Section 1 Lines 16.5
Of the Engrossed Bill
by inserting a new section 1 to read as follows and by renumbering
subsequent sections:
"SECTION 1. There is hereby appropriated to the Remediation Assistance
Revolving Fund established in Section 3 of this act from any monies not
otherwise appropriated from the General Revenue Fund of the State Treasury
for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2027, the sum of Five Hundred Thousand
Dollars ($500,000.00) or so much thereof as may be necessary to perform
the duties imposed upon the Corporation Commission to provide remediation
assistance required by Section 2 of this act.";
On page 2, Section 1, line 4 by deleting the number "2" and inserting in
lieu thereof the number "3";
On Page 3, Section 1, Line 21 by deleting the following phrase "and the
Oklahoma Energy Resources Board"; and
On Page 4, Section 2, line 17 by deleting the number "1" and inserting in
lieu thereof the number "2".
AMEND TITLE TO CONFORM TO AMENDMENTS
Amendment submitted by: Chris Sneed
Adopted: _____________________________
