9 AS INTRODUCED
10 An Act relating to criminal procedure; amending 22
O.S. 2021, Section 982a, which relates to judicial
11 review of sentences; authorizing district attorneys
to adjust sentences; providing restrictions on
12 adjustments; directing the district attorney to seek
input from victims or representatives of victims;
13 providing notification requirement; authorizing
courts to consider certain postconviction factors;
14 providing credit for time served; prohibiting
sentence adjustments by district attorneys for
15 certain persons; and providing an effective date.
