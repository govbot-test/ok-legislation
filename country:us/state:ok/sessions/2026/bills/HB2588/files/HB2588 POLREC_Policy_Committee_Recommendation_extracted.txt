8 POLICY COMMITTEE RECOMMENDATION
9 An Act relating to owners associations; amending 60
O.S. 2021, Section 854, which relates to owners
10 associations membership; requiring owners association
board members to be record owners of property in the
11 real estate development that the owners association
governs; requiring owners association board members
12 to physically reside in the real estate development
that the owners association governs; and providing an
13 effective date.
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 60 O.S. 2021, Section 854, is
18 amended to read as follows:
19 Section 854. A. Membership of said owners association shall
20 consist of recorded owners of separately owned lots in the real
21 estate development. Membership is transferred upon legal transfer
22 of title to the separately owned lots. The owners association may
23 also enforce the covenant and restrictions of the real estate
24 development when specified by the covenants and restrictions.
Req. No. 16533 Page 1
1 B. Board members of an owners association, after the real
2 estate developer relinquishes control of the owners association,
3 shall consist of members of said owners association that are current
4 recorded owners of lots in the real estate development.
5 Furthermore, owners association board members shall physically
6 reside in the real estate development that is governed by the said
7 owners association.
8 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
