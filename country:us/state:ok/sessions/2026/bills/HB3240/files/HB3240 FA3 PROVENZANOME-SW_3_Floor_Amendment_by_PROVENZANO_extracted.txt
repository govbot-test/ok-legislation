FLOOR AMENDMENT
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
State of Oklahoma
SPEAKER:
CHAIR:
I move to amend HB3240
Page 6 Section 1 Lines 10 1/2
Of the Engrossed Bill
By inserting the following language: "I. 1. Each school shall solicit for
vetted and qualified staff for supervision of students for the period of
prayer required by this section. If no qualified staff can be found, the
period of prayer required by this section is waived.

Section 2:
2. If supervision during a period of prayer is provided by certified staff
and leads to an increase in class sizes for periods of instructional time,
the period of prayer required by this section is waived.

Section 3:
3. Any individual providing supervision of students mandated and occurring
outside of instructional time shall be compensated by the school district
or charter school governing board through a stipend.";
And by updating the outline to conform.
AMEND TITLE TO CONFORM TO AMENDMENTS
