14 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 70 O.S. 2021, Section 11-101.1, is
15 amended to read as follows:
16 Section 11-101.1. A. The board of education of each school
17 district shall permit those students and teachers who wish to do so
18 to participate in voluntary prayer. Nothing in this act shall be
19 construed to permit the school curriculum to be used to teach or
20 inculcate students in sectarian or religious doctrine or to be in
21 conflict with Section 11-101 of Title 70 of the Oklahoma Statutes.
