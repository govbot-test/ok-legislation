1 d. gives off unpleasant or noxious odors,
2 e. constitutes a fire or traffic hazard, or
3 f. is dead or diseased.
4 The term “weed” shall not include tended crops on land zoned for
5 agricultural use which are planted more than one hundred fifty (150)
6 feet from a parcel zoned for other than agricultural use;
7 2. “Trash” means any refuse, litter, ashes, leaves, debris,
8 paper, combustible materials, rubbish, offal, or waste, or matter of
9 any kind or form which is uncared for, discarded, or abandoned;
10 3. “Owner” means the owner of record as shown by the most
11 current tax rolls of the county treasurer; and
12 4. “Cleaning” means the removal of trash from property; and
13 5. “Lienholder of record” means a person holding a lien on a
14 property subject to municipal action as recorded on a deed or title
15 in county records.
16 E. The provisions of this section shall not apply to any
17 property zoned and used for agricultural purposes or to railroad
18 property under the jurisdiction of the Oklahoma Corporation
19 Commission. However, a municipal governing body may cause the
20 removal of weeds or trash from property zoned and used for
21 agricultural purposes pursuant to the provisions of this section but
22 only if such weeds or trash pose a hazard to traffic and are located
23 in, or within ten (10) yards of, the public right-of-way at
24 intersections.
