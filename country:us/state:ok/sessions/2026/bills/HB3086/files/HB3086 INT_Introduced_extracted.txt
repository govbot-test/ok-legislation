7 An Act relating to prisons and reformatories;
amending 57 O.S. 2021, Section 219, which relates to
8 the Prisoners Public Works Act; requiring State Board
of Corrections approval for jurisdiction over
9 prisoners; amending 57 O.S. 2021, Sections 504, 507,
508.1, 508.3, 510, as amended by Section 1, Chapter
10 202, O.S.L. 2022 and 528 (57 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section
510), which relate to the Oklahoma Corrections Act of
11 1967; requiring approval by the State Board of
Corrections when executing powers and duties as the
12 Director; requiring the Director to assist the
Oklahoma State Bureau of Investigation in certain
13 investigations; assigning investigatory duties of
certain employee conduct to the Bureau; and providing
14 an effective date.
