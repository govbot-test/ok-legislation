13 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 57 O.S. 2021, Section 504, is
14 amended to read as follows:
15 Section 504. (a) A. The Board shall elect from its members a
16 chairman chair, vice chairman vice-chair and a secretary. It The

24 Department;
