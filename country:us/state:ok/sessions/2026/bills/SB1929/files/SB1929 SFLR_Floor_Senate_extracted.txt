10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
11 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
12 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 180.14 of Title 17, unless there
13 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
14 A. For purposes of this section:
15 1. “Electric transmission line” means a high-voltage
16 transmission line with a rating greater than three hundred (300)
17 kilovolts; and
18 2. “Transmission owner” means any person, firm, corporation, or
19 entity that develops, owns, operates, controls, manages, or
20 maintains an electric transmission line within this state and is
21 licensed to do business in this state.
22 B. A transmission owner shall pay an amount of Two Dollars
23 ($2.00) per linear foot per year of a transmission line that passes
24 over a landowner’s property.
