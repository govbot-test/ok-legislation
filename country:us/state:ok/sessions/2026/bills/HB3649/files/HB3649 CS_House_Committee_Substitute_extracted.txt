8 An Act relating to real property held by the
Department of Mental Health and Substance Abuse
9 Services; amending 43A O.S. 2021, Section 2-111, as
last amended by Section 1, Chapter 377, O.S.L. 2023
10 (43A O.S. Supp. 2025, Section 2-111), which relates
to real property trust; requiring all proceeds of
11 sales to be deposited into the Department of Mental
Health and Substance Abuse Services Real Property
12 Trust; expanding permissible use of proceeds from
sale; adding descriptions of tracts of land to be
13 allowed to be sold; and providing an effective date.
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 43A O.S. 2021, Section 2-111, as
18 last amended by Section 1, Chapter 377, O.S.L. 2023 (43A O.S. Supp.
19 2025, Section 2-111), is amended to read as follows:
20 Section 2-111. A. 1. The Board of Mental Health and Substance
21 Abuse Services is hereby authorized and directed to create a trust
22 into which all real property held by or for the benefit of the
23 Department of Mental Health and Substance Abuse Services shall be
24 transferred.
Req. No. 16502 Page 1
1 2. The trust shall provide that:
2 a. the property placed into the trust may never be sold,
3 except for the tracts specifically described in
4 subparagraph c of this paragraph, if it is determined
5 by the trustees for the Department's Real Property
6 Trust and by the Department that such a sale is in the
7 best interests of the Department, and then the
8 proceeds of any sale shall remain with and be
9 deposited into the Department of Mental Health and
10 Substance Abuse Services Real Property Trust. All
11 such proceeds shall be used exclusively for tangible
12 infrastructure improvements, capital projects, or
13 other purposes that directly benefit the persons
14 served by the Department. The provisions of 61 O.S.
15 Section 327 do not apply to the sale of real property
16 under this section, otherwise the property shall be
17 held in perpetuity for the sole benefit of the
18 Department,
19 b. the property held by the trust which is not needed for
20 use by the Department may be leased or rented to
21 others and all income received from such leases or
22 rentals shall be payable to the Department for use by
23 the Department to fulfill the purposes of the

1 d. under no condition should any real property held in
2 trust be sold to fund prevention, mental health or
3 addiction treatment services.
4 B. Upon the creation of the trust authorized in subsection A of
5 this section, the Office of Management and Enterprise Services is
6 directed to provide all necessary assistance to the Department of
7 Mental Health and Substance Abuse Services to identify and transfer
8 all property held by or for the benefit of the Department of Mental
9 Health and Substance Abuse Services to the trust. Except as
10 provided in subsection C of this section, the Office of Management
11 and Enterprise Services is authorized and directed to provide all
12 requested assistance to the Department of Mental Health and
13 Substance Abuse Services in leasing property placed in such trust.
14 C. The Commissioners of the Land Office are authorized and
15 directed to provide all requested assistance to the Department of
16 Mental Health and Substance Abuse Services in leasing mineral
17 interests placed in such trust.
18 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
