9 An Act relating to real property held by the
Department of Mental Health and Substance Abuse
10 Services; amending 43A O.S. 2021, Section 2-111, as
last amended by Section 1, Chapter 377, O.S.L. 2023
11 (43A O.S. Supp. 2025, Section 2-111), which relates
to real property trust; requiring all proceeds of
12 sales to be deposited into the Department of Mental
Health and Substance Abuse Services Real Property
13 Trust; expanding permissible use of proceeds from
sale; adding descriptions of tracts of land to be
14 allowed to be sold; and providing an effective date.
