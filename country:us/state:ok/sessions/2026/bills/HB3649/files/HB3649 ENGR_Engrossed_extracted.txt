7 An Act relating to real property held by the
Department of Mental Health and Substance Abuse
8 Services; amending 43A O.S. 2021, Section 2-111, as
last amended by Section 1, Chapter 377, O.S.L. 2023
9 (43A O.S. Supp. 2025, Section 2-111), which relates
to real property trust; requiring all proceeds of
10 sales to be deposited into the Department of Mental
Health and Substance Abuse Services Real Property
11 Trust; expanding permissible use of proceeds from
sale; adding descriptions of tracts of land to be
12 allowed to be sold; and providing an effective date.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 43A O.S. 2021, Section 2-111, as
17 last amended by Section 1, Chapter 377, O.S.L. 2023 (43A O.S. Supp.
18 2025, Section 2-111), is amended to read as follows:
19 Section 2-111. A. 1. The Board of Mental Health and Substance
20 Abuse Services is hereby authorized and directed to create a trust
21 into which all real property held by or for the benefit of the
22 Department of Mental Health and Substance Abuse Services shall be
23 transferred.
24 2. The trust shall provide that:
ENGR. H. B. NO. 3649 Page 1
1 a. the property placed into the trust may never be sold,
2 except for the tracts specifically described in
3 subparagraph c of this paragraph, if it is determined
4 by the trustees for the Department's Real Property
5 Trust and by the Department that such a sale is in the
6 best interests of the Department, and then the
7 proceeds of any sale shall remain with and be
8 deposited into the Department of Mental Health and
9 Substance Abuse Services Real Property Trust. All
10 such proceeds shall be used exclusively for tangible
11 infrastructure improvements, capital projects, or
12 other purposes that directly benefit the persons
13 served by the Department. The provisions of 61 O.S.
14 Section 327 do not apply to the sale of real property
15 under this section, otherwise the property shall be
16 held in perpetuity for the sole benefit of the
17 Department,
18 b. the property held by the trust which is not needed for
19 use by the Department may be leased or rented to
20 others and all income received from such leases or
21 rentals shall be payable to the Department for use by
22 the Department to fulfill the purposes of the
23 Department, except as provided in subparagraph d of
24 this section,

2 set forth in subparagraph a of this paragraph:

1 B. Upon the creation of the trust authorized in subsection A of
2 this section, the Office of Management and Enterprise Services is
3 directed to provide all necessary assistance to the Department of
4 Mental Health and Substance Abuse Services to identify and transfer
5 all property held by or for the benefit of the Department of Mental
6 Health and Substance Abuse Services to the trust. Except as
7 provided in subsection C of this section, the Office of Management
8 and Enterprise Services is authorized and directed to provide all
9 requested assistance to the Department of Mental Health and
10 Substance Abuse Services in leasing property placed in such trust.
11 C. The Commissioners of the Land Office are authorized and
12 directed to provide all requested assistance to the Department of
13 Mental Health and Substance Abuse Services in leasing mineral
14 interests placed in such trust.
15 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
16 Passed the House of Representatives the 10th day of March, 2026.
