10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
11 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
12 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 85.59a of Title 74, unless there
13 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
14 A. There is hereby created the Political Subdivision Liability
15 Insurance Guaranty Program for the purpose of insuring against
16 excessive liability judgments against political subdivisions.
17 B. The program shall be administered, supervised, and protected
18 by the Political Subdivision Insurance Fund Board. The Board shall
19 consist of seven (7) members to be appointed as follows:
20 1. The Governor shall appoint three members, one of whom shall
21 be a member of a governing body of a political subdivision;
22 2. The President Pro Tempore of the Senate shall appoint two
23 members, one of whom shall be a member of a governing body of a
24 political subdivision; and

7 term of office for the appointees shall be as follows:

12 2. The term of office for three positions, one each appointed
13 by the Governor, President Pro Tempore of the Senate, and Speaker of
14 the House of Representatives, shall expire on November 1, 2028; and
15 3. Thereafter, successor members shall be appointed for a
16 three-year term. Members may serve successive terms. Any person
17 appointed to fill a vacancy shall be appointed for the unexpired
18 portion of the term in the same manner as the original appointment.
19 D. Meetings of the Board shall be held at least quarterly, with
20 the first meeting to be held no later than March 31, 2027. The
21 chair and vice chair of the Board shall be elected by the Board from
22 among its members in the first meeting. The presence of a majority
23 of the members constitutes a quorum. No action shall be taken by

11 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
12 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 85.59b of Title 74, unless there
13 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
14 A. There is hereby created the Political Subdivision Liability
15 Insurance Guaranty Fund, which shall be for the purpose of insuring
16 participating political subdivisions against excessive liability
17 judgments as provided by the Political Subdivision Insurance Fund
18 Board. Monies in the fund, including interest, are not subject to
19 appropriation and shall be expended to insure political subdivisions
20 pursuant to the Political Subdivision Liability Insurance Guaranty
21 Program, operating costs of the Board, attorney fees, and other
22 costs reasonably incurred by the Board in the performance of its
23 duties.
