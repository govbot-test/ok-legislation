9 THE GIST OF THE PROPOSITION IS AS FOLLOWS:
10 This measure proposes an amendment to the Oklahoma Constitution
11 by repealing Sections 1, 2, 3, and 4 of Article 25-A, and it
12 also proposes the enactment of substantially the same language
13 from those constitutional sections as statutes. The repeal of
14 the constitutional sections and the enactment of statutes to
15 replace those constitutional provisions would not occur unless
16 the federal government reduces the amount of money it pays to
17 states in order to operate the Medicaid program. It also
18 proposes an amendment to Section 2 of Article 25-A which relates
19 to Medicaid. This change in the Constitution would become
20 effective if this measure is approved by the voters. The change
21 is related to the amount of money the federal government
22 provides for the Medicaid program. If the federal government
23 reduced the amount of money that it pays to states for the
24 Medicaid program to less than ninety percent (90%), then
