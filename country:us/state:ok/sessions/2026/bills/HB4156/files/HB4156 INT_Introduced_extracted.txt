6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to teachers; amending 70 O.S. 2021,
Section 6-101.6, which relates to categories of
8 employment contracts; creating a category for job
description and duties; requiring a specific and
9 clear description of duties; including contract
hours; requiring a lunch period provision;
10 prohibiting certain provisions in contracts for
employment; prohibiting vague, unrestricted language
11 in job description and duties; prohibiting required
additional duties outside of contract hours;
12 permitting contracts to provide for agreed upon
additional duties outside of contract hours;
13 requiring additional duties be compensated at a clear
and reasonable rate; providing for codification;
14 providing an effective date; and declaring an
emergency.

7 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
8 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 6-101.6c of Title 70, unless
9 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
10 A. Beginning with the 2026-2027 school year, contracts for
11 employment of, or related employee information worksheets for, a
12 teacher shall not:
13 1. Include vague, unrestricted language describing the job and
14 duties of an employee including, but not limited to, the phrase "all
15 other duties as assigned"; or
16 2. Require uncompensated duties outside of contractual hours.
17 B. Contracts for employment of, or related employee information
18 worksheets for, a teacher may include agreed upon specified
19 additional duties outside of contracted hours if duties are
20 compensated at a clear and reasonable hourly rate or stipend.
