An Act relating to Service Oklahoma; defining terms;
exempting certain records from notarization
requirement; specifying that certain other
transactions shall not apply; providing for
codification; and providing an effective date.
SUBJECT: Service Oklahoma
BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3-107.1 of Title 47, unless
there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
A. As used in this section:

Section 3:
1. "Electronic format" shall have the same meaning as in

Section 4:
Section 1105A of Title 47 of the Oklahoma Statutes and in a manner
approved by Service Oklahoma; and

Section 5:
2. "Eligible entity" means a firm or person who is or is
authorized to act on behalf of an automobile dealer, used motor
vehicle auction, rental car agency, salvage pool, lender or
lienholder, or any other entity or class authorized by Service
Oklahoma.
B. Any records submitted in an electronic format to Service
Oklahoma by an eligible entity shall not require notarization and
shall be exempt from any notarization requirements in this title.
This subsection shall also apply to any records being submitted to
Service Oklahoma to create an electronic format document, including,
but not limited to, vehicle titling or registration.
C. The provisions of this section shall not apply to
transactions that do not include an eligible entity.

Oklahoma.

Oklahoma by an eligible entity shall not require notarization and
shall be exempt from any notarization requirements in this title.

transactions that do not include an eligible entity.
