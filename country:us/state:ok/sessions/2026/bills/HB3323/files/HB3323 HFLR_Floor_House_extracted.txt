10 An Act relating to Service Oklahoma; defining terms;

15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3-107.1 of Title 47, unless
18 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 A. As used in this section:
20 1. "Electronic format" shall have the same meaning as in
21 Section 1105A of Title 47 of the Oklahoma Statutes and in a manner
22 approved by Service Oklahoma; and
23 2. "Eligible entity" means a firm or person who is or is
24 authorized to act on behalf of an automobile dealer, used motor

4 B. Any records submitted in an electronic format to Service

6 shall be exempt from any notarization requirements in this title.
7 This subsection shall also apply to any records being submitted to

10 C. The provisions of this section shall not apply to
11 transactions that do not include an eligible entity.
