7 An Act relating to Service Oklahoma; defining terms;
exempting certain records from notarization
8 requirement; specifying that certain other
transactions shall not apply; providing for
9 codification; and providing an effective date.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3-107.1 of Title 47, unless
15 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 A. As used in this section:
17 1. "Electronic format" shall have the same meaning as in
18 Section 1105A of Title 47 of the Oklahoma Statutes and in a manner
19 approved by Service Oklahoma; and
20 2. "Eligible entity" means a firm or person who is or is
21 authorized to act on behalf of an automobile dealer, used motor
22 vehicle auction, rental car agency, salvage pool, lender or
23 lienholder, or any other entity or class authorized by Service
24 Oklahoma.
ENGR. H. B. NO. 3323 Page 1
1 B. Any records submitted in an electronic format to Service
2 Oklahoma by an eligible entity shall not require notarization and
3 shall be exempt from any notarization requirements in this title.
4 This subsection shall also apply to any records being submitted to
5 Service Oklahoma to create an electronic format document, including,
6 but not limited to, vehicle titling or registration.
7 C. The provisions of this section shall not apply to
8 transactions that do not include an eligible entity.
9 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
10 Passed the House of Representatives the 10th day of March, 2026.
