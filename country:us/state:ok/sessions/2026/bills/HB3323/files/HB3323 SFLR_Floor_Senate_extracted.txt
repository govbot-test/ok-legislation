8 An Act relating to Service Oklahoma; defining terms;
exempting certain records from notarization
9 requirement; specifying that certain other
transactions shall not apply; providing for
10 codification; and providing an effective date.

13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
15 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3-107.1 of Title 47, unless
16 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
17 A. As used in this section:
18 1. "Electronic format" shall have the same meaning as in
19 Section 1105A of Title 47 of the Oklahoma Statutes and in a manner
20 approved by Service Oklahoma; and
21 2. "Eligible entity" means a firm or person who is or is
22 authorized to act on behalf of an automobile dealer, used motor
23 vehicle auction, rental car agency, salvage pool, lender or
24

1 lien holder, or any other entity or class authorized by Service
2 Oklahoma.
3 B. Any records submitted in an electronic format to Service
4 Oklahoma by an eligible entity shall not require notarization and
5 shall be exempt from any notarization requirements in this title.
6 This subsection shall also apply to any records being submitted to
7 Service Oklahoma to create an electronic format document, including,
8 but not limited to, vehicle titling or registration.
9 C. The provisions of this section shall not apply to
10 transactions that do not include an eligible entity.
11 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
