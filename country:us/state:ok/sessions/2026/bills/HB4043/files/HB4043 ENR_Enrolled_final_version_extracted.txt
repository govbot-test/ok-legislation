Section 1:
ENROLLED HOUSE
BILL NO. 4043 By: Caldwell (Trey) and Kane of
the House
and
Hall, Haste, and Hicks of
the Senate
An Act relating to the Oklahoma Department of
Emergency Management; appropriating certain funds in
certain amount for certain purpose; directing
utilization of certain amounts for certain purposes;
providing for noncodification; providing an effective
date; and declaring an emergency.
SUBJECT: Oklahoma Department of Emergency Management
BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
There is hereby appropriated to the Oklahoma Department of
Emergency Management from any monies not otherwise appropriated from
the General Revenue Fund of the State Treasury for the fiscal year
ending June 30, 2027, the sum of One Million Dollars ($1,000,000.00)
or so much thereof as may be necessary to perform the duties imposed
upon the Oklahoma Department of Emergency Management by law.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
Funds appropriated by Section 1 of this act shall be utilized as
follows:
One Million Dollars ($1,000,000.00) to be transferred to the
Oklahoma Task Force 1 Revolving Fund created by Enrolled House Bill
No. 3831 of the 2nd Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature.

Section 4:
SECTION 3. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.

Section 5:
SECTION 4. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
be in full force from and after its passage and approval.

An Act
ENROLLED HOUSE
BILL NO. 4043 By: Caldwell (Trey) and Kane of
the House
and
Hall, Haste, and Hicks of
the Senate
An Act relating to the Oklahoma Department of
Emergency Management; appropriating certain funds in
certain amount for certain purpose; directing
utilization of certain amounts for certain purposes;
providing for noncodification; providing an effective
date; and declaring an emergency.
SUBJECT: Oklahoma Department of Emergency Management
BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
There is hereby appropriated to the Oklahoma Department of
Emergency Management from any monies not otherwise appropriated from
the General Revenue Fund of the State Treasury for the fiscal year
ending June 30, 2027, the sum of One Million Dollars ($1,000,000.00)
or so much thereof as may be necessary to perform the duties imposed
upon the Oklahoma Department of Emergency Management by law.
SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
Funds appropriated by Section 1 of this act shall be utilized as
follows:

One Million Dollars ($1,000,000.00) to be transferred to the
Oklahoma Task Force 1 Revolving Fund created by Enrolled House Bill
No. 3831 of the 2nd Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature.
SECTION 3. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
SECTION 4. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
