I move to amend Senate Bill No. 1290 as follows:
1. On Page 1, Line 15, by inserting before the word “There”, the letter “A.”;
2. On Page 2, Line 3 1/2, by inserting the following new subsection B:
“B. No monies from the 2-1-1 Hotline Revolving Fund shall be used to provide referrals,
information, or assistance to any organization, entity, or service that performs, promotes, or
facilitates abortions. Instead, the 2-1-1 system shall prioritize referrals to crisis pregnancy centers,
adoption agencies, and other life-affirming resources that support expectant mothers and families in
choosing life. The Department of Human Services shall ensure compliance with this subsection
through annual audits and reporting to the Legislature.”; and
3. By amending the title to conform.
