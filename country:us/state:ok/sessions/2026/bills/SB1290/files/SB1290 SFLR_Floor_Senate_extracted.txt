11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
13 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3022 of Title 56, unless there
14 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
15 There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving fund
16 for the Department of Human Services to be designated the “2-1-1
17 Hotline Revolving Fund”. The fund shall be a continuing fund, not
18 subject to fiscal year limitations, and shall consist of all monies
19 received by the Department from legislative appropriations provided
20 for the purpose of maintaining a robust 2-1-1 system in this state.
21 All monies accruing to the credit of the fund are hereby
22 appropriated and may be budgeted and expended by the Department for
23 the purpose provided for in this section. Expenditures from the
24 fund shall be made upon warrants issued by the State Treasurer

8 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
