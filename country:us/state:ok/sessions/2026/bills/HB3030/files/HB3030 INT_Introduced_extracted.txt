6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to officers; prohibiting public
officials from being required or allowed to sign
8 nondisclosure agreements related to appointed or
elected office; providing for inapplicability of
9 restriction based upon matters unrelated to
performance of duties of public office; providing for
10 codification; and providing an effective date.

15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 401 of Title 51, unless there is
18 created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 A. No elected or appointed public official shall be required
20 or allowed to sign any type of nondisclosure agreement with respect
21 to a matter involving the performance of duties by the public
22 official.
23 B. The provisions of this section shall not be applicable to a
24 nondisclosure agreement that a person is requested or required to

5 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
