16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
18 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3419.1 of Title 70, unless there
19 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
20 A. As used in this section, the term "financial institution"
21 means banks, savings banks, savings and loan associations, and
22 credit unions in this state whose deposits are insured by the
23 Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation, the National Credit Union
24 Administration or any successor institutions. Such financial

1 inst itution shall be in good standing, conduct regular banking
2 business, and shall collect such drafts, bills of exchange, and
3 checks as may be deposited by the local entity or division in the
4 regular course of business. Such financial institution shall pay
5 all checks and drafts legally authorized and duly drawn on the funds
6 deposited.
7 B. The Oklahoma Agricultural Extension Division shall not,
8 directly or as a condition of providing Oklahoma Agricultural
9 Extension Division services or providing access to Oklahoma
10 Agricultural Experiment Station programming, require funding
11 generated at the local county level, as described by subsection C of
12 this section, to be:
13 1. Centralized at the Division or Station; or
14 2. Directed or expended outside the county in which the funding
15 was generated.
16 C. Each local entity or division of the county extension
17 service system, including any unincorporated association, nonprofit
18 entity or other legal entity assisting with fundraising activities
19 for the benefit of a local entity or division of the county
20 extension service system which receives an allocation of state funds
21 through the Oklahoma Agricultural Extension Division is authorized
22 to select a financial institution within the State of Oklahoma and
23 establish one or more accounts at the same for funds generated,
24 raised, or collected at the local county level to pay expenses

1 rela ted to the services delivered through the county extension
2 service system.
3 D. No state appropriated or federal funds shall be deposited in
4 any account owned or controlled by a local entity or division of the
5 county extension service system.
6 E. Accounts established under subsection C of this section are
7 authorized to retain interest, and the interest income shall not be
8 used for any purpose inconsistent with the terms of the account as
9 provided in the agreement with the applicable financial institution.
10 SECTION 2. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
11 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
12 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
13 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
