17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
18 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
19 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3419.1 of Title 70, unless there
20 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
21 A. As used in this section, the term “financial institution”
22 includes banks, savings banks, savings and loan associations, and
23 credit unions in this state whose deposits are insured by the
24 Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation, the National Credit Union
Req. No. 3946 Page 1
1 Admi nistration, or any successor institutions. Such financial
2 institution shall be in good standing, conduct regular banking
3 business, and collect such drafts, bills of exchange, and checks as
4 may be deposited by the county or district office in the regular
5 course of business. Such financial institution shall pay all checks
6 and drafts legally authorized and duly drawn on the funds deposited.
7 B. The Oklahoma Cooperative Extension Service shall not,
8 directly or as a condition of providing Oklahoma Cooperative
9 Extension Service services or providing access to OSU Ag Research
10 programming, require funding generated at the local county level, as
11 described by subsection C of this section, to be:
12 1. Deposited in any account, whether public or private, owned
13 by, controlled by, or established for the benefit of Oklahoma State
14 University or the Oklahoma Cooperative Extension Service;
15 2. Transferred to Oklahoma State University or the Oklahoma
16 Cooperative Extension Service; or
17 3. Directed or expended outside the county in which the funding
18 was generated.
19 C. Each county or district office of the county extension
20 service system, including any unincorporated association, nonprofit
21 entity, or other legal entity assisting with fundraising activities
22 for the benefit of a county or district office of the county
23 extension service system which receives services, training, support,
24 or materials associated with such services, training, or support
Req. No. 3946 Page 2
1 thro ugh the Oklahoma Cooperative Extension Service, is authorized to
2 select a financial institution within this state and establish one
3 or more accounts at the same for funds generated, raised, or
4 collected at the local county level to pay expenses related to the
5 services delivered through the county extension service system.
6 D. State appropriated or federal funds shall be deposited in
7 accounts owned or controlled by a county or district office of the
8 county extension service system and shall be kept separate from all
9 other accounts within each county or district office of the county
10 extension service.
11 E. Accounts established under subsection C of this section are
12 authorized to retain interest, and the interest income shall not be
13 used for any purpose inconsistent with the terms of the account as
14 provided in the agreement with the applicable financial institution.
15 SECTION 2. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
16 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
17 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
18 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
