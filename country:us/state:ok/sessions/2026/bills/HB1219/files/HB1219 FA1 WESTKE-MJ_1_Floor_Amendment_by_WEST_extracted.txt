9 An Act relating to state government; defining term;
prohibiting use of state funds or resources for
10 designated purposes; prohibiting display of certain
flags on state property; providing for codification;
11 providing an effective date; and declaring an
emergency.
12
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 13001 of Title 74, unless there
18 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 A. As used in this section, "agency" means any agency, board,
20 commission, department, or other state government entity created
21 pursuant to the provisions of the Oklahoma Constitution or the
22 Oklahoma Statutes.
23 B. No funds or resources may be used by any agency to develop,
24 organize, administer, engage in, promote, or endorse any activity,
Req. No. 16390 Page 1
1 incl uding any event, initiative, official communication, social
2 media post, educational program, or public campaign, that aims to
3 promote or recognize Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transgender, Queer, and
4 Intersex Pride Month or any event with a similar theme.
5 C. No flag that represents sexual orientation or gender
6 identity may be displayed by an agency on state property or grounds.
7 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
8 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
9 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
10 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
11 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
