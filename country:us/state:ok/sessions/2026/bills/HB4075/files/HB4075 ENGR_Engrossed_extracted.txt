9 SECTION 3. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
10 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
11 A. There is hereby created in the State Treasury a Statewide
12 Recovery Special Account for the Oklahoma Water Resources Board for
13 each appropriation section of this act. The duration of such
14 accounts shall continue for the period of time that monies related
15 to the American Rescue Plan Act of 2021 are being budgeted,
16 expended, or managed in the state. The ending of such period shall
17 be determined by the State Treasurer, and shall result in the
18 closing of such accounts as a matter of law. Such accounts shall be
19 continuing accounts as otherwise provided in this section, not
20 subject to fiscal year limitations, and shall exclusively consist of
21 monies related to the relevant appropriations made in this act and
22 as otherwise directed by law. All monies deposited to the credit of
23 such accounts are hereby appropriated and may be budgeted and
24 expended by the Oklahoma Water Resources Board in accordance with

1 the provisions of this act. Expenditures from such accounts shall
2 be made upon warrants issued by the State Treasurer against claims
3 filed as prescribed by law with the Director of the Office of
4 Management and Enterprise Services for approval and payment.
5 B. The Oklahoma Water Resources Board is authorized to request
6 in writing that the monies appropriated by the provisions of this
