11 An Act relating to schools; amending Section 1,

20 (70 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section 1-126), is amended to read as follows:
21 Section 1-126. A. As used in this section:
22 1. “Bell to bell” means the time between the first bell ringing
23 at the start of the school day to begin instructional time until the

3 2. “Personal electronic device” means a personal device capable

11 B. 1. For the 2025-2026 school year and each school year

13 policy prohibiting students from using cell phones and personal
14 electronic devices while on the campus of a public school district
15 from bell to bell. The policy shall include disciplinary procedures
16 for violations.
17 2. For the 2026-2027 school year and each school year
18 thereafter, each school district board of education may adopt a
19 policy prohibiting students from using cell phones and personal
20 electronic devices while on the campus of a public school district
21 from bell to bell.
22 C. The policy adopted pursuant to subsection B of this section
23 may provide for exceptions including, but not limited to:
