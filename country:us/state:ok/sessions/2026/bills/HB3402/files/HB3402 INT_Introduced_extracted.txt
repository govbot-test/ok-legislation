14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. There is hereby appropriated to the Department of
16 Environmental Quality from any monies not otherwise appropriated

18 year ending June 30, 2027, the sum of One Million Dollars

20 the duties imposed upon the Department of Environmental Quality to
21 test biosolids by law.

24 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
