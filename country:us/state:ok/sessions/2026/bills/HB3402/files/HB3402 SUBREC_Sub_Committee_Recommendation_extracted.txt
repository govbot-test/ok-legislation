1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 2nd Session of the 60th Legislature (2026)
3 SUBCOMMITTEE RECOMMENDATION
FOR
5
6
7 SUBCOMMITTEE RECOMMENDATION
8 An Act relating to Department of Environmental
Quality; creating the Biosolids Land Application
9 Research Revolving Fund; stating type of fund;
stating purpose of fund; providing for expenditures;
10 providing for codification; providing an effective
date; and declaring an emergency.
11
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
16 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 2-10-205.2 of Title 27A, unless
17 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
18 There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving fund
19 for the Department of Environmental Quality to be designated the
20 "Biosolids Land Application Research Revolving Fund". The fund
21 shall be a continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations,
22 and shall consist of all monies received by the Department of
23 Environmental Quality as provided by law. All monies accruing to
24 the credit of said fund are hereby appropriated and may be budgeted
Req. No. 16400 Page 1
1 and expended by the Department of Environmental Quality for the
2 purpose of obtaining equipment, staffing, and training for the
3 testing of biosolids. Expenditures from said fund shall be made
4 upon warrants issued by the State Treasurer against claims filed as
5 prescribed by law with the Director of the Office of Management and
6 Enterprise Services for approval and payment.
7 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
8 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
9 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
10 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
11 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
