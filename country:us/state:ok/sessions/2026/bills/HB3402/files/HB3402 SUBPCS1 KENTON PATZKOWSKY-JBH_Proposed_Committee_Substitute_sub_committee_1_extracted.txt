9 An Act relating to Department of Environmental
Quality; creating the Biosolids Land Application
10 Research Revolving Fund; stating type of fund;
stating purpose of fund; providing for expenditures;
11 providing for codification; providing an effective
date; and declaring an emergency.
12
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 2-10-205.2 of Title 27A, unless
18 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving fund
20 for the Department of Environmental Quality to be designated the
21 "Biosolids Land Application Research Revolving Fund". The fund
22 shall be a continuing fund, not subject to fiscal year limitations,
23 and shall consist of all monies received by the Department of
24 Environmental Quality as provided by law. All monies accruing to
Req. No. 16060 Page 1
1 the credit of said fund are hereby appropriated and may be budgeted
2 and expended by the Department of Environmental Quality for the
3 purpose of obtaining equipment, staffing, and training for the
4 testing of biosolids. Expenditures from said fund shall be made
5 upon warrants issued by the State Treasurer against claims filed as
6 prescribed by law with the Director of the Office of Management and
7 Enterprise Services for approval and payment.
8 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
9 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
10 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
11 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
12 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
