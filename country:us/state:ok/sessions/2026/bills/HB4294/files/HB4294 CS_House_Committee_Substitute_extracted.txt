11 An Act relating to health insurance; directing fair
coverage for individuals with epilepsy; disallowing
12 termination of coverage for individuals diagnosed
with epilepsy; providing coverage for devices or
13 surgeries for epilepsy if certain criteria are met;
providing for codification; and providing an
14 effective date.
15
16
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
18 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
19 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 6060.31 of Title 36, unless
20 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
21 A. All individual and group health insurance policies that
22 provide medical and surgical benefits shall provide the same
23 coverage and benefits to any individual who has been diagnosed with
24
Req. No. 16894 Page 1
1 epil epsy as the policy would provide coverage and benefits to an
2 individual who has not been diagnosed with epilepsy.
3 B. No insurer shall terminate coverage or refuse to renew an
4 individual's health insurance coverage solely based upon the
5 individual's diagnosis of epilepsy.
6 C. To reduce the risk of death from sudden unexpected death in
7 epilepsy (SUDEP), all individual and group health insurance policies
8 that provide medical and surgical benefits shall adopt medical
9 policies that provide coverage for medically necessary
10 neurostimulation devices if prescribed by a licensed physician who
11 is actively treating the enrollee.
12 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
