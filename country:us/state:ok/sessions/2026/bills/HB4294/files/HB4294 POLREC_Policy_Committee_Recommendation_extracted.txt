8 POLICY COMMITTEE RECOMMENDATION
9 An Act relating to health insurance; directing fair
coverage for individuals with epilepsy; disallowing
10 termination of coverage for individuals diagnosed
with epilepsy; providing coverage for devices or
11 surgeries for epilepsy if certain criteria are met;
providing for codification; and providing an
12 effective date.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 6060.31 of Title 36, unless
18 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 A. All individual and group health insurance policies that
20 provide medical and surgical benefits shall provide the same
21 coverage and benefits to any individual who has been diagnosed with
22 epilepsy as the policy would provide coverage and benefits to an
23 individual who has not been diagnosed with epilepsy.
24
Req. No. 16537 Page 1
1 B. No insurer shall terminate coverage or refuse to renew an
2 individual's health insurance coverage solely based upon the
3 individual's diagnosis of epilepsy.
4 C. To reduce the risk of death from sudden unexpected death in
5 epilepsy (SUDEP), all individual and group health insurance policies
6 that provide medical and surgical benefits shall adopt medical
7 policies that provide coverage for medically necessary
8 neurostimulation devices if prescribed by a licensed physician who
9 is actively treating the enrollee.
10 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
