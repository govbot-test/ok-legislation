3 SENATE BILL NO. 1217 By: Daniels and Guthrie
4
5
6 An Act relating to real estate brokers; prohibiting
requirement for broker to enter into certain
7 agreement to show certain real estate; allowing
certain brokers to require certain associate to enter
8 into certain agreement prior to certain action;
providing for codification; and providing an
9 effective date.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 858-355.2 of Title 59, unless
15 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 A. A broker shall not be required to enter into a brokerage
17 agreement with a buyer prior to showing real estate for purchase,
18 lease, option, or exchange.
19 B. A managing broker or proprietor broker may choose to require
20 a branch broker, broker associate, or sales associate that is
21 employed by, working with, or an independent contractor for the
22 managing broker or proprietor broker to enter into a brokerage
23 agreement with a buyer prior to showing real estate for purchase,
24 lease, option, or exchange.
