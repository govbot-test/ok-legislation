10 An Act relating to real estate brokers; prohibiting
requirement for broker to enter into certain
11 agreement to show certain real estate; allowing
certain brokers to require certain associate to enter
12 into certain agreement prior to certain action;
providing for codification; and providing an
13 effective date.
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 858-355.2 of Title 59, unless
18 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 A. A broker shall not be required to enter into a brokerage
20 agreement with a buyer prior to showing real estate for purchase,
21 lease, option, or exchange.
22 B. A managing broker or proprietor broker may choose to require
23 a branch broker, broker associate, or sales associate that is
24 employed by, working with, or an independent contractor for the
