BILL NO. 1217 By: Daniels, Guthrie, and
2 Bullard of the Senate
3 and
4 Osburn of the House
5
6 An Act relating to real estate brokers; prohibiting
requirement for broker to enter into certain
7 agreement to show certain real estate; allowing
certain brokers to require certain associate to enter
8 into certain agreement prior to certain action;
providing for codification; and providing an
9 effective date.
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
13 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 858-355.2 of Title 59, unless
14 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
15 A. A broker shall not be required to enter into a brokerage
16 agreement with a buyer prior to showing real estate for purchase,
17 lease, option, or exchange.
18 B. A managing broker or proprietor broker may choose to require
19 a branch broker, broker associate, or sales associate that is
20 employed by, working with, or an independent contractor for the
21 managing broker or proprietor broker to enter into a brokerage
22 agreement with a buyer prior to showing real estate for purchase,
23 lease, option, or exchange.
24 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
