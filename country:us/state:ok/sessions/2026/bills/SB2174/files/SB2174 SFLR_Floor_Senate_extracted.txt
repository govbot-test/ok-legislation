7 An Act relating to the State Fire Marshal Commission;
amending 74 O.S. 2021, Section 324.1, which relates
8 to the State Fire Marshal Commission; modifying
composition of Commission members; requiring
9 appointment to serve certain terms; updating
statutory language; and providing an effective date.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 74 O.S. 2021, Section 324.1, is
14 amended to read as follows:
15 Section 324.1. There is hereby re-created the State Fire
16 Marshal Commission, which shall consist of seven (7) members
17 appointed by the Governor. The Governor shall appoint initially one
18 member who shall serve for a term of five (5) years, one member from
19 a statewide association of career and volunteer firefighters fire
20 marshals who shall serve for a term of four (4) years, one member
21 from a statewide association of municipalities who shall serve for a
22 term of three (3) years, one member from a statewide association of
23 Fire Chiefs fire chiefs, both career and volunteer, who shall serve
24 for a term of two (2) years, one member who shall be a from a
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - SB2174 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 stat ewide association of Fire Investigator fire investigators who
2 shall serve for a term of one (1) year, one member representing a
3 statewide association of electrical workers general contractors who
4 shall serve a term of one (1) year, and one member two members
5 representing selected by a statewide organization of exclusively
6 professional firefighters representing businesses who shall serve a
7 term of two (2) years. The members of the Commission shall
8 thereafter be appointed for a term of five (5) years and the
9 appointments shall be subject to Senate confirmation; provided the
10 associations named shall be represented by at least one member.
11 Upon the effective date of this act, any expiration or vacancy of
12 the term of a currently appointed member shall be filled by the
13 appointing authority specified in this section.
14 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
