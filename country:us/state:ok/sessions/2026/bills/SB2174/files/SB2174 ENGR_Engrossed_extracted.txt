6 An Act relating to the State Fire Marshal Commission;
amending 74 O.S. 2021, Section 324.1, which relates
7 to the State Fire Marshal Commission; modifying
composition of Commission members; requiring
8 appointment to serve certain terms; updating
statutory language; and providing an effective date.
9
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 74 O.S. 2021, Section 324.1, is
13 amended to read as follows:
14 Section 324.1. There is hereby re-created the State Fire
15 Marshal Commission, which shall consist of seven (7) members
16 appointed by the Governor. The Governor shall appoint initially one
17 member who shall serve for a term of five (5) years, one member from
18 a statewide association of career and volunteer firefighters fire
19 marshals who shall serve for a term of four (4) years, one member
20 from a statewide association of municipalities who shall serve for a
21 term of three (3) years, one member from a statewide association of
22 Fire Chiefs fire chiefs, both career and volunteer, who shall serve
23 for a term of two (2) years, one member who shall be a from a
24 statewide association of Fire Investigator fire investigators who
ENGR. S. B. NO. 2174 Page 1
1 shal l serve for a term of one (1) year, one member representing a
2 statewide association of electrical workers general contractors who
3 shall serve a term of one (1) year, and one member two members
4 representing selected by a statewide organization of exclusively
5 professional firefighters representing businesses who shall serve a
6 term of two (2) years. The members of the Commission shall
7 thereafter be appointed for a term of five (5) years and the
8 appointments shall be subject to Senate confirmation; provided the
9 associations named shall be represented by at least one member.
10 Upon the effective date of this act, any expiration or vacancy of
11 the term of a currently appointed member shall be filled by the
12 appointing authority specified in this section.
13 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
14 Passed the Senate the 26th day of March, 2026.
