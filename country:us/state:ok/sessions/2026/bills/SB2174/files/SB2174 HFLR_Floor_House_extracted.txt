18 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 74 O.S. 2021, Section 324.1, is
19 amended to read as follows:
20 Section 324.1. There is hereby re-created the State Fire
21 Marshal Commission, which shall consist of seven (7) members
22 appointed by the Governor. The Governor shall appoint initially one
23 member who shall serve for a term of five (5) years, one member from
24 a statewide association of career and volunteer firefighters fire
