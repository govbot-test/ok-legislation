16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 74 O.S. 2021, Section 324.1, is
18 amended to read as follows:
19 Section 324.1. There is hereby re-created the State Fire
20 Marshal Commission, which shall consist of seven (7) members
21 appointed by the Governor. The Governor shall appoint initially one
22 member who shall serve for a term of five (5) years, one member from
23 a statewide association of career and volunteer firefighters fire
24 marshals who shall serve for a term of four (4) years, one member

1 from a statewide association of municipalities who shall serve for a
2 term of three (3) years, one member from a statewide association of
3 Fire Chiefs fire chiefs, both career and volunteer, who shall serve
4 for a term of two (2) years, one member who shall be a Fire
5 Investigator from a statewide association representing fire fighters
6 who shall serve for a term of one (1) year, one member representing
7 a statewide association of electrical workers general contractors
8 who shall serve a term of one (1) year, and one member two members,
9 one of which shall be selected by a statewide association
10 representing restaurants, and the other shall be representing
11 selected by a statewide organization of exclusively professional
12 firefighters representing businesses, who shall serve a term of two
13 (2) years. The members of the Commission shall thereafter be
14 appointed for a term of five (5) years and the appointments shall be
15 subject to Senate confirmation; provided the associations named
16 shall be represented by at least one member. Upon the effective
17 date of this act, any expiration or vacancy of the term of a
18 currently appointed member shall be filled by the appointing
19 authority specified in this section.
