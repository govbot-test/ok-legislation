9 [ revenue and taxation - Oklahoma Quality Events Act
10 – definition - effective date ]
11
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 68 O.S. 2021, Section 4303, is
16 amended to read as follows:
17 Section 4303. As used in the Oklahoma Quality Events Incentive
18 Act:
19 1. "Certified sponsor" means an entity or organization
20 authorized to promote and conduct a quality event, which is
21 incurring expenses for the promotion of such event to be conducted
22 within the corporate limits of an eligible municipality or an
23 unincorporated area within a county;
24 2. "Economic impact study" means a study which includes:
Req. No. 17032 Page 1
1 a. a description and, if applicable, history of the
2 quality event,
3 b. information regarding the site selection process for
4 the quality event,
5 c. an estimate of the expenses anticipated to be incurred
6 in connection with hosting the quality event,
7 d. an estimate of the total gross sales made by vendors
8 during any period of time during which no quality
9 event activity occurs,
10 e. a detailed estimate of the anticipated increase in
11 sales tax revenue directly attributable to the quality
12 event, and
13 f. the general economic impact likely to occur as a
14 result of the preparation for, occurrence of and
15 activity occurring in connection with the dissolution
16 of, a quality event;
17 3. "Eligible local support amounts" means:
18 a. any payment made by a local government entity or
19 transfer of monies from the general fund or transfer
20 of tax revenues derived from a locally imposed tax to
21 a certified sponsor for the purpose of attracting,
22 promoting, advertising, organizing, conducting or
23 otherwise supporting a quality event, or
24
Req. No. 17032 Page 2
1 b. any direct payment made by a certified sponsor to a
2 for-profit or nonprofit entity, other than the host
3 community, for the purpose of attracting, promoting,
4 advertising, organizing, conducting or otherwise
5 supporting a quality event;
6 4. "Event history" means:
7 a. historical information on the event including past
8 locations of the event,
9 b. a description of previous attempts by the host
10 community to secure the event,
11 c. information regarding attempts by other communities to
12 recruit the event, and
13 d. if applicable, the competitive bidding process for
14 securing the event by the host community;
15 5. "Host community" means any county, incorporated city or
16 town, or any combination of counties, incorporated cities or towns
17 of the state which are authorized by their respective governing
18 bodies to host or assist in the presentation of a quality event;
19 6. "Incremental sales tax revenue" means the amount of
20 additional state sales tax revenue collected as a result of the
21 quality event, as determined by an economic impact study verified by
22 the Oklahoma Tax Commission;
23
24
Req. No. 17032 Page 3
1 7. "New event" means a quality event which did not occur within
2 a period of twenty-four (24) months prior to the month during which
3 a quality event is held;
4 8. "Quality event" means:
5 a. a new event or a meeting of a nationally recognized
6 organization or its members,
7 b. a new or existing event that is a national,
8 international or world championship, or
9 c. a new or existing event that is managed or produced by
10 an Oklahoma-based national or international
11 organization, or
12 d. a nationally televised event by a nationally
13 recognized sanctioned body;
14 9. "Recurring event" means a quality event which occurred at
15 least once within the twenty-four (24) months prior to the month
16 during which a quality event is held;
17 10. "State sales tax revenue" means the proceeds from the state
18 sales tax levy imposed pursuant to Section 1354 of this title upon
19 taxable transactions occurring as a result of the quality event, as
20 determined by an economic impact study verified by the Oklahoma Tax
21 Commission; and
22 11. "Vendors" means those persons or business entities making
23 taxable sales of tangible personal property or services as a result
24 of the quality event, as determined by an economic impact study
Req. No. 17032 Page 4
1 veri fied by the Oklahoma Tax Commission and, unless the context
2 otherwise requires, shall have the same meaning as defined by
3 Section 1352 of this title.
4 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
