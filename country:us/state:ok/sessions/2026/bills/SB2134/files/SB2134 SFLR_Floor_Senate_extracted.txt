9 An Act relating to agriculture; defining term;
providing for the State Board of Agriculture to
10 promulgate rules; requiring Oklahoma Department of
Agriculture, Food, and Forestry to provide certain
11 guidance to certain county organizations; defining
term; requiring wreckers to take certain action in
12 certain circumstance; requiring certain county
organization to provide certain assistance; providing
13 for codification; and providing an effective date.
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
18 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 2-35 of Title 2, unless there is
19 created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
20 A. As used in this section, “livestock” means cattle, elk,
21 reindeer, bison, horses, deer, sheep, goats, swine, poultry
22 including egg-producing poultry, llamas, alpacas, live fish,
23 crawfish, and other animals that are:
24
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - SB2134 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 1. Part of a foundation herd including dairy producing cattle
2 or offspring; or
3 2. Purchased as part of a normal operation and not to obtain
4 additional benefits under this section.
5 B. The State Board of Agriculture shall promulgate rules
6 regarding the handling of livestock involved in vehicular accidents.
7 C. The Oklahoma Department of Agriculture, Food, and Forestry
8 shall provide guidance and recommendations to county emergency
9 management organizations in the handling of such livestock, as
10 provided in Section 2 of this act.
11 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
12 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 955.1 of Title 47, unless there
13 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
14 A. As used in this section, “livestock” means cattle, elk,
15 reindeer, bison, horses, deer, sheep, goats, swine, poultry
16 including egg-producing poultry, llamas, alpacas, live fish,
17 crawfish, and other animals that are:
18 1. Part of a foundation herd including dairy producing cattle
19 or offspring; or
20 2. Purchased as part of a normal operation and not to obtain
21 additional benefits under this section.
22 B. Any wrecker or towing service that responds to the scene of
23 an accident involving livestock shall, upon being made aware of such
24 involvement, immediately contact the emergency management
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - SB2134 SFLR Page 2
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 orga nization of the county in which such accident occurred. The
2 emergency management organization shall assist with the handling of
3 such livestock involved in the accident, in accordance with the
4 promulgated rules and guidance of the Oklahoma Department of
5 Agriculture, Food, and Forestry as provided in Section 1 of this
6 act.
7 SECTION 3. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
