6 An Act relating to agriculture; defining term;
providing for the State Board of Agriculture to
7 promulgate rules; requiring Oklahoma Department of
Agriculture, Food, and Forestry to provide certain
8 guidance to certain county organizations; defining
term; requiring wreckers to take certain action in
9 certain circumstance; requiring certain county
organization to provide certain assistance; providing
10 for codification; and providing an effective date.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
15 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 2-35 of Title 2, unless there is
16 created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
17 A. As used in this section, “livestock” means cattle, elk,
18 reindeer, bison, horses, deer, sheep, goats, swine, poultry
19 including egg-producing poultry, llamas, alpacas, live fish,
20 crawfish, and other animals that are:
21 1. Part of a foundation herd including dairy producing cattle
22 or offspring; or
23 2. Purchased as part of a normal operation and not to obtain
24 additional benefits under this section.
ENGR. S. B. NO. 2134 Page 1
1 B. The State Board of Agriculture shall promulgate rules
2 regarding the handling of livestock involved in vehicular accidents.
3 C. The Oklahoma Department of Agriculture, Food, and Forestry
4 shall provide guidance and recommendations to county emergency
5 management organizations in the handling of such livestock, as
6 provided in Section 2 of this act.
7 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
8 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 955.1 of Title 47, unless there
9 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
10 A. As used in this section, “livestock” means cattle, elk,
11 reindeer, bison, horses, deer, sheep, goats, swine, poultry
12 including egg-producing poultry, llamas, alpacas, live fish,
13 crawfish, and other animals that are:
14 1. Part of a foundation herd including dairy producing cattle
15 or offspring; or
16 2. Purchased as part of a normal operation and not to obtain
17 additional benefits under this section.
18 B. Any wrecker or towing service that responds to the scene of
19 an accident involving livestock shall, upon being made aware of such
20 involvement, immediately contact the emergency management
21 organization of the county in which such accident occurred. The
22 emergency management organization shall assist with the handling of
23 such livestock involved in the accident, in accordance with the
24 promulgated rules and guidance of the Oklahoma Department of
ENGR. S. B. NO. 2134 Page 2
1 Agri culture, Food, and Forestry as provided in Section 1 of this
2 act.
3 SECTION 3. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
4 Passed the Senate the 26th day of March, 2026.
