I move to amend Senate Bill No. 1565, on Page 3, Line 12 ½, by adding the following new
subsection D and by amending the title to conform:
“D. The Authority shall electronically submit a final report to the Governor, the President
Pro Tempore of the Senate, and the Speaker of the House of Representatives after the conclusion of
the TMaH Model. The report shall summarize the services rendered under the TMaH Model, detail
financial information, report measurable outcomes for the populations served, and evaluate the
performance of the program.”.
