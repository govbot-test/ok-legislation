1 WHEREAS, national student reading scores have been on a decline
2 since 2019 with approximately forty percent (40%) of fourth graders
3 reading below a basic reading level based on national standards,
4 which is the largest percentage since 2002, and one-third (1/3) of
5 eighth graders having failed to meet national reading benchmarks,
6 which is the largest percentage ever; and
7 WHEREAS, states and local communities possess unique knowledge

17 the spirit of the Tenth Amendment to the United States Constitution,
18 but could ensure more efficient use of taxpayer dollars and improve
19 student outcomes through greater state and local control.
