17 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
18 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3218.7-2 of Title 70, unless
19 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
20 A. An individual who was a victim of human trafficking for
21 commercial sex or human trafficking for labor, as such terms are
22 defined in Section 748 of Title 21 of the Oklahoma Statutes, who was
23 under the age of eighteen (18) and a resident of this state and a

17 Oklahoma Statutes. Tuition waivers granted pursuant to the
18 provisions of this section shall be awarded to all eligible students
19 without limitation other than the amount of funds available and the
20 number of eligible students.
21 D. The Oklahoma State Regents for Higher Education and the
22 State Board of Career and Technology Education shall promulgate
23 rules to implement the provisions of this section including, but not

3 SECTION 2. AMENDATORY 70 O.S. 2021, Section 3953.1, as
4 amended by Section 4, Chapter 330, O.S.L. 2024 (70 O.S. Supp. 2025,
5 Section 3953.1), is amended to read as follows:
6 Section 3953.1. A. There is hereby created a trust fund to be
7 known as the “Oklahoma Higher Learning Access Trust Fund”. The
8 Oklahoma State Regents for Higher Education shall be the trustees of
9 the Trust Fund.
10 B. The State Regents shall utilize the Trust Fund to implement
11 the provisions of Sections 2601 through 2605 of this title and,
12 Sections 1 2501 through 3 2503 of this act title, and Section 1 of
13 this act.
14 C. The Trust Fund principal shall consist of monies the
15 Legislature appropriates or transfers to the Oklahoma State Regents
16 for Higher Education for the Trust Fund and any monies or assets
17 contributed to the Trust Fund from any other source, public or
18 private. All monies accruing to the credit of the fund are hereby
19 appropriated and may be budgeted and expended by the State Regents.
20 Expenditures from the fund shall be made upon warrants issued by the
21 State Treasurer against claims filed as prescribed by law with the
22 Director of the Office of Management and Enterprise Services for
23 approval and payment.
