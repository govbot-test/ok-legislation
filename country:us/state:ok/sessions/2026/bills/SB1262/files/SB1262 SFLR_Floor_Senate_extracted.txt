13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3218.7-2 of Title 70, unless
15 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 A. An individual who was a victim of human trafficking for
17 commercial sex or human trafficking for labor, as such terms are
18 defined in Section 748 of Title 21 of the Oklahoma Statutes, who was
19 under the age of eighteen (18) and a resident of this state at the
20 time of the offense shall be eligible for a waiver of:
21 1. Undergraduate resident tuition at an institution of higher
22 education within The Oklahoma State System of Higher Education; or
23 2. Tuition at a technology center school under the governance
24 of the State Board of Career and Technology Education.
