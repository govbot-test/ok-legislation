10 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
11 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 201 of Title 75A, unless there

13 A. There is hereby created in the State Treasury a revolving
14 fund to be designated the "Oklahoma AI Education Innovation
15 Revolving Fund". The fund shall be a continuing fund, not subject to
16 fiscal year limitations, and shall consist of:
17 1. All monies appropriated to the fund by the Legislature;
18 2. All monies received from gifts, grants, reimbursements, or
19 other contributions from public or private sources for purposes
20 consistent with this act;
21 3. Any interest or investment earnings attributable to monies
22 in the fund; and
23 4. Any other monies designated for deposit in the fund.

1 SECTION 5. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
2 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 204 of Title 75A, unless there
3 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
4 A. The Oklahoma AI Education Advisory Council shall meet at
5 least quarterly at the call of the chair. Additional meetings may
6 be called by the chair, or upon written request of a majority of the
7 members.
8 B. At its first meeting, the Council shall elect from among its
9 members a chair and a vice-chair who shall serve for terms of two
10 (2) years. The chair and vice-chair may be reelected.
11 C. A majority of the members of the Council shall constitute a
12 quorum for the transaction of business. All official actions of the
13 Council shall require the affirmative vote of a majority of those
14 members present and voting.
15 D. The Council may establish subcommittees as it deems
16 necessary to carry out its duties. Subcommittees may include
17 individuals who are not members of the Council, provided that such
18 individuals shall serve in a nonvoting advisory capacity.
19 E. All meetings of the Council and its subcommittees shall be
20 subject to the Oklahoma Open Meeting Act.
21 F. The State Department of Education, the Oklahoma State
22 Regents for Higher Education, the Oklahoma Department of Career and
23 Technology Education, and the Oklahoma Department of Libraries shall
24 provide staff support, information, and assistance to the Council as
