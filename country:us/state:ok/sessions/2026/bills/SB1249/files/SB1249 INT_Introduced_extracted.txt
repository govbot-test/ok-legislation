6 AS INTRODUCED
6
7 An Act relating to higher education; prohibiting
7 certain institutions of higher education from using
8 state funds to provide certain scholarship, grant,
8 aid, or discount to certain students; providing for
9 promulgation of rules; providing for codification;
9 providing an effective date; and declaring an
10 emergency.
10
11
11
12
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14
15 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3242.4 of Title 70, unless there
15
16 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16
17 A. Beginning with the 2026-2027 academic year, an institution
17
18 of higher education within The Oklahoma State System of Higher
18
19 Education shall be prohibited from using state funds to provide a
19
20 scholarship, a grant, tuition aid, or discounted tuition to foreign
20
21 national students who are enrolled in the institution.
21
22 B. The Oklahoma State Regents for Higher Education shall
22
23 promulgate rules to implement the provisions of this section.
23
24 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
24

1 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
1
2 of the public peace, health, or safety, an emergency is hereby
2
3 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
3
4 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
