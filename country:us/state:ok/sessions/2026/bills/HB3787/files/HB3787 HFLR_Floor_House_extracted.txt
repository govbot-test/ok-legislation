19 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
20 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 16-115 of Title 11, unless there
21 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
22 No person who is not a qualified elector as described in Section
23 1 of Article III of the Oklahoma Constitution may file as a
24

1 cand idate for any municipal office. Such persons shall also be
2 prohibited from appointment to any municipal office.
3 SECTION 2. AMENDATORY 26 O.S. 2021, Section 5-105, as
4 amended by Section 1, Chapter 43, O.S.L. 2022 (26 O.S. Supp. 2025,
5 Section 5-105), is amended to read as follows:
6 Section 5-105. A. To file as a candidate for nomination by a
7 political party to any state or county office, a person must have
8 been a registered voter of that party for the six-month period
9 immediately preceding the first day of the filing period prescribed
10 by law and, under oath, so state. Provided, this requirement shall
11 not apply to a candidate for the nomination of a political party
12 which attains recognition less than six (6) months preceding the
13 first day of the filing period required by law. However, the
14 candidate shall be required to have registered with the newly
15 recognized party within fifteen (15) days after such party
16 recognition.
17 B. To file as an independent candidate for any state or county
18 office, a person must have been registered to vote as an independent
19 for the six-month period immediately preceding the first day of the
20 filing period prescribed by law and, under oath, so state.
21 C. To file as a candidate under either subsection A or B of
22 this section, a candidate must be a qualified elector as described
23 in Section 1 of Article III of the Oklahoma Constitution.
24

1 SECTION 3. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
2 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 13A-112 of Title 26, unless
3 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
4 No person may be elected to, or serve on, a board of education
5 of a school district or technology center school district who is not
6 a qualified elector as described in Section 1 of Article III of the
7 Oklahoma Constitution.
8 SECTION 4. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
