8 An Act relating to municipal ordinances; amending 11
O.S. 2021, Section 14-106, which relates to
9 publication of ordinances; increasing the time limit
for publication of certain ordinances; and providing
10 an effective date.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 11 O.S. 2021, Section 14-106, is
15 amended to read as follows:
16 Section 14-106. No ordinance having any subject other than the
17 appropriation of monies shall be in force unless published or posted
18 within fifteen (15) thirty (30) days after its passage. Every
19 municipal ordinance shall be published at least once in full, except
20 as provided for in Section 14-107 of this title. When publishing
21 the ordinance, the publisher or managing officer of the newspaper
22 shall prefix to the ordinance a line in brackets stating the date of
23 publication as “Published _______”, giving the month, day, and year
24 of publication.

(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
