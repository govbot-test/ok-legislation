6 An Act relating to municipal ordinances; amending 11
O.S. 2021, Section 14-106, which relates to
7 publication of ordinances; increasing the time limit
for publication of certain ordinances; and providing
8 an effective date.
9
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 11 O.S. 2021, Section 14-106, is
13 amended to read as follows:
14 Section 14-106. No ordinance having any subject other than the
15 appropriation of monies shall be in force unless published or posted
16 within fifteen (15) thirty (30) days after its passage. Every
17 municipal ordinance shall be published at least once in full, except
18 as provided for in Section 14-107 of this title. When publishing
19 the ordinance, the publisher or managing officer of the newspaper
20 shall prefix to the ordinance a line in brackets stating the date of
21 publication as “Published _______”, giving the month, day, and year
22 of publication.
23 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
