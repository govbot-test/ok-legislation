8 POLICY COMMITTEE RECOMMENDATION
9 An Act relating to schools; amending Section 1,
Chapter 106, O.S.L. 2022 (70 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section
10 11-201), which relates to the library media program;
prohibiting community standards from including
11 sexually explicit conduct; defining term; and
providing an effective date.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY Section 1, Chapter 106, O.S.L.
16 2022 (70 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section 11-201), is amended to read as
17 follows:
18 Section 11-201. As school library media center resources are
19 finite, the library media program shall be reflective of the
20 community standards for the population the library media center
21 serves when acquiring an age-appropriate collection of print
22 materials, nonprint materials, multimedia resources, equipment, and
23 supplies adequate in quality and quantity to meet the needs of
24 students in all areas of the school library media program. For
Req. No. 16122 Page 1
1 purp oses of this section, "community standards" shall not include
2 depictions nor descriptions of sexually explicit conduct as defined
3 in Section 1024.1 of Title 21 of the Oklahoma Statutes.
4 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
