6 SECTION 3. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
7 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 49-143.8 of Title 11, unless

9 A. Except as provided in subsection B of this section, any
10 person receiving benefits from the Oklahoma Firefighters Pension and

12 benefits on or after the effective date of this section, shall

16 2. Three percent (3%) if the person has been retired for at

20 (20) years or more as of June 30, 2026.
