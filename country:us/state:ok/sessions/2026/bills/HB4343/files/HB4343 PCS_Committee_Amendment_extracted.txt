14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 28 O.S. 2021, Section 49.1, is
16 amended to read as follows:
17 Section 49.1. Any appraiser or commissioner appointed by the
18 district court in condemnation or escheat cases shall be paid Three
19 Hundred Fifty Dollars ($350.00) for services rendered and expense
20 expenses incurred an amount as determined by the district judge,
21 before whom such proceedings proceeding is pending.
22 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
