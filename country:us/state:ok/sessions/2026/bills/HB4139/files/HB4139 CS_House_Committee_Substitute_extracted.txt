1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 2nd Session of the 60th Legislature (2026)
3 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
FOR
5
6
7 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
8 An Act relating to home service contracts; enacting
the Oklahoma Home Service Act; requiring home
9 warranty companies to post fees; requiring certain
certifications; setting time frame to assess certain
10 claims; providing for codification; and providing an
effective date.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
15 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 7600 of Title 36, unless there
16 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
17 A. This act shall be known and may be cited as the "Oklahoma
18 Home Service Act".
19 B. Home warranty companies shall make visible in their
20 principal place of business and official company website, if
21 applicable, by posting in an inconspicuous location the following:
22 1. All applicable fees; and
23 2. Detailed pricing for covered modifications, including labor
24 and parts.
Req. No. 16805 Page 1
1 C. Any employed or contracted repair personnel by the home
2 warranty company shall:
3 1. Be bonded and insured;
4 2. Hold a valid state license; or
5 3. Present documented proof of required training or
6 certifications.
7 D. Any employed or contracted repair personnel by the home
8 warranty company performing work regulated by Oklahoma professional
9 and occupational licensing and registration laws shall comply with
10 all Oklahoma state licensing and registration laws, including
11 holding a current license or registration in good standing.
12 E. Home warranty companies shall provide all specific licenses,
13 training standards, or certifications required of hired contractors
14 and employees to the policyholder within twenty-four (24) hours upon
15 request.
16 F. Home warranty companies shall arrange the appropriate
17 representative to assess any covered warranty item no later than
18 forty-eight (48) hours after a claim is submitted.
19 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
