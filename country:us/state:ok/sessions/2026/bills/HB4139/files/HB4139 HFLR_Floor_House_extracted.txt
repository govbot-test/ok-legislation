6 and

8
9 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
10 An Act relating to home service contracts; enacting
the Oklahoma Home Service Act; requiring home
11 warranty companies to post fees; requiring certain
certifications; setting time frame to assess certain
12 claims; providing for codification; and providing an
effective date.
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 7600 of Title 36, unless there
18 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 A. This act shall be known and may be cited as the "Oklahoma
20 Home Service Act".
21 B. Home warranty companies shall make visible in their
22 principal place of business and official company website, if
23 applicable, by posting in an inconspicuous location the following:
24 1. All applicable fees; and
HB4139 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 2. Detailed pricing for covered modifications, including labor
2 and parts.
3 C. Any employed or contracted repair personnel by the home
4 warranty company shall:
5 1. Be bonded and insured;
6 2. Hold a valid state license; or
7 3. Present documented proof of required training or
8 certifications.
9 D. Any employed or contracted repair personnel by the home
10 warranty company performing work regulated by Oklahoma professional
11 and occupational licensing and registration laws shall comply with
12 all Oklahoma state licensing and registration laws, including
13 holding a current license or registration in good standing.
14 E. Home warranty companies shall provide all specific licenses,
15 training standards, or certifications required of hired contractors
16 and employees to the policyholder within twenty-four (24) hours upon
17 request.
18 F. Home warranty companies shall arrange the appropriate
19 representative to assess any covered warranty item no later than
20 forty-eight (48) hours after a claim is submitted.
21 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
22
23 COMMITTEE REPORT BY: COMMITTEE ON JUDICIARY AND PUBLIC SAFETY
OVERSIGHT, dated 02/26/2026 - DO PASS, As Amended and Coauthored.
