7 An Act relating to compound prescription drug

12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 36 O.S. 2021, Section 3634.3, is
14 amended to read as follows:
15 Section 3634.3. A. A health insurance plan or policy or health
16 maintenance organization providing prescription drugs as a covered
17 benefit shall provide a pharmacy or group of pharmacies with the
18 right to bid on a periodic basis, but not less than every three (3)
19 years, on any pharmacy contract to provide pharmacy services,
20 including, but not limited to, prescription drugs and compound
21 prescription drugs.
22 B. Nothing in this act shall be interpreted to preclude a
23 health insurance plan or policy or health maintenance organization
24 from establishing an open pharmacy network for the provision of
