Section 2:
Section 4-112, as amended by Section 18, Chapter 282,
12 O.S.L. 2022 (26 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section 4-112),
which relates to registration applications; requiring
13 all applications for voter registration to list a
recognized political party affiliation or an
14 Independent designation; directing for applicants to
complete registration by selecting a party
15 affiliation or Independent status; and providing an
effective date.
16
17
18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
19 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 26 O.S. 2021, Section 4-112, as
20 amended by Section 18, Chapter 282, O.S.L. 2022 (26 O.S. Supp. 2025,
21 Section 4-112), is amended to read as follows:
22 Section 4-112. A. The Secretary of the State Election Board
23 shall devise and distribute a registration application form to be
24
HB3722 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 used for registering voters. Such registration application shall
2 contain the following information:
3 1. The applicant's full name and date of birth, county and
4 place of residence, and mailing address pursuant to the provisions
5 of subsection G of this section;
6 2. A space or section to designate a political party recognized
7 by the laws of the State of Oklahoma with which the applicant
8 chooses to be affiliated;
9 3. The Oklahoma driver license or identification card number if
10 the applicant has been issued a current and valid driver license or
11 identification card by Service Oklahoma, or if the applicant does
12 not have a valid Oklahoma driver license or identification card, the
13 last four digits of the voter's Social Security number;
14 4. An oath of the eligibility of the applicant to become a
15 registered voter; and
16 5. Such other information as may be deemed necessary by the
17 Secretary to identify such applicant and to ascertain his or her
18 eligibility.
19 B. A voter registration application shall be signed by the
20 applicant in writing. The applicant shall personally subscribe his
21 or her name to or make his or her mark on the application, and no
22 agent, representative or employee of the applicant may sign or mark
23 on the applicant's behalf. The signature or mark must be the
24 original, handwritten signature, autograph or mark of the applicant.

BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 No f acsimile, reproduction, typewritten or other substitute
2 signature, autograph or mark will be valid. Notwithstanding any law
3 to the contrary, the Secretary of the State Election Board shall
4 prescribe procedures to authorize any person incapable of personally
5 making a mark to complete a voter registration application with
6 assistance of an official of any voter registration agency or
7 licensed operator specified in Sections 4-109.2 and 4-109.3 of this
8 title. Provided, for applications submitted electronically, in lieu
9 of the signature requirements set forth in this subsection, the
10 applicant shall consent to the use of his or her driver license or
11 identification card signature as provided in Section 4-109.4 of this
12 title.
13 C. Persons who do not indicate a recognized political party or
14 political organization on their registration application, or select
15 an Independent designation, shall not be designated as Independents
16 deemed to have completed their registration. Such applicant shall
17 be notified in the manner prescribed by law to complete the
18 application by selecting a party affiliation or Independent status
19 before the application can be accepted.
20 D. Any person may apply in writing to the Secretary of the
21 State Election Board for permission to print, copy or otherwise
22 prepare and distribute the registration applications designed by the
23 Secretary of the State Election Board. The Secretary may revoke any
24 such permission at any time.
HB3722 HFLR Page 3
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 E. All registration applications shall be distributed to the
2 public at no charge.
3 F. The Secretary also shall prescribe procedures to accept and
4 use the National Mail Voter Registration Form, or its successor, as
5 required by the National Voter Registration Act of 1993. Provided,
6 to be accepted as a valid voter registration application, the form
7 shall include the applicant's original, handwritten signature,
8 autograph or mark as described in subsection B of this section.
9 G. Applicants for voter registration or for change of voter
10 registration in any way shall provide a residence address and, if
11 different from the residence address, a mailing address. A
12 residence address shall include the street address of the residence,
13 including a full house number, street name or number, apartment or
14 suite number, if applicable, and zip code. If a street address is
15 not available for the residence, applicants shall provide such
16 information as the Secretary of the State Election Board deems
17 necessary for voter registration purposes. A post office box may
18 not be given as a residence address. A mailing address, which shall
19 include the city and zip ZIP code, may be the actual emergency
20 notification or 911 address on file in the local community, a rural
21 route and box number, a post office box number or a street address.
22 H. A full or partial Social Security number or driver license
23 number in a voter registration record or a voter registration
24 application shall not be considered a public record and shall be

BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 kept confidential by the State Election Board and each county
2 election board.
3 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective January 1, 2027.
