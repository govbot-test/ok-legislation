7 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
8 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 2607.2 of Title 63, unless there
9 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
10 A. No state agency, subdivision, employee, contractor, or
11 public institution shall:
12 1. Provide, allocate, or reimburse any state funds for any
13 gender transition procedures, as defined in Section 2607.1 of this
14 title;
15 2. Facilitate, refer, or encourage the provision of such
16 procedures; and
17 3. Contract with any entity that knowingly performs or promotes
18 such procedures.
19 B. Any violation of this section by a state employee shall
20 constitute grounds for immediate termination and forfeiture of
21 public employment benefits.
