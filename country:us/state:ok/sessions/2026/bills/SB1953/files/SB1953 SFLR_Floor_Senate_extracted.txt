6 An Act relating to health insurance; creating the
Employer Health Plan Transparency Act; providing
7 short title; defining terms; prohibiting certain
health plan from entering into certain contract;
8 prohibiting certain contract provisions from limiting
or denying certain information; prohibiting certain
9 contracts from containing certain provisions;
prohibiting certain contracts from prohibiting or
10 penalizing certain health plans in certain
situations; requiring certain contracts in violation
11 of certain provisions to be void; requiring certain
insurer or provider to provide certain information
12 consistent with certain Health Insurance Portability
and Accountability Act of 1996 (HIPAA) requirements;
13 requiring certain health plan to comply with certain
HIPAA requirements; construing provisions; requiring
14 certain claims to be made in accordance with certain
regulations; requiring certain information to be
15 unmodified; requiring certain notices to be in
certain formats; requiring certain disclosures by
16 certain issuers or providers; requiring itemization
of certain costs; requiring certain supports;
17 requiring submission of certain annual declaration;
requiring certain submission in certain situations;
18 allowing Insurance Commissioner to asses certain
civil penalties; allowing Commissioner to issue
19 certain orders; allowing certain action against
license in certain situations; prohibiting certain
20 issuer or provider from retaliating against certain
persons; requiring Commissioner to promulgate rules
21 and regulations; providing for noncodification;
providing for codification; and providing an
22 effective date.

6 SECTION 4. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
7 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 5412 of Title 36, unless there
8 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
9 A. Any health insurance issuer or covered service provider
10 shall provide information to regulated health plans in a manner that
11 is consistent with the Health Insurance Portability and
12 Accountability Act of 1996 (HIPAA) privacy and security rules and
13 regulations.
14 B. A regulated health plan that receives a disclosure under
15 this act from a health insurance issuer or covered service provider
16 shall comply with HIPAA privacy regulations in handling such
17 information, regardless of if HIPAA is applicable to the regulated
18 health plan’s activities.
19 C. Nothing in this act shall be construed to modify HIPAA data
20 privacy requirements related to the creation, receipt, maintenance,
21 or transmission of protected health information.
