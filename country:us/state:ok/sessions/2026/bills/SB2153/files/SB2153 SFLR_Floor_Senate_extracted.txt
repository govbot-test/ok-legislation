9 An Act relating to state government; creating the
Judea and Samaria Act; providing short title;
10 defining terms; prohibiting state agencies from using
certain term in certain materials; providing for
11 certain exception; providing for codification; and
providing an effective date.
12
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 9300 of Title 74, unless there
18 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 A. This act shall be known and may be cited as the “Judea and
20 Samaria Act”.
21 B. As used in this act:
22 1. “Judea and Samaria” means the land controlled by Israel from
23 Jordan during the Six-Day War in 1967, with the land south of
24
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - SB2153 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 Jeru salem being considered Judea and the land north of Jerusalem
2 being considered Samaria;
3 2. “Official government material” means a guidance, rule,
4 material, briefing, press release, or communication prepared by a
5 state agency; and
6 3. “State agency” means any board, commission, department,
7 authority, bureau, office, or other entity created with authority to
8 make rules or formulate orders as defined in the Administrative
9 Procedures Act.
10 C. 1. Except as provided in paragraph 2 of this subsection, no
11 state agency shall use the term “West Bank” to refer to Judea and
12 Samaria in an official government material or use state funds to
13 create an official government material that refers to Judea and
14 Samaria as West Bank.
15 2. The executive head of a state agency may waive the
16 prohibition provided for in paragraph 1 of this subsection if the
17 executive head:
18 a. determines that it is in the best interest of the
19 state to do so, and
20 b. electronically submits to the Governor, the President
21 Pro Tempore of the Senate, and the Speaker of the
22 House of Representatives a written explanation of such
23 determination no later than thirty (30) days after the
24
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - SB2153 SFLR Page 2
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 date on which the executive head of the state agency
2 makes such determination.
3 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
