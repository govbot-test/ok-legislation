11 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
12 An Act relating to Oklahoma Capitol Improvement
Authority; providing for allocation of funds from the
13 Legacy Capital Financing Fund; authorizing
utilization of certain amount for the purpose of
14 benefiting Oklahoma State University; requiring
certain recapitalization payments over certain
15 period; authorizing certain distributions; placing
limiting conditions on such authorization;
16 authorizing and limiting utilization of memoranda of
understanding; and providing for codification.
17
18
19 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
20 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
21 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 187A-15 of Title 73, unless
22 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
23 A. Subject to the limitation provided in subsection E of this
24 section, and in addition to any other authorization provided by law,
HB4054 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 the Oklahoma Capitol Improvement Authority is authorized to utilize
2 available funds from the Legacy Capital Financing Fund created by
3 Section 187B of Title 73 of the Oklahoma Statutes, in the amount of
4 Forty Million Dollars ($40,000,000.00) for the benefit of Oklahoma
5 State University to construct, refurbish, or expand facilities
6 related to the study and practice of agronomy.
7 B. LCF Recapitalization Payments shall be made related to the
8 distribution of proceeds provided in subsection A of this section,
9 in accordance with the provisions of the Legacy Capital Financing
10 Act, provided that notwithstanding the provisions of subparagraph b
11 of paragraph 2 of subsection B of Section 187B of Title 73 of the
12 Oklahoma Statutes, such LCF Recapitalization Payments shall begin no
13 later than the month of May of the fiscal year in which subsection A
14 of this section becomes law.
15 C. The Authority may distribute funds authorized pursuant to
16 subsection A of this section in one or more tranches.
17 D. The Authority may enter memoranda of understanding with
18 agencies, departments, and subdivisions of the state as needed, to
19 facilitate the provisions of this act, provided that such memoranda
20 of understanding do not constitute a legal obligation of the State
21 of Oklahoma or impede the administration of the provisions of the
22 Legacy Capital Financing Act.
23 E. The Authority shall not distribute funds authorized pursuant
24 to subsection A of this section until the latter of:
HB4054 HFLR Page 2
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 1. July 1, 2027; and
2 2. Verification by the Authority that Oklahoma State University
3 has collected Ten Million Dollars ($10,000,000.00) in funds to match
4 the authorization provided for the project described in subsection A
5 of this section.
6
7 COMMITTEE REPORT BY: COMMITTEE ON JOINT COMMITTEE ON APPROPRIATIONS
AND BUDGET, dated 04/13/2026 - DO PASS, As Amended and Coauthored.
