11 An Act relating to airports; amending 21 O.S. 2021,
Section 1792, as amended by Section 699, Chapter 486,
12 O.S.L. 2025 (21 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section 1792), which
relates to trespassing on property containing
13 critical infrastructure; expanding scope of crime to
include airports; and providing an effective date.

16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 21 O.S. 2021, Section 1792, as
18 amended by Section 699, Chapter 486, O.S.L. 2025 (21 O.S. Supp.
19 2025, Section 1792), is amended to read as follows:
20 Section 1792. A. Any person who shall willfully trespass or
21 enter property containing a critical infrastructure facility without
22 permission by the owner of the property or lawful occupant thereof
23 shall, upon conviction, be guilty of a misdemeanor punishable by a
24 fine of not less than One Thousand Dollars ($1,000.00), or by

16 both such fine and imprisonment.
17 C. If an organization is found to be a conspirator with persons
18 who are found to have committed any of the crimes described in
19 subsection A or B of this section, the conspiring organization shall
20 be punished by a fine that is ten times the amount of said fine
21 authorized by the appropriate provision of this section.
22 D. As used in this section, "critical infrastructure facility"
23 means:
