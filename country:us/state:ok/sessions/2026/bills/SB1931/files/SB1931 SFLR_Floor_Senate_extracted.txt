8 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Employment Security
Commission; amending 40 O.S. 2021, Section 4-102,
9 which relates to the composition of the Oklahoma
Employment Security Commission; increasing
10 membership; providing for the composition of the
Commission; establishing certain organizational
11 requirements of the Commission; repealing 40 O.S.
2021, Section 4-105, which relates to removal by the
12 Governor; repealing 40 O.S. 2021, Section 4-107,
which relates to quorum; and providing an effective
13 date.
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 40 O.S. 2021, Section 4-102, is
18 amended to read as follows:
19 Section 4-102. COMPOSITION. A. There is hereby created a
20 Commission to be known as the Oklahoma Employment Security
21 Commission. The Commission shall consist of five (5) eight (8)
22 members, appointed by the Governor, by and with the consent of the
23 Oklahoma State Senate, two of whom shall represent employers, two
24 shall represent employees, and one shall represent the public. The
