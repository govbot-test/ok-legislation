7 An Act relating to public finance; directing the
Office of Management and Enterprise Services to
8 create reporting function to assess whether contract
is service-driven or for staff augmentation;
9 directing OMES to amend state accounting manual;
requiring permanent file location for invoices for
10 intangible assets; amending 62 O.S. 2021, Section
34.36, as amended by Section 2, Chapter 199, O.S.L.
11 2025 (62 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section 34.36), which
relates to annual estimate of funds needed by state
12 agencies; requiring agencies to report on
contractors; providing details to be reported on;
13 directing agency to publicly post final consultant
report; providing for codification; and providing an
14 effective date.

18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
19 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
20 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 34.11.11 of Title 62, unless
21 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
22 A. The Office of Management and Enterprise Services (OMES)
23 shall create a reporting function within the state's accounting
24 software to assess whether a contract is service-driven or for staff
Req. No. 14336 Page 1

1 augm entation. As used in this act, "staff augmentation" means a
2 contract under which an individual performs substantially similar
3 duties to a state employee, works under agency supervision, or fills
4 and ongoing operational role.
5 B. OMES shall amend the state accounting manual to require that
6 any invoice for intangible assets include a file path to a permanent
7 file location where the asset will be maintained during post-review.

10 Section 34.36), is amended to read as follows:
11 Section 34.36. A. On the first day of October preceding each
12 regular session of the Legislature, each state agency, including
13 those created or established pursuant to constitutional provisions,
14 shall report to the Director of the Office of Management and
15 Enterprise Services and the Chair and Vice Chair of the Legislative
16 Oversight Committee on State Budget Performance an itemized request

20 be approved by the Director of the Office of Management and
21 Enterprise Services and the Legislative Oversight Committee on State
22 Budget Performance.
23 C. The forms shall be uniform, and shall clearly designate the
24 information to be given.
