11 An Act relating to teachers; amending 70 O.S. 2021,
Section 6-133, as amended by Section 1, Chapter 349,
12 O.S.L. 2023 (70 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section 6-133),
which relates to new and innovative pathways toward
13 teacher certification; expanding definition of
provider; clarifying definition of program; replacing
14 State Board of Education with Commission for
Educational Quality and Accountability; striking
15 pilot program language; requiring the Commission to
initiate a request for information from potential
16 providers; providing a timeline for the approval or
denial of applications; requiring the Commission to
17 provide notice for a denial and an opportunity to
remedy deficiencies; directing the Commission to
18 develop, promote, and coordinate a public awareness
program; requiring the Commission to promulgate
19 rules; amending 70 O.S. 2021, Section 6-122.3, as
last amended by Section 162, Chapter 452, O.S.L. 2024
20 (70 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section 6-122.3), which relates
to alternative placement teaching certificates;
21 adding alternative teacher preparation programs to
eligibility requirements; providing an effective
22 date; and declaring an emergency.
