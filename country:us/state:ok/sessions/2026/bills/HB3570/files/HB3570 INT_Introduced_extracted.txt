1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 2nd Session of the 60th Legislature (2026)
3 HOUSE BILL 3570 By: Wolfley
4
5
6 AS INTRODUCED
7 An Act relating to state employee compensation;
providing for salary increase for certain state
8 employees; prescribing maximum salary amount;
specifying certain employees not eligible for salary
9 increase; providing for noncodification; providing an
effective date; and declaring an emergency.
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
14 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
15 A. Effective July 1, 2026, each state employee who was in full-
16 time status as of June 30, 2026, shall receive a salary increase of
17 eight percent (8%) on salary not in excess of Eighty Thousand
18 Dollars ($80,000.00).
19 B. The provisions of this section shall not be applicable to
20 any person employed by the Oklahoma State Regents for Higher
21 Education or employed by any college or university within The
22 Oklahoma State System of Higher Education.
23 C. The provisions of this section shall not be applicable to
24 any person employed by a common school district.

2 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
