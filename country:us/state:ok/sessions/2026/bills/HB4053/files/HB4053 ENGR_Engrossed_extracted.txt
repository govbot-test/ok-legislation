7 An Act relating to Oklahoma Capitol Improvement
Authority; providing for allocation of funds from the
8 Legacy Capital Financing Fund; authorizing
utilization of certain amount for the purpose of
9 benefitting the University of Oklahoma; requiring
certain recapitalization payments over certain
10 period; authorizing certain distributions; placing
limiting conditions on such authorization;
11 authorizing and limiting utilization of memoranda of
understanding; and providing for codification.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
16 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 187A-15 of Title 73, unless
17 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
18 A. Subject to the limitation provided in subsection E of this
19 section, and in addition to any other authorization provided by law,
20 the Oklahoma Capitol Improvement Authority is authorized to utilize
21 available funds from the Legacy Capital Financing Fund created by
22 Section 187B of Title 73 of the Oklahoma Statutes, in the amount of
23 Forty Million Dollars ($40,000,000.00) for the benefit of the
24
ENGR. H. B. NO. 4053 Page 1
1 Univ ersity of Oklahoma to construct, refurbish, or expand residence
2 hall facilities and classroom building facilities.
3 B. LCF Recapitalization Payments shall be made related to the
4 distribution of proceeds provided in subsection A of this section,
5 in accordance with the provisions of the Legacy Capital Financing
6 Act, provided that notwithstanding the provisions of subparagraph b
7 of paragraph 2 of subsection B of Section 187B of Title 73 of the
8 Oklahoma Statutes, such LCF Recapitalization Payments shall begin no
9 later than the month of May of the fiscal year in which subsection A
10 of this section becomes law.
11 C. The Authority may distribute funds authorized pursuant to
12 subsection A of this section in one or more tranches.
13 D. The Authority may enter memoranda of understanding with
14 agencies, departments, and subdivisions of the state as needed, to
15 facilitate the provisions of this act, provided that such memoranda
16 of understanding do not constitute a legal obligation of the State
17 of Oklahoma or impede the administration of the provisions of the
18 Legacy Capital Financing Act.
19 E. The Authority shall not distribute funds authorized pursuant
20 to subsection A of this section until the latter of:
21 1. July 1, 2027; and
22 2. Verification by the Authority that the University of
23 Oklahoma has collected Ten Million Dollars ($10,000,000.00) in funds
24

1 to m atch the authorization provided for the project described in
2 subsection A of this section.
