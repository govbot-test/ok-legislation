1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 2nd Session of the 60th Legislature (2026)
3 SENATE
4 RESOLUTION NO. 23 By: Seifried
5
6
7 AS INTRODUCED
9 Point USA cofounder and chief executive officer
10 Charlie Kirk.
11
12
13 WHEREAS, Charlie Kirk was a man of deep faith who desired above
14 all to be known for his relationship with Jesus Christ, letting his
15 life and work reflect that commitment; and
16 WHEREAS, he had a special calling for connecting with young
17 people, devoting his time to equipping the next generation to think
18 critically, live faithfully, and lead with courage; and
19 WHEREAS, Charlie Kirk was unafraid to step onto college campuses
20 that were not traditionally welcoming places for conservatives,
21 where he respectfully debated issues, engaged opposing views, and
22 modeled civil dialogue; and
23 WHEREAS, his example of conviction, courage, and prayerful
24 leadership has left a lasting mark on those he served and inspired;
25 and
Req. No. 2270 Page 1
1 WHEREAS, as our nation approaches the 250th anniversary of the
2 signing of the Declaration of Independence, we reaffirm the truth
3 that all are endowed by their Creator with certain unalienable
4 rights, among them the right to life, liberty, and the pursuit of
5 happiness, with the first right being life; and
6 WHEREAS, in this spirit, we call on all Oklahomans to reject
7 political violence, to remember that every life is precious, and to
8 honor the dignity of every individual, regardless of background.
9 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE 2ND SESSION
10 OF THE 60TH OKLAHOMA LEGISLATURE:
11 THAT the Oklahoma State Senate honors the life and legacy of
12 Charlie Kirk, recognizing his example of faith, leadership, and
13 devotion to America’s youth, and reaffirms our shared commitment to
14 the values of life, liberty, and the pursuit of happiness.
