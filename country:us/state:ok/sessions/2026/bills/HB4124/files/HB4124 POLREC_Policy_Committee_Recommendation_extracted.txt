RECOMMENDATION
4 FOR

7 POLICY COMMITTEE RECOMMENDATION
8 An Act relating to ivermectin; defining term;
allowing ivermectin to be sold or purchased without
9 prescription; providing for immunity; providing for
codification; and providing an effective date.
10
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
15 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 355.5 of Title 59, unless there
16 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
17 A. As used in this section, "over-the-counter medication" means
18 medication that may be legally sold and purchased without
19 prescription.
20 B. Notwithstanding any law to the contrary, ivermectin suitable
21 for human use may be sold or purchased as an over-the-counter
22 medication in this state without a prescription or consultation with
23 a health care professional.
24
Req. No. 16566 Page 1
1 C. Notwithstanding any other law, a pharmacy is not criminally
2 or civilly liable for supplying ivermectin in accordance with this
3 section.
4 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
