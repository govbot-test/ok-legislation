7 An Act relating to ivermectin; defining term;
allowing ivermectin to be sold or purchased without
8 prescription; providing for immunity; providing for
codification; and providing an effective date.
9
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
14 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 355.5 of Title 59, unless there
15 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
16 A. As used in this section, "over-the-counter medication" means
17 medication that may be legally sold and purchased without
18 prescription.
19 B. Notwithstanding any law to the contrary, Food and Drug
20 Administration (FDA) approved ivermectin suitable for human use may
21 be sold or purchased as an over-the-counter medication in this state
22 without a prescription or consultation with a health care
23 professional.
24
ENGR. H. B. NO. 4124 Page 1
1 C. Notwithstanding any other law, a pharmacy is not criminally
2 or civilly liable for supplying ivermectin in accordance with this
3 section.
4 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
5 Passed the House of Representatives the 25th day of March, 2026.
