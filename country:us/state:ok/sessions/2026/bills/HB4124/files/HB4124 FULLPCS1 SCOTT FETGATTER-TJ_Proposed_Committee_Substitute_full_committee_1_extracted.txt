14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
16 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 355.5 of Title 59, unless there
17 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
18 A. As used in this section, "over-the-counter medication" means
19 medication that may be legally sold and purchased without
20 prescription.
21 B. Notwithstanding any law to the contrary, Food and Drug
22 Administration (FDA) approved ivermectin suitable for human use may
23 be sold or purchased as an over-the-counter medication in this state
24

1 with out a prescription or consultation with a health care
2 professional.
3 C. Notwithstanding any other law, a pharmacy is not criminally
4 or civilly liable for supplying ivermectin in accordance with this
5 section.
6 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
