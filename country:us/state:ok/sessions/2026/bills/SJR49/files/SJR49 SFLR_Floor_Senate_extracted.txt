9 A Joint Resolution repealing certain adopted
permanent rules of the Oklahoma Wildlife Conservation
10 Commission; and directing distribution.
11
12 WHEREAS, pursuant to Section 250.2 of Title 75 of the Oklahoma
13 Statutes, the Legislature reserves to itself the right to repeal any
14 adopted rule by joint resolution.
15 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE AND THE HOUSE OF
16 REPRESENTATIVES OF THE 2ND SESSION OF THE 60TH OKLAHOMA LEGISLATURE:
17 SECTION 1. The following adopted permanent rules promulgated by
18 the Oklahoma Wildlife Conservation Commission are hereby repealed:
19 paragraph (1) of OAC 800:30-3-2.
20 SECTION 2. The Secretary of State is directed to distribute
21 copies of this resolution to the Governor and the editor of “The
22 Oklahoma Register”.
