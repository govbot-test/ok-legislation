1 STATE OF OKLAHOMA
2 2nd Session of the 60th Legislature (2026)
3 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
FOR
5
6
7 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
8 An Act relating to game and fish; amending 29 O.S.
2021, Section 5-102, which relates to tagging of
9 wildlife; exempting tag fee for tags for certain
animals; and providing an effective date.
10
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 29 O.S. 2021, Section 5-102, is
15 amended to read as follows:
16 Section 5-102. A. No person may remove any propagated or
17 released wildlife or domesticated animals hunted for sport which are
18 killed at a commercial hunting area, including exotic wildlife,
19 without attaching a tag to each of such killed wildlife or
20 domesticated animals hunted for sport.
21 B. The tags shall be supplied by the Oklahoma Wildlife
22 Conservation Commission at a cost of no more than Ten Dollars
23 ($10.00); provided, that there shall be no fee for tags for females
24 of the Cervidae family, feral swine, and exotic wildlife not native
Req. No. 16982 Page 1
1 to N orth America, which are hunted for sport and killed at a
2 commercial hunting area. The tags shall contain such information as
3 the Commission may require and shall be numbered consecutively.
4 C. Such tag must remain attached to the individual wildlife or
5 domesticated animals hunted for sport until prepared and consumed
6 and shall not be used more than once.
7 D. Any person convicted of violating the provisions of this
8 section shall be punished by a fine of not less than One Hundred
9 Dollars ($100.00) nor more than Five Hundred Dollars ($500.00), or
10 by imprisonment in the county jail not to exceed sixty (60) days, or
11 by both such fine and imprisonment.
12 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
