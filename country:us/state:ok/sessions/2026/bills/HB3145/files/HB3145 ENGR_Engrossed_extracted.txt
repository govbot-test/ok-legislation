7 An Act relating to game and fish; amending 29 O.S.
2021, Section 5-102, which relates to tagging of
8 wildlife; exempting tag fee for tags for certain
animals; and providing an effective date.
9
10
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
13 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 29 O.S. 2021, Section 5-102, is
14 amended to read as follows:
15 Section 5-102. A. No person may remove any propagated or
16 released wildlife or domesticated animals hunted for sport which are
17 killed at a commercial hunting area, including exotic wildlife,
18 without attaching a tag to each of such killed wildlife or
19 domesticated animals hunted for sport.
20 B. The tags shall be supplied by the Oklahoma Wildlife
21 Conservation Commission at a cost of no more than Ten Dollars
22 ($10.00); provided, that there shall be no fee for tags for females
23 of the Cervidae family, feral swine, and exotic wildlife not native
24 to North America, which are hunted for sport and killed at a
ENGR. H. B. NO. 3145 Page 1
1 comm ercial hunting area. The tags shall contain such information as
2 the Commission may require and shall be numbered consecutively.
3 C. Such tag must remain attached to the individual wildlife or
4 domesticated animals hunted for sport until prepared and consumed
5 and shall not be used more than once.
6 D. Any person convicted of violating the provisions of this
7 section shall be punished by a fine of not less than One Hundred
8 Dollars ($100.00) nor more than Five Hundred Dollars ($500.00), or
9 by imprisonment in the county jail not to exceed sixty (60) days, or
10 by both such fine and imprisonment.
11 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
12 Passed the House of Representatives the 25th day of March, 2026.
