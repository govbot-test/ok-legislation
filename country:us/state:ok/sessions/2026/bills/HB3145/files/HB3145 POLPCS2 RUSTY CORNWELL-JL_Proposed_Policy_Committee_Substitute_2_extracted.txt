15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 29 O.S. 2021, Section 5-102, is
16 amended to read as follows:
17 Section 5-102. A. No person may remove any propagated or
18 released wildlife or domesticated animals hunted for sport which are
19 killed at a commercial hunting area, including exotic wildlife,
20 without attaching a tag to each of such killed wildlife or
21 domesticated animals hunted for sport.
22 B. The tags shall be supplied by the Oklahoma Wildlife
23 Conservation Commission at a cost of no more than Ten Dollars
24 ($10.00); provided, that there shall be no fee for tags for females

10 Dollars ($100.00) nor more than Five Hundred Dollars ($500.00), or
11 by imprisonment in the county jail not to exceed sixty (60) days, or
