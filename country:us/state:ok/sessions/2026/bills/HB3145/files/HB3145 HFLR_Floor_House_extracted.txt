10 COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
11 An Act relating to game and fish; amending 29 O.S.
2021, Section 5-102, which relates to tagging of
12 wildlife; exempting tag fee for tags for certain
animals; and providing an effective date.
13
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 29 O.S. 2021, Section 5-102, is
18 amended to read as follows:
19 Section 5-102. A. No person may remove any propagated or
20 released wildlife or domesticated animals hunted for sport which are
21 killed at a commercial hunting area, including exotic wildlife,
22 without attaching a tag to each of such killed wildlife or
23 domesticated animals hunted for sport.
24
HB3145 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 B. The tags shall be supplied by the Oklahoma Wildlife
2 Conservation Commission at a cost of no more than Ten Dollars
3 ($10.00); provided, that there shall be no fee for tags for females
4 of the Cervidae family, feral swine, and exotic wildlife not native
5 to North America, which are hunted for sport and killed at a
6 commercial hunting area. The tags shall contain such information as
7 the Commission may require and shall be numbered consecutively.
8 C. Such tag must remain attached to the individual wildlife or
9 domesticated animals hunted for sport until prepared and consumed
10 and shall not be used more than once.
11 D. Any person convicted of violating the provisions of this
12 section shall be punished by a fine of not less than One Hundred
13 Dollars ($100.00) nor more than Five Hundred Dollars ($500.00), or
14 by imprisonment in the county jail not to exceed sixty (60) days, or
15 by both such fine and imprisonment.
16 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
17
18 COMMITTEE REPORT BY: COMMITTEE ON ENERGY AND NATURAL RESOURCES
OVERSIGHT, dated 03/04/2026 - DO PASS, As Amended and Coauthored.
