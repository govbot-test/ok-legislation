9 An Act relating to property; defining term;
prohibiting certain entities from acquiring title to
10 single-family residential real property; prohibiting
subsidiary entities from acquiring title to single-
11 family residential real property; providing for
codification; and providing an effective date.
12
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1501 of Title 60, unless there
18 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 A. As used in this section:
20 1. "Hedge fund" means a lawfully organized business entity,
21 including but not limited to a general partnership, limited
22 partnership, limited liability partnership, corporation, limited
23 liability company or other entity that accumulates capital in excess
24 of One Hundred Million Dollars ($100,000,000.00) primarily for the
Req. No. 16277 Page 1
1 purp ose of investing such capital in equity securities of for profit
2 business enterprises, in any form, with the objective of achieving
3 high rates of return for the investors of the hedge fund;
4 2. "Hedge fund" does not mean a private or public pension fund,
5 an insurance company, banks or other federally or state-regulated
6 financial institutions, the primary business activity of which does
7 not consist of equity investing, or any other business enterprise,
8 the principal purpose of which is something other than investment of
9 accumulated or invested capital in the equity securities or
10 derivatives of equity securities of a for profit business
11 enterprise;
12 3. For purposes of this section, if a lawfully recognized
13 business entity is exempt from federal securities regulation
14 pursuant to Regulation D of the United States Securities and
15 Exchange Commission, there shall be a rebuttable presumption that
16 such entity is a hedge fund within the meaning of this section; and
17 4. The capitalization requirement of One Hundred Million
18 Dollars ($100,000,000.00) prescribed by paragraph 1 of this
19 subsection shall be measured as the median value for all assets
20 under management by the fund during the period of time used by the
21 fund to report asset values to its equity owners or to any
22 regulatory entity of the federal government or state government.
23 B. No hedge fund shall acquire title to single family
24 residential property located within the state.
Req. No. 16277 Page 2
1 C. No subsidiary of a hedge fund shall acquire title to single
2 family residential property located within the state.
3 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
