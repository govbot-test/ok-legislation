12 An Act relating to building codes; requiring

13 exits; providing for codification; and providing an

14

17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
18 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
19 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1000.32 of Title 59, unless
20 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
21 The Oklahoma Universal Building Code Commission shall develop
22 guidelines and revisions to the International Building Code that
23 permit Group R-2 occupancies to be served by a single exit, provided
24 that the building has not more than four stories above grade plane.
