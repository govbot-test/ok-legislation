9 An Act relating to building codes; requiring
development of guidelines with respect to single
10 exits; providing for codification; and providing an
effective date.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
15 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1000.32 of Title 59, unless
16 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
17 The Oklahoma Universal Building Code Commission shall develop
18 guidelines and revisions to the International Building Code that
19 permit Group R-2 occupancies to be served by a single exit, provided
20 that the building has not more than four stories above grade plane.
21 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
