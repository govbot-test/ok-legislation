16 referred to as "OAH", finds that a person is not in compliance with
17 an order of child support, the court or the OAH, as applicable,
18 shall require the person to surrender to it the driver license held
19 by the person and shall forward to Service Oklahoma an order to
20 revoke the driving privilege of the person, together with any driver
21 license surrendered to the court or OAH.
22 2. Upon receipt of an order from a court or from the OAH, that
23 a person obligated to pay child support and who owns or operates a
24 motor vehicle is not in compliance with an order for support,
