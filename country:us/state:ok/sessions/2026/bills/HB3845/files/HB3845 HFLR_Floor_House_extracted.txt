18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
19 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 43 O.S. 2021, Section 139, is
20 amended to read as follows:
21 Section 139. The Legislature finds and declares that child
22 support is a basic legal right of the state's parents and children,
23 that mothers and fathers have a legal obligation to provide
24 financial support for their children and that child support payments
