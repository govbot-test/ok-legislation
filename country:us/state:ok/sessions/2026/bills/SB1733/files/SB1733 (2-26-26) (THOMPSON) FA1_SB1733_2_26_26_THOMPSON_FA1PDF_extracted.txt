I move to amend Senate Bill No. 1733 as follows:
1. On page 3, lines 9 through 11, by restoring all stricken language from the comma on line 9
through the word “of” on line 11 and by inserting after the stricken word “Statutes” the words “this
title”;
2. On page 3, line 13, by inserting after the word “section” the following: “involving sexual activity,
sexual contact, or any other inappropriate or unlawful behavior”;
3. On page 4, line 3, by inserting after the words “private school” and before the word “and” the
following: “, or a campus police department thereof,”;
4. On page 4, line 11, by inserting the word “formal” after the word “any” and on page 4, line 16, by
inserting the word “formal” after the word “conduct”;
5. And, by amending the title to conform.
