7 An Act relating to the Pardon and Parole Board;
making an appropriation; stating purpose; providing
8 an effective date; and declaring an emergency.
9
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. There is hereby appropriated to the Pardon and
13 Parole Board from any monies not otherwise appropriated from the
14 General Revenue Fund of the State Treasury for the fiscal year
15 ending June 30, 2027, the sum of Sixty-five Thousand Three Hundred
16 Thirty-three Dollars ($65,333.00) or so much thereof as may be
17 necessary to carry out the provisions established in Enrolled Senate
18 Bill No. 1330 of the 2nd Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature.
19 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
20 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
21 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
22 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
23 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
24
