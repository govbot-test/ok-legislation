16 SECTION 1. There is hereby appropriated to the Pardon and

20 Thirty-three Dollars ($65,333.00) or so much thereof as may be
21 necessary to carry out the provisions established in Enrolled Senate
22 Bill No. 1330 of the 2nd Session of the 60th Oklahoma Legislature.
