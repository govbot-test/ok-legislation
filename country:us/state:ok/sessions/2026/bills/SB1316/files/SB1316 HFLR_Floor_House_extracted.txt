21 not enforced;

24 or superseded by other laws; and

3 J. Prior to submission of permanent rules as required by
4 subsections B through I of this section, an agency shall review its
5 enabling statutes and associated statutes relating to the agency’s
6 powers and duties. Each agency shall prepare a report to accompany
7 its submission of permanent rules. The report:
8 1. Shall include any statutory provisions that are deemed by
9 the agency to be obsolete, outdated, or unnecessary. The report
10 shall include for each identified statutory provision a concise
11 notation as to why it should be repealed or otherwise removed from
12 the Oklahoma Statutes;
13 2. Shall state whether the substantive content in the submitted
14 permanent rules is still necessary and, if it is determined to be
15 necessary, the rationale for such determination;
16 3. Shall state whether necessary permanent rule content would
17 be more appropriately integrated into the Oklahoma Statutes;
18 4. Shall include the frequency with which the substantive
19 content of the permanent rules has been updated in the prior five
20 (5) years and the anticipated frequency of updates in the future;
21 5. May include information regarding additional provisions in
22 statute that are not within the agency’s purview but have been
23 identified by the agency as being obsolete, outdated, or
24 unnecessary; and
