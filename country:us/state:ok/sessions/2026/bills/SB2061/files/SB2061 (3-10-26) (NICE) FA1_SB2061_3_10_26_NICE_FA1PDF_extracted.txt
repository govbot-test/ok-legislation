I move to amend Senate Bill No. 2061 as follows:
1. On Page 2, Line 20, by deleting after the word “of” and before the word “members”, the
word “nine (9)” and inserting the word “eleven (11)”;
2. On Page 3, Line 6, by deleting after the word “appoint”, the word “three” and inserting the
word “four”;
3. On Page 3, Line 10 ½, by inserting a new subparagraph b to read as follows and relettering
subsequent subparagraphs: “one member from Langston University Cooperative Extension
Service who shall serve an initial term of one (1) year,”;
4. On Page 3, Line 18, by deleting before the word “members”, the word “three” and inserting
the word “four”;
5. On Page 3, Line 20 ½, by inserting a new subparagraph b to read as follows and relettering
subsequent subparagraphs: “one member from Oklahoma State University Cooperative
Extension Service who shall serve an initial term of one (1) year,”;
6. On Page 6, Line 2, by inserting after the period, the words: “The annual report shall be
published on the website of the Oklahoma Department of Agriculture, Food, and Forestry
beginning one (1) year after the effective date of this act.”
