1 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES - FLOOR VERSION

5 and

9 AS INTRODUCED
10 An Act relating to mental health; amending 43A O.S.
2021, Section 3-318, which relates to rules and
11 standards for certification of behavioral health case
managers, application for certification, fee, titles,
12 and compliance; providing uniformity in rules and
standards for certification for behavioral health
13 case managers; amending 43A O.S. 2021, Section 3-326,
which relates to certification as peer recovery
14 support specialist, rules, application, fees, titles,
and penalties; providing uniformity in rules and
15 standards for certification for peer recovery support
specialist; and providing an effective date.
16
17
18
19 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
20 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 43A O.S. 2021, Section 3-318, is
21 amended to read as follows:
22 Section 3-318. A. The Board of Mental Health and Substance
23 Abuse Services shall promulgate rules and standards for
24 certification of behavioral health case managers who are:
HB4275 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 1. Employed by the state, a municipality, or county;
2 2. Employed by behavioral services providers contracting with
3 the state to provide behavioral health services;
4 3. Employed by organizations certified by the Department of
5 Mental Health and Substance Abuse Services;
6 4. Employed by a tribe or tribal facility that provides
7 behavioral health services; or
8 4. 5. Employed by an Oklahoma Department of Veterans Affairs or
9 a United States Department of Veterans Affairs facility.
10 B. Such rules and standards shall address criteria for
11 certification and renewal, including minimum education requirements,
12 examination and supervision requirements, continuing education
13 requirements, and rules of professional conduct.
14 C. Application for certification as a behavioral health case
15 manager shall be made to the Department of Mental Health and
16 Substance Abuse Services on prescribed forms. The Board, or the
17 Commissioner of Mental Health and Substance Abuse Services upon
18 delegation by the Board, may certify the behavioral health case
19 manager for a period of two (2) years subject to renewal as provided
20 in the rules promulgated by the Board.
21 D. The Board is authorized to establish an application and
22 renewal fee of no more than One Hundred Dollars ($100.00) to defray
23 the costs incurred in the certification process.
24
HB4275 HFLR Page 2
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 E. Behavioral health case managers certified by the Board or
2 the Commissioner shall only use the title "certified behavioral
3 health case manager" if employed by the state, a municipality, or
4 county, employed by behavioral services providers contracting with
5 the state to provide behavioral health services, employed by
6 organizations certified by the Department of Mental Health and
7 Substance Abuse Services, employed by a tribe or tribal facility
8 that provides behavioral health services or employed by an Oklahoma
9 Department of Veterans Affairs or a United States Department of
10 Veterans Affairs facility. This section shall not be construed to
11 permit the certified behavioral health case manager to practice any
12 of the following professions or use the following titles unless also
13 licensed or accredited by the appropriate authority: physician,
14 psychologist, clinical social worker, professional counselor,
15 marital and family therapist, behavioral practitioner, or alcohol
16 and drug counselor.
17 F. Failure to comply with rules and standards promulgated by
18 the Board shall be grounds for revocation, suspension or nonrenewal
19 of certification.
20 G. No behavioral health case manager shall operate or continue
21 to operate as a behavioral health case manager unless the case
22 manager complies with the rules promulgated by the Board and is
23 certified as required by this section.
24
