9 An Act relating to mental health; amending 43A O.S.
2021, Section 3-318, which relates to rules and
10 standards for certification of behavioral health case
managers, application for certification, fee, titles,
11 and compliance; providing uniformity in rules and
standards for certification for behavioral health
12 case managers; amending 43A O.S. 2021, Section 3-326,
which relates to certification as peer recovery
13 support specialists, rules, application, fees,
titles, and penalties; providing uniformity in rules
14 and standards for certification for peer recovery
support specialists; and providing an effective date.
15
16
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
18 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 43A O.S. 2021, Section 3-318, is
19 amended to read as follows:
20 Section 3-318. A. The Board of Mental Health and Substance
21 Abuse Services shall promulgate rules and standards for
22 certification of behavioral health case managers who are:
23 1. Employed by the state, a municipality, or county;
24
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - HB4275 SFLR Page 1
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 2. Employed by behavioral services providers contracting with
2 the state to provide behavioral health services;
3 3. Employed by organizations certified by the Department of
4 Mental Health and Substance Abuse Services;
5 4. Employed by a tribe or tribal facility that provides
6 behavioral health services; or
7 4. 5. Employed by an Oklahoma Department of Veterans Affairs or
8 a United States Department of Veterans Affairs facility.
9 B. Such rules and standards shall address criteria for
10 certification and renewal, including minimum education requirements,
11 examination and supervision requirements, continuing education
12 requirements, and rules of professional conduct.
13 C. Application for certification as a behavioral health case
14 manager shall be made to the Department of Mental Health and
15 Substance Abuse Services on prescribed forms. The Board, or the
16 Commissioner of Mental Health and Substance Abuse Services upon
17 delegation by the Board, may certify the behavioral health case
18 manager for a period of two (2) years subject to renewal as provided
19 in the rules promulgated by the Board.
20 D. The Board is authorized to establish an application and
21 renewal fee of no more than One Hundred Dollars ($100.00) to defray
22 the costs incurred in the certification process.
23 E. Behavioral health case managers certified by the Board or
24 the Commissioner shall only use the title "certified behavioral
SENATE FLOOR VERSION - HB4275 SFLR Page 2
(Bold face denotes Committee Amendments)
1 heal th case manager" if employed by the state, a municipality, or
2 county, employed by behavioral services providers contracting with
3 the state to provide behavioral health services, employed by
4 organizations certified by the Department of Mental Health and
5 Substance Abuse Services, employed by a tribe or tribal facility
6 that provides behavioral health services or employed by an Oklahoma
7 Department of Veterans Affairs or a United States Department of
8 Veterans Affairs facility. This section shall not be construed to
9 permit the certified behavioral health case manager to practice any
10 of the following professions or use the following titles unless also
11 licensed or accredited by the appropriate authority: physician,
12 psychologist, clinical social worker, professional counselor,
13 marital and family therapist, behavioral practitioner, or alcohol
14 and drug counselor.
15 F. Failure to comply with rules and standards promulgated by
16 the Board shall be grounds for revocation, suspension or nonrenewal
17 of certification.
18 G. No behavioral health case manager shall operate or continue
19 to operate as a behavioral health case manager unless the case
20 manager complies with the rules promulgated by the Board and is
21 certified as required by this section.

19 rules of professional conduct.
20 C. Application for certification as a peer recovery support

23 Commissioner of Mental Health and Substance Abuse Services upon
24 delegation by the Board, may certify the peer recovery support
