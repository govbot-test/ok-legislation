8 An Act relating to the State Department of Health;
transferring funds to the Barbara Weber Amyotrophic
9 Lateral Sclerosis (ALS) Grant Program Revolving Fund;
providing for noncodification; providing an effective
10 date; and declaring an emergency.
11
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
14 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be
15 codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
16 Of the Sixty-eight Million Three Hundred Forty-eight Thousand
17 Five Hundred Fifty-eight Dollars ($68,348,558.00) appropriated to
18 the State Department of Health in Section 73 of Enrolled Senate Bill
19 No. 1177 of the 2nd Regular Session of the 60th Oklahoma
20 Legislature, the sum of One Million Dollars ($1,000,000.00) shall be
21 transferred to the Barbara Weber Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis (ALS)
22 Grant Program Revolving Fund established in Section 1-580.2 of Title
23 63 of the Oklahoma Statutes.
24 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
ENGR. H. B. NO. 4029 Page 1
1 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
2 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
3 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
4 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
5 Passed the House of Representatives the 21st day of April, 2026.
