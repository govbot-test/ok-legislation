8 PROPOSED POLICY COMMITTEE SUBSTITUTE
9 An Act relating to medication; defining term;
directing the Oklahoma Health Care Authority to
10 partner with the University of Oklahoma Health and
Sciences Center to provide medication to certain
11 inmates; directing the Oklahoma Health Care Authority
to directly pay the University of Oklahoma Health and
12 Sciences Center for medication for certain inmates;
providing for promulgation of rules; providing for
13 codification; and providing an effective date.
14
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
17 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
18 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 7500 of Title 63, unless there
19 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
20 A. As used in this section, "medication" means prescription and
21 over-the-counter drugs.
22 B. The Oklahoma Health Care Authority shall partner with the
23 University of Oklahoma Health and Sciences Center to provide
24 medication to detainees and inmates under the jurisdiction of a
Req. No. 16241 Page 1
1 muni cipality or county, and not under the jurisdiction of the
2 Department of Corrections.
3 The Oklahoma Health Care Authority shall directly pay to the
4 University of Oklahoma Health and Sciences Center for medication
5 provided to detainees and inmates under the jurisdiction of a
6 municipality or county, not under the jurisdiction of the Department
7 of Corrections.
8 C. The Oklahoma Health Care Authority shall promulgate rules to
9 implement this section of law.
10 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
