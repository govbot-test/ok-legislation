7 An Act relating to medication; defining term;
directing the Oklahoma Health Care Authority to
8 partner with the University of Oklahoma Health and
Sciences Center to provide medication to certain
9 inmates; directing the Oklahoma Health Care Authority
to directly pay the University of Oklahoma Health and
10 Sciences Center for medication for certain inmates;
providing for promulgation of rules; providing for
11 codification; and providing an effective date.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
16 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 7500 of Title 63, unless there
17 is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
18 A. As used in this section, "medication" means prescription and
19 over-the-counter drugs.
20 B. The Oklahoma Health Care Authority shall partner with the
21 University of Oklahoma Health and Sciences Center to provide
22 medication to detainees and inmates under the jurisdiction of a
23 municipality or county, and not under the jurisdiction of the
24 Department of Corrections.
ENGR. H. B. NO. 2059 Page 1
1 The Oklahoma Health Care Authority shall directly pay to the
2 University of Oklahoma Health and Sciences Center for medication
3 provided to detainees and inmates under the jurisdiction of a
4 municipality or county, not under the jurisdiction of the Department
5 of Corrections.
6 C. The Oklahoma Health Care Authority shall promulgate rules to
7 implement this section.
8 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
9 Passed the House of Representatives the 23rd day of March, 2026.
