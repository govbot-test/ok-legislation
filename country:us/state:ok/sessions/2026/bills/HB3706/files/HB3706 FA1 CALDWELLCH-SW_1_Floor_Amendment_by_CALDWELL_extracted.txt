7 FLOOR SUBSTITUTE
8 An Act relating to education; requiring a minimum
amount of math instruction per day and week for
9 kindergarten through fifth grade students; requiring
math instruction to align with the Oklahoma Academic
10 Standards for Mathematics; providing for
codification; providing an effective date; and
11 declaring an emergency.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
16 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1210.908 of Title 70, unless
17 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
18 A. Beginning with the 2026-2027 school year, all public
19 elementary schools shall provide instruction in mathematics for:
20 1. A minimum of two hundred twenty-five (225) minutes each week
21 for no less than thirty (30) minutes per school day for students in
22 full-day kindergarten and grades one and two; and
23
24
Req. No. 16756 Page 1
1 2. A minimum of three hundred (300) minutes each week for no
2 less than thirty (30) minutes per school day for students in grades
3 three through five.
4 B. The instruction required by this section shall align with
5 the Oklahoma Academic Standards for Mathematics (OAS-M) as approved
6 by the State Board of Education.
7 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
8 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
9 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
10 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
11 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
