7 AS INTRODUCED
8 An Act relating to education; requiring a minimum of
60-minute math instruction per day for kindergarten
9 through fifth-grade students; requiring math
instruction to align with the Oklahoma Academic
10 Standards for Mathematics; providing for
codification; providing an effective date; and
11 declaring an emergency.
12
13
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
16 SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
17 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 1210.908 of Title 70, unless
18 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
19 Beginning with the 2026-2027 school year, all public elementary
20 schools shall provide instruction in mathematics for a minimum of
21 sixty (60) minutes each day for students in full-day kindergarten
22 and grades one through five. The instruction required by this
23 section shall align with the Oklahoma Academic Standards for
24 Mathematics (OAS-M) as approved by the State Board of Education.
