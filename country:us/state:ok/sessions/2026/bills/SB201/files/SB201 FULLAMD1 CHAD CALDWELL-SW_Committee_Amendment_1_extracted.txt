COMMITTEE AMENDMENT
HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
State of Oklahoma
SPEAKER:
CHAIR:
I move to amend SB201
Page 12 Section 1 Lines 6 1/2
Of the Engrossed Bill
By adding the following language: "J. Notwithstanding the definition of
certified personnel described in subsection A of this section, the
provisions of this section shall not apply to supervisors, school board
members, board of trustee members, commandant of cadets, deans, assistant
or vice principals, assistant superintendents, executive assistants, chief
financial officers, instructional program directors, managers,
noninstructional program directors, principals or heads of schools, chief
executive officers, and heads of charter schools.

Section 2:
SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be codified in the
Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:
The provisions of this act shall not become operative as law unless
enrolled House Bill 3705 of the Second Session of the 60th Oklahoma
Legislature is enacted as law."
And by modifying the outline to conform.
AMEND TITLE TO CONFORM TO AMENDMENTS
Amendment submitted by: Chad Caldwell
