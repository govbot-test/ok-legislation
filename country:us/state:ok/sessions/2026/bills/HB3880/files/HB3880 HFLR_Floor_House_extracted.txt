18 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 74 O.S. 2021, Section 2237, is
19 amended to read as follows:
20 Section 2237. A. There is hereby created within the Oklahoma
21 Tourism and Recreation Department, the "Oklahoma Today Magazine"
22 Division. The Division shall be responsible for the publication of
23 the "Oklahoma Today Magazine" a minimum of six times per year.
24

1 B. "Oklahoma Today Magazine" is hereby authorized to sell
2 advertising. All advertising shall be approved by the appropriate
3 division director prior to acceptance for publication.
4 C. In Subject to the discretion of the Executive Director, in
5 addition to a regular salary, any employee of the "Oklahoma Today
6 Magazine" Oklahoma Tourism and Recreation Department who obtains
7 advertising, sponsorship, bulk subscription, newsstand, or ancillary
8 product sales, for "Oklahoma Today Magazine", at the discretion of
9 the Executive Director any print or digital publication of the
10 Department, or in connection with any tourism promotion efforts of
11 the Department, may be awarded additional compensation in the form
12 of a commission on net sales by the employee. The commission shall
13 not exceed twenty-five percent (25%), with the commission percentage
14 to be determined by the Executive Director. In no case shall the
15 additional compensation, when combined with the salary of the
16 employee, exceed ninety-five percent (95%) of the salary of the
17 Executive Director. Commission payments shall be paid monthly,
18 based on collected revenues from sales by the employee.
19 D. The sale of advertising and negotiation of rates for the
20 advertising shall not be subject to The Oklahoma Central Purchasing
21 Act of 1974 or the Administrative Procedures Act.
22 E. "Oklahoma Today Magazine" articles, and photographs produced
23 by or for "Oklahoma Today Magazine", shall be available for public
24 inspection during Department business hours. The Department shall
