7 An Act relating to schools; amending 70 O.S. 2021,
Section 5-134, which relates to fiscal estimates;
8 amending 70 O.S. 2021, Section 5-155, which relates
to school district budgets; amending 70 O.S. 2021,
9 Section 15-102, which relates to school bond
elections; eliminating references to newspaper
10 publications; requiring school district preliminary
budget estimates, notices of proposed budget
11 hearings, and notices of levy and bond elections to
be posted on the website of the State Department of
12 Education; and providing an effective date.
