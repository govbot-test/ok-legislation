11 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 36 O.S. 2021, Section 985, is
12 amended to read as follows:
13 Section 985. Ratemaking Standards. A. A rate may not be
14 excessive, inadequate or unfairly discriminatory.
15 1. No rate in a competitive market may be determined to be
16 excessive. A rate in a noncompetitive market may be determined to
17 be excessive if it, based on actuarial review, the rate is likely to
18 produce a profit that is unreasonably high for the insurance
19 provided. The Insurance Commissioner shall send an objection to the
20 insurer and shall give the insurer the opportunity to respond and
21 provide additional supporting information prior to making a final
22 determination that the rate is excessive.
23 2. A rate may not be determined to be inadequate unless:

1 a. the rate is clearly insufficient to sustain projected
2 losses, expenses and special assessments, and
3 b. the rate is unreasonably low and use of the rate by
4 the insurer has tended or, if continued, will tend to
5 create a monopoly in the market.
6 3. Unfair discrimination may be determined to exist if, after
7 allowing for practical limitations, price differentials fail to
8 reflect equitably the differences in expected losses and expenses.
9 A rate may not be determined to be unfairly discriminatory because
10 different premiums result for policyholders with like loss exposures
11 but different expense levels, or like expenses but different loss
12 exposures, or if it averaged broadly among persons insured within a
13 group, franchise or blanket policy or a mass-marketed plan. No rate
14 in a competitive market shall be considered unfairly discriminatory
15 unless it classifies risk on the basis of race, color, creed, or
16 national origin, or religion.
17 B. In determining whether rates in a noncompetitive market are
18 excessive, inadequate, or unfairly discriminatory, due consideration
19 may be given to:
20 1. Past and prospective loss experience within and outside this
21 state, in accordance with accepted actuarial principles;
22 2. Conflagration and catastrophe hazards;
23 3. A reasonable margin for underwriting profit and
24 contingencies;

12 SECTION 3. AMENDATORY 36 O.S. 2021, Section 989, is
13 amended to read as follows:
14 Section 989. Improper Rates; Disapproval; Hearing. A. Basis
15 for disapproval.
16 1. The Insurance Commissioner shall disapprove a rate in a
17 competitive market only if the Commissioner finds, pursuant to
18 subsection B of this section, that the rate is excessive,
19 inadequate, or unfairly discriminatory pursuant to Section 985 of
20 this title.
21 2. The Commissioner may disapprove a rate for use in a
22 noncompetitive market only if the Commissioner finds, pursuant to
23 subsection B of this section, that the rate is excessive, inadequate
24 or unfairly discriminatory under this subsection.
