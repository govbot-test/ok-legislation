9 An Act relating to groundwater use; amending 82 O.S.
2021, Section 1020.17, which relates to spacing
10 rules; conforming statutory reference; removing
requirement for certain public hearing; authorizing
11 adoption of certain spacing; and providing an
effective date.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 82 O.S. 2021, Section 1020.17, is
16 amended to read as follows:
17 Section 1020.17. The Oklahoma Water Resources Board may
18 promulgate rules under Article I of the Administrative Procedures
19 Act which establish a proper spacing of wells which, in its
20 judgment, is necessary to an orderly withdrawal of water in relation
21 to the allocation, by regular permits and temporary permits, of
22 water to the land overlying the basin or subbasin. The Board shall
23 conduct at least one public hearing at a location within or in close
24 proximity to each major basin or subbasin before adopting rules

2 specific spacing for each basin as part of establishing a maximum
3 annual yield pursuant to Section 1020.6 of this title.
4 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
