11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
12 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 11 O.S. 2021, Section 50-109, as
13 last amended by Section 3, Chapter 357, O.S.L. 2024 (11 O.S. Supp.
14 2025, Section 50-109), is amended to read as follows:
15 Section 50-109. Any municipality participating in the Oklahoma
16 Police Pension and Retirement System shall appropriate funds, for
17 the use and benefit of the System, as provided in the following
18 schedule:
19 1. Prior to July 1, 1991, a minimum of ten percent (10%) of the
20 actual paid base salary of each member of the System employed by the
21 municipality;
22 2. Beginning July 1, 1991, a minimum of ten and one-half
23 percent (10 1/2%) of the actual paid base salary of each member of
24 the System employed by the municipality;

1 3. Beginning July 1, 1992, a minimum of eleven percent (11%) of
2 the actual paid base salary of each member of the System employed by
3 the municipality;
4 4. Beginning July 1, 1993, a minimum of eleven and one-half
5 percent (11 1/2%) of the actual paid base salary of each member of
6 the System employed by the municipality;
7 5. Beginning July 1, 1994, a minimum of twelve percent (12%) of
8 the actual paid base salary of each member of the System employed by
9 the municipality;
10 6. Beginning July 1, 1995, a minimum of twelve and one-half
11 percent (12 1/2%) of the actual paid base salary of each member of
12 the System employed by the municipality;
13 7. Beginning July 1, 1996, a minimum of thirteen percent (13%)
14 of the actual paid base salary of each member of the System employed
15 by the municipality; and
16 8. Beginning on the effective date of this act July 1, 2025, a
17 minimum of fourteen percent (14%) sixteen percent (16%) of the
18 actual paid base salary of each member of the System employed by the
19 municipality.
20 The sum appropriated shall be paid online to the System within
21 ten (10) days following the payroll period on which the contribution
22 is based.
23 The state shall make such appropriation as is necessary to
24 assure the retirement benefits provided by the article.

1 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
2 SECTION 3. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
3 of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby
4 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
5 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
