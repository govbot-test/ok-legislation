11 An Act relating to the State Department of Health;
making an appropriation; stating purpose; requiring
12 that portions of certain appropriated funds be used
for certain purposes; stating purpose of certain
13 disbursement; requiring the use of certain data;
directing the Department to develop method of
14 disbursement; authorizing promulgation of rules;
providing an effective date; and declaring an
15 emergency.
16
17
18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
19 SECTION 1. There is hereby appropriated to the State Department
20 of Health from any monies not otherwise appropriated from the
21 General Revenue Fund of the State Treasury for the fiscal year
22 ending June 30, 2027, the sum of One Hundred Fifty Thousand Dollars
23 ($150,000.00) or so much thereof as may be necessary to carry out
24
Req. No. 3913 Page 1
1 the provisions of Enrolled House Bill No. 3644 of the 2nd Session of
2 the 60th Oklahoma Legislature.
3 SECTION 2. From the funds appropriated to the State Department
4 of Health in Enrolled Senate Bill No. 1177 of the 2nd Session of the
5 60th Oklahoma Legislature, the sum of One Hundred Fifty Thousand
6 Dollars ($150,000.00) shall be used to maintain sickle cell
7 outreach.
8 SECTION 3. From the funds appropriated to the State Department
9 of Health in Enrolled Senate Bill No. 1177 of the 2nd Session of the
10 60th Oklahoma Legislature, the sum of One Million Dollars
11 ($1,000,000.00) shall be used to contract with or employ a financial
12 expert to monitor and report to the Legislature the financial well-
13 being of the budget of the Department of Mental Health and Substance
14 Abuse Services.
15 SECTION 4. From the funds appropriated to the State Department
16 of Health in Enrolled Senate Bill No. 1177 of the 2nd Session of the
17 60th Oklahoma Legislature, the sum of Three Million Dollars
18 ($3,000,000.00) shall be disbursed to health centers authorized
19 under Section 330 of the Public Health Service Act, and incorporated
20 in the State of Oklahoma. Any disbursement shall be for the purpose
21 of increasing access to primary health care which may include
22 medical, dental, and/or mental health and substance abuse treatment
23 services in or near designated Medically Underserved Areas or
24 Medically Underserved Populations. Any disbursement for increased
Req. No. 3913 Page 2
1 acce ss to primary health care services shall be supported by data
2 according to proportions of patient volume and access for the
3 uninsured. The method of disbursement shall be developed by the
4 State Department of Health. The State Commissioner of Health is
5 authorized to promulgate the method of disbursement into rules.
6 SECTION 5. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
7 SECTION 6. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
8 of the public peace, health, or safety, an emergency is hereby
9 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
10 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
