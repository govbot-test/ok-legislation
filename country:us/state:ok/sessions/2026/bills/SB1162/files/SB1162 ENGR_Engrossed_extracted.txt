7 An Act relating to the State Department of Health;
making an appropriation; stating purpose; requiring
8 that portions of certain appropriated funds be used
for certain purposes; stating purpose of certain
9 disbursement; requiring the use of certain data;
directing the Department to develop method of
10 disbursement; authorizing promulgation of rules;
providing an effective date; and declaring an
11 emergency.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. There is hereby appropriated to the State Department
16 of Health from any monies not otherwise appropriated from the
17 General Revenue Fund of the State Treasury for the fiscal year
18 ending June 30, 2027, the sum of One Hundred Fifty Thousand Dollars
19 ($150,000.00) or so much thereof as may be necessary to carry out
20 the provisions of Enrolled House Bill No. 3644 of the 2nd Session of
21 the 60th Oklahoma Legislature.
22 SECTION 2. From the funds appropriated to the State Department
23 of Health in Enrolled Senate Bill No. 1177 of the 2nd Session of the
24 60th Oklahoma Legislature, the sum of One Hundred Fifty Thousand
ENGR. S. B. NO. 1162 Page 1
1 Doll ars ($150,000.00) shall be used to maintain sickle cell
2 outreach.
3 SECTION 3. From the funds appropriated to the State Department
4 of Health in Enrolled Senate Bill No. 1177 of the 2nd Session of the
5 60th Oklahoma Legislature, the sum of One Million Dollars
6 ($1,000,000.00) shall be used to contract with or employ a financial
7 expert to monitor and report to the Legislature the financial well-
8 being of the budget of the Department of Mental Health and Substance
9 Abuse Services.
10 SECTION 4. From the funds appropriated to the State Department
11 of Health in Enrolled Senate Bill No. 1177 of the 2nd Session of the
12 60th Oklahoma Legislature, the sum of Three Million Dollars
13 ($3,000,000.00) shall be disbursed to health centers authorized
14 under Section 330 of the Public Health Service Act, and incorporated
15 in the State of Oklahoma. Any disbursement shall be for the purpose
16 of increasing access to primary health care which may include
17 medical, dental, and/or mental health and substance abuse treatment
18 services in or near designated Medically Underserved Areas or
19 Medically Underserved Populations. Any disbursement for increased
20 access to primary health care services shall be supported by data
21 according to proportions of patient volume and access for the
22 uninsured. The method of disbursement shall be developed by the
23 State Department of Health. The State Commissioner of Health is
24 authorized to promulgate the method of disbursement into rules.
ENGR. S. B. NO. 1162 Page 2
1 SECTION 5. This act shall become effective July 1, 2026.
2 SECTION 6. It being immediately necessary for the preservation
3 of the public peace, health, or safety, an emergency is hereby
4 declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and
5 be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
6 Passed the Senate the 14th day of April, 2026.
