9 An Act relating to the Oklahoma Abstractors Act;
amending 1 O.S. 2021, Section 38, which relates to
10 qualifications for issuance of license; allowing the
Oklahoma Abstractors Board to deny certain applicant;
11 updating statutory language; and providing an
effective date.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 1 O.S. 2021, Section 38, is
16 amended to read as follows:
17 Section 38. A. An abstract license shall be issued by the
18 Oklahoma Abstractors Board to an applicant who:
19 1. Is eighteen (18) years of age or older;
20 2. Is of good moral character;
21 3. Has not been convicted of or pleaded guilty or nolo
22 contendere to a felony or crime of moral turpitude or disqualifying
23 offense that substantially relates to the practice of abstracting or
24
SB1287 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 pose s a reasonable threat to public safety in this state, another
2 state, or a federal court; and
3 4. Has passed a test for abstractors required by the Board.
4 B. The Board shall deny an abstract license to an applicant who
5 does not have legal authorization to work, is not residing in, or is
6 not physically present in the United States of America.
7 C. Each abstract license shall be valid for one (1) year. The
8 Board shall set the fees for an abstract license and for renewal not
9 to exceed One Hundred Fifty Dollars ($150.00).
10 C. D. As used in this section:
11 1. “Poses a reasonable threat” means the nature of criminal
12 conduct for which the person applicant was convicted involved an act
13 or threat of harm against another and has a bearing on the fitness
14 or ability to serve the public or work with others in the
15 occupation; and
16 2. “Substantially relates” means the nature of criminal conduct
17 for which the person applicant was convicted has a direct bearing on
18 the fitness or ability to perform one or more of the duties or
19 responsibilities necessarily related to the occupation.
20 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
