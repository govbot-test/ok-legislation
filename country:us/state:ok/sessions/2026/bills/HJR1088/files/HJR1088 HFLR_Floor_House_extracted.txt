13 WHEREAS, pursuant to Section 308 of Title 75 of the Oklahoma
14 Statutes, the Oklahoma State Legislature shall adopt a joint
15 resolution to approve or disapprove administrative rules.
16 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
17 AND THE SENATE OF THE 2ND SESSION OF THE 60TH OKLAHOMA LEGISLATURE:
18 SECTION 1. The Legislature hereby approves all proposed
19 permanent rules of the State Department of Education filed on or
20 before February 1, 2026.
21 SECTION 2. The Legislature hereby approves all proposed
22 permanent rules of the Office of Educational Quality and
23 Accountability filed on or before February 1, 2026.
