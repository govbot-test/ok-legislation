7 An Act relating to air emission standards; defining
terms; requiring municipal solid waste incinerators
8 to develop certain plan; listing emissions to sample
as part of plan; stating requirements of plan;
9 requiring plan contain certain description; requiring
submission of plan within certain time frame;
10 authorizing Department of Environmental Quality to
modify plan; requiring implementation of plan within
11 certain time frame; authorizing extension of certain
timeline; requiring the submission of certain
12 reports; limiting the amount of certain type of waste
which may be incinerated annually; providing for
13 codification; and providing an effective date.

1 SECTION 2. NEW LAW A new section of law to be codified
2 in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 2-5-132 of Title 27A, unless
3 there is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:
4 A. As used in this section:
5 1. "Hospital, medical, or infectious waste" means hospital
6 waste or medical or infectious waste, as those terms are defined in
7 40 C.F.R., Section 60.51c, as in effect on the effective date of
8 this act.
9 2. "Municipal solid waste incinerator" means any facility
10 operated before, on, or after the effective date of this act for the
11 purpose of combusting municipal solid waste, regardless of whether
12 the facility is later reclassified as another type of waste
13 combustion facility.
14 B. A municipal solid waste incinerator may not combust more
15 than eighteen thousand (18,000) tons of hospital, medical, or
16 infectious waste during a single calendar year.
17 SECTION 3. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
