7 An Act relating to crimes and punishments; amending

Section 2:
Section 2, Chapter 336, O.S.L. 2025 (21 O.S. Supp.
8 2025, Section 365), which relates to the corrupt use
of nonpublic information; prohibiting current or
9 former officers, employees, or contractors of a
political subdivision from corrupt use of nonpublic
10 information; clarifying that violation shall be
grounds for termination from government employment;
11 and providing an effective date.
12
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
15 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY Section 2, Chapter 336, O.S.L.
16 2025 (21 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section 365), is amended to read as
17 follows:
18 A. Any current or former officer, employee, or contractor of a
19 state agency or political subdivision who obtains or obtained
20 nonpublic information because of his or her government employment,
21 government office, or contract with a state agency or political
22 subdivision, and who corruptly, as defined by Section 94 of Title 21
23 of the Oklahoma Statutes:
24 1. Uses such information for personal gain or benefit;
Req. No. 15276 Page 1
1 2. Acquires or divests a pecuniary interest in any property,
2 transaction, or enterprise which may be affected by such
3 information;
4 3. Speculates or wagers on the basis of such information;
5 4. Uses such information in a way that would affect any
6 personal pecuniary interest, including an interest held by an
7 immediate family member, or business entity with which the immediate
8 family member is associated;
9 5. Discloses such information to any person, group, entity, or
10 others not entitled to receive such information; or
11 6. Aids another to commit any act set forth in paragraphs 1
12 through 5 of this subsection, shall, upon conviction, be guilty of a
13 felony punishable by a fine not to exceed Ten Thousand Dollars
14 ($10,000.00), by imprisonment in the custody of the Department of
15 Corrections for a term not to exceed five (5) years, or by both such
16 fine and imprisonment. Any person convicted of violating any
17 provision of this section shall be prohibited from holding any
18 public office in this state and shall be prohibited from entering
19 into any state contract with a state agency or political
20 subdivision.
21 B. Violation of any provision of this section shall be grounds
22 for removal from office or termination from state government
23 employment.
24
Req. No. 15276 Page 2
1 C. For purposes of this section, the term "immediate family
2 member" means:
3 1. A spouse;
4 2. A spouse's parents;
5 3. A child by birth or adoption;
6 4. A stepchild;
7 5. A parent;
8 6. A grandparent;
9 7. A grandchild;
10 8. A sibling;
11 9. A spouse's siblings; or
12 10. A spouse of any immediate family member listed in this
13 subsection.
14 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
