18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:
19 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY Section 2, Chapter 336, O.S.L.
20 2025 (21 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section 365), is amended to read as
21 follows:
22 A. Any current or former officer, employee, or contractor of a
23 state agency or political subdivision who obtains or obtained
24 nonpublic information because of his or her government employment,
HB3419 HFLR Page 1
BOLD FACE denotes Committee Amendments.
1 gove rnment office, or contract with a state agency or political
2 subdivision, and who corruptly, as defined by Section 94 of Title 21
3 of the Oklahoma Statutes:
4 1. Uses such information for personal gain or benefit;
5 2. Acquires or divests a pecuniary interest in any property,
6 transaction, or enterprise which may be affected by such
7 information;
8 3. Speculates or wagers on the basis of such information;
9 4. Uses such information in a way that would affect any
10 personal pecuniary interest, including an interest held by an
11 immediate family member, or business entity with which the immediate
12 family member is associated;
13 5. Discloses such information to any person, group, entity, or
14 others not entitled to receive such information; or
15 6. Aids another to commit any act set forth in paragraphs 1
16 through 5 of this subsection, shall, upon conviction, be guilty of a
17 felony punishable by a fine not to exceed Ten Thousand Dollars
18 ($10,000.00), by imprisonment in the custody of the Department of
19 Corrections for a term not to exceed five (5) years, or by both such
20 fine and imprisonment. Any person convicted of violating any
21 provision of this section shall be prohibited from holding any
22 public office in this state and shall be prohibited from entering
23 into any state contract with a state agency or political
24 subdivision.

1 B. Violation of any provision of this section shall be grounds
2 for removal from office or termination from state government
3 employment.
4 C. For purposes of this section, the term "immediate family
5 member" means:
6 1. A spouse;
7 2. A spouse's parents;
8 3. A child by birth or adoption;
9 4. A stepchild;
10 5. A parent;
11 6. A grandparent;
12 7. A grandchild;
13 8. A sibling;
14 9. A spouse's siblings; or
15 10. A spouse of any immediate family member listed in this
16 subsection.
17 SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2026.
