6 An Act relating to schools; amending 70 O.S. 2021,
Section 11-103.9, which relates to physical education
7 program requirements; requiring certain schools to
provide students in certain grades certain amount of
8 daily recess as a condition of accreditation;
allowing daily recess to be broken into segments;
9 defining term; requiring daily recess to occur
outdoors when permitted; removing language
10 encouraging certain amount of recess in certain
grades; requiring certain subject matter standards to
11 address certain plan; updating statutory language;
providing an effective date; and declaring an
12 emergency.

16 SECTION 1. AMENDATORY 70 O.S. 2021, Section 11-103.9, is
17 amended to read as follows:
18 Section 11-103.9. A. Except as otherwise provided for in this
19 section, the State Board of Education shall require, as a condition
20 of accreditation, that school districts provide to all students
21 physical education programs which may include athletics.
22 B. The Board shall require, as a condition of accreditation,
23 that public elementary schools provide instruction, for students in
24 full-day kindergarten and grades one through five, in physical

1 F. G. The physical education curriculum shall be sequential,
2 developmentally appropriate, and designed, implemented, and
3 evaluated to enable students to develop the motor and self-
4 management skills and knowledge necessary to participate in physical
5 activity throughout life. Each school district shall establish

11 1. Emphasize the knowledge and skills capable of being used
