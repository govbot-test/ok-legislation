8 POLICY COMMITTEE RECOMMENDATION
9 An Act relating to criminal procedure; amending 22
O.S. 2021, Section 2002, as amended by Section 2,
10 Chapter 327, O.S.L. 2025 (22 O.S. Supp. 2025, Section
2002), which relates to the Oklahoma Criminal
11 Discovery Code; directing the state to disclose
certain evidence prior to the initial appearance of
12 defendants; requiring the disclosure of evidence
favorable to the defendant; requiring disclosure
13 despite its form; declaring the ongoing duty to
disclose information; increasing time limitation for
14 introducing testimony from informants and
codefendants; updating informant references;
15 modifying scope of certain defined term; clarifying
disclosure duties of defendants; providing for the
16 filing of discovery motions any time following
initial appearances; establishing time limitation for
17 completing discovery issues; providing an exception
to certain witnesses; authorizing courts to compel
18 discovery or order sanctions; directing courts to
order disclosure of evidence and sanctions upon
19 certain finding; providing exceptions; directing
courts to make certain considerations when ordering
20 sanctions; specifying types of available sanctions;
requiring the disclosure of certain documents
21 favorable to defendants; and providing an effective
date.
